# Reaver

## Reaver

### Type

Melee DPS/Tank

### Primary stats

Strength, Intelligence, Agility

Reavers are powerful warriors who train in combat stances in order to adapt to the changing situations of battle. Reavers make use of gain access to four stances, two focused on improving ability to serve as a tank, while two focused on DPS.

As a DPS, the Reaver makes use of a combination of stats, including Intelligence, Strength and Dexterity. Reaver is a high-risk, high-reward DPS playstyle that tries to maintain low health to increase their damage output. DPS Reavers should use a 2-handed weapon.

As a tank, the Reaver is very good at maintaining aggro, but has noticeably weaker defenses than a Paladin. Like Paladin tanks, both Endurance and Agility offer the most significant defensive benefits. However, they can output much higher damage than a Paladin tank, however, and gain access to utility like fear abilities and slows which are not available to paladins. A Reaver tank can either use a 1-hander and shield or a 2-handed weapon.
