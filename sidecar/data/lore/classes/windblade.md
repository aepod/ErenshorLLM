# Windblade

# Windblade

## Windblade

### Type

DPS fighter

### Primary stats

Dexterity and Strength

The Windblade is a physical fighter. The Windblade should focus on Dexterity and Strength. The Windblade is a master of offensive combat and loves to get in close to strike mortal wounds with their duel wielding talent. They excel at lifestealing which allows them to stay in the fight much longer than most, have skills to increase their attack speed, attacks per turn and increased critical hit rates which ensures anything unlucky enough to cross their path will know for just a moment what it's like to look death in the face.

They bring utility to the group in the form of debuffs which reduce their target's attack speed.
