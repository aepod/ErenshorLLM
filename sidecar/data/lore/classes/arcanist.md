# Arcanist

# Arcanist

## Arcanist

### Type

DPS caster

### Primary stats

Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma

The Arcanist is a magical being who thrives in cloth armor. The Arcanist's major stat focus is on Intelligence due to scaling for high DPS with stats like Wisdom and Charisma not scaling as much. More Wisdom will increase mana regen, cast time and cooldown. More charisma increases the chance of hitting enemies with spells.Arcanists are great DPS and they focus on crowd control to mitigate damage while blasting down their group targets with elements such as fire and ice.
