# Paladin

## Paladin

### Type

Tank / DPS

### Primary stats

Strength, Endurance, Agility

### Secondary stats

Dexterity

**Paladin** is one of the playable classes in Erenshor.

## Description

The Paladin is a physical fighter with both great defensive and offensive capabilities. They excel when using heavy armor and either two-handing heavy weapons or using a shield. Depending on their build, Paladins should focus on Strength for more damage, Endurance for more health, Agility for a higher base AC, and Resists for protection versus spells and elemental damage.

The Paladin is a master of defensive combat, and can mitigate damage for their team through healing and by generating threat to gain the attention of enemies. They also have access to some excellent crowd control and AoE abilities. Being a Paladin connects you heavily to The Solunarian Brotherhood.

## Builds

There are two primary builds for Paladins: **Tank** and **DPS**.

### Tank

As the name implies, tank Paladins focus on drawing and keeping enemy aggression by building up aggro through the use of  Lunar Madness and its later variants. A shield is a tank Paladin's most important tool, allowing them to both absorb hits after learning the various  Block skills, as well as use  Shield Bash to deal additional damage and proc their shield's ability.

Positioning is incredibly important, as **only enemies in front of the Paladin have a chance of their attacks being blocked.**

Tank Paladins should focus primarily on gear that raises their HP, AC, Endurance, and Resists to absorb the most damage possible. For a list of the best equipment for each slot, see Recommended Gear.

### DPS

DPS Paladins forgo the defense offered by shields in favor of wielding powerful 2-Handed Weapons. In addition to their high base stats, these weapons also receive an additional **70% bonus** to their DPS, making them an ideal choice for Paladins focusing on damage output.

Speccing into a 2-Handed DPS build also allows Paladins use of these weapons' related skills, such as  Cleave for AoE damage and  Stunning Strike for high single-target damage and crowd control.

While Strength and Dexterity are important for DPS Paladins, they should also prioritize Resonance to take full advantage of the on-hit effects many 2-Handed Weapons come equipped with. For a list of the best equipment for each slot, see Recommended Gear.
