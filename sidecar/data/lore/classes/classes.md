# Classes

# Classes

Erenshor features six classes: Arcanist, Druid, Paladin, Reaver, Stormcaller, and Windblade.

### Arcanist

The Arcanist is a magical being, who thrives in cloth armor.

Arcanists command magic of all types and they have many tools to avoid close physical combat.

An Arcanist should focus on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma.

### Druid

The Druid is a master of all things nature - both pleasant and unpleasant.

The Druid can call upon not only the force of life, but he also commands the power of death.

A Druid should focus on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma.

### Paladin

The Paladin is a master of weapon combat and excels at using heavy armor and weapon types.

To sustain in battle, a Paladin can depend on Solunarian Magic of the Day and Night.

A Paladin should focus on Strength, Agility, and Endurance.

### Reaver

Reavers are powerful warriors who train in combat stances in order to adapt to the changing situations of battle.

A Reaver has the ability to both deal and sustain heavy damage, and is a good compliment to any party composition.

Reavers should focus on Strength, Intelligence, and Agility.

### Stormcaller

The Stormcaller is a master of bows and lightning magic.

Stormcallers can imbue their arrows with devastating magical attacks and supplement that damage with various status effects from bow skills.

A Stormcaller should focus on Intelligence, Strength, and Dexterity.

### Windblade

The Windblade is a master of offensive combat and is best while using light armor and weapon types.

Windblades thrive on combat. They gain health by dealing damage, and excel at crippling their opponents' abilities.

A Windblade should focus on Strength, Dexterity, and Intelligence.
