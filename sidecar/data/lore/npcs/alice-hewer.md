Alice Hewer is the quest giver for Alice Hewer's Necklace. She's on the Docks on Stowaway's Step. To complete this quest kill A Brackish Croc Whelp and return the Plain Necklace to her.

Greeting message:

A croc jumped right out of the water and I got so scared I dropped my necklace... and he ATE IT! He went off towards the lighthouse... if you get it back I'll be so grateful!

Greeting message after completing Alice Hewer's Necklace:

Thanks again!

Prompts:

necklace and croc: He ate my necklace and ran off...
lighthouse and where: It's right over there... the lighthouse is just past the office.

Giving her the  Plain Necklace for Alice Hewer's Necklace:

Thank you! Here, take this as a token of my appreciation!

Giving her another  Plain Necklace after completing Alice Hewer's Necklace:

You've already given me my necklace back. This one's not mine... wierd that you found two!
