He's on the Docks on Stowaway's Step. Kio is required for Kio's Papers.

Stowaway's Step Dialogue

Greeting message:

When you stare at the sea too long, it can start to peer back... it's full of eyes. So many eyes...

Prompts:

eyes: The sea warps their eyes... their eyes... do not let them gaze upon you!
occuphage: Do not look at him! Do not let him see your eyes!
weird, crazy, nuts and strange: I wish it was that simple...

Giving  Kio's Leave Order to Kio during Kio's Papers:

I...am so happy. I will leave this place at once... it's cursed here! You'll see! All of you will see! Take this wretched key and be away with you!

Giving  Kio's Leave Order to Kio after finishing Kio's Papers:

You've done this to me already! Why rub it in?

Shivering Step Dialogue

Greeting message:

A torch to carry! You'll scare the frights! You'll need to see, in this light!

_Kio will then give the player a Shivering Torch which they will then need to equip in their offhand. The lighting will change to be very dark once you have the it.

You will then get a new quest Find The Hider_
