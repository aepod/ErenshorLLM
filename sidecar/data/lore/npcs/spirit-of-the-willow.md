The Spirit of the Willow is an NPC in Fernalla's Revival Plains that allows players to respec their proficiency points. When players approach, they will receive a message "A sensation of free will washes over you..." indicating they are able to respec their proficiency points.

Greeting message:

"A place of rebirth invites reinvention. Quiet your confusion, set right your wrongs, and stand as yourself.

(At this place, you may respec your proficiencies, and those of your friends - open your inventory or theirs)"

Prompts:

rebirth: Fernalla took her true form in this very spot, and left behind The Willow in her wake. Her gift to all of us is the opportunity to find our own truths.
