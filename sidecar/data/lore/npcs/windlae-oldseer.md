Windlae is an NPC in Hidden Hills. She is a Vendor for Stormcallers and quest giver for Grizzlepaw's Chain.

Greeting message:

Ah, welcome to the settlement. Looking for a way to test

Prompts:

test: There's an old bear in the Brake - Grizzlepaw. Wedged in his teeth, there is an old chain from a pendant. Bring it to me, and I'll pay handsomly.
chain: Belonged to a friend of mine, I suspect. Haven't seen it myself, but have heard enough tales to believe it's true.

If you give her Weathered Chain for the quest Grizzlepaw's Chain:

Ah, so it is true then. Tragedy has befallen my friend but at least revenge has been taken. Thank you for your services, please take this as a token of my appreciation.
