Elden is located at the archaeologist dig site in the Braxonian Desert. He is the quest giver for The Scent of Brashell

Handing him both Braxonian Scriptures completes Braxonian Scripture

Greeting message:

We have reason to believe that the lost people of this place may exist beneath our very feet.

Prompts:

people: Our lost ancestors, buried by these holy sands sowed by Brax Himself to stop the blight from consuming the world. The writings we've uncovered allude to warnings given by Brax, and hint at a network of underground tunnels meant to sustain life.
blight: The spire to the east sprung from the ground centuries ago and with it came decay and disease. It spread rapidly through the land until Brax scorched this place with a flourish of His Flameblade. In doing so, he sacrificed his own followers. It is our duty to uncover their story, and honor them in history.
tunnels: This grate has proved impenetrable, but something lies below. A constant wind blows from it. The air smells of Brashell... Brashell was thought to be extinct - it is an herb used in Braxonian rituals long ago.

_This beginsThe Scent of Brashell_
impenetrable: We've been unable to move it. If you can find a way in... the things you may find may change our world forever. Please, you look like the type to try. If you do, keep us apprised of your findings.

Completing The Scent of Brashell will prompt the response:

Where... did you get this? This is unheard of! If you find more remnants of the old Braxonians, bring them to me - here, a reward for your troubles and a token of friendship.

Giving an extra Brashell will prompt Elden to say:

It's so interesting to see this!

Giving a single Braxonian Scripture will prompt Elden to say:

Incredible.. there seems to be another page that goes with this. Do you have it?

Giving both Braxonian Scriptures will prompt Elden to say:

Amazing! So Brax's decision to smite this land was made easy by the defiance of his own followers! Had they been less selfish, the entire land may have suffered. Thius is incredible history.. here, have this as a reward for your troubles!
