Hand him 4 treasure map pieces to complete the repeatable quest Repairing Treasure Maps

Greeting message:

I'm afraid I can't be much help to you. I'm just a writer. I could give you directions. I suppose, If you wanted me to.

Prompts:

directions: Port Azure is just down the road past the next house. Across the pond along the road, that's Fernalla's Revival Plains. If you take a right before you get to town, you'll find Vitheo's Watch and an old abandoned Fort called Krakengard.
writer: Wow, I don't get asked to elaborate on that very often! I write about lost treasures. I study maps, can usually put 'em together and find their secrets pretty quickly.
