Cerbantias is an NPC located by the Port Azure Lighthouse. This NPC will refuse to talk to you if you are any class other than Arcanist. He is the quest giver for Accessing the Ethereal Planes.

Greeting message only for Arcanists:

For brax, brother. You've found us! The brotherhood of Braxtons has given us quarter, and it is from here we will launch our expeditions!

Prompts:

Braxtons and brotherhood: They follow the ways of Brax just as we Arcanists do. They just do it for less... material reasons. They still offer a lot of knowledge that we all can still benefit from.
launch: There are artifacts in other worlds that were not meant for human hands, and by the gods we'll prove we can manage them! Our hunt began centuries ago and we are close now!
hunt or expeditions!: The keys to the ethereal planes are not easy to find, but rumours of their existences do grace the winds. We must be diligent.
