Tristan is a shady looking NPC located just inside the main gates of Port Azure down a little alley in-between a house and the rampart. He is the quest giver for A Magical Sword in Port Azure.

Greeting message:

You know anything bout swords? I've come into possession of a few that are real special. I might pass one of 'em over to you, for a favor...

Prompts:

swords: Color me surprised. I figured you wouldn't have the time to bother. I need some sea spice - love the stuff, hate huntin' round to get it. You can find it here and there. Bring me a couple sea spices - two should do me for a while. And for my wife, bring me a ruby. A real one, no fakes. Then I'll pass you the sword. It's a nice one, let me tell ya!

_This begins the questA Magical Sword in Port Azure_

If you give him one Ruby and two Seaspice for A Magical Sword in Port Azure:

You found it all? Well, deal's a deal. Here's your sword. Hope you can use it better than I would've!
