Chance is the NPC on the sandbar beneath the statue of Vitheo ready to take you to the mainland should you complete Whispers of Wyland and receive A Way To Erenshor.

Greeting message:

So you're the one who's tagging along? Fine. Not a word of what you've seen though, or else you'll be a topic we revisit. Are you ready to go?

Prompts:

If you say ready or go: Good. The ship is coming soon. Jump in one of these boats and we'll row out to meet it. We'll drop you away from the city so we don't raise suspicion around you. Then we're square.

yes: Go to the boats and we'll leave.
