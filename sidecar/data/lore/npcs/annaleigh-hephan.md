Annaleigh lives in Blacksalt Strand by a windmill on the hill. She is the quest giver for Annaleigh's Nagalok Claws.

Greeting message:

Heya! What brings ya to The Strand? Quite a place, it is. Claimed my little corner here. Self sufficient, I am! And an inventor too!

Prompts:

inventor: I can make anything outta anything! Know what I'm most interested in though? Some of those Nagalok claws. Ugly monsters, them. Moved in up the hill a while back and leave me alone mostly, but I betcha I could make a thing or two from those claws. Bring me a few - say... 5? You can keep whatever it is I whip up.
