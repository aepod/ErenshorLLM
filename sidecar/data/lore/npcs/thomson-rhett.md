Thomson is an NPC located in Fernalla's Revival Plains in an isolated house. He is traumatised by the Sivakayans' uprising and wants to find refuge ASAP.

Greeting message if you are a Paladin?:

Those Sivakayans must have marched right by my door. I didn't hear a thing... I can't stay here anymore after this - what if they came here??

Greeting message for everyone else:

My apologies, traveller, but I don't have time for you right now.
