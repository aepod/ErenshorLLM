Yarren is an NPC located on the beach at his campsite at night. He is part of the quest The Mathers' Demise. And giving him the  The Mathers Note triggers the 2nd part of the quest.

Greeting message:

You're not who I'm looking for.

Prompts:

looking: Eldoth.
Eldoth: Have you seen him? It's imperative that I know where he is.
Brice: Brice...? What about him? Did he give you a message for me?

When you give him  The Mathers Note:

Oh no... this was Brice's worst fear! We believe Eldoth is involved with The Wisp and that he is behind something far more sinister than I have time to explain. Brice gave me this key for safe keeping. He was afraid Eldoth's men would come for it. He discovered an old Sivakayan church buried in the hills of Blacksalt and this key is the only one in existence. See what's in there... and bring any tomes you find back to me. For Brice...

If you give him another  The Mathers Note:

You have the key... if you've lost it I don't know what to tell you. I do not have another.

If you give him parts 1-4 of the   Passages of Descent:

Oh my, this is alarming! These are the pages Eldoth must have been after when he killed Brice for the church key! Eldoth is trying to uncover information about Sivakaya or The Wisp... but why? If you discover anything more, show me!

_This begins the questThe Mather's Demise Part 3._
