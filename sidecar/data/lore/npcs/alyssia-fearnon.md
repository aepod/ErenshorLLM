Alyssia is a vendor in Hidden Hills, she is a Druid Merchant. She is the quest giver for A Skeleton Eye for Alyssia Fearnon.

Greeting message (Druids Only):

Welcome to our little settlement. We're all fellow adventurers here. Take a look at what i've got for sale... I'm sure it would be useful for you. I could also use a favor

Prompts:

favor: There's a place just down the road tucked into the cliffside called 'The Bonepits'. It's full of skeletons and probably some necromancers. I need a skeleton eye... it's rare to find one on a dead skeleton, but they do exist. Bring me one, and I've got something in it for you in return.

_This begins the questA Skeleton Eye for Alyssia Fearnon_
