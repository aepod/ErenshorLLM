Leyor is an NPC located in the church-like building in Port Azure. And is the Quest giver for Soluna's Flameblade (Paladin Quest). This NPC will refuse to talk to you if you are any class other than Paladin.

Greeting message only for Paladins:

Another Paladin is always welcome here. Take comfort in this place, it was the first building raised in Port Azure. Praise Soluna! Have you found your destiny yet?

Prompts:

destiny: Your destiny is for you alone to determine. For some, it's power and for others it's fortune. For me, it's Soluna's Celestial Blade.
Celestial, Blade: The very sword Soluna bestowed upon her avatar here in these lands and now lost to time, it's nothing short of fabled. Where to look - that's another question that I do not have the answer to.
