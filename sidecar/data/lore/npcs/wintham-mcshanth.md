Wintham is an NPC located in Port Azure beside the Church as you head to the Port Azure Lighthouse. He is also found in Fernalla's Revival Plains in Restful Valley.

## in Port Azure:

Greeting message:

"This town holds secrets, lots of 'em. The kind that are best not found. Stay out of the shadows. That's my advice."

Prompts:

shadows

"There's dark corners here. All around. Under your feet, even. Seen 'em with my own eyes. Lucky, I guess, that I'm still here. If you listen close in the right places, you can hear... their presence."
