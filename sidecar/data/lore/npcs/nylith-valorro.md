Nylith is an NPC in Hidden Hills. He is a Vendor for Arcanists and quest giver for Faerie Dust for Nylith Valarro.

Greeting message:

If you're learning the ways of the wizard, I have some spells you may need. Buy 'em or don't, doesn't bother me any. Need a job to do? Got one of them too.

Prompts:

job, help and quest: If you head over into Faerie's Brake, and you can track down one of those nasty little Faeries, kill it. If it has any Faerie dust, bring it to me. I've got something for you if you do.
