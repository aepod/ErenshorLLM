Orion is an NPC located in the outskirts of Port Azure by a shrine. And is the Quest giver for The Great Willow's Branch (Druid Quest). This NPC will refuse to talk to you if you are any class other than Druid.

Greeting message only for Druids:

Fellow child, Fernalla bless you. Step into our circle and stay a while. Our cause and crusade continues.

Prompts:

cause: Lots of trouble! There's Molarai in the old beach fort, the beach is swarmin' with crocs, and those giant ghosts in the ruins... no thank
crusade: Fernalla willing, we'll recover the fallen branch of Fernalla's willow, and our chosen one will wield it in our name.
willow, chosen and one: A tree so large it can't be seen by human eyes. A tree so lush it cannot be felt by human hands. Its branches fall from the heavens and land with such impact that a human does not hear. The one who holds the branches, sees all and becomes everything.

_This begins the questThe Great Willow's Branch._

For handing him the Tempest Stone for Orion Mycon's Lost Ring

Where did you find this...? It was stolen from me in the night. I was able to fight off the intruder before he did me harm, but he was able to unsocket the gem from this Elderstone band. I have no need for the ring any longer nor the trouble it has brought. You can keep it. *He fits the gem loosely into the slot and hands it back to you* Might even be fixable by a jeweler.
