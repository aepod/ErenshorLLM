The Hider can be found in the Shivering Step if you go straight from the entrance portal and take a slight left near some old ruins. A sound cue will play when you are near him.

Greeting message:

Oh well... the game is over. Inconsequential, isn't it? Your prize - but do not tell Kio! He likes the island in this light!

Upon speaking to him you will complete the quest Find The Hider and he will give you the item Instructions for Warmth. A ghostly roar will also echo across the island.
