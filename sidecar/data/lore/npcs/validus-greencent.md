Validus is an NPC located in Stowaway's Step. He functions as a bank and he has storage space for 32 items, it is possible for all players on your account to access this storage making transferring items from 1 character to another possible.

Greeting message:

Welcome! This is my bank wagon. I can store anything you need. Even give it to your pals, by your request of course.

Alternative greeting message for people with low reputation (I think):

You don't live by the blade, I aint talkin' to ya.
