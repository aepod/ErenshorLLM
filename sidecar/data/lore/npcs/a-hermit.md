The Hermit is an NPC located in a sneaky area in-between two mountains to the east of the gate between Fernalla's Revival Plains and Faerie's Brake. He is the quest giver for A Hermit's Request.

Greeting message:

I saw it, it's true, and I wrote it down, and now they took it, and I NEED IT! I have to tell someone...

Prompts:

they: They took it! My toolbox. It's not tools though, it's not. It's everything else. The highwaymen... across the field they went, one has it...

_This beginsA Hermit's Request_

For handing him the Ornate Toolbox to complete A Hermit's Request.

Praise all, the lock has stood firm! *The Hermit produces a key and unlocks the box, before handing you its contents.* He says: "See that this finds its way."

For handing him a second Ornate Toolbox:

What sorcery is this... that is not mine! I have mine here!
