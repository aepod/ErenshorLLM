The Town Crier is a special NPC located in various towns/cities in the game. On a daily basis he shouts out Patron's who subscribe to Patreon as a thank you for their contribution to the development team and game, this only happens if you're in the same zone as them.

Greeting message:

Oh, hi there. I'm just here to announce the latest news. Right now I'm recognizing the folks who have contributed to help the development of our fine island!
