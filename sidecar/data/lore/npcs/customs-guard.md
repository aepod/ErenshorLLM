The Customs Guard is the first NPC you'll meet on entry to Stowaway's Step he gives the quest Stowaway Registration upon talking to him.

Greeting message:

Welcome to Stowaway's Step, stranger. Please go check in at the office up ahead. Here is your dock pass. Give it to the agent inside. Do not lose it!

Prompts:

office: Yes, the big building just to the right of the dock here. See Wendyl inside with your papers to register.

If you hail him again after recieving the  Dock Pass:

You already have the item!

If you hail him again after completing Stowaway Registration

You've already registered... get off my docks.
