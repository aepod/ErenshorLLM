Thella is an NPC located in Port Azure. She functions as Port Azure's auction broker. You are able to buy items from other sim players through her.

Greeting message:

Oi, I'm the broker here in Port Azure. Got buyin' or sellin' to do? I'm your best way to do it.

1. ↑ If the player is already holding  Ruby in their inventory, another will not drop.
2. ↑ If Thella Steepleton has  Pathfinder's Staff equipped, it is guaranteed to drop.
