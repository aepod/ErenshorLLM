Garrey is a fisherman located on the Docks on Stowaway's Step. He is the friend who Isabel Schegg mentions finds treasure while fishing, one of which is the  Stowaway Step Lighthouse Key.

Greeting message:

This is a great fishing spot. Hoping to get something soon! They sell poles up the road at the shop, if you're interested.

Prompts:

treasures: Got this old key. If you want it you can have it. I was just going to throw it back - doesn't look like any of the house keys 'round here.
catch,caught and crazy: Haven't caught much. Got a few frownies, some old treasures, and a very used pair of boots.
Moongill: A moongill? No. I haven't caught one of those. Just this old key. Not sure what it's to.

If you say treasures again with the  Stowaway Step Lighthouse Key in your posession:

You already have the item!
