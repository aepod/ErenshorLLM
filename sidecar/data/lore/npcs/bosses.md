# Boss NPCs

Boss NPCs in Erenshor are powerful named enemies that provide greater experience and better loot than regular enemies. Bosses have unique aggro messages and emotes. Each boss has a defined loot table with guaranteed, common, uncommon, rare, legendary, and ultra-rare drops.

---

Starting zone bosses on Stowaway's Step include Plaxitheris, Vevillo Polter, Occuphage Eater of Eyes, and Rattlewrists. Island Tomb bosses are Ghost of Wyland Sercher, Mourning, Woe, Failed Cremation, and Rotting Mummy.

---

Early mainland bosses: Hidden Hills has Gort, Priel Lieutenant Bovo, and Priel Lieutenant Lance. Faerie's Brake has Alpha Wolf, Bassalt Vallano, Grizzlepaw, and Tinkles. Willowwatch Ridge has Autmis the Lost, Battlefist, Blunga, Priel Lieutenant Laflin, and Wiggleribs. Vitheo's Watch has The Lost Sea Giant King and Old Brackey.

---

Mid-level bosses: Fernalla's Revival Plains has Gruhglor and the Sylvan Giant. Bone Pits has Zashlyn Bloodbane, An Apparition of Sivakaya, and A Lesser Avatar of Sivakaya. Old Krakengard boss is Overseer Orlok. Underspine Hollow has Druo the Reborn (always spawns), Agsha of the Soil, Rosle of the Spirits, Ughl of the Skies, and Obba of the Warkind (random spawns).

---

Blacksalt Strand bosses: Acceptor, Ecclesiarch, Goliath, Lazalith Soldier of Sivakaya, Prioress, The Corpse of Brice Mathers, and The Corpse of Cyndreela Mathers. Duskenlight Coast: Dusken Giant, Massia the Fallen, Overseer Laseal. Lost Cellar: Spark Queen.

---

Braxonian Desert bosses: Apha and Upha (Priests of Brax), Evadne the Corrupted, Overseer Refleigh, Seed of Blight, Blazefiend, Candlekeeper, Kingsman, Monarch of the Flame. Elderstone Mines: Slumbering Queen, Ravager The First Infected.

---

Windwashed Pass bosses: Algaeth Bilgeback, Granitus. Rockshade Hold: A Lava Spirit, Diamond Colossus, Ruby Fiend, Cinder, Sapphire Fiend, Pyroclast, Stoneman Colossus, Volcanic Giant. Loomingwood: Aviak, Devoratrix, Loomingwidow, The Gardener of Loomingwood.

---

Malaroth's Nesting Grounds: Demented Malaroth, Shivunax. Silkengrass Meadowlands: Morian the Ascended, Sylvan Giant. Prielian Cascade: Addison Priel, Julianna Priel, Savannah Priel.

---

Soluna's Landing: Raval The First Paladin, Astra Rogue of the Stars, Freed Spirit of Raval. The Blight: Harbinger of Corruption, Fenton the Blighted, Blight Wyrm. Fallen Braxonia: Prince Oslind, Prince Ardant.

---

Ripper's Keep boss is Ripper DeMerth. Abyssal Lake: Nipper King, Abyssal Lord. Blooming Sepulcher: A Protected Memory (x3), Frenzy, Maple, Vinx, Fallen Fernalla.

---

Vitheo's Rest: Balaste the Fallen, Oarik the Failure, Pamia the Coward, Nazura the Traitor, Elwio the Traitor, The Abomination, The Crypt Guardian, Apparition of Vitheo, Darkwarden Ilyth.

---

Jaws of Sivakaya: Balthom Soldier of Sivakaya, Aeiros Soldier of Sivakaya, Vessel Siraethe, Sableheart Steed of Sivakaya. Azynthi's Garden: Flicker of Souls, Gargoth.

---

Secluded Sanctuary: Shadow of Vitheo, Shadow of Brax, Shadow of Soluna, Shadow of Fernalla, Azynthian Keeper. Shivering Step: enemies like Fearantula, Ghost of Plaxitheris, Mephallo serve as boss-level encounters.
