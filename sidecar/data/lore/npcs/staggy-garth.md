Staggy Garth is an NPC on Stowaway's Step on the cliffs to the left on the rock by Plaxitheris, above and to the side of the bandit cave on that path that leads to the Ruins.

Greeting message:

What're you doing way out here? Lost like the rest of us? Headed to the ruins?

Prompts:

Lost

In the right light you can see the beaches of Erenshor off in the distance. Too foggy today, but it's out there. I've seen a ship come back and forth in the night - unmarked, too. No idea who's sailing it, but there's something going on...

Ruins

Just down the path there. The old fortress. Wouldn't go down there at night if I were you. Odd place, that.
