## Fallen Fernalla (NPC)

Fallen Fernalla offers the final step of the "A Dying Wish" quest chain, A Dying Wish, Part 4.

Greeting message:

Child, you have my deepest gratitude for all you have done... yet there is still more to be faced. My body stands before you as a remnant of what it once was. Hollow and riddled with unnatural darkness and guilt. Vitheo cannot rest, nor can I.

_This will startA Dying Wish, Part 4_

Prompts:

Vitheo: Vitheo was spared the darkness that Sivakaya was able to wrap around me, but he still does not rest, held suspended by some necromancy, some attempt to pull him to her. She tries to reach his ascended being and take it for herself as she has done with me.
ascended: Vitheo has graduated this realm, more powerful than ever, as have I. But Sivakaya has claws in my ascended form. We must put Vitheo to rest before she gets his too, if she hasn't already. The key to his sanctum his held by a great abomination \- placed there to protect Vitheo from those believed to be traitors - it is the only key. You must take it.
abomination: Within the traitor's halls, you will find two braziers. You must light them both, for only then will the abomination awaken. It holds the only key to Vitheo's sanctum... and I have the last remaining component for the life flame to ignite it. I bestow it upon you, please use it wisely.

_This will provide Gift of Fernalla for The Life Flame_

light: You will find the recipes for the flames among those who once followed Vitheo. But to kindle the flame of Life, you will need this gift from me for I have the only remaining one. Please, child... do what you must to put Vitheo to rest. Free him from Sivakaya's grasp. And may he find it in his heart to forgive me for the burden I placed upon him.
