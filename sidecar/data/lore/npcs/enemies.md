# Enemies and Creatures

Enemies in Erenshor provide experience, equipment, consumables, and special items upon defeat. There is approximately a 20% chance that an enemy will spawn with a level one greater or one less than its base level. Respawn timers reduce with party size (up to 44% reduction in a full group).

---

Enemy difficulty is indicated by color-coded rings relative to player level. All enemies level 16 and above may drop Planar Stone Shards (1%), Sivakrux (0.1%), and global drops. Level 21+ enemies may drop Inert Diamonds (0.8%). Level 31+ enemies may drop Planar Stones (0.07%). All enemies may drop Torn Treasure Maps (0.3%).

---

Levels 1-5: Brackish Croc Whelps, Grass Spiders, Young Wolves, Islander Bandits, Enraged Spirits, Brittle Skeletons, Brown Bear Cubs, Gasper, Risen Pilgrims, Catnip, Rotting Mummies. These inhabit Stowaway's Step, Hidden Hills, Willowwatch Ridge, and Faerie's Brake.

---

Levels 6-10: Priel Bandit Recruits, Daggerlegs, Neophyte Necromancers, Ancient Horrors, Molorai Militia units (Arcanist, Archmage, Druid, Warrior), Seafiend variants, Rottenfoot Tribalists/Warriors/Witchdoctors, Molorai Attack Dogs. Found in Vitheo's Watch, Old Krakengard, and Bone Pits.

---

Levels 11-15: Grassland Ogre variants (Sentinel, Worker, Shaman, Bruiser), Lost Sea Giants, Spark Beetles, Grizzly Bears, Highwayman Raiders/Marauders, Nagalok variants, Night Terrors, Silk Cobras. Found in Fernalla's Revival Plains, Blacksalt Strand, and Lost Cellar.

---

Levels 16-20: Seafiend Manathites/Manalords, Acolytes, Molorai Archaeologists, Nipper variants (Lashfist, Manatooth), Prioress, Deepling variants (Crimsonite, Lunarite, Verdite), Braxon Fanatics, Kodiaks, Priel Deceivers/Enforcers, Sand Mummies. Found in Rottenfoot, Braxonian Desert, Elderstone Mines.

---

Levels 21-25: Priel Tamers, Nightmarian Knights/Hounds, Seeds of Blight, Albino Kodiaks, Desert Jackals, Dire Wolves, Mature Malaroths, Pteriaped Chicks, Loomingwood Minotaur variants, Stone Hounds, Volcano Scarabs. Found in Loomingwood, Rockshade Hold, Silkengrass Meadowlands.

---

Levels 26-30: Ancient Corpses/Guards/Celestarians, Living Meteorites, Sivakayan units (Cleric, Raider, Reaver, Devoted, Legionnaire, Voidweaver), Faerie variants (Enchantress, Foliagian, Windchild), Abyssal Fungus Protectors/Warriors, Nipper Diremaws/Farseers/Whiptails, Obsidian Horrors. Found in Soluna's Landing, The Blight, Abyssal Lake.

---

Levels 31-35: Ancient Spectres/Demons/Educators/Knights, Cursed Vithean Conspirers, Molorai Truthseekers, Braxonian High Guards/Weapon Masters, Darkwarden variants, Azynthian Icebones/Firebones, Botanists, Deadweb Spiders, Vithean Immortals, Faceless Necromancers. Found in Azynthi's Garden, Vitheo's Rest, Jaws of Sivakaya.

---

Levels 36-40: Summoned Protector (36), Constructed Faithful (38). These highest-level enemies are found in the Jaws of Sivakaya endgame dungeon.
