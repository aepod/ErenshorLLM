A Memory of Sivakaya appears in two different places in the dungeon if the player has the Nighthollow Candle in their inventory.

1. The Alcove near the entrance to the dungeon
2. The floating platform in the centre of Aieros's room

#### Alcove

When summoned:

... *a memory can be felt in the air and a low voice echoes through your mind* - "The guardian has awoken, let her gaze upon you*

_This accepts and completesThe Guardian Awakens_

Greeting message:

Let her gaze upon you.

#### Floating Platform

Greeting message:

... *a memory can be felt in the air and a low voice echoes through your mind* - "Only the truly pious shall pass. He who speaks the name of the one who gifted Sivakaya her ascension may pass unharmed."
