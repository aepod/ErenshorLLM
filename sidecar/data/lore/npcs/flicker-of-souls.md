There are four total Flicker of Souls located throughout Azynthi's Garden. One is located at the top of the main staircase, one is located in the hedge maze, one is located in the graveyard, and the other is located in the basement of the castle.

Greeting message:

... *it flickers in and out of existence, as though behind some unseen shroud*

When traded a Riftsnare Gem

The Flickering Soul suddenly bursts into existence and you feel it brush against your hand.
