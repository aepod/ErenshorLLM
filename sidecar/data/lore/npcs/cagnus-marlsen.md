Cagnus Marlsen is an NPC in Willowwatch Ridge. He is the quest giver for Willow Soil (Quest).

Greeting message:

Can't get nothin' to grow up on these rocks. This moss is everywhere but nothing else. Wish I could get to that shrine and try there...
