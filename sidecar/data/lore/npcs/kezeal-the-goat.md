Kezeal is a renowned bargain hunter, fisherman, miner and avid mountain climber, he has probably seen more angles of Erenshor than all of us combined. He holds the title of the wealthiest person in all of Erenshor.

Greeting message:

Just bought this place. Saved up for years. Never sold a thing til there was a deal! Climbed all over the world looking for deals, you see! Spent whole nights fishing and mining! Now I'm rich!
