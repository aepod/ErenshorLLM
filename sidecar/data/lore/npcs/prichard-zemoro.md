Prichard is an NPC located in Port Azure in a building down in the slums.

Greeting message:

Ho there! You bring a map? An Elixir?

Prichard gives the quest Maps for Prichard, where players can exchange a Treasure Map and an Elixir of Enlightenment II for a Sivakrux.
