There are 4 Watchlight's in Stowaway's Step. They are used in a binding spell to keep Aragath Swellsteer imprisoned. The player can choose to help The Necromancers of the Island Tomb to finish off Aragath (Destroying Aragath) or choose to help Aragath by using the necromancers soul stones on the watchlight's to sever their grasp (Aragath's Imprisonment).

## Stalzo's Watchlight

Located beside Plaxitheris but a little further back:

Greeting message:

... *it does not speak*

If you say Stalzo to this watchlight it responds with:

... *the light dims briefly, and you hear a soft sign from within.
