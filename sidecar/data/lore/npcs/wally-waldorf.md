Wally Waldorf is an NPC who is located on Stowaway's Step.

He is the quest giver for Wally's Moongill.

Greeting message:

Woe is me! I must have a Moongill! I crave a Moongill! But the shop has none for sale and I don't know how to fish!

Moongill: I need someone who knows how to fish to catch me one! There's an old dusty 'how-to' book for sale in there, but who has time for that? To make it worse, they only come out at night! I'll give you a fancy reward if you catch me one...
