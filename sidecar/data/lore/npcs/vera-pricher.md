Greeting message only for Druids:

I've stumbled upon something notable about The Willow in my studies of Fernallan Lore. Would you like me to share it with you?

yes, share and notable

The Willow is everywhere, and the branches could be anywhere. However, I've learned that the Goblin tribes in the Fernallan meadows might hold more clues! If you find anything, bring it back to me. One day we'll wield it's branches...
