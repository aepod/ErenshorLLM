# Completing the Ritual

Back to Quests

To start and complete the quest, hand the following items to Lucian Revald

* Loss Stone
* A Remnant of Sivakaya
* Demented Brain
* Nightmare Crystal

Upon completion, a portal to Secluded Sanctuary appears.

A Loss Stone
A Remnant of Sivakaya
A brain warped by the Sivakayan Dementia
A Nightmare Crystal
