# Intel from the Lost Cellar

Back to Quests

To start this quest speak to Foreman Hauck who is under the tent in front of the mines, which are to the left of the entrance gates in Port Azure just near the entrance and prompt him with prisoners. It can be completed by handing him a Aged Interrogation Note dropped by a Tortured Prisoner in the Lost Cellar

1: Intel from the Lost Cellar: Foreman Hauck in Port Azure has asked you to explore the Lost Cellar and find any Intel that may or may not exist down there. Bring it to him if you find it.
