# Recovering the Nighthollow Candle

Back to Quests

To start this quest speak to Larviso Poltir in Loomingwood with the prompt Nighthollow

To complete this quest you must gather and hand him all the parts of the nighthollow candle. These are:

1. Skull-Shaped Candleholder from Eldoth Molorai
2. Nighthollow Wax created with Mold: Nighthollow Wax obtained from Candlekeeper
3. Nighthollow Wick from Darkwarden Awxa

Stage: Name: Description
1: Recovering the Nighthollow Candle: Larviso in Loomingwood has told you about the Nighthollow Candle. It was stolen from the Wisp long ago. If you find any trace of it, bring it to him.
He mentioned that a Sivakayan Darkwarden may have interest in it, or perhaps its lost to the Braxonian Desert.. worse yet, an antiquities dealer may have recovered it.

Upon handing some of the items:

Amazing... this is a piece of the candle! There should be a base, wax and a wick. Find them all and bring them here!

Completion Message:

I cannot believe you have recovered the candle! We must piece it together at once and light it. Only a flame of Brax will be sufficient - you must find a place where the flames of Brax burn strong, and ignite the candle! It will guide you into the Jaws of Sivakaya, and we may finally learn how to restore her.
