# A Skeleton Eye for Alyssia Fearnon

This quest is for Druid only. To start this quest speak to Alyssia Fearnon in Hidden Hills and prompt her with favor.

The Skeleton Eye can be obtained from A Skeleton Soldier and A Skeleton Captain.
