# The Riftstone

Back to Quests

You will start and complete this quest when you say Umbran to A Strange Hermit. You will be handed the Azynthian Riftstone which can be activated to switch between two different version of Azynthi's Garden

To complete stage 1, Gather Riftsnare Gems from the bosses in the dimensional rift version of the zone and trade them to the 4 Flicker of Souls dotted around the island, which will allow you to kill them.

Flicker of Souls Locations:

1. Outside the keep at the top of the stairs from the docks
2. Behind the sealed wall in the basement
3. In the middle of the hedge maze
4. In a crypt in the graveyard

Once all four flickers are destroyed, Eldoth Molorai becomes much weaker able to be killed.
