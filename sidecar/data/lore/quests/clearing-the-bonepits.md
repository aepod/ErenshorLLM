## Clearing the Bonepits

### Location:

Willowwatch Ridge

### Repeatable:

No

## Requirements

### Prerequisite:

-

### Item(s) Required:

Ritual Stone

## Rewards

### Experience:

55

### Faction Changes:

+15 The Citizens of Erenshor

### Item(s):

Sivakrux

1\. Talk to William Cardigo atop the cliff in Willowwatch Ridge and ask him about the demon who resides in the nearby Bone Pits

Journal Entry
Clearing the Bonepits: William Cardigo at Willowwatch has hinted that if you clear out the bonepits, and bring him proof of slaying the "glowing red demon" deep within, he'd make it worth your while.

2\. Head into The Bone Pits and slay An Apparition of Sivakaya to receive a  Ritual Stone.

3\. Hand the  Ritual Stone to William Cardigo, who thanks you with some experience and a  Sivakrux.
