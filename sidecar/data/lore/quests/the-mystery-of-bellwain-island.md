# Walkthrough

1\. Head up the hill and enter the house. On the cabinet near the front door, pick up  The Warning.

2\. Pick up the other notes and items scattered around the house:

*  Bellwain Study Key: In the unlocked closet at the end of the long hallway.1
*  The Recipe: On the center island of the kitchen.
*  Bread: Inside a cabinet in the center island of the kitchen.
*  Brackfruit Juice: Inside a cabinet against the wall in the kitchen.
*  Detective Books: On the table in the middle of the room near the bookshelves.
*  The Sun Lilies: On a table in the back corner of the house, next to a vase of flowers.
*  Eroding Rockwall: Outside on a table on the back patio.
*  Drafty Baths: Next to the fireplace in the living room.
*  Coal: Next to the fireplace in the living room.

3\. Taking direction from the clue in The Recipe note, Fish off the coast of Bellwain Island for an  Island Fin. Drag the Island Fin from your inventory to the Baking Oven in the kitchen, along with the previously acquired Bread, Brackfruit Juice, and Coal to receive 20 experience and a  Breaded Island Fin.

4\. Head out back and walk on top of the picnic blanket near the large tree. Pick up the note  Mid Day on the Isle that appears.

5\. The Detective Books note hints at something being hidden on the roof. On the back patio, use the stacked boxes to jump onto the awning over the door, then pick up the  Bellwain Bedroom Key next to the chimney.

6\. Jump off the back patio and find a hole in the cliffs, as hinted at by the Eroding Rockwall note. Inside the cave, pick up the  Bellwain Attic Key from inside a treasure chest.

7\. The bedroom is the first doorway on the left in the long hallway. Inside, pick up  The Garden Song from the drawer beneath the mirror.

8\. The study is the door on the right. Inside, pick up the  Bellwain Bathroom Key near the sewing machine, a note about  The Flint on the corner desk, and the  Rainbow Flint from inside the small safe against the wall.

9\. Use the Bathroom Key to enter the door on the other side of the house. Pick up  Relaxation from on top of the sink.

10\. The clue given in the Drafty Bath note mentions the walls. Click the wall between the sink and bathtub to open a secret door, then take the stairs up to the attic. Pick up  The Stash, which is hidden in the wardrobe near the door.

11\. Open the door using the Bellwain Attic Key. On the far side of the room, pick up  Evening on the Isle.

12\. Outside near the fenced-in garden, click on the bells in the correct order according to The Garden Song:

* Vision, Passion, Luck, Flourish, Passion, Vision, Luck (2, 3, 4, 1, 3, 2, 4)

13\. Pick up  Morning on the Isle, which appears in the middle of the garden once the bells are correctly rung.

14\. Return to the docks and jump into the water, as hinted at by The Stash note. Sink underwater and swim through the gap before surfacing, then make your way through the cave to discover  Bellsie's Wine.

15\. As instructed by the Relaxation note, deliver the wine to the chair with a missing drink beyond the pergola, beneath the large tree in the corner of the island. Pick up  Afternoon on the Isle, which appears after moving near the chair.

16\. Head to the opposite end of the island, where the torches surround a large statue. Using the clue given in The Sun Lilies note, move to the torch surrounded by yellow flowers: it represents 6:00.

17\. Combining all the information found in the Morning, Mid Day, Afternoon, and Evening Poems, click on the torches multiple times to change their colors (Red → Blue → White → Orange → Green → Red) to the correct orientation, starting from the torch surrounded by sun lillies:

* 6:00: Orange
* 7:00: White
* 8:00: Blue
* 9:00: Green
* 10:00: Blue
* 11:00: Red
* 12:00: Blue
* 1:00: Green
* 2:00: White
* 3:00: White
* 4:00: Blue
* 5:00: Orange

18\. Upon lighting all torches correctly, the statue will lift up to reveal a chest containing  A Collection of Notes.

19\. Captain Bellwain and Bellsie's spirits speak to each other before noticing your presence. Meet them at the entrance to the estate. Hand the notes to Bellsie, who thanks you with 100 experience and gives you  Bellwain's Master Key.

20\. Use the key to open the final locked door in the long hallway, where you can find  Bellwain's Last Entry and the  Mooring Coin.

# Notes

1. ↑ It is possible to skip most of the quest by picking up the Study Key and using the Rainbow Flint to light the torches without ever picking up any other items.
