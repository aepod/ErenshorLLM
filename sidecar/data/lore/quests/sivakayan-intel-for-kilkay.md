# Sivakayan Intel for Kilkay

Back to Quests

To start this quest read the Sivakayan Intel. Hand it to Captain Kilkay to complete stage 1. Hand Ealdred's Journal to him to complete stage 2.

Stage: Name: Description
1: Sivakayan Intel for Kilkay: A recovered page of Sivakayan Intel was handed to Guard Captain Kilkay, who was appreciative of the info.

He has asked you to continue to search for information on how Sivakayans may be entering Port Azure.
2: Secure Port Azure: Keep your eyes open for any intel on how Sivakayans are gaining access to Port Azure. Share it with Guard Captain Kilkay if you find it.

Completion Message (Stage 1):

I have suspected the Sivakayans have been within our walls for some time now. This only confirms my fears. I implore you to find more information... If you learn of how the Sivakayans are entering Port Azure, please bring that information to me!

Completion Message (Stage 2):

Eldoth... I should have known. The tragedy at Goodsoil was done by his hand too? He seems to have the ear of the Sivakayans, but why? We shall continue to hold fast here in Port Azure. Thank you for this information.

Stage 2 appears to be a placeholder at this time.
