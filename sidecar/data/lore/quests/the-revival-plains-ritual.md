# The Revival Plains Ritual

Giving Asaga Underloft the Small Engraved Compass will begin this quest.

To complete the quest trade all 4 items listed with the Ceremonial Brazier in Fernalla's Revival Plains.

Finishing the quest opens a portal to the Mysterious Portal.
