# Main Story and Quest Lines

Erenshor's quests drive character progression and story advancement. Quests are given by NPCs and require collecting items, defeating enemies, or exploring zones. Rewards include experience points, gold, and sometimes unique equipment. Quest chains connect multiple quests in sequence, creating longer narrative arcs.

---

The starting quest line on Stowaway's Step introduces players to the game. Key quests include "Stowaway Registration" (5 EXP), "Alice Hewer's Necklace" (15 EXP, rewards Braided Belt), "Percy's Seaspice" (20 EXP), "Kio's Papers" (10 EXP, rewards gate key), and "A Way To Erenshor" (25 EXP) which sends players to the mainland. "Whispers of Wyland" (55 EXP) connects to the Island Tomb dungeon.

---

The Sivakayan conflict is the central story thread. "Sivakayans in Port Azure" (300 EXP) uncovers their presence in the capital, unlocking Vitheo's Rest "Under the Lighthouse" section. "Sivakayan Intel for Kilkay" (450 EXP in Rottenfoot, 100 EXP in Ripper's Keep) tracks intelligence across zones. "Sivakayan Ore for Tipp" (250 EXP) rewards the Corruption of Sivakaya aura.

---

The Bounty quests target major antagonists. "A Bounty on Ripper DeMerth" (255 EXP, 500 Gold, rewards Amulet of Azure) targets the warlord who took over Ripper's Keep. "A Bounty on Guard Captain Kilkay" (255 EXP, 500 Gold, rewards Ripparian Pendant) is the counterpart quest from Ripper's side.

---

The Dying Wish quest chain spans the Blooming Sepulcher and Vitheo's Rest (Parts 1-4, each 300 EXP). This multi-dungeon series requires progression through interconnected areas and boss defeats. Protected Memory encounters gate advancement through the Blooming Sepulcher.

---

The Ritual quest line unlocks Mysterious Portals across three tiers. "The Duskenlight Ritual" (15 EXP) opens tier 1 at Duskenlight Coast. "The Revival Plains Ritual" (25 EXP) opens tier 2 at Fernalla's Revival Plains. "The Ripper Ritual" (25 EXP) opens tier 3 at Ripper's Keep. Completing all rituals leads to "Completing the Ritual" which opens the Secluded Sanctuary.

---

The Rune collection quest line gathers inert runes from across Erenshor. "A Lost Portal" and "A Lost Portal Pt. 2" (155 EXP each) provide the Inert/Active Rune of Brax from the Braxonian Desert. "Raval's Last Wish" (400 EXP) provides the Inert Rune of Soluna from Soluna's Landing. Additional runes come from other zones. These runes are required for the Secluded Sanctuary and ultimately for activating raid portals in the Reliquary Hall.

---

The Jaws of Sivakaya unlock sequence requires multiple prerequisite quest chains. Start with "A Hermit's Request" in Fernalla's Revival Plains, then "A Note for C. Pillot," then "Recovering the Nighthollow Candle" and "Lighting the Nighthollow Candle" from Loomingwood (650 EXP, rewards Unlit Nighthollow Candle). Finally, complete "The Guardian Awakens," "She Sees You" (rotating statue puzzle), and "A Pious Guest" (speak "Azynthi" to the Ward of Piety) inside the dungeon.

---

The Orion's Ring quest chain involves "Repairing Orion's Ring" (100 EXP, rewards Unsocketed Ring) and "Repairing Orion's Ring, Part 2" (100 EXP, rewards Tempestalia Ring of Storms), starting with "Orion Mycon's Lost Ring" (100 EXP) in the Braxonian Desert.
