1\. Talk to Aragath Swellsteer at the northwestern ruins of Stowaway's Step and ask him about the four Necromancers who have imprisoned him. Aragath can only be found at night, between the hours of **11:00 PM - 7:00 AM**.

2\. Aragath asks you to help free him by defeating the four that have trapped him. He tells you they reside in the Island Tomb behind a throne of stone, but warns you not to trust them.

Journal Entry
Aragath's Imprisonment: Aragath claims to have been imprisoned by a group of necromancers. He thinks they're behind a hidden door in the Island Tomb near a Stone Throne. Destroy the necromancers and collect their soul-stones, and reunite those stones with their linked watchlights to absorb their power. Return the four charged stones to Aragath.

3\. Find the secret passage in the Island Tomb. It's behind a throne on the way leading up to Failed Cremation. Upon descending the stairs for the first time, players receive a tutorial prompt:

Consider NPCs!

TUTORIAL TIP: Just because it looks scary, doesn't mean you can't talk to it... don't attack everything on sight until you know what it's intentions are with you.

4\. Choose whether to speak to the Necromancers and begin Destroying Aragath or attack them. Note that you can only complete one quest; only kill the Necromancers if you wish to continue with this quest.

5\. Defeat the Necromancers and loot their corpses to find Revid's  Soul Gem, Leah's  Soul Gem, Posta's  Soul Gem, and Stalzo's  Soul Gem.

6\. Return to Stowaway's Step and give each gem to the appropriate color Watchlight:

*  : On top of the Registration Office.
*  : On top of the hill near Rattlewrists.
*  : Next to the mausoleum entrance to the Island Tomb.
*  : Behind Plaxitheris.

Upon using each Soul Gem, you'll receive **5 EXP** and a  Charged Soul Gem.

7\. Return and give Aragath the four Charged Soul Gems. He thanks you for releasing him with **45 EXP** and a Seafarer's Pendant.

## Notes

* Completing this quest will make Destroying Aragath void; you can only complete one.
* It is possible to receive the  Empty Soul Gem from Stalzo before killing him, but Aragath will not accept it once he's already been given the four Charged Soul Gems.
* There are no known consequences for either aiding Aragath or destroying him.
