## Sivakayan Ore for Tipp

### Location:

Port Azure

### Repeatable:

No

## Requirements

### Prerequisite:

-

### Item(s) Required:

Sivakayan Ore x4

## Rewards

### Experience:

250

### Gold:

-

### Item(s):

Corruption of Sivakaya

Quest Progression Previous:: Next:
-: -

1\. Talk to Supply Sergeant Tipp near the northern exit to Blacksalt Strand in Port Azure. Tell him you'll do him a favor and he will ask you to bring him 4 Sivakayan Ores.

Journal Entry
Sivakayan Ore for Tipp: Supply Sergeant Tipp wants Sivakayan Ore. He says their soldiers carry it sometimes, but it's hard to find.

2\. Obtain the ores by fighting any of the various Sivakayan Followers in Abyssal Lake, Rottenfoot, Windwashed Pass, Silkengrass Meadowlands, and Ripper's Keep.

3\. Give Tipp the ores, who rewards you with some experience and a Corruption of Sivakaya.

## Notes

* Although this quest can be obtained quite early, the Sivakayan enemies players need to defeat to obtain the ore are quite powerful and may pose a challenge for players until later in the game. As such, players may wish to wait before taking on this quest.
* A single Sivakayan Raider can also be found wandering the Faerie's Brake.
