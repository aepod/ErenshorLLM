# The Loss Stone

# The Loss Stone

Back to Quests

To start the quest say Sivakaya to the Arcane Headmaster whilst wearing a Pupil's Pin

You are handed Loss Stone when starting this quest.

1. Hand the stone to Larviso Poltir in Loomingwood to complete step 1
2. Craft and hand Liani Bosh the Angler's Ring to complete The Angler's Ring
3. Talk to Bassle Wavebreaker in Blacksalt Strand and then board the boat to return to Stowaway's Step
4. Hand Loss Stone to Lucian Revald to obtain the quest Completing The Ritual

Stage: Name: Description
1: The Loss Stone: A Ghostly Figure has handed you a stone that contains the very sadness that has been brought upon the world by Azynthi. She instructed you to bring it to someone who might know its true power., she suggested someone who mourns the loss of Sivakaya may be best.
2: The Loss Stone Pt. 2: You've been told that it's imperative to get the Loss Stone back to Lucian at Stowaway's Step.
