1\. Find the secret passage in the Island Tomb. It's behind a throne on the way leading up to Failed Cremation. Upon descending the stairs for the first time, players receive a tutorial prompt:

Consider NPCs!

TUTORIAL TIP: Just because it looks scary, doesn't mean you can't talk to it... don't attack everything on sight until you know what it's intentions are with you.

2\. Talk to Stalzo and ask him about the soul gem. He gives you an  Empty Soul Gem and asks you to give it to Aragath Swellsteer, which will destroy him and release the Necromancers.

Journal Entry
Destroying Aragath: A group of necromancers in the island tomb claim to be holding a demon named Aragath captive through magic.

They have asked you to hand the demon an empty soulstone, to absorb his power and finally allow them to leave the island.

3\. Choose whether to give the gem to Aragath, or instead kill the Necromancers to complete Aragath's Imprisonment. Note that you can only complete one of these quests.

4\. Giving the gem to Aragath kills him instantly, giving players **45 EXP** and allowing them to loot his corpse to find a Seafarer's Pendant.

## Notes

* Completing this quest will make Aragath's Imprisonment void; you can only complete one.
* It is possible to kill the Necromancers after receiving the Empty Soul Gem and still complete the quest.
* There are no known consequences for either aiding Aragath or destroying him.
