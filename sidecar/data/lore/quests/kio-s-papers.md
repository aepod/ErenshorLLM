**Kio 's Papers** is a Quest in Stowaway's Step.

# Walkthrough

1\. Talk to Corporal Slate next to the gate up to Stowaway's Step and ask about his problem.
2\. He will give you  Kio's Leave Order1 and ask you to deliver them to Kio the Light Keeper outside the lighthouse.

Journal Entry
Kio's Papers: Kio - that guy down at the Lighthouse there - he's been making folks uncomfortable. We're gonna send him on leave for a bit. Go deliver the news and collect his gate key. Its all yours.

3\. Hand Kio's Leave Order to Kio, who is all too happy to leave. He will give you the  Stowaway's Step Gate Key.2

# Notes

1. ↑ If Kio's Leave Order is destroyed, another can be obtained by returning to Corporal Slate and prompting him with problem again.
2. ↑ The Stowaway's Step Gate Key allows players to leave the Docks and access the rest of Stowaway's Step by opening the gate next to Corporal Slate.
