# A Note for C. Pillot

# A Note for C. Pillot

Back to Quests

The quest starts when you hand the Ornate Toolbox to A Hermit during the quest A Hermit's Request . He gives you a Notes of a Hermit that triggers this quest. Chazz Pillot is located in Port Azure between the market and docks. The quest reward Mold: A Strange Figurine can be used to craft A Strange Figurine to summon Candlekeeper at the Braxonian Desert chessboard, which provides a quest item needed for Recovering the Nighthollow Candle.
