## A Dying Wish

A Dying Wish is a multi-part quest chain that unlocks access to deeper areas of the Blooming Sepulcher. Progressing in the quest requires unlocking Vitheo's Rest with  Key_to_Vitheo's_Rest, killing various bosses, and bringing their dropped items back to the appropriate A Dying Wish in the Sepulcher.

## Quest Steps

To start the quest, kill the first A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher and interact with the A Dying Wish that spawns, following the conversation and prompting with the word Vitheo.

### Part One

Stage: Name: Description
1: A Dying Wish, Part 1: You've encountered a dying wish of Fernalla in the Blooming Sepulcher, and it has asked you to find her brother's tomb and defeat its guardian at the entry chamber. Upon doing so, you should return to this wish with a memory of the Burial

To complete this quest, hand the Dying Wish the  Memory of a Guardian received by killing The Crypt Guardian in Vitheo's Rest.

On Completion

You've found it... I feel the possibility of hope within you... I have removed the ward and I ask that you enter this place. Please, child, help me.

_This opens the first area ofBlooming Sepulcher_

### Part Two

To begin the next step of the quest chain, kill the second A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher and interact with the A Dying Wish that spawns. Upon providing the conversation prompt Balaste, you will receive  Shrines Key to grant access to the first wing of Vitheo's Rest.

Stage: Name: Description
1: A Dying Wish, Part 2: You've encountered a second wish of Fernalla in the Blooming Sepulcher. This one has asked you to find Balaste, in Vitheo's Rest, and see if you can sense a memory that might reveal if Vitheo was indeed laid to rest there, and what happened next.

To complete this quest, hand the Dying Wish the  Memory of Balaste received from killing Balaste the Fallen in Vitheo's Rest.

On Completion

Balaste kept his word, and for that I am grateful beyond measure. My brother was there once... and perhaps he remains still. Please, child, continue down this path with me. Let us see this through together.

_This opens the second area ofBlooming Sepulcher_

### Part Three

The third part of the quest chain starts when you kill the third A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher and interact with the A Dying Wish that spawns. After providing the conversation prompt Elwio you will receive  Key to Traitor's Halls, which unlocks the second wing of Vitheo's Rest, the Traitor's Halls.

Stage: Name: Description
1: A Dying Wish, Part 3: A wish of Fernalla has asked that you find Elwio the Traitor in Vitheo's Rest, within the Traitor's Halls. She feels a great sadness for him, but cannot recall why.

To complete this quest, hand the Dying Wish the  Memory of Elwio received from killing Elwio the Traitor in Vitheo's Rest.

On Completion

Elwio... I asked him to grant a mercy, to free Vitheo from this realm should the shadow of Sivakaya ever reach for him. I begged that he not allow her to sink her cruel claws into him and twist his will to serve her dark desires. I regret burdening Elwio with this task, but he was our most trusted general... Please, continue onward, I must make a final request.

_This opens the final area ofBlooming Sepulcher_

### Part Four

To start the quest, speak to Fallen Fernalla (NPC) in Blooming Sepulcher. Starting the quest by speaking the prompt abomination. This gives the item  Gift of Fernalla required for The Life Flame. Completing both The Life Flame and The Dark Flame is required in order to spawn The Abomination in Vitheo's Rest, which drops Key to Vitheo's Tomb upon death.

Stage: Name: Description
1: A Dying Wish, Part 4: Fernalla herself has asked that you find Vitheo and be sure his wordly remains are at rest. She has given you a key ingredient for lighting one of the braziers in his tomb to awaken the Guardian who watches over his inner sanctum's key.

To complete this quest, hand the Fallen Fernalla (NPC) in Blooming Sepulcher the  Memory of Vitheo received from killing the Apparition of Vitheo in Vitheo's Rest.

On Completion

He... he understands. He knows I only sought to help him. That is... such a relief. But... no... something is wrong... I can feel it. The darkness is upon me! My will to aid Vitheo held it at bay, but now... I cannot fight it any longer! Quickly, child, leave this place! I can no longer command what stirs within me! My mortal body is no longer important to me.

_This permanently spawnsFallen Fernalla, the final boss of Blooming Sepulcher_
