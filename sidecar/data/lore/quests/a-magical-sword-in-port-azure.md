## A Magical Sword in Port Azure

Yes

### Gold:

### Faction Changes:

+1 The Blades of Vitheo

### Item(s):

Short Sword of Viropha

Quest Progression Previous:: Next:
-: -

1\. Talk to Tristan Warton behind the house next to the main gate of Port Azure and ask him about his swords. He will offer to trade you a sword if you bring him a Ruby and two Seaspices.

Journal Entry
A Magical Sword in Port Azure: There is a magical sword to be had in Port Azure. Find a Ruby and two Seaspices for Tristan, who is loitering in the back lots of the houses near the main gate.

2\. Hand the items to Tristan, who gives you a Short Sword of Viropha in return.

## Notes

* Seaspice is a guaranteed drop from Ghost of Wyland Sercher and The Lost Sea Giant King.
* Rubies can be farmed from Tinkles and the Molorai Brewmistress.
