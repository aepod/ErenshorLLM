# Willow Seeds

# Willow Seeds

Back to Quests

This quest does not appear in your journal. To complete it, you must trade all five willow seeds to the Watcher on top of a mountain in Willowwatch Ridge.

Seed: Zone
Azure Willow Seed: Port Azure
Braxonian Willow Seed: Braxonian Desert
Duskenlight Willow Seed: Duskenlight Coast
Fernallan Willow Seed: Fernalla's Revival Plains
Ripparian Willow Seed: Ripper's Keep

Partial Completion:

These are Willow seeds? I have never seen them so close up! Are there more?

Completion Message:

You have bestowed upon me a gift, adventurer. Your travels prove your strength and perhaps you can help protect this world. Sivakayans walk among us - watching and invisible. Take this, it'll allow you to see them. Whether you dispatch them is up to you. Beware though - if they sense your awareness, they may attack... and some hold great power.

Upon completion, the player will receive  Watcher's Lens.
