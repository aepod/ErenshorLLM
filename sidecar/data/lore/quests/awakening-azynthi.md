Steps

2\. Talk to A Strange Hermit and say the word Azynthi.
Journal Entry
Awakening Azynthi: A strange hermit in Azynthi's Garden has taken note of you shouting his name. He asked you to speak it to him in person.

Completing this quest will spawn Acolyte of Azynthi.
