__1\. After being handed the Old Church Key by Yarren, head back to Blacksalt Strand.

Journal Entry
The Mathers' Demise, Part 2: Yarren Mathers has given you a key to an old church he says is buried in the hills of Blacksalt Strand.

Locate the church, and see what secrets it holds.

Return any tomes you find to Yarren and he may share more of what he knows with you.

2\. The church is well-hidden between the exit to the Braxonian Desert and the small graveyard. Walk into the bushes to find a gap in the mountains. Follow it until you find the church.

3\. Use the key to open the door and investigate, defeating the Acolytes and Prioresses to find the first two pages of the Chapters of Descent:

*  Chapters of Descent, Passage 1
*  Chapters of Descent, Passage 2

4\. Defeat Acceptor, a rare spawn next to the Statue of Sivakaya, to find the third page:

*  Chapters of Descent, Passage 3

5\. Where the statue faces the wall inside the church, click the wall to reveal a secret passage.

6\. Follow the newly revealed hallway and defeat Lazalith, Soldier of Sivakaya to find the fourth page:

*  Chapters of Descent, Passage 4.

7\. Return to Yarren between the hours of **11:00 PM and 7:00 AM.** and give him all four passages. He thanks you with **55 EXP,** but asks you to continue your investigation by finding the rest of the pages.

## Notes

* Ecclesiarch can also drop the 4th Passage, but it is a rare spawn and has a very low chance of dropping it.
