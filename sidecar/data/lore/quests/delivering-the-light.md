# Delivering the Light

WARNING: This article or section contains spoilers! Proceed with caution.

Back to Quests

To complete this quest give the  Captured Light to Kio the Light Keeper in the Stowaway's Step near the lighthouse.

Note: This is in Stowaway's Step (the tutorial zone) and NOT Shivering Step (the event zone).

Stage: Name: Description
1: Delivering the Light: Give the Kio the Light Keeper the  Captured Light you obtained from defeating Warded Shadow.

On Success:

W... what is this? I feel so strange! Like I've held this before... Like I've been to places that do not exist. Why are you giving this to me? What am I to do with this? *In his confusion, Kio hands you a lantern and waves you off...*
