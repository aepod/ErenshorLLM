__1\. Enter the house in easternBlacksalt Strand to find The Corpse of Brice Mathers and The Corpse of Cyndreela Mathers. Defeat them and loot their corpses to find  The Mathers Note.

2\. Hover over the note in your inventory, which initiates the quest.

Journal Entry
The Mathers' Demise: You've found a note in the pocket of Brice Mathers. It instructs you to find his brother in Goodsoil. His brother's name is Yarren - if you cannot find him, ask for him.

3\. _Optional:_ Talk to Angus Hawer in the camp south of the ruins of Goodsoil in Fernalla's Revival Plains and ask about Yarren. He suspects Yarren has fled southeast to Restful Valley.

4\. _Optional:_ Ask Vaneyer Dallo in the small town in southeast Fernalla's about Yarren. He tells you Yarren has been waiting outside Old Krakengard to confront Eldoth Molorai.

5\. Yarren Mathers can be found by his tent on the coast of Vitheo's Watch, but only at night between the hours of **11:00 PM and 7:00 AM.**

6\. Give Yarren the note, who asks you to investigate an old church in Blacksalt. He gives you the Old Church Key and **55 EXP.**

## Notes

* Be careful not to lose the Old Church Key, as there is currently no known way to obtain another.
