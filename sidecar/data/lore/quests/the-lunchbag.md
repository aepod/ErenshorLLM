1\. Talk to the Shady Fisherman at the end of the dock in Port Azure, who asks you to pick up the bag next to him. Do so, or alternatively, give him a  Bread.

Journal Entry
The Lunchbag Pt. 2: You should ask the Shady Fisherman on the Port Azure docks to tell you a tale. He claims to have found something in the sea that may interest an adventurous person like you.

2\. Ask the Fisherman about Bellwain. He says he's found Captain Bellwain's secret island, and offers to take you there if you help him by Fishing.

Journal Entry
Octorunners: The Fisherman on the Azure Docks has promised you a trip to a lost island in exchange for helping him finish his day's fishing quota.

He needs 4 Octorunners.

3\. Bring the Fisherman 4  Octorunners1. He thanks you and offers you a ride.

4\. Get on the nearby boat to be taken to Bellwain Island.

## Notes

1. ↑ Octorunners are most commonly found at night off the coast of the Braxonian Desert.
