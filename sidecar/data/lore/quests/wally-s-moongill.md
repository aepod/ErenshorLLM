## Wally's Moongill

### Location:

Stowaway's Step

### Repeatable:

No

## Requirements

### Prerequisite:

-

### Item(s) Required:

A Moongill

## Rewards

### Experience:

45

### Gold:

-

### Item(s):

Waldorf Family Ring

Quest Progression Previous:: Next:
-: -

1\. Talk to Wally Waldorf near the steps leading down to the Island Tomb. Prompt him with moongill and he will ask you to bring him one.

Journal Entry
Wally's Moongill: Wally Waldorf is near the shop in Stowaway's Step and he really wants a Moongill fish. Moongill are known to come out at night and are famously hard to catch.

2\. Catch A Moongill by Fishing in the ocean anywhere off the coast of Stowaway's Step between 10:00 pm and 6:00 am.

3\. Give Wally the Moongill, who rewards you with some experience and his Waldorf Family Ring.

## Notes

* A  Fishing Pole can be purchased for 10 gold from the nearby merchant, Amethi Plazzo.
* To use the Fishing Pole, equip it in your main hand and then left click it again while it is already equipped.
* You can check the in-game time by typing /time in the chat.
* You can skip the beginning of the quest by simply giving Wally the Moongill before speaking to him.
