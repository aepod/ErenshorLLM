1\. Talk to Percy Ildia behind the office at the Docks on Stowaway's Step and ask him what he wants. He will offer you passage off the island in exchange for a Seaspice.

Journal Entry
Percy's Seaspice: Percy has asked for some Seaspice and in return he has promised passage off of the island. He is behind the registration office.

2\. Give Percy a Seaspice. In return, he will give you **15 EXP** and  A Rolled Note.

Journal Entry
Passage off the Island: In return for Seaspice. Percy Ildia gave you a note with instructions to give it to the boat captain on the docks who was preparing to leave.

3\. Give the note to Captain Genevieve Voldance by her ship on the docks. Although she still refuses to take you to Erenshor, the player will receive an additional **5 EXP** for their trouble.

### Notes

* The first step can be skipped by simply giving Percy a Seaspice before talking to him.
* Seaspice is easily obtained from killing Ghost of Wyland Sercher.
