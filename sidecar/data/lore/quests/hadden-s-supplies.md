# Hadden's Supplies

Back to Quests

Prompting Hadden Passican with Ripper's will begin this quest and hand you the  Box of Supplies which must be handed to Leena Halsted in Ripper's Keep. She will give you  Leena's Receipt to hand back to Hadden Passican to complete this quest.

Stage: Name: Description
1: Hadden's Supplies: Hadden in Fernalla's Revival Plains has asked you to take a package of supplies to Ripper's Keep. They'll give you a receipt, and if you bring that back to Hadden he'll reward you.
2: Hadden's Supplies 2: Take the receipt of delivery from Ripper's keep back to Haden in Fernalla's Revival Plains.
