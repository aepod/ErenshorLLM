# Albixt's Engagement Ring

Back to Quests

To start this quest speak to Albixt Munger in Silkengrass Meadowlands with the prompt Lost

To complete this quest you must gather and hand him a Simple Engagement Ring

Stage: Name: Description
1: Albixt's Engagement Ring: Albixt Munger in Silkengrass fears that his missing wife is now one of the risen pilgrims in the area. If you find her engagement ring on one of them, return it to him for a reward.

Completion Message:

Oh... I thought I would be happy to know that she's at rest but I hadn't imagined how real this moment would be. thank you for your help, but please leave me be. You can have this as a token of my gratitude.

Total Rewards  EXP gained: Gold Gained: Items gained
250: 0: Elixir of Enlightenment II
