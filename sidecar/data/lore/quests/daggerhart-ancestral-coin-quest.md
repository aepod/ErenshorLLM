Quest Progression Previous:: Next:

1\. Talk to Neziroth Daggerhart near the statue of Vitheo on Stowaway's Step and ask him about his coin to discover it was stolen by bandits.

Journal Entry
Daggerhart Ancestral Coin: Neziroth Daggerhart had his prized ancestral coin stolen by bandits somewhere in Stowaway's Step. If you find it, return it to him near the Shrine to Vitheo.

2\. Defeat Vevillo Polter in the Bandit Lair on Stowaway's Step to find the Daggerhart Ancestral Coin.
3\. Return to Neziroth and give the coin to him. He thanks you and gives you some experience and a Copper Sword in return.

## Notes

* You can skip the quest entirely by simply giving Neziroth the coin before asking about it.
* Further coins dropped from Vevillo Polter have no use and can be freely destroyed.
