# The Dark Flame

Back to Quests

To start this quest, read the Dark Flame Recipe.

To complete it, place 2 Blight Crystals, 1 Blightwood Slab and 1 Spark of the First Flame on the Blackflame brazier in the Abomination wing.

Completing both The Dark Flame and The Life Flame will cause The Abomination to permanently spawn in the Traitor's Halls wing of Vitheo's Rest
