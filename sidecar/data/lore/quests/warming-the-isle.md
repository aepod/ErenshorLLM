# Warming the Isle

# Warming the Isle

Back to Quests

To start this quest you must obtain the Instructions for Warmth from The Hider in the Shivering Step. Once you have the instructions you must head to the bandit cave and at the end will be a Warming Flame. To light the Warming Flame you must put the following items into it - Thief of Breath, Death's Decay, Eternal Ice, Ancient Fang, Soul of a Ghostly Beast in not particular order. This will light the flame causing the entire island of Shivering Step to light up causing more enemies to spawn.
