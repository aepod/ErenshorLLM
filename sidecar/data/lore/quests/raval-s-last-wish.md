Stage: Name: Description
1: Raval's Last Wish: Raval, the First Paladin, has been trapped in an insanity wracked body for centuries. You've freed him and found his spirit, and his last wish is to know that Astra, the Celestial Dragon, has finally been removed from this plane.

Completion Text, when handed the Heart of Astra:

You... you've done it? I cannot believe it. Your power must be immeasureable!

It is a shame that the Solunarian Guard will never know how much pain they've
caused us by standing idly by at the Shrine. Perhaps *you* can find the
answers to the questions that have tortured us for so long. Here... take this.
If you can find a way to use it, you may learn secrets that will change the
world forever...
