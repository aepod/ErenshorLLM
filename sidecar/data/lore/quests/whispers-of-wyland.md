# Whispers of Wyland

# Whispers of Wyland

Back to Quests

To start the quest use the Ghostly Key inside the Island Tomb and slay Ghost of Wyland Sercher, he will drop a Torn Note that triggers the quest when read.

Alternatively you can speak to Revan Gavault at the altar and he will mistaken you for Wyland, thus giving you the quest to find him. To complete this quest all you have to do is hand the Torn Note to Lucian Revald.

Stage: Name: Description
1: Whispers of Wyland: The odd cultists won't say much, but one has mistaken you for their friend 'Wyland'. If you find this 'Wyland', he may be holding a note or a clue as to what they're up to...
2: Wyland's Note: You've found a note on the Ghost of Wyland Sercher. Upon reading it, it has instructed you to speak a secret password to an "Elder brother at the ritual".
