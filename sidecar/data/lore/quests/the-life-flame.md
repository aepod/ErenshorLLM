# The Life Flame

Back to Quests

To start this quest, read the  Life Flame Recipe.

To complete it, place 1  Essence of Nature, 1  Gift of Fernalla 1  Loomingwood Branch and 1  Droplet of the First Frost on the Lifeflame brazier in the Abomination wing.

Completing both The Dark Flame and The Life Flame will cause The Abomination to permanently spawn in the Traitor's Halls wing of Vitheo's Rest
