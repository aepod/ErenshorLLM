The Mathers' Demise, Part 2: -

1\. After completing all prerequisites and gaining entry to the Jaws of Sivakaya in Duskenlight Coast, head there to find the final passages of the Chapters of Descent.

Journal Entry
The Mathers' Demise, Part 2: Yarren Mathers has studied the Chapters of Descent you found in the old Church.

The tale of Sivakaya, Azynthi, and The Wisp has become more clear, but is still shrouded in mystery.

Yarren has asked you to search for more pages from the Chapters of Descent.

2\. Obtain Passages 5-8 of the Chapters of Descent:

*  Chapters of Descent, Passage 5
*  Chapters of Descent, Passage 6
*  Chapters of Descent, Passage 7
*  Chapters of Descent, Passage 8

Most of the enemies and bosses in the Jaws have a chance to drop these pages, including:

* Orinthar
* Vextral
* Astorim
* Karsith
* Balthom, Soldier of Sivakaya
* Aeiros, Soldier of Sivakaya

3\. Once you have obtained them, return to Yarren between the hours of **11:00 PM and 7:00 AM.** to give him all four passages. He thanks you with **550 EXP** and an Elixir of Enlightenment IV.
