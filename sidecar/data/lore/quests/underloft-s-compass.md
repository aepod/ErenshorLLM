# Underloft's Compass

Back to Quests

This quest is not directly given to you, first you must obtain the Small Engraved Compass from the Mysterious Portal

Once you have obtained the Small Engraved Compass hand it to Asaga Underloft in Port Azure

The Quest will complete once the item is given to Asaga Underloft and he will give you The Fernallan Ritual
