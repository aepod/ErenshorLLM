# A Trade in Ripper's Keep

Back to Quests

To start this quest speak to Jeremie Galyard in Ripper's Keep with the prompt Azynthi

To complete it, hand him 3 Azure Mead and 1 Seaspice.

Stage: Name: Description
1: A Trade in Ripper's Keep: Jeremie Galyard has offered to trade a trinket he found in Ripper's Keep that he says may have belonged to Azynthi.

He wants 3 bottles of Azure Mead and a Seaspice.

Completion Message:

Ah, fantastic! And for you, this weird little rock. Whatever it's for, I hope you can use it
