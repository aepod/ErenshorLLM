### Walkthrough

1. The quest is obtained by handing Lost Soul all 7 journal entries found from various dungeon bosses:
* Pg. 1: Faded Entry
* Pg. 2: Faded Entry
* Pg. 3: Faded Entry
* Pg. 4: Faded Entry
* Pg. 5: Faded Entry
* Pg. 6: Faded Entry
* Pg. 7: Faded Entry
2. You must then give the Treasure Hunter's Pin to Julie Xargate in Port Azure
3. The quest is currently unfinished, and ends here for now.

*A trick to get Lost Soul to stop moving long enough to turn in the items is to "Hail".

Stage: Name: Description
1: The Lost Soul: You've found the writings of a treasure hunter and you found his phantom on the Duskenlight Coast near the Malroth Nesting Grounds. He has asked you to continue the hunt in his name, and directed you to his wife for help. He does not recall where he lived, but he says it was in a busy place. He gave you a small pin to prove to his wife that you've met him.
2: The Lost Soul Pt. 2: You've reminded a phantom of who he was in a past life, and he has asked you to continue his treasure hunt. He gave you a pin and directed you to speak to his wife, using the pin as proof of having met him. He said she will help you pick up on his quest where he left off.
3: The Lost Soul Pt. 3: You have a deceased treasure hunter's journal, and a request from his widow to continue his quest. You should bring whatever you find back to him and put him at rest.
