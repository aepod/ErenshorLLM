# Lighting the Nighthollow Candle

Back to Quests

Immediately handed to you upon completion of Recovering the Nighthollow Candle

This quest can be completed by handing the Unlit Nighthollow Candle to the Braxonian Flame Well, found in a room behind Prince Oslind in Fallen Braxonia. The Nighthollow Candle rewarded is needed to complete The Guardian Awakens and unlock Jaws of Sivakaya.

Stage: Name: Description
1: Lighting the Nighthollow Candle: You have restored the Nighthollow Candle, but it is not lit. You must find a Braxonian Flame to ignite the wick and restore the Candle's true power.

Completion Message:

...*The candle comes alive with a flame that can only be extinguished by magic, and while holding it things that you should not be able to see are illuminated in its eerie light*
