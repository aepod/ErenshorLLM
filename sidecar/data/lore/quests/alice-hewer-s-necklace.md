**Alice Hewer 's Necklace** is a Quest in Stowaway's Step.

# Walkthrough

1\. Talk to Alice Hewer under the gazebo by the pier. She says a croc jumped out of the water, ate her necklace, and ran off to the lighthouse just past the office.

Journal Entry
Alice Hewer's Necklace: Alice Hewer's necklace was eaten by a small crocodile near the lighthouse! If you find it, she would like for you to bring it back to her.

2\. Defeat A Brackish Croc Whelp on the way to the lighthouse and collect a  Plain Necklace.1
3\. Give the necklace to Alice Hewer, who thanks you with 15 experience and a  Braided Belt.

## Notes

1. ↑ You can skip the beginning of the quest by simply giving Alice Hewer the necklace without ever speaking to her.
