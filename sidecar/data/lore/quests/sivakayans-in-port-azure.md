# Sivakayans in Port Azure

Back to Quests

First, you need to complete Willow Seeds first and acquire the  Watcher's Lens. Put it in a green cosmetic slot to equip it.

Now, you have a 10% chance to spawn a Spectre every time you are entering a zone. Eligible zones are all **except** Port Azure, Stowaway's Step, Island Tomb, Bellwain Island, Shivering Step, and Shivering Tomb.

If a Spectre spawns in that zone, the zone is marked and a Spectre won't spawn again until you triggered a spawn in at least 5 other zones, or fully restarted the game.

When spawned, the Spectre is placed at a random spawn point in the zone and then wanders the zone, patrolling all other spawn points.

Which Spectre is spawned is random:

* Sivakayan Voidmaster dropping  Sivakayan Encrypted Plans
* Sivakayan Shadow dropping  Sivakayan Codex 1 of 4
* Shrouded Sivakayan dropping  Sivakayan Codex 2 of 4
* Sivakayan High Shadow dropping  Sivakayan Codex 3 of 4
* Sivakayan Doomshade dropping  Sivakayan Codex 4 of 4

Eventually, hand all five items to Captain Kilkay to complete the quest.
