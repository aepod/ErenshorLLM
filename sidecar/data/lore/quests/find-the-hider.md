# Find The Hider

# Find The Hider

Back to Quests

To start this quest you must obtain the Shivering Torch from Kio the Light Keeper. When you have the torch you will be tasked with finding The Hider. He can be found near the entrance portal to Shivering Step near some old ruins.

Find  Stage: Name: Description
1: Find The Hider: Have the Shivering Torch equipped and look around for The Hider, once you find him and speak to him the quest will complete. He will give you Instructions for Warmth.
