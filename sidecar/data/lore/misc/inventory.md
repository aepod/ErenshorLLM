# Inventory

The inventory screen as seen in the current version of the game.

The inventory is where you'll find all the loot you've acquired and where you can equip it. The inventory consists of 15 equipment slots, 25 inventory slots and an additional 'Destroy Item' slot. The window is opened by pressing 'i'.

Below you'll find a list of current equipment slots. For a list of available items, see Weapons and Armor.

* Waist

* Back

Holding right click on an item like the Idol of Sivakaya gives you a quick overview of the item. Some information includes: stats, description, requirements, slots.

The equipment slots are where you can place an item to equip it. To do this, left click an item and drag it to the corresponding slot. Holding right click on an item in the inventory will give you a quick overview. Here you'll find more information about the Stats you'll gain, flavor text about the item, the class requirements and the usable slots.

### Inventory slots

The inventory slots are where you can place loot after killing an enemy. This is done by left-clicking the item and dragging it, or pressing right-click to quickloot. There's a total of 25 slots that can be filled after which items will be placed in the 'Destroy Item' slot. Any items placed in this slot will be lost upon placing another item over it.

Consumables can be right-clicked in the inventory to be consumed, which will give a temporary buff. Additionally, one can split stacks by utilising Left CTRL and Left SHIFT before clicking. To break a stack of items into a singular one just press Left CTRL+Left-Click on the items. To break a stack of items into 10, Left SHIFT+Left-Click on the items.

Currently, most items (apart from Weapons and Armor) stack indefinitely.

### Gold window

The inventory is also where you'll find your current gold balance. Gold is gained upon looting and by selling items to a merchant or a Simulated player. In the demo version it can be used to buy items from merchants.
