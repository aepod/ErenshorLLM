# Erenshor

# Erenshor

**Erenshor** is the primary continent the game takes place in.

# History

As detailed in the game's various Lore books, Siva and Brax gave birth to the world of Amarion and created several continents upon its surface, Erenshor being one of them. Upon settling on the continent, Siva and Brax opened the Siva-Braxon School of Magic. In time, a man named Azynthi began to train at the school, and upon his graduation, set out to the north to venture beyond the Twin Soldiers. His subsequent return caused a chain reaction of tragedies to befall the continent, the echoes of which can still be felt to this day.

# Notable Locations

A map of all the currently known locations in Erenshor.

Erenshor contains many different Zones and biomes, as well as towns and settlements:

## Port Azure

Port Azure is the largest town in Erenshor, having been founded in 1552. The government of Merosavilla currently runs the city through its military as it prepares for an inevitable confrontation with The Children of Sivakaya.

## Ripper's Keep

Not everyone was happy with the militaristic takeover of Port Azure, with some defecting as mercenaries to try and save the many small villages and settlements dotted throughout Erenshor. Led by Ripper DeMerth, they make their home in Ripper's Keep.

## Stowaway's Step

A sort of "halfway point" between Merosavilla and Erenshor, this is where travelers find themselves stranded upon trying to reach the main continent now that contact with the outside world has been completely cut off. A small town, Wickliff, has begun construction on the island, and it is here that players begin the game as they try to find a way to Erenshor.1
