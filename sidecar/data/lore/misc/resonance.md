# Resonance

# Resonance

Gives a % chance to instantly recast a spell for free. Doubled spells cause half of their normal damage.

Instant-cast spells cannot be affected by resonance.

The % chance is directly proportional to your Resonance in a 1:1 ratio. E.g. A resonance of 50 will give the character a 50% chance to double-cast spells.

Arcanists and Druids can learn the Arcane Recovery ability which restores mana when resonance procs.
