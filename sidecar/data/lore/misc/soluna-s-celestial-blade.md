# Soluna's Celestial Blade

# Soluna's Celestial Blade

Quest starts in the Azynthi's Garden. Once given the Azynthian Riftstone you can traverse between night and day. You can then talk to Apostalo Ostagla who is inside of the crumbling tower. After learning the word Azynthi ( and completing the quest to kill Eldoth Molorai ), stand beside the Hermit and /shout Azynthi. Acolyte of Azynthi will then spawn along with 2 Greater Azynthian Icebone and 2 Greater Azynthian Firebone. Kill Acolyte of Azynthi and loot Stolen Memories. Return to Apostalo Ostagla and hand him the Stolen Memories. This should complete both Soluna's Celestial Blade part 1 and part 2 ( part 3 is to be continued )
