# Wishing Well

**Wishing Wells** are respawn points throughout Erenshor for the player and their party.

# Description

Left-clicking a Wishing Well sets it as your current respawn point.1 When you die, pressing the Respawn button on-screen will resurrect you back at your last activated Wishing Well. Only one Wishing Well can be active at a time.

The  Mooring Coin will allow you to teleport to your last activated Wishing Well at any time.

Page 13 of the Erenshor **Official Player's Guide** describes Wishing Wells:

> The world of Erenshor is a very dangerous place. If you die alone, or if

>
