# Ability Books

# Ability Books

**Ability Books** are consumable items that teach the player various spells and abilities.

# Usage

Right click on a Spell Scroll or Skill Book in your inventory to read it. If you meet or exceed the required level, the item will be consumed and you will learn the corresponding ability.

Simulated Players automatically acquire spells and skills at the appropriate levels once the player changes Zones or logs out, with the exception of the Level 35 Skill Book for each Class.

# How to Acquire

Ability Books can be bought from various merchants throughout Erenshor, as well as found as loot from various Enemies and Bosses. Some noteworthy Spell and Skill Book Merchants for all classes include:

* Amethi Plazzo in Stowaway's Step
* Aldoa Manday in Stowaway's Step
* Roreli Gilmare in Port Azure
* Aspen Dechaffe in the Braxonian Desert
* Juctin Mysia in the Loomingwood

## Arcanist

Arcanists begin the game with  Spell Scroll: Magic Bolt and  Spell Scroll: Dazzle.

In addition to general merchants, Arcanists can also buy Spell Scrolls from Nylith Valorro in Hidden Hills, as well as Cerbantias Flameward and Edwin Ansegg by the lighthouse in Port Azure.

A Simulated Player Arcanist must be given  Skill Book: Lingering Inferno in order for them to learn the ability.
