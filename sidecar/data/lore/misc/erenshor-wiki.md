# Erenshor Wiki

**Welcome to the Official Erenshor Wiki!**

The definitive Wiki for the single player MMORPG, Erenshor.

**MAJOR SPOILERS INSIDE! BE WARNED!**
The Erenshor wiki is a culmination of all of the information gathered from within the game and as such is spoiler heavy. Browse at your own risk!

Erenshor is a single player nod to the MMORPGs of the past. Partner up with our signature "Simulated Players" to experience all of the joys of open-world MMORPG gameplay on your own schedule and without the time commitments. There are thousands of items, quests, raids, and secrets to discover!

How to Help

If you want to contribute to Erenshor Wiki, here are some tips.

* Read the Rules of this Wiki.
* Register an account to keep track of your contributions.
* Remember to maintain a standard for grammar and punctuation in contributions.
* See Recent Changes and New Pages to check if there is something you can improve.
* See the Missing Pages list. If you think you can create one of them, do it!
* Find and add details for items with Unknown Sources \- there might even be bosses that aren't on the wiki yet!
