# Mid-Level (~20) Recommended

## Stormcaller

**Stormcaller Gear**
Suggested Proficiencies: Physicality: 6
Hardiness: 6
Finesse: 12
Defense: 6
Arcanism: 34
Restoration: 6
Mind: 6

Stormcallers spells are much more powerful than bow abilities in the mid levels, offering around 30% more DPS than strength or dexterity builds. For this reason, we prioritize intelligence over everything else as much as possible. This will leave you with low HP, so you may want to make some substitutions for gear that features higher HP or endurance.  The Gift of Eloquence is mandatory for this build to function effectively. Without it, you will run out of mana often and your DPS will suffer greatly. Recommended auras are  Rising Moon,  Freedom of the Sky, and  Glow of Eternity. If you want to purchase  Gem of Echoes off the auction house, it will help significantly, but you will not be able to acquire the drop itself until the late 20s or early 30s as it comes from Granitus.

Alternative Gear  Damage: Survivability
Fungus Covered Armband: Desert Silk Sleeves
Layer Cape: Lost Cape
Braxonian Wrap: Spidersilk Shirt
Boots of Grounding: Devout Moccasins
Adorned Mitts: Nagalok Claw Gloves
Reed Trousers: Cavesilk Trousers

The  Humanbone is preferred over the  Seastring for the  Theft of Life proc, but both will provide similar DPS. For proficiencies, you want to put as many points as possible into intelligence without sacrificing your survivability. If you are struggling with spells being resisted, you can shift some points into charisma. Putting points into endurance will increase your HP. Agility is not recommended as you'll have low AC regardless.

# Pre-Raid Recommended

This item guide will be the best possible gear obtainable before you enter Raid endgame content. Note that all "suggested proficiencies" include the proficiency point bonuses obtained from the charm recommended by the build, and for Arcanist include the proficiency bonus to Intelligence from putting 3 points into the Mind Proficiency Ascension Skill.

If you believe there are better alternatives to what is listed please reach out to Sned on the Erenshor Discord. This page is in a constant state of refinement and will be adjusted if optimizations are found and tested to be accurate.

## Windblade

**Strength/Dexterity/Resonance**
Suggested Proficiencies: Physicality: 30
Hardiness: 10
Finesse: 27
Defense: 5
Arcanism: 7
Restoration: 2
Mind: 6

Haste is very important to this build so using  Wisp's Presence is recommended. Using  Flowing Blight Silk Sash is recommended for the  Flow buff, but  Sableheart's Harness is a good alternative for higher resonance. You can use  Seaspice as well for an additional buff, though this will not work for simplayers.

The optimal haste buffs will be -  Death's Embrace II (15%),  Embrace of Shadow (15%),  Whispers of Wind (21%), and  Wisp's Presence (10%)

Because we're focusing proficiencies on strength and dexterity, running  Eclipse is recommended. Using the  Gem of Echoes will provide additional resonance for an increase in DPS, but can be swapped out if you wish to run something else.

## Arcanist

Last updated Dec. 12, 2025

**Resonance**
Suggested Proficiencies: Physicality: 3
Hardiness: 10
Finesse: 3
Defense: 3
Arcanism: 40
Restoration: 9
Mind: 19

Stacking resonance is very important for arcanists due to the "Roaring Echoes" ascension skill, which increases spell damage for resonating casts for each point over 100 resonance. However, it is only possible to get a maximum of 102 resonance right now on an arcanist, which minimizes the potential benefit of this ascension Skill. This requires usage of both the  Gem of Echoes and  Hallows Eve auras as well as upgrading all items to godly (purple) tier, making it unlikely for the average player to obtain at the moment without a very large amount of grinding and farming for materials.

Using  Grip of Darkness over  Gloves of Vitheo, sacrifices one resonance in favor of the increased attack speed from  Embrace of Shadow for higher DPS. Note that  Soul Echo can be swapped with  Crest of Braxonia at no cost, as the  Mana Charge II effect is already obtained from  Ring of Echoes and multiple copies of  Mana Charge II do not stack.

Recommended auras are  Whispers of Wind,  Hallows Eve,  Gem of Echoes, and  Lightning Strike as these provide the highest amount of haste and intelligence. This will potentially sacrifice the DPS of melee classes such as paladins or windblades so that should be taken into consideration. The fifth slot depends on the player's needs. If mana is an issue, the  Sage's Trinket is an excellent option. Running  Wisp's Presence provides no benefit as its haste effect does not stack with the ones provided by other gear.

## Druid

**Druid Guide**

Important: For Druid the reason you prioritize resonance over everything is because of the stat ratio. The ratios for Druid are as follows: 1 Resonance = 11 Intelligence (For your damage spells), 1 Resonance = 28 Wisdom (For your crit heals), and 1 Resonance = 30 Wisdom (For your non-crit heals). Do keep in mind this ratio is only applicable to max level end game Druid and does not apply to early game. In the end game you also want to have as many Vengeful Healing procs from your Ascension Skills as possible which Resonance enables.

Recommended: If you or a sim player in your party are using a  Gem of Echoes and you have all purple blessed gear you can drop the  Soul Echo and use a  Temporal Shard as an alternative as there is no reason for Druid to exceed 100 Resonance. If you or a sim player are not using a  Gem of Echoes this setup will have you at exactly 100 Resonance.

You can opt into using a  Cursed Hide Shield in your off-hand if you want to have a much more tanky setup.

If you enjoy getting into melee range you can mainhand a  Twisted Branch for  Blight procs and swap  Ulor's Encyclopedia for  Eternox, Remnant of the Void

We are using  Rotten Hands over  Grip of Darkness because we get  Embrace of Shadow from  Eldoth's Heirloom Ring. The buff  Gift of Rot also gives +20 Str, Dex, Wis, and Int to the player as a hidden bonus making them superior in stat bonuses.  Gift of Rot also applies a -25 resistance debuff to any enemy it hits.

## Tank Paladin

**Paladin Tank Guide**

Important: Against strong enemies  Eon, Blade of Time is better than the  Azynthian Blade for healing but against weaker enemies that lack high resistances the  Azynthian Blade is superior in healing. The  Azynthian Blade is also superior in damage over all when compared to  Eon, Blade of Time but against the toughest enemies  Eon, Blade of Time will provide you and your party with the most survivability. So pick whichever you believe will suit your needs the most.

The reason you run  Resonating Brace over  Officer's Bracer is for resonance procs on your  Cursed Hide Shield and  Eon, Blade of Time

The reason we are running  Fury Helm over  Monarch's Mask is because  Contagious Rage overrides  Embrace of Shadow from  Eldoth's Heirloom Ring which allows you to use the ring which has amazing stat bonuses which make up for stat loss on  Monarch's Mask and allows for more aggro generation.

If you are having no issues with staying alive and you can keep your health high you can swap  Cursed Hide Shield for  Griefwarden to have a more utility focused build

If you want to min-max buffs you are going to want to have these items to swap on for their buff quickly -  Ocean Aged Ring for  Protection and  Amulet of Azure for  Soluna's Solar Blessing. Keeping  Nourished and  Hydrated active is also recommended.

## Stormcaller

Last updated Nov. 14, 2025

**Resonance**

**Intelligence**
Suggested Proficiencies: Physicality: 9
Hardiness: 6
Finesse: 12
Defense: 6
Arcanism: 40
Restoration: 6
Mind: 8

As stormcallers are casters first, focusing on intelligence will provide the best results, relying on spells cast with  Imbued Arrow for major damage. With Imbue Mastery procs, you will get the benefits of  Imbued Arrow without the increased cool downs. Because of Imbue Mastery,  Trick Shot, and procs, stormcaller DPS is heavily affected by RNG. The resonance build leans into this, offering the potential for higher spikes of damage, but at the cost of consistency. The intelligence build varies less, though it is still noticeably impacted by RNG. The importance of intelligence is further reinforced by the  Arcane Proficiency skill as well as the ascension skills Imbue Mastery and Lightning Rod. Players should still keep  Envenomed Arrow on most targets as well as using  Razor Tipped Arrow on bosses for additional damage.

The  Mana Charge II effect from the  Siraethe's Prayercrest or  Soul Echo and  Mana Flood from the  Bracelet of the Vessel provide ample mana recovery both in and out of combat, meaning there is a low chance of running out of mana while fighting and much faster mana recovery outside of combat. This can be further supplemented with the  Sage's Trinket if the player still needs additional mana regeneration, though this is less of an issue with the ability to sit. Players should focus on imbuing spells with every cast if possible. Bow damage is negligible compared to spell damage, so strength is not as emphasized. Dexterity ends up being more important as it affects proc rate to a small degree.

Damage is also enhanced by higher haste, which means using the  Flowing Blight Silk Sash is important. Using  Seaspice will provide additional benefit should you choose to use it. The haste provided by the  Vampirism on  Plaguewind and  Embrace of Shadow from either  Grip of Darkness or  Sivakayan Tricorner make them more valuable than items that may have higher intelligence or resonance.

Recommended auras are  Whispers of Wind,  Hallows Eve,  Gem of Echoes, and  Lightning Strike as these provide the highest amount of haste and intelligence. This will potentially sacrifice the DPS of melee classes such as paladins or windblades so that should be taken into consideration. The fifth slot depends on the player's needs. If mana is an issue, the  Sage's Trinket is an excellent option. Running  Wisp's Presence provides no benefit as its haste effect does not stack with the ones provided by other gear.

#### Alternative Options

The  Naturewalker's Breeches can be used in lieu of the  Creationist's Legwrap. It will provide additional resonance and additional resistances at the cost of some charisma, mana, and armor. This will provide a little more survivability against casters as well as a minuscule amount of damage while making the player slightly more vulnerable to melee damage. The  Mana Charge passive does not stack with  Mana Charge II, however. If the player is doing fine on mana regen, this would allow swapping out the  Soul Echo for  Temporal Shard, which would increase intelligence significantly at the cost of resonance. Players can also consider swapping the  Resonating Brace for the  Bracelet of the Vessel for higher resonance and burst damage from  Focus, though this would be more dependent on RNG.
