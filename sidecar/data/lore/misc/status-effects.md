# Status Effects

# Status Effects

There are different types of effects: Buffs and Debuffs.

### Buffs

Name: Effect: Notes
Nourished: Increases Hit Points, Mana, and Run Speed: +25 HP & MP, +1% Movement Speed
Hydrated: Increases Stats and Attack Speed: +5 Str, Dex, End, Agi, Wis, Int, +3% Attack Speed
Minor Protection: Increases Armor: +35 AC
Spiced Fury: Adds Attack Speed: +15%

### Debuffs

Name: Effect: Notes
Dazzle: Briefly stun your target, rendering them helpless.: 4.0s duration
Tangle: Immobilize a target for a short time.: 54s duration
Weak Poison: Deals 50 poison damage over time to your target.: 30s duration
