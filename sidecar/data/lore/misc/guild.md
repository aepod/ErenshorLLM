# Guild

# Guild

**A guild** is a group of SimPlayers that play together in Erenshor. The player character may also join or create a guild of their own, and invite SimPlayers to it. SimPlayers may initiate guild quests occasionally. The player can interact with and ask simple questions of their guildmates by using /guild.

## Guild Management

To access the **Guild Manager** , click the Guild Manager button in the menu bar. The Guild Manager shows a list of guilds with their Guild Rankings, and lists players in the selected guild. From here you can summon the guild to the guild hall, invite members to your group directly, invite or kick members, and create or leave a guild.

### Inviting SimPlayers

To invite a SimPlayer to your guild, you should first party with them and increase their friendliness toward you. It also helps if you add them to your friends list. After a bit, ask them if they will join your guild, and if their friendliness is high enough, they'll agree. They may also agree if you send them an invite directly, though they will suggest that you should ask first. Once the SimPlayer agrees, they will travel to you for an in-person invite. If they like you enough, they'll even leave their existing guild to join yours. Friends' Club members are an exception; they will not leave their guild for yours, nor party with you.

## Guild Quests

Occasionally, SimPlayers will ask for help acquiring a specific item. This will be displayed as a yellow ! next to their entry in the guild roster. If you happen to have the item in your pocket, you can simply give it to them, but they will appreciate it much more if you invite them to your party, and they get to be present for the drop.

## Guild Rank

Guild rank is calculated from the roster strength of your top 15 players, plus world event wins. The highest ranked guild on the server gets boosts to loot drops, including Sivakruxes, Planar Stones, and Shards.

Occasionally, the Spirits of Erenshor will declare a Guild Race Target. The guild that wins the race gains additional rank.

## Fixed Guilds

### Friends' Club

Friends' Club is an oppositional guild in Erenshor. Its members are abrasive, constantly proclaim their greatness, and don't party or associate with non-guild members (including the player).

### Erenshor's Young

Erenshor's Young is the default guild that all characters default to.
