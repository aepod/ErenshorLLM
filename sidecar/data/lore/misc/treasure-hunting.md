# Treasure Hunting

# Treasure Hunting

WARNING: This article or section contains spoilers! Proceed with caution.

# Treasure Hunting Guide

1\. Reading a  Treasure Map will reveal a zone in which a Lost Treasure is located.

2\. Once you arrive in the zone, the treasure's co-ordinates will be revealed and you will need to go and find it!

If you forget the co-ordinates or the message gets lost, the location will also be displayed beside the compass.

3\. At the location, you'll find a pile of dirt and leaves with a golden aura.

4\. Interacting with the dirt will reveal a chest.

5\. Attack the chest and it'll spawn a wave of enemies which scale with the player's level. There are **2 to 5 waves** in total. They will consist of any combination of **3 to 5** of the following enemies:

* Ancient Skeleton
* Ancient Demon
* Ancient Horror

**Warning: If you die during the fight for the treasure you will not receive another chance and the treasure will be lost.**

6\. Once the final wave has been defeated, attacking the chest will open it, allowing you to loot the contents inside.

# Treasure Loot

There are four tiers of treasure chest, which spawn depending on the player's level:

* Lost Treasure (1-10)
* Lost Treasure (10-20)
* Lost Treasure (20-30)
* Lost Treasure (30+)
