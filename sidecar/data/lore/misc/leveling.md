# Leveling

In Erenshor you can level up your character by killing Enemies or completing quests. Each class gets different bonuses to their HP, AC, and MP stats upon a level-up. Every class starts with 5 in each stat, and 5 in each resistance. You can gain experience by killing enemies/NPCs or completing quests. Targets 5 levels below you no longer grant experience (considering will be green at 5 or more levels below you).

### Ascension

Once a player has reached the level cap (currently 35) they will begin to recieve Ascension Experience. Once enough has been earned the player will recieve and Ascension point which can be spent on various perks - some general, and some specific to their class.

Simplayers will also earn and automatically spend their ascension points. To see what they've spent, target them and type /ascview

Each Ascension point requires 38000 ascension experience. For full details on the Ascension bonuses, go to Ascension Skills
