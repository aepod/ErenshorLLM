# Shivering Step Walkthrough

# Shivering Step Walkthrough

WARNING: This article or section contains spoilers! Proceed with caution.

This is a complete walkthrough to the entire Shivering Step zone. Beware of Spoilers! You will likely want to be in the early 20s before attempting to complete this questline.

# Phase 1

1\. To enter Shivering Step, first defeat the Crypt Keeper on Stowaway's Step, and loot the  Shivering Key (Halloween Event). Use the key on the crypt behind the Crypt Keeper to enter Shivering Step.

2\. Upon entering Shivering Step, you will acquire the quest Meet Shivering Step. You'll have to travel to the four NPCs marked on the Phase 1 map above to complete this quest.

3\. First, head left from the entry portal until you reach a fenced in area, where you'll find the Curator. Talking to the Curator will complete The Curator.

4\. Drop down the nearby cliff towards the Lighthouse. Optional: Talk to Kio near the lighthouse to learn that you can get an item, the  Guide to Sight by fishing in Shivering Step. Fish in the nearby water until you get  Guide to Sight, and then read it to complete Seeing the Truth. This will make a ghostly bridge appear elsewhere in the zone.

5\. Using the map above, head towards the Gatekeeper. You should pass by Enterprising Spirit, talk to it and purchase  Eternal Ice.

6\. Continue towards the Gatekeeper and talk to it to complete The Gatekeeper.

7\. Continue heading up the hill, past the cave, and around the back of the island towards the Harbinger. Talk to it to complete The Harbinger.

8\. Double back, and head towards the Bridgekeeper. Talk to it to complete The Bridgekeeper. You will also now complete Meet Shivering Step and acquire The Shivering Torch.

9\. (Optional): Cross the nearby illusory bridge to fight Mephallo. This is not required by any quests, but Mephallo drops some strong items.

10\. Return to Kio at the lighthouse to complete The Shivering Torch and acquire the  Shivering Belt Lamp and Find the Hider. Equip the  Shivering Belt Lamp in a cosmetic slot.

# Phase 2

11\. Use the laughing sound effects to find The Hider, although it should be on the cliffs almost directly above where you are right now. Talk to it to complete Find the Hider and acquire  Instructions for Warmth.

12\. You are now in the heavy combat section of the dungeon. As you fight your way towards Dream, try to acquire both  Thief of Breath and  Death's Decay. Don't fret if you don't get them, you'll have more opportunities to before you need to go out of your way to acquire them.

13\. When you reach where Dream is marked on the map above, you will need to drop down off a cliff into the water, and then turn around towards the island and swim into a cave. Inside, defeat Dream and loot the  Ancient Fang.

14\. Leave the cave, and head towards Ghost of Plaxitheris, which spawns in the same location Plaxitheris would in Stowaway's Step. Continue fighting through adds and try to loot  Thief of Breath and  Death's Decay.

15\. Defeat Ghost of Plaxitheris and loot  Soul of a Ghostly Beast. If you do not have  Thief of Breath and  Death's Decay, now you will need to farm until you have both.

16\. Head to the Warming Flame deep inside the cave where the bandits are in Stowaway's Step. Hand in  Thief of Breath,  Death's Decay,  Soul of a Ghostly Beast,  Ancient Fang, and  Eternal Ice to the Warming Flame to complete Warming the Isle and acquire Changing the Lighthouse.

# Phase 3

17\. Exit the cave and head up the hill to where the small town would be on Stowaway's Step. Defeat the Fearantula and loot the  Blue Burnstone.

18\. (Optional): In the same location where you talked to the Harbinger early, find and defeat Nightmare for some loot and to acquire  Demonic Statue which gives a hint on how to proceed further.

19\. Head to the large gargoyle Vitheo statue, and climb it to find Outgrown Terror. Talk to it and say "Sned" to receive  Shivering Step Lighthouse Key.

20\. Head to the Lighthouse and enter it using your newly acquired key. Inside, give  Blue Burnstone to Lighthouse Flame to complete Changing the Lighthouse.

21\. Kio the Darkbringer will now spawn just outside of the entrance to the Shivering Step equivalent of the Island Tomb. Be careful when fighting Kio, as he will get healed when the beam of light from the lighthouse touches him.

22\. After defeating Kio the Darkbringer, loot  Awaken the Angel and read it to open the entrance to the Island Tomb and complete Stand, Angel!. Be sure to loot  Dockhouse Key as well.

23\. Enter the Shivering Step version of the Island Tomb, the Shivering Tomb behind where Kio spawned.

24\. Head straight inside the dungeon until you find a hole in the floor. Drop down the hole.

25\. Head through the door at the bottom and defeat the Soul-Linked Spiders and loot  Shadowed Key from one of them. Use the key to open the door in front of you.

26\. Inside the next room is the Warded Shadow boss. You won't be able to damage it yet, however. Enter the two side rooms and destroy the Phantom's Ward in each room. The wards will become invulnerable to damage partway through fighting them; destroy the Undying Light that is damageable to be able to damage the ward again (the one you can damage will have glowing particles that are a different color).

27\. Head back and defeat Warded Shadow, and loot  Captured Light. Note that you will need two of this item to fully complete all of Shivering Step, so you may want to either wait for a respawn or come back later.

28\. Head back to the Soul-Linked Spider room, and go into the side room with a ladder to exit the dungeon.

29\. You now have two possible places to turn in  Captured Light. The "less important" one can be done while still inside Shivering Step. Head to the dockhouse, and use the  Dockhouse Key you acquired earlier to enter. Give the  Captured Light to the Founder inside to complete Shivering Step (Quest) and receive  Slumberbloom Ring.

30\. With a  Captured Light in hand, leave Shivering Step. Give it to Kio the Light Keeper in Stowaway's Step to complete Delivering the Light. This gives you the very useful item  Keeper's Lantern which acts as a light source and (on right-click) allows you to set your respawn location (like a wishing well) almost anywhere in the open world. In combination with  Mooring Coin, this allows you to freely set a teleport point almost anywhere in the world.
