### Status

Active

The Azure Guard are the military force entrusted by the Merosavillan government of Port Azure to protect their interests in Erenshor.

They are led by Captain Kilkay who is a staunch believer that with the return of Sivakayans to Erenshor that the only way that he can guarantee everyone's survival is by putting everyone behind the huge walls of Port Azure This led to him creating a decree to recall all soldiers and peoples back to the city.

Unfortunately, not all of his soldiers agreed with this and a faction led by Ripper DeMerth broke off from the Azure Guard, leading to conflict between the soldiers loyal to the Azure Guard and Ripper's Soldiers.

An uneasy stalemate now exists between the two factions, as neither are powerful enough to defeat the other without both heavy losses and losing their ability to protect their citizens. Each faction is now simply attempting to find ways to eliminate the other's leader in the hopes that it will bring their soldiers back into the fold.

## The Decree of Port Azure

In light of new intelligence indicating that the fabled Sivakayans have awoken, the Council of Port Azure, in the interest of safeguarding our citizens and preserving the security of our lands, hereby issues the following decree:

**I. Closure of the Ports:**
It is declared that, effective immediately, the ports of Erenshor shall be closed to all travelers and settlers. All inbound settlers, pilgrims, or travelers must be redirected to Stowaway’s Step and allowed to return from whence their travel began, or to settle on Stowaway’s Step.

**II. Deployment of the Azure Guard:**
The full strength of the Azure Guard shall be recalled and permanently garrisoned within the walls of Port Azure. Their orders are only to fortify the city’s defenses. Their protection of outlying settlements is terminated effective immediately and all citizens are invited to shelter within the walls, if necessary, within inns, alleys, streets, or warehouses.

**III. Priority for Outlying Settlements:**
Residents of outlying settlements shall be given first priority for access to the protection and shelter provided by Port Azure. The Council encourages all citizens seeking refuge to leave their homes and make prompt arrangements to secure their safety within the fortified city limits.

**IV. Warning to the Citizens:**
Let it be known that those who choose to remain in their current homes, outside the protective embrace of our city walls, do so at their own peril. The emerging threat of the Sivakayan soldiers is not to be underestimated, and the protection offered by Port Azure is hereby the sole guarantee of security during these perilous times. This decree is issued in the spirit of caution and all citizens are urged to comply without delay. May wisdom and vigilance guide our actions as we face the challenges ahead. Issued this day by the Captain Kilkay, Acting Authority of the Azure Guard
