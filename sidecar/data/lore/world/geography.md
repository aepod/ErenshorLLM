# Geography of Erenshor

Erenshor is a continent within the world of Amarion, personally designed by the gods Siva and Brax. The continent of Merosavilla also exists in Amarion and governs Port Azure. The landscape ranges from coastal zones to deserts, forests, mountains, swamps, and islands.

---

The southern coast includes the starting zones: Stowaway's Step (island, levels 1-6), Hidden Hills (levels 4-8), Faerie's Brake (levels 3-10), and Vitheo's Watch (levels 4-10). Willowwatch Ridge (levels 1-8) provides an alternative starting area to the north. These zones transition into mid-level content.

---

The western region includes Duskenlight Coast (levels 7-19) with the giant skull landmark containing Jaws of Sivakaya, Windwashed Pass (levels 15-30) serving as a major crossroads, Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (levels 20-30), and the Prielian Cascade dungeon (levels 26-33).

---

The central region features Fernalla's Revival Plains (levels 6-18) with the Blooming Sepulcher beneath its great willow, Rottenfoot swamp (levels 12-20) to the north, Silkengrass Meadowlands (levels 10-30) with its vast grasslands, and Loomingwood forest (levels 18-26) further north.

---

The eastern region includes the Braxonian Desert (levels 16-25) with Fallen Braxonia dungeon beneath it, The Blight (levels 26-33) showing Sivakaya's spreading corruption, and Azynthi's Garden island (levels 30-35) accessible by ferry from The Blight.

---

The northern region contains Soluna's Landing (levels 25-33) where the goddess arrived, Ripper's Keep (levels 28-33) with the Abyssal Lake dungeon, and Azynthi's Garden Dimensional Rift.

---

Port Azure (southeastern coast, levels 1-35) is the capital city, containing the Lost Cellar dungeon, Vitheo's Rest dungeon, the Auction House, the Reliquary Hall guild hall, and all major services. Blacksalt Strand (levels 14-21) lies just north of Port Azure with the Elderstone Mines.

---

Event zones exist outside the main map: Bellwain Island (puzzle zone, any level), Shivering Step (dream zone, levels 19-24), and the Secluded Sanctuary (level 35 mini-dungeon accessed from Stowaway's Step). The unimplemented Planes include Vitheo's Plane of War, Soluna's Celestial Plane, Fernalla's Plane of the Willow, Sivakaya's Plane of Destruction, and Brax's Elemental Plane (all planned for levels 32-35).

---

Zone progression by level: Stowaway's Step/Willowwatch Ridge (1-8) to Hidden Hills/Faerie's Brake/Vitheo's Watch (3-10) to Fernalla's Revival Plains/Duskenlight Coast (6-19) to Rottenfoot/Blacksalt Strand/Braxonian Desert (12-25) to Silkengrass/Windwashed/Loomingwood/Malaroth's (10-30) to Soluna's/The Blight/Ripper's Keep/Azynthi's (25-35).
