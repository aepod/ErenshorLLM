The Solunarian Brotherhood are a faction of Paladins 'allegedly'. They built Port Azure and are in search for the legendary weapon Flameblade. Their main base of operation is the church in Port Azure.

The goddess Soluna arrived on Erenshor in an area now known as Soluna's Landing \- causing a great crater to appear where she landed.

Soluna's devoted elemental servants, the Solunarians, passed on Soluna's teachings to the humans that dwelled there - training them to become the first Paladins.
