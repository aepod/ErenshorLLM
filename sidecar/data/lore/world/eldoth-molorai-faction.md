The Eldoth Molorai seem to be a faction of elves. They believe that they are the righteous protectors of Erenshor, and their goal is to keep the marauders and interlopers (pretty much anyone who isn't them) from gathering the powerful trinkets scattered across Erenshor that could and would do damage in the wrong hands. Not much is known yet of if they mean well or not.
