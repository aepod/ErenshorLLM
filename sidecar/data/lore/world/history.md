# History of Erenshor

The world of Amarion was created by two divine lovers, Siva and Brax. They designed the continents of Merosavilla and Erenshor, banishing a great darkness beneath the surface of the world. Siva personally crafted much of Erenshor's landscape. The Siva-Braxon School of Magic was established as an educational institution on Erenshor.

---

Azynthi was a magical prodigy and star pupil of the Siva-Braxon School. He journeyed beyond the Twin Soldiers in northern Erenshor and returned fundamentally changed. He offered Siva forbidden dark power, manipulating her into a ritual that stripped her essence. This transformed Siva into Sivakaya, "an unholy winged agent of death." A shadowy monolith formed as a physical manifestation of the corruption, spreading blight across the land.

---

Brax created the demigoddess Fernalla in Siva's image, representing abundance, life, and fertility. Siva had previously created the demigod Vitheo with powers of Water and Air, sworn to protect the land. Both demigods fell in the War of the Gods against corrupted Sivakaya's forces, achieving godhood through their sacrifice.

---

Brax responded to the blighted monolith by burying all of Braxonia beneath desert sands, creating the Braxonian Desert. He then abandoned the world in despair, leaving Amarion godless.

---

Eldrin Shadowmire led loyal Sivakaya followers in founding The Wisp, a faction dedicated to restoring Siva to her original form. They created the Nighthollow Candle artifact for their restoration attempts. Meanwhile, the Children of Sivakaya embraced the corrupted power.

---

The goddess Soluna arrived at Soluna's Landing, spreading celestial teachings through the Solunarians led by Valao the Holy. Her arrival had unintended consequences, including the destruction of Rockshade Hold when magical debris was flung into the skies, cursing its scholarly inhabitants into living stone. The flame elemental Pyroclast was born from this onslaught and enslaved the cursed population.

---

In the year 1552, Solunarian Paladins discovered evidence of Soluna's presence in Erenshor, leading to the founding of Port Azure as a capital city. The Fernallans, Braxonians, and Vitheans joined together to build the city. During early construction, a structural collapse destroyed a Sivakayan prison facility, the remains of which became the Lost Cellar.

---

Soluna eventually departed Erenshor, apparently pursued by a mysterious figure called Astra. The Solunarian Brotherhood maintains her legacy. Port Azure is now governed by Merosavilla with strict enforcement by the Azure Guard. The continent remains a battleground between followers of the four deities and the corrupted forces of Sivakaya.
