The The Children of Sivakaya are a faction of Necromancers, risen undead and perhaps something else entirely who are followers of Sivakaya, the Goddess of War.. not much is currently known about them but this faction plays either the main role as the leading antagonist or is in the guise of it. Their main force can be found in The Bone Pits.
