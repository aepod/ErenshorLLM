# Deities and Divine Beings of Erenshor

Siva is one of the two creator gods who designed the world of Amarion alongside her lover Brax. She personally crafted much of Erenshor's landscape. After being manipulated by Azynthi into a forbidden ritual, her essence was stripped away, transforming her into the corrupted entity Sivakaya. Her original nature is mourned by The Wisp faction.

---

Brax is the other creator god, Siva's lover and partner in creating Amarion. He created the demigoddess Fernalla in Siva's image. In response to the blighted monolith's spread, Brax buried all of Braxonia under desert sands, creating the Braxonian Desert. He then abandoned the world in despair after losing his children in the War of the Gods. The Torchbearers of Brax preserve his memory.

---

Sivakaya is the corrupted form of Siva, described as "an unholy winged agent of death." The Children of Sivakaya embrace her corrupted power, while The Wisp seeks to restore her. Sivakayan forces are found throughout Erenshor, from raiders in low-level zones to Soldiers and Templars in endgame dungeons. The Jaws of Sivakaya dungeon and the shadowy monolith are physical manifestations of her corruption.

---

Vitheo is a demigod created by Siva with powers of Water and Air. He was sworn to protect the land in Siva's name. Vitheo fell in the War of the Gods against corrupted Sivakaya, achieving godhood through sacrifice. His legacy is preserved at Vitheo's Watch, Vitheo's Rest, and through the Blades of Vitheo faction. The Windblade class draws power from his influence.

---

Fernalla is a demigoddess created by Brax in Siva's image, representing abundance, life, and fertility. She fell in battle against Sivakaya, achieving godhood. Her legacy lives on in Fernalla's Revival Plains, the Blooming Sepulcher, and through Fernalla's Children faction. The Druid class channels her nature magic.

---

Soluna is a goddess who arrived in Erenshor at Soluna's Landing, spreading celestial teachings through the Solunarians. She was pursued by the mysterious figure Astra and eventually departed. Her legacy includes the founding of Port Azure in 1552 and the Solunarian Brotherhood. The Paladin class draws power from Solunarian Magic of Day and Night.

---

Azynthi was a mortal magical prodigy who became corrupted after journeying beyond the Twin Soldiers. He manipulated Siva into a forbidden ritual, stripping her essence and creating Sivakaya. He now resides on Azynthi's Garden, an island in eastern Erenshor described as "The home of the Corruptor of Gods."
