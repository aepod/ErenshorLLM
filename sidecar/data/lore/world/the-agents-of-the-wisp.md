## An Introduction to the Agents of the Wisp

Agents of the Wisp are a secretive society that exists in the peripheries of Erenshor. They are not hostile to outsiders but they are also unwelcoming to any not within their ranks.

From time to time they can be seen traveling across the land, however, it is not widely known where they call home. Their motives are not known but their strong presence on Erenshor indicates that they are up to something. The Wisp is widelythought to be responsible for the Stowaway’s Step rituals.

They are the Followers of the original goddess Sivakaya, who wish to revive and restore her to her uncorrupted form.

Most members of the Wisp have blue-tinged skin.
