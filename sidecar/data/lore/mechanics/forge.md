# Forge

Back to Crafting

The furnace depicted here is located in Port Azure behind Parmalee Gemlyn's market stall

Furnaces are used to smelt down ores into specific mold's obtainable in the world. To use a furnace simply get close to it and left-click it, this will bring up the Smithing Window. From there drag in the materials required into their designated spaces and combine, if it's successful you will get a message prompt in the chat 'item successfully forged!' and the item will be in the Components window for you to claim.
