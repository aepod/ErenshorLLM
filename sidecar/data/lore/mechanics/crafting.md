## Smithing

To smith an item you first need a template, once you have a template you need to gather the materials and take them to a Forge (Example: There is 1 located in Port Azure behind Parmalee Gemlyn's market stall). The fuel sources available in the game have an effect on the end quality of the item produced. For example, using  Ancient Coal causes the resulting item to be a Blue Blessed Item.

Important: Blessed and Godly charms do not do anything. It is a waste to use your  Ancient Coal on them! In addition, the  Angler's Ring cannot be turned in if it's blessed, so you should only use  Coal to craft it unless you are planning to equip it.

## Mining

To mine you need to be holding a pickaxe. Each tick you have a chance to obtain a rock or a chance at something of a higher rarity.

The "Mining Power" stat on Picks currently serves no function, but it is planned for higher power to allow multiple hits of ore veins before they break.
