# Auction House

The Auction House is where all players and Simulated Players can sell their unwanted loot. Two auction brokers operate: Thella Steepleton in Port Azure and Goldie Retalio in Ripper's Keep.

---

Players can filter items by slot and class using AND logic (selecting multiple classes shows only items usable by all). A search function finds items by name. The "Include General Items" filter covers potions, spells, and consumables.

---

To purchase, select an item and click "Buy Item." A confirmation popup appears before deducting the price. If inventory is full, a warning message displays. To sell, access "Your Listings" (18 available slots), click an item in inventory, enter the price, and press Enter.

---

SimPlayers mark up prices 4-6x the listed buy price, with some exceptions reaching 8x or higher. They purchase items up to 6x-1 the tooltip price. Notable greed modifiers: Cyndara and Sparkles have a 2.0 modifier (10x markup), Jethro has 1.75 (8.75x markup).

---

SimPlayers only buy gear (armor and weapons) from the Auction House. Scrolls, potions, and consumables will not sell to SimPlayers. The Auction House data persists across play sessions through the save system.
