# Achievements

Achievements in Erenshor are milestones triggered by quest completion, enemy defeats, exploration, and other activities. They serve as collectible goals without listed in-game rewards.

---

Leveling achievements track character progression from level 2 through 35. The "Ascending!" achievement marks the first ascension point earned after reaching endgame.

---

Quest achievements include "Papers, please!" (turn in dock papers via Stowaway Registration), "The Gate Key" (get your own gate key via Kio's Papers), and "Eyes..." (feed Occuphage via Seaslimed Bracelet). Portal ignition quests (Duskenlight, Fernallan, Ripper) each grant achievements.

---

Boss defeat achievements follow the format "Goodbye, [Boss Name]!" Examples: "Croc Blocker" for defeating a Brackish Croc Whelp, "Goodbye, Lazalith!" for Lazalith in Blacksalt Strand, "Goodbye Demented Malaroth!" for the boss in Nesting Grounds.

---

Social achievements include "Besties Forever!" for grouping with a SimPlayer. Item achievements include "Ooo Sparkley!" for finding a blessed item. Exploration achievements include "Erenshor at last!" for arriving on the Mainland (entering Hidden Hills) and "Book Smarts" for reading a book.

---

The "Climbing is a bug" achievement requires reaching specific elevated coordinates across four zones, rewarding players who explore vertical terrain boundaries.
