# Stats and Attributes

All characters start with 12 points in each of the seven main stats regardless of class. Stats do not increase through leveling; they are enhanced through equipment (weapons, armor, charms, auras) and temporary effects (buffs, debuffs, consumables, spells).

---

Strength (Str) factors into melee and bow damage. Primary for Paladins and Windblades; secondary for Druids. Reavers also benefit from Strength for their DPS builds.

---

Endurance (End) increases maximum HP and health regeneration. Primary for Paladins; secondary for Druids. Tank builds across all melee classes benefit from Endurance. The Hardiness proficiency enhances Endurance scaling: 0.2/0.4/0.8 HP per character level at 10/20/40 proficiency points.

---

Dexterity (Dex) influences melee damage, hit chance, crit chance, and block chance. Primary for Windblades; secondary for Paladins and Stormcallers. DPS Paladins and Windblades prioritize Dexterity alongside Strength.

---

Agility (Agi) governs Armor Class (AC) and dodge chance. Primary for Paladins; secondary for Windblades. Tank builds across classes benefit from Agility for damage mitigation.

---

Intelligence (Int) boosts spell damage, spell crit chance, maximum MP, and wand attack damage. Primary for Arcanists and Stormcallers; secondary for Druids and Windblades. Reavers also use Intelligence for their DPS stance abilities.

---

Wisdom (Wis) influences healing spell strength, MP regeneration speed, cast time reduction, and cooldown reduction. Primary for Druids; secondary for Arcanists.

---

Charisma (Cha) determines spell hit chance, spell damage, and resistances. Primary for Arcanists and Druids. Important for any class relying on spell accuracy.

---

Additional stats tracked on equipment: HP, MP, AC, Resonance, Haste, Lifesteal. Resistance types: Res (general), MR (Magic Resistance), PR (Poison Resistance), ER (Elemental Resistance), VR (Void Resistance). Hidden stats include running speed, attack speed, attack roll, and mana regeneration.
