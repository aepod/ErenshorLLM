# Proficiencies

Proficiencies enhance the effectiveness of individual stats at a ratio of 1% per proficiency point. There are seven proficiencies: Physicality, Hardiness, Finesse, Defense, Arcanism, Restoration, and Mind. Maximum cap per proficiency is 40 points.

---

Each class begins with 52 pre-allocated points plus 10 freely assignable points. Players gain one additional point every other level for a total of 27 earnable points through leveling. Respeccing is available at the Spirit of the Willow in Fernalla's Revival Plains, or via the Wisp Garden furniture in the Reliquary Hall.

---

Starting allocations by class -- Arcanist: Physicality 3, Hardiness 10, Finesse 3, Defense 3, Arcanism 14, Restoration 9, Mind 10. Druid: 5, 10, 5, 10, 7, 10, 5. Paladin: 10, 10, 5, 6, 6, 10, 5. Windblade (Duelist): 10, 10, 12, 5, 7, 2, 6. Stormcaller: 6, 6, 12, 6, 10, 6, 6.

---

Charms can provide additional proficiency-equivalent stat points. Crafted charms give +3 to +4 points in specific stats. Fossil charms from Braxonian Fossils (5% chance at 1 million gold cost) provide +6 in a primary stat and +2 in a secondary stat.
