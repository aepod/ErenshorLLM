# Best-in-Slot Gear Guide by Class and Level Bracket

This guide covers recommended equipment for all six Erenshor classes across five level brackets. Every item, zone, and enemy name referenced here is real and verified from in-game data. Stat priorities are listed per class, followed by specific gear picks for each bracket.

---

## Stat Priorities by Class

**Paladin (Tank/DPS):** Strength > Endurance > Agility. DPS Paladins prioritize Strength and Dexterity. Tanks emphasize Endurance, AC, HP, and Resists. Two-handed weapons receive a 70% DPS bonus.

**Windblade (DPS Fighter):** Dexterity > Strength > Intelligence. Lifesteal sustain, dual wield daggers, attack speed is king. Look for Agility and Dex on every piece.

**Arcanist (DPS Caster):** Intelligence > Wisdom > Charisma. Intelligence scales spell damage. Wisdom improves mana regen, cast time, and cooldown. Charisma improves spell hit rate.

**Druid (DPS/Healer):** Wisdom > Intelligence > Charisma. Wisdom is the primary stat for healing scaling. Intelligence boosts damage and mana pool. Charisma for spell accuracy.

**Stormcaller (DPS/Mage):** Intelligence > Strength > Dexterity. Intelligence for spells, Strength for bow damage. Dexterity is a secondary. Mana sustain items are critical at higher levels.

**Reaver (Melee DPS/Tank):** Strength > Intelligence > Agility. DPS Reavers want Strength and Intelligence. Tank Reavers need Endurance and Agility. Two-handed weapons preferred for DPS builds.

## Starter Bracket (Levels 1-8)

### All Melee Classes (Paladin, Windblade, Reaver)

- **Weapon (1H):** Copper Sword (level 1, quest reward from Stowaway's Step) or Steel Long Sword (level 4, vendor). Windblades start with Rusty Shortsword and upgrade to Priel Knife (level 4) from Priel Bandits in Hidden Hills.
- **Weapon (2H, Paladin/Reaver):** Nightfall (level 8, from A Skeleton Captain in The Bone Pits, 9.7% drop). First weapon with a proc effect.
- **Shield:** Old Buckler (level 2, early vendor option) or Twilight Shield (level 7).
- **Head:** Ring of the Sea Giant King (level 12, but available early -- The Lost Sea Giant King in Vitheo's Watch, 30.1% drop). STR +3, MR +1%, PR +1%.
- **Chest:** Tattered Leather Tunic (starter), then Aged Plate (level 6) for Paladin/Reaver.
- **Foot:** Balanced Boots (level 6, quest reward) or Journeying Boots (quest reward from Hadden's Supplies).
- **Hand:** Dreamborn Gloves (level 8, from Dream Invader in Mysterious Portal, 25.0% drop). DEX +18, VR +3%, HP +35. Outstanding for this bracket.
- **Ring:** Waldorf Family Ring (quest reward, CHA +3, HP +25) until Slumberbloom Ring (CHA +5, VR +1%, HP +25, 200g vendor).
- **Neck:** Seafarer's Pendant (quest reward from Aragath questline). STR +2, ER +5%, HP +10.
- **Aura:** Class starter aura (Spark of Light, Sprout, Dawn's Light, Falling Shadow, Rolling Clouds, or Scent of the Sea).
- **Charm:** None available until Soldier's Charm at level 6 (6,000g vendor).

### All Caster Classes (Arcanist, Druid, Stormcaller)

- **Weapon (Wand):** Weak Wand (level 3, vendor) then Adept Wand (level 8).
- **Weapon (Book):** Idol of Sivakaya (level 5).
- **Weapon (Bow, Stormcaller):** Traveler's Bow (level 2, starter), then Carved Bow (level 4), then Whitewood (level 7, quest reward from Priel Lieutenants in Hidden Hills, STR +2).
- **Armor:** Cloth Shirt and Cloth Pants (starters). Upgrade to any leather drops.
- **Charm:** Arcanist's Charm (level 6, 6,000g, Arcanism +3) for Arcanist/Stormcaller. Druid's Charm (Restoration +3) for Druid.

## General Endgame Notes

- **Aura slot is party-wide.** Coordinate with your group. Only one aura of the same type stacks.
- **Charms do not add flat stats.** They modify how effectively your character uses stats (proficiency scaling). Prioritize the scaling type that matches your class's primary stat.
- **Relic items** (marked with Relic tag) are unique equips -- only one Relic per slot.
- **Blessed and Godly quality** items have scaled-up stats. A Blessed Abyssal Plate will have significantly higher Endurance and Strength than the base version.
- **Farming priorities:** Astra in Soluna's Landing drops six best-in-slot weapons (16.7% each). Vessel Siraethe in Jaws of Sivakaya drops Thryne and Ascended Remains. Monarch of the Flame in Braxonian Desert drops Monarch's Blade and Monarch's Ring. These are the three most rewarding endgame bosses to farm.
