# Notable Items of Erenshor: 50 Highlights

An experienced player's opinionated guide to the items that matter. Every weapon, armor piece, and trinket here earned its place through raw power, unique utility, or the sheer pain of farming it.

---

## Legendary Weapons

**Arc, Crescent of the Void** -- The best stat stick in the entire game. INT +55, DEX +40, HP +600 on a bow. If you are a Stormcaller and you do not own this, you are not done with the game. Drops from Astra, Rogue of the Stars in Soluna's Landing at 16.7%.

**Parallax, Key to the Void** -- The melee equivalent of Arc. STR +35, DEX +30, MR +5%, with a proc. This is what DPS Paladins and Reavers dream about. Same boss, same 16.7% rate. You will see Arc six times before Parallax drops for you. That is just how it goes.

**Eon, Blade of Time** -- The definitive one-handed sword. STR +15, END +10, MR +6%, 20% attack proc. Tank Paladins pair this with a good shield and never look back. Astra drops it alongside everything else at 16.7%.

**Lux, Carver of Stars** -- The Windblade's crown jewel. DEX +15, END +10, MR +6%, 8% proc. Fast, deadly, and aesthetically named. Another Astra drop. At this point you understand why Soluna's Landing is always crowded.

**Corruptor** -- Best-in-slot wand for Arcanists and Druids. INT +40, CHA +25, HP +400, 20% proc. Drops from the Azynthian Keeper in Secluded Sanctuary at 33.3%. More farmable than Astra gear and arguably better for casters who need raw INT.

**Singularity, Vessel of Creation** -- The other endgame wand option. INT +25, WIS +10, MR +6%, 33% proc. If Corruptor is the sledgehammer, Singularity is the scalpel. Astra drop. Your choice depends on whether you want INT or proc frequency.

**Reaver's Steel** -- STR +35, END +15, MR +2%, 33% proc. Created by Sivakaya herself. Drops from Reaver of Sivakaya at 6.8%. The low drop rate makes this a prestige piece. Most Reavers use Parallax instead, but Reaver's Steel has character.

**Thryne, Sledge of Creation** -- STR +30, END +20, MR +10%. That MR alone makes it worth farming. The 3% proc is rare but the base stats are where this weapon shines. Vessel Siraethe in Jaws of Sivakaya, 28.8%.

**Monarch's Blade** -- STR +40. Just read that number again. Plus END +10, MR +6%, and a proc. The highest raw Strength weapon in the game. Monarch of the Flame in Braxonian Desert, 33.3%.

**Molten Axe** -- STR +25, END +25, ER +10%. The balanced endgame two-hander. Equal parts offense and defense. Demented Malaroth drops it at 50%. One of the more farmable BiS weapons.

**Blackbone Axe** -- STR +10, END +10, VR +1%, 33% proc rate. The proc frequency is what makes this special. Sableheart in Jaws of Sivakaya at 33.3%.

**Tome of Necromancy** -- INT +30, WIS +20, VR +12%, and it summons a skeleton. This is not a weapon, it is a lifestyle. Darkwarden Ilyth in Vitheo's Rest, 50% drop rate. Easy to farm, impossible to replace.

## Mid-Game Weapons That Punch Above Their Weight

**Diamond Claymore** -- STR +25, END +15, HP +55, 25% proc. Level 27. This weapon carries you from Rockshade Hold to Jaws of Sivakaya. Stoneman Colossus in Rockshade Hold, 33.3%.

**Soulshiver** -- STR +25, END +20, ER +6%, 20% proc. Made of ice older than time and it performs like it. Braxonian Weapon Master in Fallen Braxonia, 33.3%.

**Fungal Greatsword** -- STR +20, END +15, PR +6%, 25% proc. The Blooming Sepulcher's gift to Paladins and Reavers. Vinx drops it at 25%.

**Volcanic Sceptre** -- INT +12, WIS +10, ER +15%. Fifteen percent elemental resistance on a weapon. Volcanic Giant in Rockshade Hold, 50%. Every caster should own one for fire content.

**Bombastic Wand** -- INT +12, WIS +12, HP +85, 25% proc. The name is stupid and the weapon is excellent. Vinx in Blooming Sepulcher, 25%.

**Garg Wand** -- INT +12, WIS +12, HP +50, 55% proc. The 55% proc rate is absurd. Molorai Curator in Azynthi's Garden drops it at 100%. You will get it on your first kill.

**Siva Bow** -- DEX +30, INT +25, MR +3%, proc. The pre-Arc bow for Stormcallers. Bowmistress of Sivakaya at 7.1%. The low drop rate is annoying but the stats justify the grind.

**Oldenbow** -- DEX +30, STR +25, HP +225, proc. An alternative to Siva Bow with more survivability. Balthom in Jaws of Sivakaya, 33.3%.

**Nightfall** -- Level 8, 13 damage, proc. Your first two-hander with a proc effect. A Skeleton Captain in The Bone Pits at 9.7%. Welcome to Erenshor.

**Sea Giant's Cutlass** -- Level 12, STR +5, ER +5%, 20 damage. The Lost Sea Giant King in Vitheo's Watch, 30.1%. The weapon that teaches new Paladins what a real upgrade feels like.

## Shields Worth Carrying

**Nature's Will** -- INT +25, WIS +25, MR +2%, 50% bash proc. Best shield for caster-tanks and Druid builds. Fallen Fernalla in Blooming Sepulcher, 35.6%.

**Cursed Hide Shield** -- WIS +20, STR +10, MR +2%, 99% bash proc. Nearly guaranteed to proc on every bash. Demented Malaroth, 50%.

**Bastion** -- INT +20, STR +15, MR +3%, 99% bash proc. The tank's shield. Constructed Faithful in Jaws of Sivakaya, 4.9%. The low drop rate from trash mobs means you farm it passively while doing other Jaws content.

**Hopewarden** -- STR +20, INT +20, MR +2%, 66% bash proc with healing property. Sivakayan Doomshade, 9.0%.

**Erandian Avenger** -- STR +15, WIS +15, MR +2%, 75% bash proc. Raval, The First Paladin in Soluna's Landing, 20%. A shield with a story.

## Armor That Defines Builds

**Abyssal Plate** -- END +30, STR +25, MR +3%. The endgame chest for melee classes. Physical blows are absorbed into nothingness. Sivakayan Voidmaster at 4.5%. Painful drop rate, but there is no substitute.

**Eldoth's Finery** -- INT +30, WIS +30, MR +3%. The caster chest. Eldoth Molorai in Azynthi's Garden, 25%. You need to clear the full zone to reach him, making each attempt an investment.

**Ascendant's Embrace** -- END +25, STR +20, MR +4%. The accessible alternative to Abyssal Plate. Balthom in Jaws of Sivakaya, 33.3%. Better MR, slightly less raw stats.

**Statuesque Greaves** -- STR +15, END +15, PR +4%. BiS legs for Paladin and Reaver. Blight Wyrm in The Blight, 33.3%. They lead you into battle on their own accord.

**Creationist's Legwrap** -- DEX +15, INT +15, MR +1%. Endgame caster and Windblade legs. Aeiros in Jaws of Sivakaya, 50%.

**Armguards of Presence** -- STR +15, DEX +15, MR +2%. BiS arm slot. Azynthian Keeper in Secluded Sanctuary, 41.1%. "It whispers in your ear: Fight."

**Terra Crown** -- CHA +35, MR +5%, PR +5%. A headpiece that defies classification. The raw Charisma is unmatched and the dual resists make it universally useful. Nipper King in Abyssal Lake, 33.3%.

**Diamond Helm** -- STR +16, END +10, MR +6%. The melee head slot staple for the entire late game. Sivakayan Shadow drops at 9.0%.

**Jhalmathir, Helm of Generals** -- STR +8, MR +3%, PR +3%. Relic. Worn by the famed Generals of Brax. Evadne the Corrupted in Braxonian Desert, 29.2%.

## Rings and Necklaces

**Monarch's Ring** -- INT +25, WIS +25, PR +3%. BiS ring. Those stats on a ring slot are obscene. Monarch of the Flame in Braxonian Desert, 33.3%.

**Ring of the Ascended** -- DEX +25, STR +10, VR +3%. Vitheo's prized ring. Apparition of Vitheo in Vitheo's Rest, 35.1%. The melee counterpart to Monarch's Ring.

**Flawless Diamond Ring** -- CHA +10, MR +18%, HP +5. Eighteen percent magic resistance. On a ring. From Lost Treasure hunting (level 10-20 and 20-30 maps, 7.0%). Every class should own one for resist checks.

**Wild Colossus Ring** -- STR +20, MR +2%, PR +4%. A mid-game ring that some players never replace. Xasxi in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds, 33.3%.

**Solar Forged Ring** -- CHA +15, STR +10, VR +6%. The highest single-slot void resistance in the ring category. Raval in Soluna's Landing, 40.0%.

**Living Ruby Ring** -- STR +15, ER +6%, HP +45. You get used to the squirmy feeling. Ruby Fiend in Rockshade Hold, 9.0%.

**Tempestalia, Ring of Storms** -- WIS +5, MR +3%, ER +3%. Quest reward from Repairing Orion's Ring Pt. 2. An early ring that stays relevant deep into mid-game.

**Ripparian Pendant** -- INT +15, WIS +15, MR +4%. BiS neck. Vendor purchase for 50,000g from the Ripparian faction. No farming required, just gold and reputation.

**Frozen Mana Charm** -- END +10, AGI +10, MR +4%. Endgame necklace. Nazura the Traitor in Vitheo's Rest, 66.7%. Easy farm, strong stats.

**Forest Pendant** -- INT +8, PR +3%, HP +150. Repeatable quest reward. The HP alone keeps this relevant for a long time.

## Capes and Back Slot

**Sivakayan Wings** -- STR +25, DEX +25, MR +5%. BiS back slot for melee. Drops from Reaver of Sivakaya, Assassin of Sivakaya, and Grand Arcanist of Sivakaya in Jaws of Sivakaya at roughly 10% each. Your movements appear fluid and effortless.

**Azynthian Cape** -- INT +20, WIS +20, VR +4%. BiS back slot for casters. Oracle Miress in Azynthi's Garden Dimensional Rift, 33.3%.

**Abyssal Shell** -- PR +2%, VR +2%, HP +300. Relic. The shell fits comfortably along your back and the HP is massive for the bracket. Undertoes in Abyssal Lake, 66.7%.

**Arboreal Cape** -- DEX +10, AGI +10, HP +200. Woven from leaves and twigs. Sylvan Giant in Fernalla's Revival Plains, 50.0%. The reliable mid-game back slot.

**Dreamthread Wings** -- WIS +12, VR +2%, HP +45. Ghost of Plaxitheris in Shivering Step, 50.0%. Your first meaningful back piece for most players.

## Auras That Change Everything

**Sage's Trinket** -- Mana Regen +100 for the entire party. Volcanic Giant in Rockshade Hold, 25.0%. If you play a caster, this aura is not optional. It is the foundation your mana economy is built on.

**Ancient Presence** -- HP +500, all stats +10, Damage Shield 40. From Lost Treasure (30+), 12.8%. The most well-rounded aura in the game. Good in every situation, best-in-slot for DPS who do not need class auras.

**Gift of Azynthi** -- WIS +20, CHA +20, Lifesteal 8%. Oracle Tretha in Azynthi's Garden Dimensional Rift, 33.3%. The Druid and Stormcaller sustain aura.

**Wisp's Presence** -- Attack Speed +10%, Lifesteal +5%, Attack Roll Modifier +1. Vendor, 13,500g. The Windblade's best friend, available without a single boss kill.

**Aged Prism** -- All four resists +8 for the party. Plunkey in Rottenfoot, 33.3%. Available at level 16 and never stops being relevant. Every group should have one.

**Chunk of Living Stone** -- HP +100, AC +100, END +8, ER +10. Cinder in Rockshade Hold, 20.0%. The tank aura for elemental content.

---

## Charms: The Quiet Power

**Sorcerer's Charm** -- Arcanism +6. Braxonian Fossil (5.3% chance, 1 million gold per fossil). The endgame caster charm. Expensive to pursue but the proficiency scaling is unmatched.

**Protector's Charm** -- Hardiness +6. Same Braxonian Fossil source. The tank charm.

**Precisionist's Charm** -- Finesse +6. Braxonian Fossil. The Windblade and Stormcaller charm.

**Healer's Charm** -- Restoration +6, Arcanism +2. Braxonian Fossil. The Druid's dream charm, boosting both healing proficiency and spell power.

## Unique and Utility Items

**Reaper of Regrowth** -- WIS +15, INT +10, HP +100. 1% chance on attack to respawn all mobs in your zone. A farming tool disguised as a weapon. Fallen Fernalla in Blooming Sepulcher, 27.5%.

**Apotheosis** -- DEX +10, AGI +10, HP +225. 2% proc. Used by Vitheo himself for showmanship during duels. Apparition of Vitheo in Vitheo's Rest, 17.8%. A Windblade off-hand with swagger.

**Horn of Aeiros** -- INT +15, DEX +10, HP +275. Torn from the head of a corrupted beast. Aeiros in Jaws of Sivakaya, 50.0%. High HP and easy to farm.

**Dreamborn Gloves** -- DEX +18, VR +3%, HP +35. Available at level 8 from Dream Invader in the Mysterious Portal. These gloves are comically powerful for how early you can get them.

**Age-Old Armlet** -- DEX +25, STR +15, ER +3%. From Ripper DeMerth at level 15. Another absurdly strong item for its bracket. Worn by Ripper herself.

**Exalted Belt** -- WIS +25, CHA +25, HP +150. Relic waist for Druids and Stormcallers. Exalted Firebone in Azynthi's Garden Dimensional Rift, 66.7%. Who knew a belt could be so prestigious.

**Drakescale Cuirass** -- STR +15, END +10, MR +1%. From Lost Treasure (20-30). Listed as level 1 requirement but the stats say otherwise. Almost as neat as a dragonscale one.
