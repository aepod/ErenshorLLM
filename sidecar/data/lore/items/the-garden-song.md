# The Garden Song

# The Garden Song

## The Garden Song

### Type

Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

The Mystery of Bellwain Island

**The Garden Song** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

This note can be found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

The Garden Song contains the lyrics for a song that seemingly makes plants grow.

The Garden Song

"The Garden Song"
By Bellsie Bellwain

I saw a vision in the petals' glow,
A passion stirred in blossoms below.
A hint of luck flows through the vine,
And now we flourish, intertwined!
The passion paid back with love,
A vision bloomed, the stars aligned.
Our luck was true, a fate designed.

(Written below)
It really does make the plants grow!

Each of the bells near the garden has a word printed on it. Using the lyrics in the song, click the bells to ring them in the correct order:

* Vision
* Passion
* Luck
* Flourish
* Passion
* Vision
* Luck

The order of the bells (with their names visible) from left to right, is: 2 → 3 → 4 → 1 → 3 → 2 → 4.
