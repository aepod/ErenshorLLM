Stonelined Plate is a solid upgrade chest piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Loomingwood Minotaur Hero (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (25.0% drop rate). Endurance +5, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%. "It's really really heavy." Costs 4550g from vendors.
