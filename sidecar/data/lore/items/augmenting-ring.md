Augmenting Ring is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Fearantula (Boss) in Shivering Step (33.3% drop rate). Magic resistance +8%, Poison resistance +8%, Elemental resistance +8%. "Effect applied once item is equipped." Costs 6000g from vendors.
