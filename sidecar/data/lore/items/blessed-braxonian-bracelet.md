Blessed Braxonian Bracelet is a endgame bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Kingsman (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Also drops from Hand of the King. Strong Intelligence (+12), Elemental resistance +3%, +25 Health. "Legendary Bracelet of Brax's Arcanists, once held by Brax himself." Costs 10000g from vendors.
