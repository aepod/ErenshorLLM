Boots of Grounding is a mid-level foot \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Overseer Orlok (Boss) in Old Krakengard (3.2% drop rate). Intelligence +4, Void resistance +3%, +15 Health. "Wearing these makes you feel connected to the land and its magic." Costs 900g from vendors.
