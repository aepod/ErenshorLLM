Fury Helm is a endgame head \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Wardwarped Alpha (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Endurance +20, Poison resistance +4%. "This helmet has been severly dented by repeated attempts to hit its wearer" Costs 14500g from vendors.
