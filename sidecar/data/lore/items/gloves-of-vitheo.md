Gloves of Vitheo is a endgame hand piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Apparition of Vitheo (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (51.9% drop rate). Massive Dexterity (+20), Strength +15, Magic resistance +3%. "Worn by Vitheo prior to his ascension." Costs 19500g from vendors.
