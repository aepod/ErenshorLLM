Scarab Necklace is a mid-level neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Nipper Lashfist in Rottenfoot (4.5% drop rate). Also drops from Nipper Manatooth. Strength +4, +10 Health. "Sometimes the legs twitch and it's really an awful feeling." Costs 1000g from vendors.
