## Shivering Belt Lamp

Shivering Belt Lamp

It trembles as you hold it and its aroma smells like fear and nightmares. It clips handily onto your belt though, and just having it in your inventory will do well enough.

Passive: Place it in a cosmetic general inventory slot to enable its environmental light.
