Royal Waistband is a endgame waist \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Prince Ardant (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (66.7% drop rate). Strong Agility (+10), Intelligence +10, Magic resistance +10%. "The Belt of a Prince" Costs 2105g from vendors.
