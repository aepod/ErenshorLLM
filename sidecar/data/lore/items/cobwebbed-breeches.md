Cobwebbed Breeches is a starter leg piece. Dropped by Lost Treasure (20-30) in Treasure Hunting (25.6% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+15), Endurance +10, Poison resistance +2%. "They're stiff and itchy" Costs 6000g from vendors.
