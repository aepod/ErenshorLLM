Age-Old Armlet is a mid-level arm piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ripper DeMerth (33.3% drop rate). Massive Dexterity (+25), Strength +15, Elemental resistance +3%. "Worn by Ripper herself." Costs 15545g from vendors.
