Minoaxe is a solid upgrade gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Loomingwood Minotaur Hero (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (50.0% drop rate). Strong Endurance (+10), Poison resistance +4%, +10 Health. "Used by the fabled minotaurs of loomingwood." Costs 3200g from vendors.
