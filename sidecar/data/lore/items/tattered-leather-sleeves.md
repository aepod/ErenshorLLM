Tattered Leather Sleeves is a starter arm piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Plaxitheris (Boss) in Stowaway's Step (19.4% drop rate). Also drops from Grassland Ogre Gatherer and Islander Bandit. "They fit poorly." Costs 2g from vendors.
