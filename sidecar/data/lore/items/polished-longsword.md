Polished Longsword is a mid-level weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Bassalt Vallano (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (6.2% drop rate). Also drops from A Skeleton Soldier and A Skeleton Captain. 10 damage. "A well cared for standard issue long sword." Costs 25g from vendors.
