Concentration Band is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Soul Linked Spider in Shivering Tomb (2) (4.5% drop rate). Also drops from Soul Linked Spider and Gasper. Intelligence +5. "Concentration comes as easily as breath" Costs 4200g from vendors.
