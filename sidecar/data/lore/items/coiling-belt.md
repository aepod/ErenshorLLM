Coiling Belt is a endgame waist \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Coiling Dusker in Mysterious Portal (3) (7.6% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +3%. "It's never where you last left it" Costs 9500g from vendors.
