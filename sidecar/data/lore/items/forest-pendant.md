Forest Pendant is a starter neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Available from vendors for 1805g. Intelligence +8, Poison resistance +3%, +150 Health. "Worn by a society of Rangers from a land far away."
