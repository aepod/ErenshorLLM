Resonating Brace is a endgame bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Assassin of Sivakaya (6.5% drop rate). Also drops from Grand Arcanist of Sivakaya and High Paladin of Sivakaya. Strength +2, Void resistance +5%, +25 Health. Special: 10% chance on CAST:. "It rings out a wonderful tone" Costs 22800g from vendors.
