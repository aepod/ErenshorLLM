Adept Wand is a starter weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Zashlyn Bloodbane (Boss) in The Bonepits (33.3% drop rate). Also drops from Neophyte Necromancer. Charisma +5, 6 damage. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Resist Type: Elemental" Costs 755g from vendors.
