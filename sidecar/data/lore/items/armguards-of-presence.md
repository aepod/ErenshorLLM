Armguards of Presence is a best-in-slot arm piece for Paladins, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Azynthian Keeper (Boss) in Secluded Sanctuary (41.1% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Dexterity +15, Magic resistance +2%. "It possesses you. It lingers in your periphery. It whispers in your ear: Fight." Costs 20000g from vendors.
