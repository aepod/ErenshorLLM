Volcanic Shield is a solid upgrade gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Volcanic Giant (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (25.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. Special: 20% chance on BASH:. "It just never stops burning." Costs 2650g from vendors.
