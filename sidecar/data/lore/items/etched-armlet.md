Etched Armlet is a mid-level arm \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Obba of the Warkind (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (66.7% drop rate). Strength +3, Poison resistance +1%, +25 Health. "A symbol of willingness for Battle among the Grassland Ogres" Costs 850g from vendors.
