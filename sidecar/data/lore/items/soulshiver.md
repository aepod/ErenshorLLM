Soulshiver is a endgame gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Braxonian Weapon Master (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+25), Endurance +20, Elemental resistance +6%. Special: 20% chance on ATTACK:. "Made of Ice older than time" Costs 21000g from vendors.
