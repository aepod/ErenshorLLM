Azynthian Dagger is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Oracle Miress (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (33.3% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Wisdom +10, +150 Health. Special: 4% chance on ATTACK:. "Favored by Azynthian Necromancers" Costs 25445g from vendors.
