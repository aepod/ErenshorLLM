Officer's Bracer is a mid-level bracer piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Armorer Nell (4.0% drop rate). Also drops from Captain Ovid and Celeso the Historian. Strength +9, Void resistance +5%, +115 Health. "A sign of status amongst the Ripparian forces" Costs 18545g from vendors.
