Braxonian Leather Boots is a endgame foot piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (12.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Strong Dexterity (+10), Agility +10, Magic resistance +2%. "Legendary Leather Armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 11000g from vendors.
