Twinkling Pendant is a consumable item. Dropped by Winkles in Ripper's Keep (18.4% drop rate). Also drops from A Faerie Enchantress and A Faerie Foliagian. Strong Intelligence (+15), Wisdom +15, Magic resistance +7%. "From within its gem, a soft lullaby plays" Costs 10000g from vendors.
