Tarnished Greaves is a mid-level leg piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Druo the Reborn (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (27.5% drop rate). Also drops from A Grassland Ogre Plaguebringer. Magic resistance +6%, Poison resistance +6%, Elemental resistance +6%. "They look like they spent years in a swamp." Costs 1000g from vendors.
