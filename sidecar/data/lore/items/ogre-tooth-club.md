Ogre Tooth Club is a starter gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by A Grassland Ogre Elder in Fernalla's Revival Plains (10.1% drop rate). Also drops from A Grassland Ogre Chieftan. Intelligence +6, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. Special: 15% chance on ATTACK:. "Ogre teeth are magical and wonderful things... but they're so gross." Costs 425g from vendors.
