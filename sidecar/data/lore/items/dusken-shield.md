Dusken Shield is a solid upgrade gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Dusken Giant in The Duskenlight Coast (66.7% drop rate). Strong Endurance (+10), Dexterity +10, Magic resistance +3%. "Worn by the salt wind of the sea." Costs 1800g from vendors.
