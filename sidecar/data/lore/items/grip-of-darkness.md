Grip of Darkness is a solid upgrade hand \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Molorai Eldermistress (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (33.3% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+15), Wisdom +15, Magic resistance +2%. "Decreases your aggro generation by 60%" Costs 17000g from vendors.
