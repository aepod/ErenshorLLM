Fungus Covered Tunic is a solid upgrade chest \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Devoratrix in Loomingwood Forest (6.7% drop rate). Also drops from Diva. Strong Dexterity (+10), Agility +10, Magic resistance +3%. "About once a day you can harvest mushrooms off of its back." Costs 2100g from vendors.
