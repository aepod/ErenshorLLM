Linked Chain Shirt is a mid-level chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Captain Sigrid (Boss) in Old Krakengard (10.9% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Veteran and Molorai Militia Warrior. Dexterity +4, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "It hangs heavy off all the wrong parts" Costs 300g from vendors.
