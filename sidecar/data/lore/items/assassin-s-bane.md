Assassin's Bane is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Acquirer (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (33.3% drop rate). Strength +8. "Fits beneath your clothing and deters an unseen blade with great efficiency." Costs 7550g from vendors.
