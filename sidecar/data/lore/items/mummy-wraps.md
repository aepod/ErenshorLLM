Mummy Wraps is a starter hand piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Rotting Mummy in Island Tomb (7.3% drop rate). Strength +2, +5 Health. "It's not even sticky and it barely stays on." Costs 45g from vendors.
