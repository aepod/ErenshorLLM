Dreamborn Gloves is a starter hand \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Dream Invader (25.0% drop rate). Also drops from Invader of Dreams. Strong Dexterity (+18), Void resistance +3%, +35 Health. "Your hands feel both heavy but free at the same time" Costs 6500g from vendors.
