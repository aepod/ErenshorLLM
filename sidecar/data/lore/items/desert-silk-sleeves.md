Desert Silk Sleeves is a solid upgrade arm \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Apha, Priest of Brax (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (67.9% drop rate). Also drops from Upha, Priest of Brax and Molorai Archaeologist. Dexterity +8, Magic resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. Costs 2200g from vendors.
