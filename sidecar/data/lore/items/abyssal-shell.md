Abyssal Shell is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Undertoes (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (66.7% drop rate). Poison resistance +2%, Void resistance +2%, +300 Health. "It fits comfortably along your back." Costs 7550g from vendors.
