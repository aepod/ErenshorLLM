Sivakayan Tricorner is a best-in-slot head \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sableheart, Steed of Sivakaya (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (33.3% drop rate). Massive Dexterity (+30), Strength +10, Magic resistance +4%. "It feeds a constant stream of dark thoughts into your mind" Costs 1950g from vendors.
