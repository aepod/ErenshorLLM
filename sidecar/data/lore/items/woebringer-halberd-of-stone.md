Woebringer - Halberd of Stone is a mid-level gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Gruhglor (Boss) in Fernalla's Revival Plains (19.5% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, Elemental resistance +4%. "The blade is sharpened by Elderstone and never dulls." Costs 1450g from vendors.
