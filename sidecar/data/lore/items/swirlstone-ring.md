Swirlstone Ring is a mid-level ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Archmage in Old Krakengard (70.9% drop rate). Intelligence +2, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%. "The gem swirls and changes, as though projecting its will through reflections" Costs 700g from vendors.
