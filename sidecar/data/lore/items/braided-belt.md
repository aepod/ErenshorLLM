Braided Belt is a starter waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Available from vendors for 5g. Strength +1, Magic resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. "It's braided leather with a metal clasp. It might double as a weapon on a really bad day."
