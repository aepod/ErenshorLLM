Flowing Cloak is a mid-level back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Restless Thoughts (Boss) in Mysterious Portal (1) (33.3% drop rate). Strength +8, +75 Health. "Worn Effect: Vitheo's Blessing of the Wind" Costs 3500g from vendors.
