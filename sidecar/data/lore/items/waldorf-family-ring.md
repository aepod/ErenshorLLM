Waldorf Family Ring is a starter ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Available from vendors for 65g. Charisma +3, +25 Health. "The members of the Waldorf Family are clinging to importance in the most desperate way."
