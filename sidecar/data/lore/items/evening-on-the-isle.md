# Evening on the Isle

## Evening on the Isle

### Type

Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

The Mystery of Bellwain Island

**Evening on the Isle** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

Evening on the Isle is a note found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

Evening on the Isle is a poem with clues on what color to light the torches surrounding the statue.

Evening on the Isle

In decidedly feminine handwriting:

The Day Ends
5pm brings the orange glow of the cooking fire
6pm, the sky burns gently with that same warmth and desire

Evenings spent laughing and love - forever and always together.

This note instructs players to light the torch at 5 o'clock orange. Because it also hints at 6 o'clock being lit with the "same warmth and desire," it should also be lit orange as well.
