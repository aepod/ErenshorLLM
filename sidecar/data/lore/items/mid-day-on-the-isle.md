# Mid Day on the Isle

## Mid Day on the Isle

### Type

Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

**Mid Day on the Isle** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

Mid Day on the Isle is a note found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

Mid Day on the Isle is a poem with clues on what color to light the torches surrounding the statue.

Mid Day on the Isle

In decidedly feminine handwriting:

The Day Comes:
At 10am, the sky stretches into forever
Soon, red bugs arrive and flitter together
At noon, a plate of blue berries refreshes the soul
Then comes a rest on the soft, emerald grass below

This is one of the trickiest notes in the quest. As the sky is generally associated with the word blue, the torch at 10 o'clock should be lit as such. Although it does not offer us an exact time, red is between 10 and 12, so the 11 o'clock torch should be turned red. 12 o'clock should again be blue, corresponding to the berries, while the 1 o'clock torch should be green, as hinted at by the "emerald grass."
