Molorai Sigil is a endgame neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Mason (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (2.2% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Acquirer and Molorai Ancille. Magic resistance +12%, Poison resistance +12%, Elemental resistance +12%. "A sign of loyalty to Eldoth" Costs 12450g from vendors.
