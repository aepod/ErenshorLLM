Chitin Bracer is a solid upgrade bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Shelled Monstrosity (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (66.7% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+10), +65 Health. "Made from the shell of a huge bug" Costs 2651g from vendors.
