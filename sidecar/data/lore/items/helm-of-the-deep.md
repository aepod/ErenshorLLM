Helm of the Deep is a mid-level head \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spike (Boss) in The Duskenlight Coast (66.7% drop rate). Endurance +6, Magic resistance +5%, Poison resistance +4%. "It gives visions of calm seas and soft breezes" Costs 1855g from vendors.
