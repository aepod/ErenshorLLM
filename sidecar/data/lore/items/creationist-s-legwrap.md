Creationist's Legwrap is a endgame leg piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aeiros, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (50.0% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+15), Intelligence +15, Magic resistance +1%. "Created during hopeful times, but worn through the worst." Costs 18650g from vendors.
