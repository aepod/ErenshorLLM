Gambler's Cape is a mid-level back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Highwayman Marauder in Fernalla's Revival Plains (3.0% drop rate). Also drops from A Highwayman Raider. Strong Charisma (+10), Elemental resistance +2%, Void resistance +2%. "It's a breakway, so if you get caught by it you aren't slowed down a bit." Costs 1300g from vendors.
