Whitewood is a starter gear piece. Dropped by Priel Lieutenant Bovo (Boss) in Hidden Hills (4.9% drop rate). Also drops from Priel Lieutenant Laflin and Priel Lieutenant Lance. Strength +2, 21 damage. "Made of a wood that's light and... light." Costs 325g from vendors.
