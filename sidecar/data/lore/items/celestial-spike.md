Celestial Spike is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Stardust in Soluna's Landing (4.9% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Wisdom +10, Void resistance +3%. "From the heavens" Costs 2455g from vendors.
