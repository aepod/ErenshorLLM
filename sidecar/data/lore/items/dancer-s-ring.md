Dancer's Ring is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sivakayan High Shadow (4.5% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Agility +15, +100 Health. "You could nimbly-bimbly jump from tree to tree, like a cat." Costs 20000g from vendors.
