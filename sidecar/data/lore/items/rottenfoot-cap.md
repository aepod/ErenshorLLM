Rottenfoot Cap is a mid-level head piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Rottenfoot Mauler in Rottenfoot (4.6% drop rate). Dexterity +4, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +4%. "For keeping the sun off." Costs 1500g from vendors.
