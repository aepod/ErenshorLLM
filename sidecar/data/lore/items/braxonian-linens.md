Braxonian Linens is a endgame leg piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (12.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Strong Intelligence (+15), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +2%. "Legendary Garb of Brax's Arcanists" Costs 12500g from vendors.
