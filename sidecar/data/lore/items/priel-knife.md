Priel Knife is a starter weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Priel Bandit Recruit in Hidden Hills (13.5% drop rate). Also drops from Islander Bandit. Dexterity +2, 6 damage. "A small knife used by low ranking Priel Bandits." Costs 3g from vendors.
