Muddy Jerkin is a mid-level chest \- relic item piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Druo the Reborn (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (27.5% drop rate). Also drops from A Grassland Ogre Plaguebringer. Strength +4, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +3%. "Mud crumbles off of it constantly and never seems to run out." Costs 1355g from vendors.
