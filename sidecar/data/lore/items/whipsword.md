Whipsword is a mid-level weapon piece. Dropped by Overseer Orlok (Boss) in Old Krakengard (25.0% drop rate). Dexterity +5, +15 Health, 12 damage. Special: 1% chance on ATTACK:. "It bends and flexes as you swing, always snapping back into perfect position upon impact." Costs 1950g from vendors.
