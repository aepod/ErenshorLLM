Blackflame Torch is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Acceptor (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (3.9% drop rate). Intelligence +6, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%. "The flame is barely warm, and the smoke's aroma is intoxicating." Costs 2000g from vendors.
