Molorai Axe is a mid-level weapon piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Bodyguard in Old Krakengard (17.8% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Veteran and Molorai Militia Warrior. Strength +5, 13 damage. "It's light and sharp." Costs 400g from vendors.
