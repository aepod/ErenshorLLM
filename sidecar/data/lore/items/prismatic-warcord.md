Prismatic Warcord is a solid upgrade waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ember Acolyte (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Endurance +8, Elemental resistance +4%, +125 Health. Costs 2700g from vendors.
