Brax's Candle is a solid upgrade weapon piece. Dropped by Braxonian Ascended in Fallen Braxonia (4.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Tempest. Strong Intelligence (+10), Charisma +10, +100 Health. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "It flickers but never goes out" Costs 12855g from vendors.
