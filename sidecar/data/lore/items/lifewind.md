Lifewind is a solid upgrade back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher (50.0% drop rate). Strong Endurance (+15), Intelligence +10, +300 Health. "Even on still days, it flows freely on some unseen breeze" Costs 4850g from vendors.
