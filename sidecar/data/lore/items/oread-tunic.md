Oread Tunic is a starter chest piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Autmis the Lost (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (53.7% drop rate). Strength +2. "It's fraying at every edge but remnants of a great power linger in its threads" Costs 15g from vendors.
