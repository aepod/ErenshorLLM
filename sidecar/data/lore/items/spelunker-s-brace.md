Spelunker's Brace is a mid-level bracer \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Fungus Ravaged Brute in The Elderstone Mines (13.4% drop rate). Dexterity +9. "It supports your wrist, and your fingers feel more nimble than ever." Costs 1435g from vendors.
