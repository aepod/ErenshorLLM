Blessed Helm is a endgame head piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Raval, The First Paladin (Boss) in Soluna's Landing (20.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +2%. "A lightweight helm worn by the first Paladins" Costs 7100g from vendors.
