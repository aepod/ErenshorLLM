End of Life is a mid-level gear piece. Dropped by Goliath (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (3.4% drop rate). Also drops from Gruhglor. Special: Party gets a 3% chance on-hit to proc Void Surge. Costs 2500g from vendors.
