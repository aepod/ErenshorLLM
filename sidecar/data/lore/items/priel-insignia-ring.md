Priel Insignia Ring is a starter ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Priel Bandit Recruit in Hidden Hills (14.8% drop rate). Strength +2, Magic resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +1%. "A rather low quality ring adorned with costume jewels." Costs 8g from vendors.
