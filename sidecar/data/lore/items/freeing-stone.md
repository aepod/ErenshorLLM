Freeing Stone is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Soul Linked Spider in Shivering Tomb (2) (4.5% drop rate). Also drops from Soul Linked Spider and Gasper. Strength +3, +25 Health. "Your movements become fluid and effortless" Costs 6000g from vendors.
