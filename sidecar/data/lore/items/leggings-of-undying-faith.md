Leggings of Undying Faith is a mid-level leg \- relic item piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by An Apparition of Sivakaya (Boss) in The Bonepits (25.0% drop rate). Agility +2, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +1%. "The pockets hold traces of ancient herbs" Costs 125g from vendors.
