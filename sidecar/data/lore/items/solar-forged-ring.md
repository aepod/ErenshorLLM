Solar Forged Ring is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Raval, The First Paladin (Boss) in Soluna's Landing (40.0% drop rate). Strong Charisma (+15), Strength +10, Void resistance +6%. "Rumored to have been forged in the Sun, by Soluna herself." Costs 12585g from vendors.
