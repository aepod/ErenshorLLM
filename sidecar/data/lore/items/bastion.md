Bastion is a endgame gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Constructed Faithful in Jaws of Sivakaya (4.9% drop rate). Also drops from Astorim and Karsith. Massive Intelligence (+20), Strength +15, Magic resistance +3%. Special: 99% chance on BASH:. "For defenders of the faith" Costs 15855g from vendors.
