Wyrm Skin Protector is a endgame chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Swamp Wyrm (Boss) in Rottenfoot (25.0% drop rate). Massive Dexterity (+25), Agility +25, Magic resistance +8%. "Conveniently, it fell right off the Wyrm in your size" Costs 18500g from vendors.
