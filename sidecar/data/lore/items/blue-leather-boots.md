Blue Leather Boots is a mid-level foot piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Bassalt Vallano (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (50.0% drop rate). Agility +6, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "Suede shoes would be more comfortable, but the color is nice." Costs 650g from vendors.
