Diamondine Mace is a mid-level gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Stoneman Diamondine (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (5.2% drop rate). Strength +5, +45 Health, 15 damage. Special: 90% chance on ATTACK:. "Magic courses through the hammer and into your hands as you hold it" Costs 2855g from vendors.
