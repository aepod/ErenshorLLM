Ascended Remains is a best-in-slot weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Vessel Siraethe (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (28.8% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+15), Wisdom +15, Void resistance +8%. "Remains of a holy figure" Costs 23999g from vendors.
