Frostmaker is a endgame gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Candlekeeper (Boss) (50.0% drop rate). Strength +8, +225 Health, 35 damage. Special: 65% chance on ATTACK:. "Air crackles and forms ice as the blade passes by." Costs 35000g from vendors.
