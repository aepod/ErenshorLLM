Sivakayan Ceremonial Garb is a solid upgrade chest piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sivakayan Voidweaver in Silkengrass Meadowlands (13.5% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+16), Magic resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Worn by Sivakayan Necromancers" Costs 4000g from vendors.
