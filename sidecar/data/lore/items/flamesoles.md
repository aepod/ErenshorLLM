Flamesoles is a solid upgrade foot \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Ember Acolyte (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Endurance +8, Poison resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +5%. "They leave little burnt footprints wherever you go." Costs 2600g from vendors.
