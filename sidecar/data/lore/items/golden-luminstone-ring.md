Golden Luminstone Ring is a solid upgrade ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher (7.6% drop rate). Also drops from A Restless Spirit and A Sorrowful Spirit. Endurance +2, Magic resistance +2%, +15 Health. "A simple luminstone ring with a golden band." Costs 45g from vendors.
