Obsidian Bracer is a mid-level bracer piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Captain Sigrid (Boss) in Old Krakengard (55.4% drop rate). Strength +4. "Forged from volcanic stone and polished smooth." Costs 754g from vendors.
