Linen Bedpants is a starter leg piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Dream Invader (25.0% drop rate). Also drops from Invader of Dreams. Strong Agility (+10), Magic resistance +8%, Elemental resistance +8%. "It's like wearing nothing at all" Costs 3250g from vendors.
