Bladeturner is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Nazura the Traitor (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (33.3% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Endurance +12, Void resistance +5%. "The feeling of safety is overwhelming" Costs 12000g from vendors.
