# Charms

# Charms

Charms are items that give you proficiency points in various stats without needing to spec into them. They offer another way to customize your build in different ways.

## Crafted

These charms are created via crafting and serve as all purpose charms until you reach the end game.

## Fossils

These charms come from fossils at a 5% drop rate at a cost of 1,000,000 for each fossil. They are considered end game charms and will take a lot of money and time to acquire.

Item: Proficiencies: Source

Precisionist's Charm: Dexterity: +6, Agility: +2: Braxonian Fossil (5% chance)
Protector's Charm: Endurance: +6, Agility: +2: Braxonian Fossil (5% chance)
Sorcerer's Charm: Intelligence: +6, Charisma: +2: Braxonian Fossil (5% chance)
Warlord's Charm: Strength: +6, Dexterity: +2: Braxonian Fossil (5% chance)
