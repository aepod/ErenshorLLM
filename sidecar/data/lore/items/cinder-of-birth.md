Cinder of Birth is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Beady (Boss) in Rottenfoot (25.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Wisdom +10, Elemental resistance +2%. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "A preserved cinder from the flames that forged the world." Costs 2700g from vendors.
