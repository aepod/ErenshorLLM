Liturgical Pantaloons is a mid-level leg piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lazalith, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (33.3% drop rate). Strong Agility (+12), Magic resistance +2%, Void resistance +4%. "Worn during Sivakayan rituals" Costs 1715g from vendors.
