Rusty Dagger is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Algaeth Bilgeback (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (26.1% drop rate). Also drops from A Seafiend and A Seafiend Manalord. 4 damage. "A meek little knife." Costs 7g from vendors.
