Archer's Vambrace is a starter arm \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sylvan Giant Runt (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (25.0% drop rate). Dexterity +6, Elemental resistance +1%, +15 Health. "Protective, but also holds one's arm in postion on a heavy drawn bow" Costs 775g from vendors.
