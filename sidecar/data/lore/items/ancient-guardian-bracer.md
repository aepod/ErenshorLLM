Ancient Guardian Bracer is a mid-level bracer piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Worn Ancient Guardian in Faerie's Brake (1.6% drop rate). Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Simple and effective" Costs 1000g from vendors.
