Aged Plate Boots is a mid-level foot piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral Guard in Lost Cellar (1.6% drop rate). Also drops from Spectral High Guard and Spectral King's Guard. Strength +4, Magic resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +1%. "Tarnished by time, but as effective as ever" Costs 550g from vendors.
