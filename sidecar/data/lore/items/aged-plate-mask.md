Aged Plate Mask is a mid-level head \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral King's Guard (Boss) in Lost Cellar (25.0% drop rate). Also drops from Spectral Castle Guard. Endurance +8, Magic resistance +2%, +32 Health. "Offers a surprising amount of visibility" Costs 900g from vendors.
