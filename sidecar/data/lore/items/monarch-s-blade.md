Monarch's Blade is a best-in-slot gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Monarch of the Flame (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+40), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +6%. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "Resist Type: Elemental" Costs 72000g from vendors.
