Braxonian Bow is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Braxonian High Guard in Fallen Braxonia (4.6% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+18), Agility +18, Elemental resistance +3%. Special: 12% chance on ATTACK:. "A remnant of Old Braxonia" Costs 7500g from vendors.
