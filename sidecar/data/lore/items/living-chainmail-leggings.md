Living Chainmail Leggings is a mid-level leg \- relic item piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Spiritist (Boss) in Old Krakengard (41.8% drop rate). Also drops from Botanist. Strength +4, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +1%. "It crawls across your skin as you wear it." Costs 855g from vendors.
