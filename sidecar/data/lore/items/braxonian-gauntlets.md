Braxonian Gauntlets is a endgame hand piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Braxonian Weapon Master (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (1.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Honor Guard and Braxonian High Guard. Massive Strength (+20), Endurance +10, Elemental resistance +3%. "Legendary Plate Armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 9500g from vendors.
