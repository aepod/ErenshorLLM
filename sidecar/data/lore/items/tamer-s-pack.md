Tamer's Pack is a solid upgrade back \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Priel Tamer in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (3.4% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, Poison resistance +1%. "Effect applied once item is equipped." Costs 2100g from vendors.
