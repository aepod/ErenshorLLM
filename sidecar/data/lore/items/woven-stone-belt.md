Woven Stone Belt is a solid upgrade waist piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Cinder (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (40.0% drop rate). Strong Endurance (+10), +150 Health. "Made from stone cut so precisely that it became like thread." Costs 2250g from vendors.
