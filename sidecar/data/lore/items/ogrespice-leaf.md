Ogrespice Leaf is a mid-level gear piece. Dropped by A Grassland Ogre Bruiser in Underspine Hollow (19.6% drop rate). Also drops from A Grassland Ogre Mystic and A Grassland Ogre Sentinel. Costs 4g from vendors.
