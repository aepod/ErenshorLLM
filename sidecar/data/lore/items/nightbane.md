Nightbane is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Hypnic (Boss) in Mysterious Portal (3) (33.3% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+20), Dexterity +12, +200 Health. "Its golden threads flow into a strange map-like design" Costs 7550g from vendors.
