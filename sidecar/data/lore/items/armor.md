# Armor

Armor in Erenshor increases defense, which increases the chances enemies will miss attacks. Armor comes in slot types: head, neck, chest, shoulder, arm, bracer, ring, hand, foot, leg, back, and waist. Each piece provides Armor Class (AC) and various stat bonuses. Armor is class-restricted, with higher-defense options limited to Paladins and Windblades (previously called Duelists).

---

Arm slot items include sleeves and vambraces. Notable pieces: Cloth Sleeves (level 1, starter), Aged Plate Sleeves (level 6, AC 20), Archer's Vambrace (level 8), Age-Old Armlet (level 37, AC 65).

---

Back slot items include capes and wings. Notable pieces: Distinguished Cape (level 19), Abyssal Shell (level 28, 300 HP), Arboreal Cape (level 30), Azynthian Cape (level 35).

---

Bracer slot items include wrist pieces and bracelets. Notable pieces: A Twisted Spider Leg (level 16), Blessed Braxonian Bracelet (level 30), Mirrored Bracer (level 33, quest reward from Braxonian Scripture), Perfect Links (level 30).

---

Chest slot provides the highest AC values. Notable pieces: Cloth Shirt (level 1, starter), Ascendant's Embrace (level 35), Abyssal Plate (level 38, AC 175), Eldoth's Finery (level 38, 650 HP).

---

Foot slot items include boots. Notable pieces: Balanced Boots (level 6, quest reward), Cloth Shoes (level 1), Journeying Boots (quest reward from Hadden's Supplies), Braxonian Leather Boots (level 31), Pious Sandals (level 38).

---

Ring and neck slot items are usable by all classes. They provide stat bonuses focused on HP, Mana, Resistance (Res), Magic Resistance (MR), and Elemental Resistance (ER). Notable rings: Waldorf Family Ring (quest reward), Tempestalia Ring of Storms (quest reward from Repairing Orion's Ring Pt. 2), Slumberbloom Ring (Shivering Step reward). Notable necklaces: Seafarer's Pendant (quest reward), Forest Pendant (repeatable quest reward), Ripparian Pendant (quest reward).

---

Charm and Aura slot items provide passive benefits. Charms give proficiency points in various stats. Crafted charms include Charm of Rage (Strength +4), Charm of Brilliance (Intelligence +4), Charm of the Jester (Dexterity +4), Charm of the Stone (Endurance +4), and class-specific variants. Fossil charms from Braxonian Fossils (5% chance, 1 million gold each) provide +6 in a primary stat.

---

All armor stats tracked on the wiki include: Level requirement, HP, Mana, AC, Strength, Endurance, Dexterity, Agility, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma, Res, MR, PR (Poison Resistance), ER (Elemental Resistance), and VR (Void Resistance).
