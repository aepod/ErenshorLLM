Pupil's Pin is a charm item usable by Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Historian of Braxonia (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (100.0% drop rate). Modifiers: Arcanism: +1 / 40, Restoration: +1 / 40. Costs 6000g from vendors.
