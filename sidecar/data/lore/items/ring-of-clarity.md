Ring of Clarity is a mid-level ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Tinkles (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (84.7% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Archmage and Molorai Archmage. "Effect applied once item is equipped." Costs 655g from vendors.
