Chapters of Descent, Passage 5 is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Aeiros, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (2.8% drop rate). Also drops from Balthom, Soldier of Sivakaya and Astorim.
