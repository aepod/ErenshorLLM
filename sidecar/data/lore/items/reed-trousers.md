Reed Trousers is a mid-level leg piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Plunkey (Boss) in Rottenfoot (66.7% drop rate). Strength +4, Poison resistance +5%, +10 Health. "They make sitting down very awkward" Costs 2500g from vendors.
