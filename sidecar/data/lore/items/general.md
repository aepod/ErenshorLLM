# General Items

General items in Erenshor include quest items, crafting components, consumables, trade goods, spell vessels, bags, and currency. These items cannot be equipped in armor or weapon slots but are essential for progression.

---

Gold is the primary currency. Gold drops from defeated enemies based on loot tables with minimum and maximum values per creature. Gold is used for purchasing from vendors, trading at the Auction House, and buying Braxonian Fossils (1 million gold each).

---

Quest items are required for completing quests. Examples include gate keys (Stowaway's Step Gate Key, Key to Vitheo's Rest, Key to Vitheo's Tomb, Reliquary Key), notes and intel (Notes of a Hermit, Samuel's Note, Siva-Braxonian Teachings), runes (Inert Rune of Brax/Fernalla/Soluna/Vitheo, Rune of Brax, Rune of Winds, Rune of Soluna's Landing, Rune of Ripper's Keep, Rune of The Hills), and various collection items.

---

Crafting materials include ores (Iron Ore, Copper Ore, Coal, Ancient Coal, Rock) obtained from mining. Molds are recipe items for smithing: Mold for Copper Sword, Copper Sceptre, Ghostly Key, Charm of Compassion/Brilliance/Rage/Jester/Vanity/Stone/Age, and Arcanist's/Druid's/Duelist's/Soldier's Charms. Special materials include Nighthollow Wax and Braxonian Fossils.

---

Consumables include Bread, Water, and various Elixirs (Elixir of Enlightenment II from quest rewards, Elixir of Enlightenment IV from The Mathers' Demise). Antidote is a spell that cures poison.

---

Special items include the Azynthian Riftstone, Nightmare Crystal (from Mysterious Portal quest), Bag of Offering Stones, Bottomless Elixir (repeatable quest reward), Box of Portals (Reliquary Hall teleporter), Eldrich Crystal (from Azynthian Keeper for raid unlocks), Planar Stones and Planar Stone Shards (global rare drops), Sivakrux (0.1% global drop level 16+, trades for Vitheo's Rest access), Inert Diamonds (0.8% global drop level 21+), and Torn Treasure Maps (0.3% global drop).

---

Mining requires pickaxes: Basic Pick (2 power), Journeyman's Pick (4 power, quest reward), Mason's Pick (6 power). Mineral deposits are found in Stowaway's Step, Hidden Hills, Vitheo's Watch, Port Azure, and Braxonian Desert. Drop rates: Rock 56.6%, Iron Ore 21%, Coal 18.3%, Copper Ore 2.6%, Ancient Coal 1.3%.
