Sailor's Belt is a mid-level waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by The Lost Sea Giant King (Boss) in Vitheo's Watch (30.1% drop rate). Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +3%, Elemental resistance +3%. "Worn by sailors for its wonderful utility." Costs 700g from vendors.
