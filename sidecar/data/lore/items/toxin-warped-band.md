Toxin Warped Band is a mid-level ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by King Cobra in Silkengrass Meadowlands (15.3% drop rate). Strength +3. "The metals are corroded and bent" Costs 1650g from vendors.
