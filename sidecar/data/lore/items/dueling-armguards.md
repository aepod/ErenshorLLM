Dueling Armguards is a mid-level arm \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Chazza Priel (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (50.0% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+10), Agility +10, Magic resistance +2%. "Worn by Chazza Priel, sister of Savannah Priel" Costs 8500g from vendors.
