Ring of the Sea Giant King is a mid-level head \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by The Lost Sea Giant King (Boss) in Vitheo's Watch (30.1% drop rate). Strength +3, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "While it fit his finger, for you, it's more of a headpiece." Costs 450g from vendors.
