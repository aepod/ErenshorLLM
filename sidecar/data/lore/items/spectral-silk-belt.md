Spectral Silk Belt is a mid-level waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Boneweaver (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (66.7% drop rate). Agility +8, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "Weightless and silky" Costs 2655g from vendors.
