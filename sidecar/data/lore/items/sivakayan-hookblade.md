Sivakayan Hookblade is a mid-level weapon piece. Dropped by Lazalith, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (33.3% drop rate). Dexterity +6, Void resistance +6%, 18 damage. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "A very intimidating weapon to have swung at you." Costs 1750g from vendors.
