Pirate's Hair Tie is a mid-level head piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Drowned Corpse in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (5.4% drop rate). Agility +6, Elemental resistance +2%, Void resistance +2%. "Battered by the sea but still intact" Costs 1950g from vendors.
