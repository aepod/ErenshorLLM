Skill Book: Control Chant

Required Level:

Arcanist: 1

Skill Type: Other

Activate any time during the channeling of a DAMAGE SPELL to control your chant. Activate again to immediately end the spell. Damage is scaled by how far along the channel was. Going over 100% increases damage further, but adds a cooldown penalty.
