Stinging Bracer is a mid-level bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Spark Queen (Boss) in Lost Cellar (50.0% drop rate). Intelligence +5, Magic resistance +3%, Void resistance +3%. Special: 10% chance on CAST:. "Resist Type: Magic" Costs 1485g from vendors.
