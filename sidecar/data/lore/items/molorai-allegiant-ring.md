Molorai Allegiant Ring is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Eldermistress (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (66.7% drop rate). Also drops from Cerynth Duskveil and Myrre Morrigane. Strength +6, +25 Health. "Worn by the most loyal of Eldoth's followers" Costs 13335g from vendors.
