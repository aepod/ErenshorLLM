### Type

### Related quest(s)

**The Stash** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

This note can be found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

The Stash hints at a secret stash hidden below the docks.

The Stash

Our favorite wine was... illicit. It's hard to source, and so I've resorted to keeping a stash in a nook beneath the docks. It's not the most accessible but its safe from prying eyes.

I could not fathom running out, Bellsie would be heartbroken too.

I had never planned to sail again, unless it was for our wine.

Using the hint, jump into the water next to the docks and sink below the surface. Swim into the hole and make your way through the cavern;  Bellsie's Wine can be found in the corner.
