Monarch's Mask is a best-in-slot head \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Monarch of the Flame (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+22), Endurance +22, Elemental resistance +3%. "The mask of a King" Costs 29585g from vendors.
