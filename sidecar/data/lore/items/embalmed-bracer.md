Embalmed Bracer is a starter bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Zashlyn Bloodbane (Boss) in The Bonepits (37.2% drop rate). Intelligence +5, +25 Health. "They burn your skin" Costs 20g from vendors.
