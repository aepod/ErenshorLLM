Hardened Scale Jacket is a solid upgrade chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Shivunax (Boss) in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (33.3% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Dexterity +10, Magic resistance +2%. "Made from the scales of a Malaroth" Costs 2955g from vendors.
