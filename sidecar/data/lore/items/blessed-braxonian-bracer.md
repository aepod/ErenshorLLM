Blessed Braxonian Bracer is a endgame bracer \- relic item piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Kingsman (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Also drops from Hand of the King. Strong Strength (+12), Elemental resistance +3%, +50 Health. "Legendary Plate armor of Brax's Soldiers, once held by Brax himself." Costs 10000g from vendors.
