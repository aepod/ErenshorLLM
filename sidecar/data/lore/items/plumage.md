Plumage is a solid upgrade chest piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aviak (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (50.0% drop rate). Massive Charisma (+20), Wisdom +15, Poison resistance +3%. "You are so hideous with this on" Costs 15000g from vendors.
