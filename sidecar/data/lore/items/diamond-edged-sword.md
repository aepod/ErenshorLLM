Diamond Edged Sword is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sivakayan Shadow (4.5% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Dexterity +10, Magic resistance +5%. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Crafted with extreme precision and meticulously sharpened" Costs 25000g from vendors.
