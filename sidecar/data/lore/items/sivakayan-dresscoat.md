Sivakayan Dresscoat is a endgame chest piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Darkwarden Ealdred (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (66.7% drop rate). Also drops from Sivakayan Emissary and Darkwarden Awxa. Massive Intelligence (+20), Wisdom +20, Poison resistance +5%. "Worn by the Sivakayan Elite" Costs 17890g from vendors.
