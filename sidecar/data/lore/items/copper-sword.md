Copper Sword is a starter weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gort (Boss) in Hidden Hills (10.8% drop rate). Also drops from Summoned Fallen Warrior and The Corpse of Brice Mathers. 8 damage. "A standard copper sword." Costs 56g from vendors.
