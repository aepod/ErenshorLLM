Funeral Shroud is a starter head piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Mourning (Boss) in Island Tomb (10.1% drop rate). Intelligence +3, Poison resistance +3%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Boy does it stink." Costs 100g from vendors.
