Argo's Grimoire is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Tinkles (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (23.9% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Brewmistress and Elizah Vernor. Intelligence +4, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Argo was a boring guy and he owned boring books." Costs 900g from vendors.
