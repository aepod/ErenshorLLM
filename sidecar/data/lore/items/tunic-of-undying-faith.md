Tunic of Undying Faith is a mid-level chest \- relic item piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by An Apparition of Sivakaya (Boss) in The Bonepits (25.0% drop rate). Wisdom +4, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Ancient etchings cover the interior" Costs 400g from vendors.
