Eternox, Remnant of the Void is a consumable item. Dropped by Astra, Rogue of the Stars (Boss) in Soluna's Landing (16.7% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+25), Intelligence +10, Magic resistance +6%. "Held within is the last instance of non-existence." Costs 25455g from vendors.
