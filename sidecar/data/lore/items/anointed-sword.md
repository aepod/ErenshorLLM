Anointed Sword is a mid-level gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Overseer Orlok (Boss) in Old Krakengard (25.0% drop rate). Strength +5, +20 Health, 25 damage. Special: 8% chance on ATTACK:. "Someone rubbed some wonderfully scented oil on this thing." Costs 1655g from vendors.
