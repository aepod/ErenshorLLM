Bronzed Solunarian Plate is a mid-level chest piece. Dropped by Tinkles (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (23.9% drop rate). Strength +6, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "Often given to and worn by Paladin recruits" Costs 1000g from vendors.
