Molten Axe is a best-in-slot gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Demented Malaroth (Boss) (50.0% drop rate). Massive Strength (+25), Endurance +25, Elemental resistance +10%. Special: 8% chance on ATTACK:. "Once forged in Magma, it seems to only just be cooling" Costs 32000g from vendors.
