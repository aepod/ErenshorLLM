Monarch's Ring is a best-in-slot ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Monarch of the Flame (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+25), Wisdom +25, Poison resistance +3%. "An ancient king wore this ring" Costs 14500g from vendors.
