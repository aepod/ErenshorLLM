Pull of Insomnia is a starter bracer \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Wiggleribs (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (6.8% drop rate). Strength +5, +15 Health. "As though a hand pulls you by your wrist back to waking mind" Costs 300g from vendors.
