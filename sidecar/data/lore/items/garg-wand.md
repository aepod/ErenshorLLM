Garg Wand is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Molorai Curator (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (100.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+12), Wisdom +12, +50 Health. Special: 55% chance on ATTACK:. "A relic of the Azynthian occupants of Erenshor" Costs 23999g from vendors.
