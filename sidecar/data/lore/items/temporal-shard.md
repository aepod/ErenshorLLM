Temporal Shard is a best-in-slot neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Eldoth Molorai (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (7.7% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Endurance +15, Magic resistance +10%. "An amulet that has been eroded by the flow of time." Costs 22000g from vendors.
