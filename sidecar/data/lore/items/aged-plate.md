Aged Plate is a mid-level chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral King's Guard (Boss) in Lost Cellar (25.8% drop rate). Also drops from Rotting Royal Hound. Strong Dexterity (+10), +25 Health. "Worn by the original Azure Guard" Costs 1000g from vendors.
