Tattered Leather Gloves is a starter hand piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Plaxitheris (Boss) in Stowaway's Step (19.4% drop rate). Also drops from Grassland Ogre Gatherer and Grassland Ogre Hunter. "The material has been beaten thin from use." Costs 2g from vendors.
