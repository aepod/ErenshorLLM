Treewoven Leggings is a solid upgrade leg piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher (25.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Wisdom +12, Magic resistance +1%. "Legendary Garb of Brax's Arcanists" Costs 13250g from vendors.
