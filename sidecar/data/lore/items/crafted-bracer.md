Crafted Bracer is a mid-level bracer piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gruhglor (Boss) in Fernalla's Revival Plains (19.5% drop rate). Dexterity +6, Magic resistance +5%, Void resistance +2%. "Surprisingly well made, given where you found it." Costs 1485g from vendors.
