Dreamthread Wings is a solid upgrade back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ghost of Plaxitheris (Boss) in Shivering Step (50.0% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+12), Void resistance +2%, +45 Health. "Looking directly at it provokes visions of images you cannot quite grasp" Costs 2000g from vendors.
