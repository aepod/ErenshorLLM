Elder's Slippers is a solid upgrade foot \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Arcane Headmaster (50.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+12), Wisdom +12, Poison resistance +5%. "The soles have been worn smooth by the heat of a welcoming fireplace" Costs 21600g from vendors.
