Scorched Walking Stick is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Peon (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Intelligence +6, Magic resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +3%. "Scorched by the ancient flames of Brax" Costs 1900g from vendors.
