Flamelicked Gorget is a solid upgrade neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ember Acolyte (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Strength +5, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "The patterns burned into the metal look like a small forest." Costs 1805g from vendors.
