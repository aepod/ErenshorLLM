Lamentation is a endgame gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Parzgil (Boss) in The Blight (66.7% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Endurance +12, +100 Health. "The axe weeps a strange red fluid from its gems" Costs 8500g from vendors.
