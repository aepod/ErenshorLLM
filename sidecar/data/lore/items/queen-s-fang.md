Queen's Fang is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Slumbering Queen (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (52.3% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+10). Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "Potent venom leaks from every surface." Costs 2100g from vendors.
