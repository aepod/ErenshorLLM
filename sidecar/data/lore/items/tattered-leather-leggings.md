Tattered Leather Leggings is a starter leg piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Plaxitheris (Boss) in Stowaway's Step (19.4% drop rate). Also drops from Grassland Ogre Gatherer and A Seafiend. "The backside is worn to an embarrassing extent." Costs 5g from vendors.
