Empath's Cord is a solid upgrade waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Failed Experiment in Prielian Cascade (87.1% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+25), +100 Health. "You feel more connected to others while wearing this." Costs 3955g from vendors.
