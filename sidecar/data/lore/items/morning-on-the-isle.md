# Morning on the Isle

# Morning on the Isle

## Morning on the Isle

### Type

Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

The Mystery of Bellwain Island

**Morning on the Isle** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

Morning on the Isle is a note found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

Morning on the Isle is a poem with clues on what color to light the torches surrounding the statue.

Morning on the Isle

In decidedly feminine handwriting:

Morning on the Isle:
At 7, the dew shines white, like fresh snow on the ground
At 8am, the blue sky lifts, waving softly to the sea
At 9, the green grass stirs awake, brushing gently in the breeze

This note is quite literal, instructing players to turn the torch at 7 o'clock white, the torch at 8 o'clock blue, and the torch at 9 o'clock green.
