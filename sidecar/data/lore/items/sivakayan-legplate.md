Sivakayan Legplate is a solid upgrade leg piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gherist Morthario (Boss) in Rottenfoot (27.6% drop rate). Also drops from Bowmistress of Sivakaya and Reaver of Sivakaya. Strong Strength (+10), Agility +10, Magic resistance +10%. "The joints have been meticulously oiled and cared for." Costs 3100g from vendors.
