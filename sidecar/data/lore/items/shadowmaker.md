Shadowmaker is a solid upgrade bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Fearantula (Boss) in Shivering Step (33.3% drop rate). Also drops from Gasper and Shadow of Sleep. Strength +5, Magic resistance +3%, Void resistance +3%. "Your entire presence fades, and your shadow darkens" Costs 2145g from vendors.
