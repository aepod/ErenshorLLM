Charred Boots is a starter foot piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Failed Cremation (Boss) in Island Tomb (48.6% drop rate). Endurance +3, Elemental resistance +1%, Void resistance +1%. "They'll forever leave ashen footprints" Costs 125g from vendors.
