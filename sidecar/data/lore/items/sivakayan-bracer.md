Sivakayan Bracer is a solid upgrade bracer piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gherist Morthario (Boss) in Rottenfoot (27.6% drop rate). Also drops from Bowmistress of Sivakaya and Reaver of Sivakaya. Strength +7. "So heavy they add weight to your swings." Costs 2550g from vendors.
