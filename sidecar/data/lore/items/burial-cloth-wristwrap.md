Burial Cloth Wristwrap is a starter bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Woe (Boss) in Island Tomb (4.7% drop rate). Intelligence +4, Magic resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +1%. "It's stiff and stained, but it feels empowering to wear." Costs 20g from vendors.
