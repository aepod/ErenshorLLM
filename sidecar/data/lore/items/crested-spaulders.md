Crested Spaulders is a mid-level arm piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Armorer Nell (4.0% drop rate). Also drops from Captain Ovid and Celeso the Historian. Strong Strength (+12), Endurance +12, Magic resistance +3%. "Meant to add bulk to your silhouette, and they totally do" Costs 16555g from vendors.
