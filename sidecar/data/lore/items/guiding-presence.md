Guiding Presence is a endgame bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Fallen Fernalla (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (35.6% drop rate). Also drops from Fallen Fernalla. Strong Strength (+11), Intelligence +11, Magic resistance +5%. Special: 4% chance on CAST:. "You feel as though you're being led forward by a protective hand" Costs 22455g from vendors.
