Dreamy Wand is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Dream (Boss) in Shivering Tomb (1) (50.0% drop rate). Intelligence +8, Void resistance +4%, +155 Health. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "It's not real, but it still works" Costs 4000g from vendors.
