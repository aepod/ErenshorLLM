Wingwoven Cape is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Winkles in Ripper's Keep (18.4% drop rate). Also drops from A Faerie Enchantress and A Faerie Foliagian. Massive Agility (+20), Dexterity +12, Magic resistance +3%. "Every now and then you float gracefully on a passing breeze" Costs 7550g from vendors.
