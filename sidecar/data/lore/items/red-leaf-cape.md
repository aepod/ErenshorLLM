Red Leaf Cape is a solid upgrade back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Diva (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (50.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+15), Wisdom +15, Poison resistance +2%. "A magical red leaf, as big as you are" Costs 9500g from vendors.
