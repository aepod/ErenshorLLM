Blackbone Axe is a best-in-slot gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Sableheart, Steed of Sivakaya (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (33.3% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, Void resistance +1%. Special: 33% chance on ATTACK:. "Long-lodged in the bones of an ancient dragon, it began to take on their characteristics." Costs 28500g from vendors.
