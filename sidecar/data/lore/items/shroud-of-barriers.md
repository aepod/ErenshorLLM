Shroud of Barriers is a mid-level head piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Braxon Fanatic in Braxonian Desert (1.9% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+10), Void resistance +2%, +15 Health. "Protects you from the spirits." Costs 1500g from vendors.
