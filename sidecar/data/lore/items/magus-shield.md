Magus Shield is a mid-level gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Sagan Ilth (Boss) in Vitheo's Watch (7.6% drop rate). Intelligence +8, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "Resist Type: Void" Costs 2400g from vendors.
