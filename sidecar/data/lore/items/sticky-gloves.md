Sticky Gloves is a mid-level hand piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Highwayman Marauder in Fernalla's Revival Plains (3.0% drop rate). Also drops from A Highwayman Raider. Endurance +5, Poison resistance +3%, +15 Health. "A favorite of bandits." Costs 1000g from vendors.
