Asp's Fang is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Asp in Braxonian Desert (5.1% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Dexterity +10. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "Filled with a deadly toxin" Costs 20000g from vendors.
