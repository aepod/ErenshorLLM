Virophan Leather Slippers is a solid upgrade foot \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Balaste the Fallen (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (33.3% drop rate). Dexterity +6, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +2%. "Made from an ancient but fine leather" Costs 2950g from vendors.
