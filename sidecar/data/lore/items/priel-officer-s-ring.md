Priel Officer's Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Priel Deceiver in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (4.9% drop rate). Also drops from Priel Enforcer and Priel Malarist. Dexterity +5, Magic resistance +3%, Elemental resistance +2%. "A high quality ring, adorned with pristine stones" Costs 1855g from vendors.
