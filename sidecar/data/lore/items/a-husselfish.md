A Husselfish is a consumable item. Dropped by A Rottenfoot Witchdoctor in Fernalla's Revival Plains (22.9% drop rate). Also drops from A Rottenfoot Warrior and Rottenfoot Swampwalker. Costs 15g from vendors.
