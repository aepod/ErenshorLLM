Rusty Shortsword is a starter weapon piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Brittle Skeleton in Braxonian Desert (5.9% drop rate). Also drops from Risen Pilgrim. 5 damage. "It probably used to be sharp and dangerous, now it's more of a blunt weapon." Costs 10g from vendors.
