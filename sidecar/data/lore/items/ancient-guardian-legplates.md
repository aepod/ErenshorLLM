Ancient Guardian Legplates is a mid-level leg piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Worn Ancient Guardian in Faerie's Brake (1.6% drop rate). Poison resistance +4%, Elemental resistance +4%, 125 Armor. "These metals seem to contain veins of stone" Costs 1855g from vendors.
