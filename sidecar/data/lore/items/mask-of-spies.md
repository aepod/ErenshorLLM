Mask of Spies is a endgame head piece for Arcanists, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sivakayan Emissary in Azynthi's Garden (50.0% drop rate). Also drops from Sivakayan Spymaster. Strong Dexterity (+15), Charisma +15, Magic resistance +10%. "Your actions are still your responsibility" Costs 16505g from vendors.
