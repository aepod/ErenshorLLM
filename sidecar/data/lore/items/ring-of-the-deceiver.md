Ring of the Deceiver is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Assassin of Sivakaya (6.5% drop rate). Also drops from Grand Arcanist of Sivakaya and High Paladin of Sivakaya. Massive Dexterity (+25), Intelligence +25, +85 Health. "Said to have been taken from Eldrin's wardrobe after his betrayal" Costs 12000g from vendors.
