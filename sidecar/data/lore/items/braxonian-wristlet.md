Braxonian Wristlet is a endgame bracer piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (24.0% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Dexterity +8, Elemental resistance +3%, +15 Health. "Legendary Leather armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 8500g from vendors.
