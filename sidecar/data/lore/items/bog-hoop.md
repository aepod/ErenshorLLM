Bog Hoop is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Nipper Lashfist in Rottenfoot (4.5% drop rate). Also drops from Nipper Manatooth. Endurance +5, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +6%. "Made of dried flora" Costs 1000g from vendors.
