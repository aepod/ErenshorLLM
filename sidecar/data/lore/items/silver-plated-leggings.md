Silver Plated Leggings is a solid upgrade leg \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ravager, The First Infected (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (45.8% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Dexterity +10, Magic resistance +4%. "Silvery and lined with a nice fur interior." Costs 1875g from vendors.
