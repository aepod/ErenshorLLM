Golden Plate is a endgame chest piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Exalted Firebone (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+25), Endurance +25, Magic resistance +2%. "Immaculate Golden Armor" Costs 30000g from vendors.
