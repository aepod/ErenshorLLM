Tarnished Steel Belt is a mid-level waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral Guard in Lost Cellar (6.9% drop rate). Also drops from Spectral High Guard. Magic resistance +6%, Poison resistance +6%, Elemental resistance +6%. "It's ugly. No wonder they buried it." Costs 3200g from vendors.
