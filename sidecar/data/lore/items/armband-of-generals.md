Armband of Generals is a endgame arm piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Reaver of Sivakaya (10.8% drop rate). Also drops from Assassin of Sivakaya and Grand Arcanist of Sivakaya. Massive Strength (+20), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +2%. "Reserved for the highest Sivakayan Generals" Costs 25446g from vendors.
