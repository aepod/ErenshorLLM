Mino Hatchet is a solid upgrade gear piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Loomingwood Minotaur Legionnaire in Loomingwood Forest (13.5% drop rate). Also drops from Loomingwood Minotaur Soldier. Poison resistance +5%, 13 damage. "Just a standard hatchet for a minotaur" Costs 2975g from vendors.
