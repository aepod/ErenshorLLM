Priel Steel Armguards is a solid upgrade arm \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Julianna Priel (Boss) in Prielian Cascade (50.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Dexterity +15, Magic resistance +1%. "Worn by Priel swordsmen" Costs 3200g from vendors.
