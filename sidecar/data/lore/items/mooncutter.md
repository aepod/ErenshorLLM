Mooncutter is a solid upgrade gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Nightmare (Boss) in Shivering Step (25.0% drop rate). Strength +8, Magic resistance +2%, +50 Health. Special: 4% chance on ATTACK:. "If the moon ever shows up, it's gonna be in big trouble." Costs 8000g from vendors.
