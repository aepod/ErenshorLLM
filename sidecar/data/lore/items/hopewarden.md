Hopewarden is a endgame gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Sivakayan Doomshade (9.0% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Intelligence +20, Magic resistance +2%. Special: 66% chance on BASH:. "The sparks that fly off it with impacts have a strange healing property to them" Costs 25850g from vendors.
