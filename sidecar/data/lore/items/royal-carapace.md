Royal Carapace is a mid-level gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Spark Royal (Boss) in Lost Cellar (50.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Wisdom +10, Magic resistance +2%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "Apply Effects on Target:" Costs 1200g from vendors.
