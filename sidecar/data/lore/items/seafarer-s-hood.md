Seafarer's Hood is a solid upgrade head \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Dockmaster (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (66.7% drop rate). Massive Endurance (+20), Agility +20, Magic resistance +1%. "Waterproof and wind proof, likely not blade proof." Costs 18000g from vendors.
