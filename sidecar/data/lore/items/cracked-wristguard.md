Cracked Wristguard is a starter bracer piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gort (Boss) in Hidden Hills (50.0% drop rate). Strength +1. "Nothing special, just an old piece of armor." Costs 15g from vendors.
