Seawashed Cloak is a mid-level back piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spike (Boss) in The Duskenlight Coast (33.3% drop rate). Strength +5, Elemental resistance +3%, +30 Health. "Aged by decades of exposure to the ocean air." Costs 855g from vendors.
