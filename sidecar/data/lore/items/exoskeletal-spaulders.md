Exoskeletal Spaulders is a mid-level arm \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spark Royal (Boss) in Lost Cellar (50.0% drop rate). Endurance +5, +20 Health. "Made of some sort of insect shell" Costs 1899g from vendors.
