Warlord's Charm is a charm item usable by Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Braxonian Fossil (5.3% drop rate). Modifiers: Finesse: +2 / 40. Costs 6000g from vendors.
