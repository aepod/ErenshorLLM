Braxonian Greaves is a endgame leg piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (1.1% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice) and Braxonian Weapon Master. Strong Strength (+15), Endurance +15, Magic resistance +2%. "Legendary Plate armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 12500g from vendors.
