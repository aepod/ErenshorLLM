Spectral Sceptre is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Cursed Vithean Traitor in Vitheo's Rest (7.2% drop rate). Intelligence +7, +15 Health, 14 damage. "You cannot feel it in your hand, but somehow its strikes land true." Costs 2120g from vendors.
