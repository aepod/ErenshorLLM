Gardener's Wrist Wrap is a endgame bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Grounds Keeper (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (66.7% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+12), Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +6%. "Protection from brambles and demons" Costs 14852g from vendors.
