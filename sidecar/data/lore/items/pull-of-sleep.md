Pull of Sleep is a starter bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Wiggleribs (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (6.8% drop rate). Intelligence +4, +15 Health. "As though a hand pulls you by your wrist into a place of dreams" Costs 300g from vendors.
