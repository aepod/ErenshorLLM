Malaroth Sinew Cord is a solid upgrade waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Shivunax (Boss) in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (66.7% drop rate). Intelligence +4, Magic resistance +5%, Poison resistance +5%. "Strong and Stiff" Costs 2105g from vendors.
