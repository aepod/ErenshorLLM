Sylvan Cord is a starter waist \- relic item piece for Druids and Stormcallers. Dropped by Sylvan Giant Runt (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (25.0% drop rate). Wisdom +3, Poison resistance +4%, +10 Health. "It ushers a sense of nature into your mind" Costs 300g from vendors.
