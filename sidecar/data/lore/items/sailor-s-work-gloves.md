Sailor's Work Gloves is a mid-level hand piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by The Lost Sea Giant King (Boss) in Vitheo's Watch (30.1% drop rate). Also drops from Overseer Orlok. Strength +2, Elemental resistance +3%. "Even the gloves have blisters" Costs 245g from vendors.
