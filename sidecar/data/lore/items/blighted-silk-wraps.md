Blighted Silk Wraps is a endgame bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Fenton the Blighted (Boss) in The Blight (75.0% drop rate). Dexterity +6, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +1%. "Made from the silk of a diseased spider" Costs 4855g from vendors.
