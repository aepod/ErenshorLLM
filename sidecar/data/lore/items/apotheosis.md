Apotheosis is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Apparition of Vitheo (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (17.8% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+10), Agility +10, +225 Health. Special: 2% chance on ATTACK:. "Legend says it was used by Vitheo himself for showmanship during duels" Costs 30000g from vendors.
