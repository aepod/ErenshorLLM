Fire Emerald Band is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Blazefiend (Boss) (25.0% drop rate). Strength +5, Elemental resistance +6%, +100 Health. "A ring worn by the most devout Braxonians." Costs 955g from vendors.
