Grips of Faith is a endgame hand \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Constructed Faithful in Jaws of Sivakaya (4.9% drop rate). Also drops from Astorim and Karsith. Massive Strength (+25), Dexterity +10, Magic resistance +2%. "Worn by the truest believers" Costs 13650g from vendors.
