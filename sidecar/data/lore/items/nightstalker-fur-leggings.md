Nightstalker Fur Leggings is a solid upgrade leg piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Tiallia Priel (Boss) in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (75.0% drop rate). Strong Agility (+12), Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +3%. Costs 3100g from vendors.
