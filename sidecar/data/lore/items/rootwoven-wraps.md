Rootwoven Wraps is a solid upgrade hand \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by The Gardener of Loomingwood (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (50.0% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+20), Wisdom +20, Magic resistance +3%. "The roots seem to still be growing." Costs 7500g from vendors.
