Nagalok Claw Gloves is a starter hand \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Available from vendors for 1500g. Dexterity +7, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Woven from some sort of seaweed and reinfoced with Nagalok claws."
