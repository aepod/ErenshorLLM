Crest of Braxonia is a endgame neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Prince Oslind (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (66.7% drop rate). +50 Health. "It vibrates against your chest and you feel a sense of power grow inside yourself." Costs 10000g from vendors.
