Gator Gloves is a mid-level hand \- relic item piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lurk (Boss) in Rottenfoot (66.7% drop rate). Strength +4, +25 Health. "Just a few quick simple modifications to the gator's claws." Costs 2100g from vendors.
