Unholy Bonesaw is a solid upgrade gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Headless in Shivering Tomb (2) (6.7% drop rate). Strength +6, Void resistance +5%, +35 Health. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Sometimes, it lands on your target 'just right' and that's not pleasant for anyone." Costs 3100g from vendors.
