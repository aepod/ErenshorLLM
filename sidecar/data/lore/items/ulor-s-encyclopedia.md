Ulor's Encyclopedia is a consumable item. Dropped by Lost Treasure (30+) in Treasure Hunting (12.8% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+20), Wisdom +15, Magic resistance +4%. "Said to contain all wordly knowledge, the pages are written over in distasteful jokes." Costs 9000g from vendors.
