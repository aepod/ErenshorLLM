Battlemage Sleeves is a solid upgrade arm \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher (50.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Intelligence +12, Magic resistance +4%. "A strange mix of physical and mental energy lingers" Costs 8500g from vendors.
