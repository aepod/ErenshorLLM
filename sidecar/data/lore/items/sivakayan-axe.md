Sivakayan Axe is a endgame gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Darkwarden Awxa (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (1.4% drop rate). Also drops from Darkwarden Ealdred and Darkwarden Ilyth. Strength +5, Poison resistance +5%, +35 Health. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "A standard Sivakayan War Axe" Costs 2975g from vendors.
