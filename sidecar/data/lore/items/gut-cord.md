Gut Cord is a endgame waist \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Swamp Wyrm (Boss) in Rottenfoot (50.0% drop rate). Strength +7, Poison resistance +4%, +650 Health. "Getting it tied on is hard, overcoming the stench is harder" Costs 14000g from vendors.
