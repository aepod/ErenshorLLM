Battleworn Plate is a mid-level chest piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Goliath (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (20.0% drop rate). Strength +8, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +1%. Costs 2300g from vendors.
