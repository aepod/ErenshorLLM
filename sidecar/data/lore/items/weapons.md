# Weapons

Weapons in Erenshor come in several types: 1H Melee, 2H Melee, Daggers, 2H Staves, 2H Bows, Wands, Books/Tomes, and Shields. Each weapon has base damage, a delay value (attack speed), and stat bonuses. Weapons are class-restricted. The six classes are Arcanist, Paladin, Windblade, Druid, Stormcaller, and Reaver.

---

Notable endgame weapons (level 39-40): Eon Blade of Time (1H, Paladin, level 40, 42 dmg), Arc Crescent of the Void (Bow, Stormcaller, level 40, 43 dmg), Parallax Key to the Void (2H, Reaver, level 40, 35 dmg/1.0 delay), Reaver's Steel (2H, Reaver, level 39, 35 dmg/1.0 delay), Lux Carver of Stars (Dagger, level 40), Corruptor (Wand, level 40), Eternox Remnant of the Void (Wand, level 40), Singularity Vessel of Creation (Wand, level 40), Reaper of Regrowth (1H, level 40), Nature's Will (Shield, level 41).

---

1H Melee weapons are used by Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Key weapons: Copper Sword (1, quest reward), Steel Long Sword (4), Polished Longsword (6), Molorai Broadsword (16), Fungal Edge (20, 28 dmg), Sivakayan Axe (24, 33 dmg), Diamond Edged Sword (30), Azynthian Blade (33, 32 dmg), Blackbone Axe (38, 44 dmg), Esen (37).

---

2H Melee weapons deal the highest raw damage. Paladins and Reavers favor these. Key weapons: Rusty Greatsword (3), Nightfall (6), Sea Giant's Cutlass (8), Awaken (16), Diamond Claymore (25, 37 dmg), Fungal Greatsword (30), Soulshiver (31), Molten Axe (39, 35 dmg), Monarch's Blade (39), Thryne Sledge of Creation (39, 36 dmg).

---

Bows are Stormcaller-exclusive. Key weapons: Traveler's Bow (2), Carved Bow (4), Rotwood Bow (6), Whitewood (7, quest reward), Recurve Bow (11), Molorai Bow (14), Seastring (14), Merosavillian Bow (21), Spectral Bow (24), Braxonian Bow (28), Deathgiver (30), Oldenbow (34), Siva Bow (34, 40 dmg), Arc Crescent of the Void (40, 43 dmg).

---

Daggers are used primarily by Windblades. Key daggers: Rusty Dagger (1), Priel Knife (4), The Interrogator (12), Ebonshade (19), Ephermalia Blade of Waning (28), Asp's Fang (29), Oslind's Eviscerator (31), Azynthian Dagger (35), Messenger (36), Apotheosis (38), Horn of Aeiros (39), Lux Carver of Stars (40).

---

Wands and books/tomes are used by Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Key wands: Weak Wand (3), Adept Wand (8), Eyestalk Wand (12), Fungal Bouquet (18), Dreamy Wand (22), Volcanic Sceptre (26), Bombastic Wand (30), Garg Wand (33), Corruptor/Eternox/Singularity (all level 40). Key books: Idol of Sivakaya (5), Argo's Grimoire (10), Priest's Stein (18, Druid), Celestial Spike (25), Braxonian Testament (31), Siva-Braxonian Teachings (32), Ulor's Encyclopedia (36), Ascended Remains (37), Tome of Necromancy (39, Arcanist).

---

Shields are secondary weapons providing defensive stats. Key shields: Old Buckler (2), Twilight Shield (7), Erandian Avenger (29), Hopewarden (35), Bastion (36), Cursed Hide Shield (38), Griefwarden (38), Nature's Will (41). The Shivering Torch (level 50) and Nighthollow Candle (level 100) are unique secondary items.

---

Two-handed weapons receive a 70% DPS bonus for Paladins. Weapon quality is determined by drop rarity tier: guaranteed, common, uncommon, rare, legendary, and ultra-rare. Higher rarity weapons have better stats and require farming specific bosses.
