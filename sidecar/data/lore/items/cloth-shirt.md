Cloth Shirt is a starter chest piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aragath Swellsteer (78.2% drop rate). Also drops from Zashlyn Bloodbane and Neophyte Necromancer. "A simple cloth shirt" Costs 3g from vendors.
