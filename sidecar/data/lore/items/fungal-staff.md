Fungal Staff is a endgame gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Abyssal Lord (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (50.0% drop rate). Also drops from Abyssal Fungus Protector and Abyssal Fungus Protector. Strong Intelligence (+10), Wisdom +10, +75 Health. Special: 20% chance on ATTACK:. "The staff is an everchanging collage of fungi" Costs 7855g from vendors.
