Hardened Copper Sword is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Bassle Wavebreaker (68.1% drop rate). Also drops from Cagnus Marlsen and Molorai Anointed. 10 damage. "A well-forged copper sword." Costs 76g from vendors.
