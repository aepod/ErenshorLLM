Salt Encrusted Boots is a endgame foot piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Mason (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (66.7% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Dockmaster. Strength +8, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +1%. "They're so hardened by salt that they make you walk funny." Costs 12533g from vendors.
