## Drafty Baths

### Type

Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

**Drafty Baths** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

This note can be found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

Drafty Baths describes a mysterious draft coming from the walls in the bathroom.

Drafty Baths

Bellsie alwas said that the bathtub is in a dreadfully drafty spot. She said the walls held nothing back, and implored me to move the source to another room, however, it was architecturally impossible to do that.

Warming up by the fire after a bath is not the worst, after all.

The note refers to a hidden wall between the sink and the bathtub; click on the wall to open it, revealing a staircase leading to the estate's attic.
