Stirrer of Sleep is a endgame gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Cloaked Shadow in Mysterious Portal (3) (5.2% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Intelligence +20, +300 Health. Special: 3% chance on ATTACK:. "It will rouse one from any slumber... by hitting them with it" Costs 15000g from vendors.
