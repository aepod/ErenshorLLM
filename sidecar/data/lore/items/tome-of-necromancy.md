Tome of Necromancy is a consumable item. Dropped by Darkwarden Ilyth (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (50.0% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+30), Wisdom +20, Void resistance +12%. "Activatable: Summon Skeleton" Costs 18500g from vendors.
