Naturewalker's Breeches is a endgame leg piece for Druids and Stormcallers. Dropped by Corrupted Fungal Bear in Blooming Sepulcher (33.3% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Dexterity +15, Magic resistance +1%. "Effect applied once item is equipped." Costs 19500g from vendors.
