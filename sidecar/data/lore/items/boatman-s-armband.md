Boatman's Armband is a mid-level arm \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Drowned Corpse in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (5.4% drop rate). Strong Charisma (+10), Magic resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +1%. "Yarrrrr! You're a pirate!" Costs 2000g from vendors.
