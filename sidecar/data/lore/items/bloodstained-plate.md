Bloodstained Plate is a endgame chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by The Abomination (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (50.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Dexterity +15, Magic resistance +1%. "Covered in dried blood" Costs 12500g from vendors.
