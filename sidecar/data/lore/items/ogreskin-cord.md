Ogreskin Cord is a mid-level waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gruhglor (Boss) in Fernalla's Revival Plains (19.5% drop rate). Agility +3, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Goes great with some Ogreskin pants" Costs 1000g from vendors.
