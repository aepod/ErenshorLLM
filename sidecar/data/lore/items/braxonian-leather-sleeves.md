Braxonian Leather Sleeves is a endgame arm piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (12.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Strong Strength (+15), Dexterity +15, Magic resistance +2%. "Legendary Leather Armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 13250g from vendors.
