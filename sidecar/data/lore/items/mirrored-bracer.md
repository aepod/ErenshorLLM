Mirrored Bracer is a mid-level bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Available from vendors for 17855g. Strong Charisma (+12), Magic resistance +6%, Poison resistance +2%. "A Bracer that reflects light into incredible prismatic images around you."
