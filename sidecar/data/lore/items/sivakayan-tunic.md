Sivakayan Tunic is a mid-level chest piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Acceptor (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (3.9% drop rate). Wisdom +4, Magic resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Ancient etchings cover the interior" Costs 200g from vendors.
