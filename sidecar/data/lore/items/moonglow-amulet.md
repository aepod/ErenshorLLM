Moonglow Amulet is a solid upgrade neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ancient Corpse in Soluna's Landing (6.7% drop rate). Also drops from Ancient Celestarian and Ancient Guard. Void resistance +4%, +100 Health. "Once worn by all Paladins of Soluna, many are now lost to time" Costs 9850g from vendors.
