### Related quest(s)

A Dying Wish, Part 3

Memory of Elwio

It recalls sorrow. A task has been given by a dying hero that cannot be declined. Her voice echoes as her spirit ascends, visibly battling a darkness with her own light.

"Spare him of this", it cries. "If the odds say he will fall by his mother's twisted hand, fell him by your own - for she will never let go, not even in death."

"You must not share this wish - it must be you who carries the burden. Your mortal legacy will be tarnished, but your immortal legacy will be revered by the Gods..." and the voice fades away.
