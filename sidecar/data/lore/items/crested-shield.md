Crested Shield is a mid-level gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Goliath (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (20.0% drop rate). Intelligence +4, Void resistance +2%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "Decorated with a crest long forgotten." Costs 1350g from vendors.
