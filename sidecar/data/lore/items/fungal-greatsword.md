Fungal Greatsword is a solid upgrade gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Vinx (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (25.0% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Endurance +15, Poison resistance +6%. Special: 25% chance on ATTACK:. "It flexes more than a steel sword would, but holds no matter how hard the swing" Costs 21000g from vendors.
