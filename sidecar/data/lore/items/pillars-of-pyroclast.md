Pillars of Pyroclast is a solid upgrade leg piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Pyroclast (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (33.3% drop rate). Strength +8, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +1%. "As though lava was poured into molds and left to harden" Costs 5560g from vendors.
