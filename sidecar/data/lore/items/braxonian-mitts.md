Braxonian Mitts is a endgame hand piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (24.0% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Massive Dexterity (+20), Wisdom +10, Elemental resistance +3%. "Legendary Leather Armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 9500g from vendors.
