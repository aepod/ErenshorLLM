Swirlstone Amulet is a mid-level neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Brewmistress (Boss) in Old Krakengard (10.9% drop rate). Also drops from Elizah Vernor and Thella Steepleton. Magic resistance +7%, Poison resistance +7%, Elemental resistance +7%. "The color inside is everchanging. Maybe it's based on your mood...?" Costs 700g from vendors.
