Eon, Blade of Time is a best-in-slot gear piece. Dropped by Astra, Rogue of the Stars (Boss) in Soluna's Landing (16.7% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +6%. Special: 20% chance on ATTACK:. "This blade has existed since before time itself." Costs 38550g from vendors.
