Pious Sandals is a endgame foot piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Constructed Faithful in Jaws of Sivakaya (4.9% drop rate). Also drops from Astorim and Karsith. Strong Strength (+12), Endurance +12, Magic resistance +4%. "For the most devout" Costs 14540g from vendors.
