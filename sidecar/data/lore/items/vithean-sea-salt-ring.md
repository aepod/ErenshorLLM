Vithean Sea Salt Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Captain Sigrid (Boss) in Old Krakengard (55.4% drop rate). Also drops from Overseer Orlok. Intelligence +3, Magic resistance +5%, Poison resistance +5%. "Its stone is crystallized salt from the sea." Costs 700g from vendors.
