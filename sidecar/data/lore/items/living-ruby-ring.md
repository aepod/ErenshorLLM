Living Ruby Ring is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ruby Fiend in Rockshade Hold (9.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Elemental resistance +6%, +45 Health. "You get used to the squirmy feeling." Costs 2355g from vendors.
