Wrath Wrap is a solid upgrade bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Frenzy (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (33.3% drop rate). Strength +8, Void resistance +3%, +15 Health. "A longing for battle eats at you" Costs 2858g from vendors.
