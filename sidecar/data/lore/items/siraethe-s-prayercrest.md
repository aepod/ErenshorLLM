Siraethe's Prayercrest is a best-in-slot head \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Vessel Siraethe (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (20.0% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+25), Wisdom +25, Void resistance +5%. "It feeds a constant stream of dark thoughts into your mind" Costs 19500g from vendors.
