Crystallized Tactics is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Pamia the Coward (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (33.3% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+12), Wisdom +12, +100 Health. "Carried by the warriors of Vitheo, its energies are said to give an edge in battle." Costs 3150g from vendors.
