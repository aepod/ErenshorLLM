Molorai Battle Boots is a mid-level foot piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Bodyguard in Old Krakengard (7.8% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Veteran. Agility +4, Magic resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +1%. "This unfortunately won't be your last pair of used shoes." Costs 450g from vendors.
