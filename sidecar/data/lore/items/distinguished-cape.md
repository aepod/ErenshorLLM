Distinguished Cape is a mid-level back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral Torturer (Boss) in Lost Cellar (33.3% drop rate). Strength +8, Magic resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +1%. "Worn by the most elite of the original Azure Guard" Costs 2250g from vendors.
