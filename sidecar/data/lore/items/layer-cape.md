Layer Cape is a mid-level back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Goliath (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (20.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Charisma +10, Magic resistance +2%. "It tickles your legs as you run in the most wonderful way." Costs 2000g from vendors.
