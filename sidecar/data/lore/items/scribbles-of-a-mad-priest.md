Scribbles of a Mad Priest is a solid upgrade gear piece. Dropped by Upha, Priest of Brax (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (100.0% drop rate). Also drops from Scribbles of a Mad Priest and Finally, we have discovered the door to the ancients, but Evadne holds the only key. She will not be felled without first quelling the remnants of Brax's flames from which she feeds..
