Braxonian Bracelet is a endgame bracer piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (24.0% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Intelligence +8, Elemental resistance +2%, +25 Health. "Legendary Bracelet of Brax's Arcanists" Costs 8500g from vendors.
