Shadowclasp is a solid upgrade back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Shrouded Sivakayan (4.5% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Intelligence +20, Void resistance +4%. "Worn by a Sivakayan Spectre, it seems to emit small clouds of shadow as it flows in the breeze" Costs 1300g from vendors.
