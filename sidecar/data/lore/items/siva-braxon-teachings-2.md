Siva-Braxon Teachings (2)

To protect their paradise, Siva created a warrior unmatched by any who came before, and so not to encroach on the kingdom of Brax, she imbued him with the power of water and the speed of air. He was named Vitheo.

His oath to Sivakaya was sworn on the very altar upon which he was built, undying in its truth and unwavering in its tethers, it was forever done.
