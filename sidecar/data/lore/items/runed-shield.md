Runed Shield is a endgame gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Undertoes (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (33.3% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+12), Wisdom +12, Magic resistance +5%. "The writings are faded and unreadable now" Costs 12575g from vendors.
