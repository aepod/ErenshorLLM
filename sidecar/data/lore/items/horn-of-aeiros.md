Horn of Aeiros is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aeiros, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (50.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+15), Dexterity +10, +275 Health. Special: 3% chance on ATTACK:. "Torn from the head of a corrupted beast." Costs 45000g from vendors.
