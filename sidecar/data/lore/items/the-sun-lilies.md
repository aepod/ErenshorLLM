### Type

### Related quest(s)

**The Sun Lilies** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

This note can be found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

The Sun Lilies helps the player determine the corresponding time for the torches surrounding the statue on Bellwain Island.

The Sun Lilies

Bellsie always said the Sun Lilies bloomed at 6pm, but I've never seen them in the garden, even though she sang her song there each day for the plants.

I will not forget her voice, I wish I could remember the words.

Look for the torch surrounded by yellow flowers; this torch corresponds to 6:00.
