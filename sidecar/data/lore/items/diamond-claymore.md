Diamond Claymore is a solid upgrade gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Stoneman Colossus (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+25), Endurance +15, +55 Health. Special: 25% chance on ATTACK:. "Hardened by time and pressure, its edge never fades" Costs 4122g from vendors.
