Braxonian Spaulders is a endgame arm piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (1.1% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice) and Braxonian Weapon Master. Strong Strength (+15), Dexterity +10, Magic resistance +2%. "Legendary Plate Armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 13250g from vendors.
