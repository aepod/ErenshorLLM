Hilltop Ring is a starter ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Autmis the Lost (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (53.7% drop rate). Strength +2, +15 Health. "Makes you feel as steady as a mountain" Costs 200g from vendors.
