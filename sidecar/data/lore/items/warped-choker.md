Warped Choker is a endgame neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sivakayan Voidmaster (9.0% drop rate). Massive Strength (+25), Intelligence +25, +225 Health. "Clasped around the neck of followers by Sivakaya herself, it holds her power within" Costs 25550g from vendors.
