Blue Leaf Cape is a mid-level back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Thorn (50.0% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+15), Strength +10, Magic resistance +3%. "A magical blue leaf, as big as you are" Costs 9500g from vendors.
