Charm of Talent is a charm item usable by Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (30+) in Treasure Hunting (12.8% drop rate). Modifiers: Hardiness: +2 / 40, Finesse: +2 / 40, Arcanism: +2 / 40, Restoration: +2 / 40. Costs 25000g from vendors.
