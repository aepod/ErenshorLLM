Virophan Helm is a endgame head \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Elwio the Traitor (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (66.7% drop rate). Strong Strength (+13), Endurance +13, Magic resistance +2%. "Gifted to the most elite warriors under Vitheo" Costs 1855g from vendors.
