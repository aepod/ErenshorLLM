Bonebanded Armguard is a endgame arm piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Balthom, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (33.3% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+25), Endurance +12, Magic resistance +3%. "Held together by dark magic" Costs 17450g from vendors.
