## Bellwain Attic Key

### Type

Key, Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

The Mystery of Bellwain Isle

**Bellwain Attic Key** is a Quest Item in Erenshor.

# Location

Bellwain Attic Key is found in a cave at the back of the island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island. Its location is hinted at by the note  Eroding Rockwall.

# Description

Bellwain Attic Key is used to open the door in the attic of the house on Bellwain Island.
