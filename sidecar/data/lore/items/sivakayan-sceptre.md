Sivakayan Sceptre is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Assassin of Sivakaya (1.4% drop rate). Also drops from Grand Arcanist of Sivakaya and High Paladin of Sivakaya. Strong Wisdom (+10), +50 Health, 6 damage. "Wielded by the Clerics of Sivakaya" Costs 2515g from vendors.
