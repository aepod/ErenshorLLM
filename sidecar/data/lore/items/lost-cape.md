Lost Cape is a starter back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (10-20) in Treasure Hunting (20.0% drop rate). Also drops from Lost Treasure (1-10). Strength +5, +100 Health. "Worn by who knows who on who knows when" Costs 3500g from vendors.
