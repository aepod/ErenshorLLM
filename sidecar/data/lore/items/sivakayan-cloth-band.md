Sivakayan Cloth Band is a solid upgrade head piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sivakayan Voidweaver in Silkengrass Meadowlands (7.4% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+18), Magic resistance +4%, Poison resistance +4%. "Emits a strange light that only you can see." Costs 1005g from vendors.
