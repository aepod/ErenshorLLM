Azynthian Blade is a endgame gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Molorai Curator (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (6.7% drop rate). Strength +8, +250 Health, 32 damage. Special: 75% chance on ATTACK:. "Used by ancient Azynthian warriors, its dark history feeds its unholy power." Costs 28500g from vendors.
