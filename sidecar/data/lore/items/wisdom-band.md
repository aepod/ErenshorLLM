Wisdom Band is a starter ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (10-20) in Treasure Hunting (20.0% drop rate). Also drops from Lost Treasure (1-10). Strong Wisdom (+10), Magic resistance +5%, Poison resistance +5%. "Brilliance flows through it" Costs 4500g from vendors.
