Cloth Pants is a starter leg piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aragath Swellsteer (78.2% drop rate). Also drops from Zashlyn Bloodbane and Neophyte Necromancer. "Simple Cloth Pants" Costs 3g from vendors.
