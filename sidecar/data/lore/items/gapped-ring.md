Gapped Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ughl of the Skies (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (50.0% drop rate). Endurance +3, Magic resistance +3%, Elemental resistance +3%. "One size fits all." Costs 335g from vendors.
