Adventure Charm is a charm item usable by Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Available from vendors for 2500g. Modifiers: Hardiness: +1 / 40, Finesse: +1 / 40, Arcanism: +1 / 40, Restoration: +1 / 40.
