# Rainbow Flint

## Rainbow Flint

### Type

Quest Item

### Reward from

The Mystery of Bellwain Island

**Rainbow Flint** is a Quest Item in Erenshor.

# Location

Rainbow Flint is found in the study as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island. It is in a small safe against the wall of the room.

# Description

Rainbow Flint is used to light the torches surrounding the statue on the island. Clicking a torch multiple times changes its color:

Red → Blue → White → Orange → Green → Red

Once all the torches are lit the correct colors, the statue in the middle will rise.

Rainbow Flint

It sparks a different color, depending on where you strike it. It's powerful enough to light something as large as a torch.
