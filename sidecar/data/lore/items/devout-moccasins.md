Devout Moccasins is a mid-level foot piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Ecclesiarch (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (9.1% drop rate). Endurance +4, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%. "The arch support is off the charts." Costs 1700g from vendors.
