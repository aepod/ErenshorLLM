Feathered Cape is a solid upgrade back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Pteriaped Matron in Loomingwood Forest (33.3% drop rate). Intelligence +8, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%. "It's not really made of feathers, but it looks like it from a distance." Costs 7550g from vendors.
