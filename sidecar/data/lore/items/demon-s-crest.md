Demon's Crest is a starter neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Faerie Drone in Faerie's Brake (4.5% drop rate). Strength +2, +20 Health. "Its eyes follow your own gaze" Costs 255g from vendors.
