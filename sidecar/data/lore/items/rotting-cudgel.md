Rotting Cudgel is a starter weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Skeleton Soldier in Faerie's Brake (3.5% drop rate). Dexterity +3, Void resistance +9%, +5 Health. "It feels like it could break at any moment, but it never does." Costs 15g from vendors.
