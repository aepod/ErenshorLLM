Moonstone Bangle is a solid upgrade bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Celestial Matter in Soluna's Landing (7.7% drop rate). Intelligence +5, +75 Health. "Moonstones aren't really from the moon" Costs 2145g from vendors.
