Thorned Knuckle Gloves is a starter hand \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Thorn (50.0% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+15), Intelligence +10, Poison resistance +3%. "Small fibrous thorns protrude from the joints" Costs 7900g from vendors.
