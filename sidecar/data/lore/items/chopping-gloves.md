Chopping Gloves is a mid-level hand piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Chef (Boss) in Old Krakengard (13.4% drop rate). Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Knife proof. For chefs... and for warriors!" Costs 245g from vendors.
