Executioner of Azure is a endgame gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Captain Kilkay (100.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, Void resistance +6%. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "Stained by the blood of the enemies of Port Azure" Costs 35000g from vendors.
