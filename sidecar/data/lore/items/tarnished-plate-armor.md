Tarnished Plate Armor is a mid-level chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Druo the Reborn (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (27.5% drop rate). Also drops from A Grassland Ogre Plaguebringer. Strength +5, Magic resistance +4%, +50 Health. "Coated by rust, it maintains its integrity." Costs 1025g from vendors.
