Rottenfoot Sash is a mid-level waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Rottenfoot Mauler in Rottenfoot (32.7% drop rate). Also drops from A Rottenfoot Warrior and Rottenfoot Witchdoctor. Dexterity +4, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +3%. "A sash worn by Rottenfoots who have achieved greatness in battle." Costs 5g from vendors.
