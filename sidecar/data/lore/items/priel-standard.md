Priel Standard is a solid upgrade back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Tiallia Priel (Boss) in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (6.7% drop rate). Also drops from Addison Priel and Chazza Priel. Charisma +5, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "It's sanded smooth and well made." Costs 450g from vendors.
