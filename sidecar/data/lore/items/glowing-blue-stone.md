Glowing Blue Stone is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Ancient Spectre in Fernalla's Revival Plains (7.3% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Wisdom +10, Magic resistance +3%. "You've never seen the color blue look so vivid" Costs 18500g from vendors.
