Sleeper's Pin is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Nightmarian Arcanist in Mysterious Portal (2) (7.6% drop rate). Charisma +5, +25 Health, 15 damage. Special: 4% chance on ATTACK:. "Pins and needles surge through your hand" Costs 1200g from vendors.
