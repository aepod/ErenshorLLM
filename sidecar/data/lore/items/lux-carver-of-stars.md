Lux, Carver of Stars is a best-in-slot weapon piece. Dropped by Astra, Rogue of the Stars (Boss) in Soluna's Landing (16.7% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+15), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +6%. Special: 8% chance on ATTACK:. "A dagger only known from legends, it holds the power of the stars." Costs 25445g from vendors.
