Plaguewind is a best-in-slot back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Blight Wyrm (Boss) in The Blight (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+25), Dexterity +15, +200 Health. "Rot and horror flows off of its fabric freely" Costs 17550g from vendors.
