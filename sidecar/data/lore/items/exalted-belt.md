Exalted Belt is a endgame waist \- relic item piece for Druids and Stormcallers. Dropped by Exalted Firebone (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (66.7% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+25), Charisma +25, +150 Health. "Who knew a belt could be so prestigious" Costs 21250g from vendors.
