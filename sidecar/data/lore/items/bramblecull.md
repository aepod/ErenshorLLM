Bramblecull is a endgame gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Grounds Keeper (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (33.3% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+12), Strength +10, +185 Health. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "The vines continuously wrap around your hands." Costs 24789g from vendors.
