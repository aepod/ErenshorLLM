Famish is a endgame waist \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Darkwarden Ealdred (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (33.3% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, +155 Health. "The hunger you feel while wearing this is undescribable" Costs 12500g from vendors.
