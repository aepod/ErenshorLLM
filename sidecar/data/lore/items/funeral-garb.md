Funeral Garb is a starter chest piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Mourning (Boss) in Island Tomb (10.1% drop rate). Wisdom +2, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "It's threadbare and torn." Costs 200g from vendors.
