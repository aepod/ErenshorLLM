Icy Bow is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Sivakayan Doomshade (4.5% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+40), Magic resistance +3%, Void resistance +5%. Special: 99% chance on ATTACK:. "It's cold and stiff and the draw on it is ever so slow" Costs 20000g from vendors.
