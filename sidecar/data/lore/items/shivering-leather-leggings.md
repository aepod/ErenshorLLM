Shivering Leather Leggings is a mid-level leg piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Militia Druid in Old Krakengard (3.1% drop rate). Endurance +3, Magic resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Made of some oddly textured skin that you've never seen before" Costs 5g from vendors.
