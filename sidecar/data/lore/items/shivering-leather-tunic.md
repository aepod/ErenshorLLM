Shivering Leather Tunic is a mid-level chest piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Spiritist (Boss) in Old Krakengard (41.8% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Militia Druid. Strength +3. "Made of some oddly textured skin that you've never seen before" Costs 5g from vendors.
