Aged Plate Greaves is a mid-level leg piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral Castle Guard in Lost Cellar (2.0% drop rate). Also drops from Spectral King's Guard and Rotting Royal Hound. Strength +4, Magic resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +4%. "They still have spots of immaculate detail" Costs 899g from vendors.
