Gourd Carver is a starter weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Battlefist (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (33.3% drop rate). Strength +3, Void resistance +1%, 6 damage. Special: 8% chance on ATTACK:. "If it's really just for carving gourds, somebody definitely oversharpened it." Costs 55g from vendors.
