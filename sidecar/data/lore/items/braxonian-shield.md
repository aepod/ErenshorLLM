Braxonian Shield is a mid-level gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Peon (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Intelligence +8, Magic resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +2%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "Worn by the lost warriors of Brax" Costs 1800g from vendors.
