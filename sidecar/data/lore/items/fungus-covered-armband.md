Fungus Covered Armband is a solid upgrade arm \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ravager, The First Infected (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (45.8% drop rate). Also drops from A Deepling Crimsonite and A Deepling Lunarite. Intelligence +8, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +3%. "This used to be normal plate armor, but now it's covered in a strange fungus." Costs 1750g from vendors.
