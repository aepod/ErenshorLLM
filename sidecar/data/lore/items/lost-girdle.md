Lost Girdle is a starter waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (10-20) in Treasure Hunting (20.0% drop rate). Also drops from Lost Treasure (1-10). Strength +6, Elemental resistance +3%, Void resistance +5%. "It's ugly. No wonder they buried it." Costs 3200g from vendors.
