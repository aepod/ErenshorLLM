Ephermalia, Blade of Waning is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Morian the Ascended (Boss) in Silkengrass Meadowlands (33.3% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Void resistance +3%, 14 damage. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "A cursed old blade that has seen horror beyond imagination" Costs 2155g from vendors.
