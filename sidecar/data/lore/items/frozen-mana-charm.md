Frozen Mana Charm is a endgame neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Nazura the Traitor (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (66.7% drop rate). Strong Endurance (+10), Agility +10, Magic resistance +4%. "The physical manifestation of mana itself" Costs 12450g from vendors.
