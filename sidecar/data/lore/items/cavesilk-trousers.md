Cavesilk Trousers is a solid upgrade leg \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Slumbering Queen (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (52.3% drop rate). Strong Charisma (+10), Magic resistance +4%, Poison resistance +2%. "They're so pretty it hurts." Costs 1750g from vendors.
