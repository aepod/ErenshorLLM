Slime Coated Helm is a mid-level head \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Subterranean Goop in The Elderstone Mines (7.6% drop rate). Also drops from A Subterranean Magmite and Gloopa (Quarter). Strength +6, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +4%. "You dropped it 3 times trying to put it on." Costs 1855g from vendors.
