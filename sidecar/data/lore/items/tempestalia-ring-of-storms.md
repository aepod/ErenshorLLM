## Tempestalia, Ring of Storms

### Reward from

Repairing Orion's Ring, Part 2

You can summon a thunderstorm by activating the ring's effect Invoke Storm. This is the only way to disable the brazier to make Evadne the Corrupted vulnerable to damage.
