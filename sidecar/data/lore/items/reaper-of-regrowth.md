Reaper of Regrowth is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Fallen Fernalla (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (27.5% drop rate). Also drops from Fallen Fernalla. Strong Wisdom (+15), Intelligence +10, +100 Health. Special: 1% chance on ATTACK:. "Causes all mobs to respawn in your zone if their spawn criteria is met." Costs 50000g from vendors.
