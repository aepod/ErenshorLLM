Rotting Sivakayan Helm is a mid-level head piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by An Apparition of Sivakaya (Boss) in The Bonepits (25.0% drop rate). Endurance +5, Magic resistance +1%, Void resistance +2%. "It's oddly polished." Costs 1005g from vendors.
