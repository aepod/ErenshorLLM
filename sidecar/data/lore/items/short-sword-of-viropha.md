Short Sword of Viropha is a starter weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aquilamar Blade (10.1% drop rate). Also drops from Aquilamar Protector and Cirreus Alxanth. Strength +5, Void resistance +4%, 14 damage. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Said to have been forged in the fabled lands of Viropha, under the supervision of Vithean generals." Costs 1550g from vendors.
