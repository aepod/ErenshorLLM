Spectral Sword is a mid-level weapon piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Reliquary Fiend (Boss) in Reliquary Hall (20.4% drop rate). Also drops from Vithean Elite and Vithean Immortal. Strong Strength (+10), +10 Health, 14 damage. "You cannot feel it in your hand, but somehow its strikes land true." Costs 2120g from vendors.
