Slab of Meteorite is a solid upgrade gear piece for Druids, Paladins, and Reavers. Dropped by Living Meteorite in Soluna's Landing (14.6% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +4%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "Its natural shape creates a convenient handle" Costs 2650g from vendors.
