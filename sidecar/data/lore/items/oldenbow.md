Oldenbow is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Balthom, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (33.3% drop rate). Massive Dexterity (+30), Strength +25, +225 Health. Special: 8% chance on ATTACK:. "The wood is of an indescribable quality and the string feels as though it were wound yesterday" Costs 9255g from vendors.
