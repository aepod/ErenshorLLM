Thorn is a mid-level weapon piece. Dropped by Massia the Fallen (Boss) in The Duskenlight Coast (50.0% drop rate). Wisdom +6, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. Special: 15% chance on ATTACK:. "A dagger made of enchanted vines and twisted thorns" Costs 425g from vendors.
