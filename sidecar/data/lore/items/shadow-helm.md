Shadow Helm is a solid upgrade head \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Warded Shadow (Boss) in Shivering Tomb (2) (38.1% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Once worn by a shadow, it's been witness to unimaginable horror" Costs 5000g from vendors.
