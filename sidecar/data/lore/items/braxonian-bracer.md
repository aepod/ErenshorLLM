Braxonian Bracer is a endgame bracer piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Braxonian Weapon Master (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (2.4% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Honor Guard and Braxonian High Guard. Strength +8, Elemental resistance +15%, +50 Health. "Legendary Plate armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 8500g from vendors.
