Messenger is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Windblades. Dropped by Rotting Guardian in Blooming Sepulcher (0.7% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+15), Intelligence +15, +100 Health. Special: 70% chance on ATTACK:. "Said to have carved tablets bearing Fernalla's word during her Ascension" Costs 25000g from vendors.
