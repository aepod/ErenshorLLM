The Gift of Eloquence is a mid-level neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Deepling Orator (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (43.4% drop rate). Intelligence +5, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Effect applied once item is equipped." Costs 2600g from vendors.
