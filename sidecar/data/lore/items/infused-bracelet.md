Infused Bracelet is a consumable item. Dropped by Blunga in Willowwatch Ridge (16.7% drop rate). Strength +2, Poison resistance +4%, Void resistance +4%. "It consistently drips some sort of gooey syrup" Costs 20g from vendors.
