Spell Scroll: Necrosis is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Azynthian Firebone in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (2.6% drop rate). Also drops from Azynthian Icebone and Greater Azynthian Firebone. Costs 25000g from vendors.
