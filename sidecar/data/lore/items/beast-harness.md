Beast Harness is a starter waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sylvan Giant Runt (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (25.0% drop rate). Strength +2, Magic resistance +2%, +8 Health. "Meant to restrain a terrifying thing." Costs 5g from vendors.
