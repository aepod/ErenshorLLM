Dusken Staff is a solid upgrade gear piece for Arcanists, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Dusken Giant in The Duskenlight Coast (33.3% drop rate). Strength +6, Poison resistance +5%, 31 damage. "Seemingly made of night clouds" Costs 2800g from vendors.
