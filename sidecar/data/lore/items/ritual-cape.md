Ritual Cape is a solid upgrade back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Cinder (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (20.0% drop rate). Strength +8, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "This cape has been worn to horrific occassions." Costs 3250g from vendors.
