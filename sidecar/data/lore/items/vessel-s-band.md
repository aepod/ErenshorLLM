Vessel's Band is a best-in-slot ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Vessel Siraethe (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (28.8% drop rate). Massive Strength (+25), Intelligence +25, Void resistance +4%. "Worn by the Vessel of Sivakaya" Costs 16500g from vendors.
