Obsidian Boots is a solid upgrade foot piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Nightmare (Boss) in Shivering Step (50.0% drop rate). Magic resistance +7%, Poison resistance +7%, Elemental resistance +7%. "They're hard, but something about the material repels magical effects with great ease." Costs 3550g from vendors.
