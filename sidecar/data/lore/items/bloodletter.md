Bloodletter is a mid-level weapon piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Highwayman Marauder in Fernalla's Revival Plains (8.3% drop rate). Also drops from Shambler. Strength +3, 21 damage. "The name "Benny" is scratched into the handle" Costs 2500g from vendors.
