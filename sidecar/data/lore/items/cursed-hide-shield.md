Cursed Hide Shield is a best-in-slot gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Demented Malaroth (Boss) (50.0% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+20), Strength +10, Magic resistance +2%. Special: 99% chance on BASH:. "It's both living and decaying at once..." Costs 15855g from vendors.
