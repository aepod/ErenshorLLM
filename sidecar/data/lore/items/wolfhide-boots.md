Wolfhide Boots is a mid-level foot piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Obba of the Warkind (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (33.3% drop rate). Agility +6, Magic resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +1%. "Carefully crafted from the hide of a predator." Costs 365g from vendors.
