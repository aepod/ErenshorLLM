Ogrespice Bundle is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Agsha of the Soil (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (66.7% drop rate). Also drops from Druo the Reborn and A Grassland Ogre Mystic. Strong Intelligence (+10), Void resistance +3%, +45 Health. Special: 75% chance on ATTACK:. "Renowned for its magical properties, and if you eat it the clouds will tell you secrets." Costs 1355g from vendors.
