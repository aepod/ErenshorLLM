Bark Shield is a solid upgrade gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, and Reavers. Dropped by A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher (25.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+18), Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +2%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "The bark has petrified and is nearly indestructable" Costs 1455g from vendors.
