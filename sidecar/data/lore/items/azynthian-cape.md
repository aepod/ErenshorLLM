Azynthian Cape is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Oracle Miress (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (33.3% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+20), Wisdom +20, Void resistance +4%. Costs 18454g from vendors.
