Voidbloom Ring is a consumable item. Dropped by Lazalith, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (33.3% drop rate). Endurance +8, Void resistance +3%, +30 Health. "A gorgeous representation of a rare flower" Costs 1520g from vendors.
