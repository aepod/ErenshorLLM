# Masks

## Masks

Masks are cosmetic items that can drop from any enemy during the Halloween season.1

There is a 1/1000 (0.1%) chance per kill for a mask to drop. When a mask drops, there is a 1/50 chance it will be Mask: Eldoth. Otherwise, one of the other 15 masks is chosen at random. This results in an overall drop rate of 1/50 000 (0.002%) for Mask: Eldoth and about 1/15 306 (0.0065%) for each of the other masks.

### Usage

There are 16 masks in total and it is possible to receive duplicate masks. Masks are worn by placing them in the Cosmetic Items slots in your inventory. You may equip up to three simultaneously, allowing you to mix and match for interesting combinations. Simulated Players cannot wear masks at this time.
