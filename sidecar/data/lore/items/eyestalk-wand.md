Eyestalk Wand is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Old Brackey (Boss) in Vitheo's Watch (40.1% drop rate). Intelligence +5, Void resistance +2%, +30 Health. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "You're literally waving around a lobster eye." Costs 1200g from vendors.
