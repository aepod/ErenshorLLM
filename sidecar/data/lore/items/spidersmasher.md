Spidersmasher is a starter gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Battlefist (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (33.3% drop rate). Also drops from Obba of the Warkind and A Grassland Ogre Bruiser. Strong Strength (+10), Elemental resistance +5%, 25 damage. "The Ogres use this to keep the grass spiders at bay." Costs 350g from vendors.
