The Interrogator is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Jailor (Boss) in Old Krakengard (20.4% drop rate). Charisma +5, 30 damage. Special: 35% chance on ATTACK:. "Not meant to cause mortal damage... at least not right away." Costs 655g from vendors.
