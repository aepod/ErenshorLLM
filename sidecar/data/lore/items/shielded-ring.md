Shielded Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Acolyte in Blacksalt Strand (6.7% drop rate). Endurance +5, +30 Health. "You can't quite identify the metal..." Costs 1900g from vendors.
