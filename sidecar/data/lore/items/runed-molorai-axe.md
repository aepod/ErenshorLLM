Runed Molorai Axe is a mid-level weapon piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Archmage in Old Krakengard (70.9% drop rate). Strength +5, 14 damage. Special: 15% chance on ATTACK:. "It's light and sharp." Costs 400g from vendors.
