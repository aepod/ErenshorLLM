Charmed Belt is a endgame waist \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Winkles in Ripper's Keep (18.4% drop rate). Also drops from A Faerie Enchantress and A Faerie Foliagian. Massive Charisma (+20), Agility +15, Magic resistance +5%. "The sound from every step is a symphony" Costs 2105g from vendors.
