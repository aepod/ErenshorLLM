Decayed Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by An Apparition of Sivakaya (Boss) in The Bonepits (25.0% drop rate). Also drops from Risen Druid. Strength +2, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%. "It almost feels as though it'll crumble at any moment." Costs 255g from vendors.
