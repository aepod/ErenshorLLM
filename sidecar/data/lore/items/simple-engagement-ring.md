Simple Engagement Ring is a starter ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Risen Pilgrim in Silkengrass Meadowlands (1.9% drop rate). Charisma +6. "A plain engagement ring." Costs 5g from vendors.
