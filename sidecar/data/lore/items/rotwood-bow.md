Rotwood Bow is a starter gear piece. Dropped by Wiggleribs (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (12.1% drop rate). Also drops from A Sturdy Skeleton and A Skeleton Soldier. 18 damage. "It could snap at any moment but for now it has a nice pull" Costs 225g from vendors.
