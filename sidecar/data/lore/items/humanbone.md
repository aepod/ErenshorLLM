Humanbone is a mid-level gear piece. Dropped by A Grassland Ogre Sentinel in Underspine Hollow (12.1% drop rate). Also drops from A Grassland Ogre Shaman. Strength +6, Poison resistance +3%, +40 Health. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "It's hard not to feel uneasy holding this weapon" Costs 980g from vendors.
