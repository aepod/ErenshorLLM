Tattered Leather Boots is a starter foot piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Rattlewrists in Stowaway's Step (100.0% drop rate). Also drops from Plaxitheris and Grassland Ogre Gatherer. "Worn through the soles, they offer little protection." Costs 2g from vendors.
