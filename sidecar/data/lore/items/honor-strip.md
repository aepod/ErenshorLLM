Honor Strip is a mid-level back \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Deepling Orator (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (43.4% drop rate). Strength +7, Poison resistance +3%, +45 Health. "Once worn by high ranking soldiers of a lost army." Costs 1780g from vendors.
