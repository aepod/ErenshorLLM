Widow's Wraps is a consumable item. Dropped by Fearantula (Boss) in Shivering Step (33.3% drop rate). Strength +8, Elemental resistance +2%, Void resistance +2%. "Spun from web, they seem to attract various eight-legged things from the shadows." Costs 5500g from vendors.
