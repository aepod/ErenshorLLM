Aged Ring of Barriers is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Worn Ancient Guardian in Faerie's Brake (1.6% drop rate). Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%, Elemental resistance +3%. "An old protective stone sits firmly in its socket" Costs 65g from vendors.
