Fungal Bouquet is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Ravager, The First Infected (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (45.8% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+10), Poison resistance +2%, +15 Health. Special: 15% chance on ATTACK:. "Great to bring for a first date." Costs 1935g from vendors.
