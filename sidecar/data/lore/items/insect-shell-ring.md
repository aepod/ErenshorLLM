Insect Shell Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spark Queen (Boss) in Lost Cellar (50.0% drop rate). Charisma +7. "The metals are corroded and bent" Costs 1650g from vendors.
