Islander Bandit Armwrap is a starter arm piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aranuil Olaseo (19.6% drop rate). Also drops from Odyss Repman and Vevillo Polter. Strength +2, Poison resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +1%. "It bears the insignia of the Islander Bandits." Costs 15g from vendors.
