Wyrm Bone is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Swamp Wyrm (Boss) in Rottenfoot (25.0% drop rate). Strong Endurance (+10), Dexterity +10, 18 damage. Special: 3% chance on ATTACK:. "Apply Effects on Target:" Costs 13000g from vendors.
