A Twisted Spider Leg is a solid upgrade bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Slumbering Queen (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (28.4% drop rate). Also drops from A Fungal Sentinel and A Fungal Drone. Strength +6, Poison resistance +5%, Void resistance +2%. "You could swear that it's still moving a little bit." Costs 1470g from vendors.
