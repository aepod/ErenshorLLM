Vinewrapped Vambracers is a mid-level arm \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Elizah Vernor (8.6% drop rate). Also drops from Thella Steepleton and Molorai Militia Druid. Intelligence +4, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +3%. "Handwoven from a vine you don't recognize." Costs 200g from vendors.
