Idol of Sivakaya is a starter weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Neophyte Necromancer in The Bonepits (3.9% drop rate). Also drops from Woe and Boatman. Intelligence +4. "An ancient idol once held by Sivakayan disciples." Costs 200g from vendors.
