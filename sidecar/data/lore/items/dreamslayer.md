Dreamslayer is a solid upgrade gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Nightmarian Knight in Mysterious Portal (2) (4.9% drop rate). Strength +4, Void resistance +2%, +45 Health. Special: 4% chance on ATTACK:. "Its presence rouses the resting from their slumber" Costs 5000g from vendors.
