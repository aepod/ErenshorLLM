Terra Crown is a endgame head \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Nipper King (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (33.3% drop rate). Massive Charisma (+35), Magic resistance +5%, Poison resistance +5%. "Forged in the darkest depths of the continent" Costs 21000g from vendors.
