Living Sapphire Ring is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sapphire Fiend in Rockshade Hold (9.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+15), Charisma +15, Magic resistance +6%. "You'll never get used to the squirmy feeling." Costs 2355g from vendors.
