Phoenix Loop is a consumable item. Dropped by Pyroclast (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (33.3% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+12), Poison resistance +4%, +50 Health. "Activatable: Major Healing" Costs 20g from vendors.
