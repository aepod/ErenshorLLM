# Afternoon on the Isle

## Afternoon on the Isle

### Type

Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

The Mystery of Bellwain Island

**Afternoon on the Isle** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

Afternoon on the Isle is a note found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

Afternoon on the Isle is a poem with clues on what color to light the torches surrounding the statue.

Afternoon on the Isle

In decidedly feminine handwriting:

The Day Ages:
2pm brings snow-like seedlings drifting on the breeze
And then come more, carried with carefree ease
At 4, the sea thrashes, stretching high for the sky

This note is less direct than others in the quest and requires some extrapolation: the torch at 2 o'clock should be lit white, corresponding with the "snow-like seedlings." As more come next, 3 o'clock should also be white. Finally, the 4'clock torch should be lit blue, hinted at by the color of the sea.
