Pyrelink Pantaloons is a solid upgrade leg \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Evadne the Corrupted (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (29.2% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+15), Magic resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. "The chain links are tempered by holy flames." Costs 2465g from vendors.
