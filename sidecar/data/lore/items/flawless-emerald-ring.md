Flawless Emerald Ring is a starter ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (20-30) in Treasure Hunting (7.0% drop rate). Also drops from Lost Treasure (10-20). Strong Charisma (+10), Poison resistance +18%, +5 Health. "The emerald is the deepest, most crisp green you've ever laid eyes on" Costs 10000g from vendors.
