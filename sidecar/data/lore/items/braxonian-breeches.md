Braxonian Breeches is a endgame leg piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (12.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Strong Dexterity (+10), Wisdom +10, Magic resistance +1%. "Legendary Leather armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 12500g from vendors.
