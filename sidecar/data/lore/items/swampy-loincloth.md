Swampy Loincloth is a mid-level waist \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Rottenfoot Mauler in Rottenfoot (4.6% drop rate). Also drops from Rottenfoot Swampwalker and Rottenfoot Swampwalker. Strength +3, Poison resistance +5%, Void resistance +10%. "If you wear this, make a doctor's appointment." Costs 1000g from vendors.
