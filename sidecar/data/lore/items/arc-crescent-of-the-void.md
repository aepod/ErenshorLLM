Arc, Crescent of the Void is a best-in-slot gear piece. Dropped by Astra, Rogue of the Stars (Boss) in Soluna's Landing (16.7% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+55), Dexterity +40, +600 Health. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "It is made of some strange metal that flexes easily under your pull but snaps back to shape with a frightening fury" Costs 26550g from vendors.
