Seastring is a mid-level gear piece. Dropped by Greater Vithean Elemental in The Duskenlight Coast (33.3% drop rate). Dexterity +5, Elemental resistance +3%, +25 Health. "It seems that Vitheo himself has heard of this bow" Costs 400g from vendors.
