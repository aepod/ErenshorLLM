# Relaxation

## Relaxation

### Type

Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

**Relaxation** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

This note can be found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

Relaxation talks about how Bellwain would leave Bellsie's drink on her seat near the campfire.

Relaxation

Bellsie always came here to "wash off the smoke" after our drinks at the pergola campfire.

Thing is, she always would forget her drink and made me come back to the bar to get it for her. I'd leave it on her chair if she was off in the gardens.

Truly a nuisance in the moment, but a task I miss dearly now.

Using the clues give, find  Bellsie's Wine1 and deliver it to the empty seat near the campfire behind the pergola to find  Afternoon on the Isle.
