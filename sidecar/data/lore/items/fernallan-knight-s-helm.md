Fernallan Knight's Helm is a solid upgrade head piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Maple (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (50.0% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+12), Magic resistance +1%, Void resistance +2%. "You've never heard of Fernallan Knights in literature, but this helm proves they existed" Costs 2500g from vendors.
