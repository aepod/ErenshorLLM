Demon Claw is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Nightmare (Boss) in Shivering Step (25.0% drop rate). Also drops from Gasper and Shadow of Sleep. Strength +3, 15 damage. Special: 2% chance on ATTACK:. "They carry a curse that tugs at your very being" Costs 4000g from vendors.
