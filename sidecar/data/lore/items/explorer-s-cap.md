Explorer's Cap is a mid-level head piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Goliath (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (20.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "The bugs barely bite you with this on." Costs 1500g from vendors.
