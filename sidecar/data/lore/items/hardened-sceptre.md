Hardened Sceptre is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Molorai Archmage in Old Krakengard (47.1% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Archmage. Intelligence +6, Magic resistance +5%, Elemental resistance +5%. Special: 2% chance on ATTACK:. "Resist Type: Void" Costs 1785g from vendors.
