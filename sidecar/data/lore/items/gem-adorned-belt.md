Gem Adorned Belt is a endgame waist \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Brood Mother (Boss) in Prielian Cascade (66.7% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+15), Agility +15, Magic resistance +3%. "It draws attention to your pants in an uncomfortable way" Costs 2105g from vendors.
