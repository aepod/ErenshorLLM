Molorai Parrying Dagger is a mid-level gear piece. Dropped by Overseer Laseal (Boss) in The Duskenlight Coast (50.0% drop rate). Strength +8, Void resistance +4%, +100 Health. "Almost as good as a shield except... sometimes you hurt yourself on this thing." Costs 1750g from vendors.
