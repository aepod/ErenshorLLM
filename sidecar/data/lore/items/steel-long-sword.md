Steel Long Sword is a starter weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Lesser Avatar of Sivakaya in The Bonepits (20.5% drop rate). 12 damage. "It's a little heavy, but pretty sharp." Costs 55g from vendors.
