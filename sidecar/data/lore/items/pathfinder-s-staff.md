Pathfinder's Staff is a mid-level gear piece. Dropped by Elizah Vernor (8.6% drop rate). Also drops from Thella Steepleton and Molorai Militia Druid. Strong Wisdom (+14), Intelligence +10, Magic resistance +5%. "Wielded by druids, it's said to light the way to fortune." Costs 350g from vendors.
