Petrified Club is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Tek-Nu Dah (Boss) in Rottenfoot (51.9% drop rate). Also drops from A Rottenfoot Warrior and A Rottenfoot Witchdoctor. Strength +5, 13 damage. Special: 15% chance on ATTACK:. "Resist Type: Magic" Costs 650g from vendors.
