Hero Bracer is a solid upgrade bracer piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Loomingwood Minotaur Hero (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (35.1% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Magic resistance +3%. "Worn by minotaurs who have achieved the rank of "Hero"." Costs 775g from vendors.
