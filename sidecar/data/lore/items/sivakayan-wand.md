Sivakayan Wand is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Shrouded Sivakayan (2.5% drop rate). Also drops from Sivakayan Doomshade and Sivakayan High Shadow. Strong Intelligence (+11), Wisdom +11, Magic resistance +5%. Special: 1% chance on ATTACK:. "Its ruby gemstone hums with hatred" Costs 9550g from vendors.
