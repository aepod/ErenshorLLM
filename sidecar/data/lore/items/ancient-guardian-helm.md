Ancient Guardian Helm is a mid-level head piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Worn Ancient Guardian in Faerie's Brake (1.6% drop rate). Strength +8, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Nobody wins in a headbutt... except the person wearing this." Costs 1465g from vendors.
