Plain Necklace is a starter neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Brackish Croc Whelp (Boss) in Stowaway's Step (100.0% drop rate). +3 Health. "This necklace is very unremarkable." Costs 5g from vendors.
