Oslind's Eviscerator is a endgame weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Prince Oslind (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (33.3% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+12), Elemental resistance +3%, Void resistance +3%. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "It rips and tears without effort" Costs 21000g from vendors.
