Cryptid Claws is a starter weapon piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (30+) in Treasure Hunting (12.8% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+15), +275 Health, 22 damage. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "The origin of these claws is best left to history" Costs 47500g from vendors.
