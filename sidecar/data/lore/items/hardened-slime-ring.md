Hardened Slime Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gloopa (Quarter) (38.5% drop rate). Also drops from Gloopa (Quarter) and A Subterranean Goop. Intelligence +5, Poison resistance +4%, Void resistance +4%. "The stone is still slimey." Costs 1225g from vendors.
