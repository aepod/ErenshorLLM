Songmaker is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral Torturer (Boss) in Lost Cellar (33.3% drop rate). Charisma +7, Void resistance +9%, +80 Health. Special: 15% chance on ATTACK:. "The torturer claimed to be able to make his victims sing in whatever tune he wanted." Costs 2100g from vendors.
