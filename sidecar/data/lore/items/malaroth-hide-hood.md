Malaroth Hide Hood is a solid upgrade head \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Vizijivi (Boss) in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (50.0% drop rate). Intelligence +8, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%. "Hastily crafted from the skin of the beast" Costs 1985g from vendors.
