Braxonian Mace is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Braxonian High Guard in Fallen Braxonia (4.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Honor Guard and Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire). Dexterity +8, Void resistance +9%, 15 damage. Special: 8% chance on ATTACK:. "A mace made of Braxonian Steel" Costs 11000g from vendors.
