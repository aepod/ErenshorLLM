World Builder is a solid upgrade gear piece for Druids, Paladins, and Reavers. Dropped by Bloom Preserver in Blooming Sepulcher (1.7% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, +95 Health. Special: 15% chance on ATTACK:. "Said to have been used by Fernalla's followers to construct the first villages of Erenshor" Costs 8500g from vendors.
