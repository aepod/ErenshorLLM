Dreamy Sleeves is a endgame arm piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Hypnic (Boss) in Mysterious Portal (3) (33.3% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+15), Dexterity +10, Void resistance +4%. "The metal is almost ethereal, it feels weightless" Costs 15545g from vendors.
