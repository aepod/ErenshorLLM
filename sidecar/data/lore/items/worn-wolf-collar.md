Worn Wolf Collar is a starter neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Alpha Wolf in Faerie's Brake (39.3% drop rate). Also drops from Ancient Canine. Strength +3, Poison resistance +3%, Elemental resistance +1%. "Once a sign of domestication, it's now a sign of power." Costs 25g from vendors.
