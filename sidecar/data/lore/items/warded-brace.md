Warded Brace is a endgame bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Wardwarped Alpha (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (33.3% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+11), Wisdom +11, +55 Health. Special: 20% chance on CAST:. "It feels as though fingers are gripping your wrist, leading you onward" Costs 16550g from vendors.
