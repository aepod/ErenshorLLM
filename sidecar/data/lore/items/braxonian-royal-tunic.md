Braxonian Royal Tunic is a endgame chest piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (12.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Massive Intelligence (+20), Wisdom +20, Magic resistance +5%. "Legendary Garb of Brax's Arcanists" Costs 15000g from vendors.
