Thorned Branch is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Elder Guardian in Blooming Sepulcher (1.6% drop rate). Strength +8, 12 damage. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "The thorns are plentiful and needle-sharp" Costs 3200g from vendors.
