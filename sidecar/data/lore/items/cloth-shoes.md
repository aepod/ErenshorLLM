Cloth Shoes is a solid upgrade foot piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Kio the Darkbringer (Boss) in Shivering Step (64.4% drop rate). Also drops from Cloaked Shadow and Neophyte Necromancer. "Thin Cloth Shoes" Costs 2g from vendors.
