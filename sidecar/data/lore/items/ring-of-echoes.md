Ring of Echoes is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Hypnic (Boss) in Mysterious Portal (3) (33.3% drop rate). Dexterity +5, +5 Health. "Suddenly, you feel like you get two tries at everything" Costs 16500g from vendors.
