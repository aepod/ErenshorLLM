Shivering Leather Boots is a mid-level foot piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Crypt Keeper in Stowaway's Step (6.8% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Militia Druid. Endurance +3. "Made of some oddly textured skin that you've never seen before" Costs 2g from vendors.
