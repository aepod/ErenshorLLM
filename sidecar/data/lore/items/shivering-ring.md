Shivering Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Shadow of Sleep in Shivering Step (9.4% drop rate). Intelligence +5, +100 Health. "The ring trembles and clutches your finger as though it truly feels fear" Costs 1500g from vendors.
