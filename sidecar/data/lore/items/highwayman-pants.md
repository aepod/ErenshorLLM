Highwayman Pants is a mid-level leg piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Highwayman Marauder in Fernalla's Revival Plains (3.0% drop rate). Also drops from A Highwayman Raider. Charisma +5, Poison resistance +10%, Void resistance +10%. "One leg at a time... your pants don't define you." Costs 785g from vendors.
