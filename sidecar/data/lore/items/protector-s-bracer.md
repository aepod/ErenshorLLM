Protector's Bracer is a solid upgrade bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Warded Shadow (Boss) in Shivering Tomb (2) (38.1% drop rate). Endurance +8, Magic resistance +2%, +65 Health. Special: 8% chance on CAST:. "Those around you feel comforted by its presence" Costs 2000g from vendors.
