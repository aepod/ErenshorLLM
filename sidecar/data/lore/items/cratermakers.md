Cratermakers is a solid upgrade foot \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Granitus (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (20.0% drop rate). Strength +7, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +1%. "You step heavily and confidently" Costs 4000g from vendors.
