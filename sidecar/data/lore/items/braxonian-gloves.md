Braxonian Gloves is a endgame hand piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (24.0% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Strong Intelligence (+15), Wisdom +10, Elemental resistance +3%. "Legendary Garb of Brax's Arcanists" Costs 9500g from vendors.
