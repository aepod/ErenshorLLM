Ancient Guardian Plate is a mid-level chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Worn Ancient Guardian in Faerie's Brake (1.6% drop rate). Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Covered in the hints of worn etchings" Costs 3550g from vendors.
