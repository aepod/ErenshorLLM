Eternal Diadem is a endgame head \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Candlekeeper (Boss) (50.0% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+20), Intelligence +15, Magic resistance +3%. "The origins of this piece are a mystery." Costs 1950g from vendors.
