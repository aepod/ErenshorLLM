Decayed Amulet is a mid-level neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by An Apparition of Sivakaya (Boss) in The Bonepits (25.0% drop rate). Also drops from Risen Druid. Strength +3, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "You can feel an ancient power flowing through the metals." Costs 255g from vendors.
