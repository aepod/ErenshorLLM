Sash of the Lost Guard is a mid-level waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral King's Guard (Boss) in Lost Cellar (25.0% drop rate). Agility +8, +55 Health. "Worn by the Royal Azure Guard of the past" Costs 2235g from vendors.
