Sivakayan Plate is a solid upgrade chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gherist Morthario (Boss) in Rottenfoot (27.6% drop rate). Also drops from Bowmistress of Sivakaya and Reaver of Sivakaya. Strong Strength (+12), Endurance +12, Magic resistance +5%. "Worn by Sivakayan Soldiers" Costs 4000g from vendors.
