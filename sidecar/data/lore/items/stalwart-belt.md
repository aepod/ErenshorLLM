Stalwart Belt is a mid-level waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Grizzlepaw (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (10.8% drop rate). Strength +5, Poison resistance +3%, +25 Health. "Long ago, Grizzlepaw ate someone wearing this belt." Costs 100g from vendors.
