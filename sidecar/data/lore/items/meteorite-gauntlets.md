Meteorite Gauntlets is a solid upgrade hand \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, and Reavers. Dropped by Living Meteorite in Soluna's Landing (13.1% drop rate). Strength +8, Elemental resistance +2%, Void resistance +2%. "Made of a strange, burnt metal" Costs 1985g from vendors.
