Armbands of Order is a starter arm piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (30+) in Treasure Hunting (12.8% drop rate). Massive Dexterity (+20), Agility +20, Magic resistance +2%. "Some dark brotherhood from long ago would have worn these" Costs 16500g from vendors.
