Illusionist Trousers is a endgame leg piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Molorai Acquirer (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (66.7% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+15), Wisdom +15, Magic resistance +3%. "There are hidden pockets everywhere." Costs 18545g from vendors.
