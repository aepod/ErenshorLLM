Malaroth Scale Chestguard is a endgame chest piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Brood Mother (Boss) in Prielian Cascade (33.3% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+15), Agility +15, Magic resistance +4%. "Light and rugged" Costs 12550g from vendors.
