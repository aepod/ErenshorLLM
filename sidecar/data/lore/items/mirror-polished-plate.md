Mirror Polished Plate is a mid-level chest piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Stoneman Diamondine (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (33.3% drop rate). Strength +5, Magic resistance +10%, Poison resistance +10%. Costs 29550g from vendors.
