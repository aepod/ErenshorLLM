Ring of The Mystics is a consumable item. Dropped by Rosle of the Spirits (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (66.7% drop rate). Also drops from Agsha of the Soil. Wisdom +6, +25 Health. "Said to soothe even the deepest of wounds" Costs 20g from vendors.
