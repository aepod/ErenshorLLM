Woodland Gauntlets is a endgame hand \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sylvan Giant in Fernalla's Revival Plains (50.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Endurance +10, Poison resistance +3%. "Empowered by the purity of undisturbed nature." Costs 7500g from vendors.
