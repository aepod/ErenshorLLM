Sea Giant's Cutlass is a mid-level gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by The Lost Sea Giant King (Boss) in Vitheo's Watch (30.1% drop rate). Also drops from A Lost Sea Giant. Strength +5, Elemental resistance +5%, 20 damage. "The giants made this thing look light. It certainly is not light." Costs 350g from vendors.
