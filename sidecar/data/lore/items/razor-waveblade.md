Razor Waveblade is a mid-level weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Bodyguard in Old Krakengard (5.4% drop rate). Dexterity +8, 17 damage. "This thing looks like it would really hurt to get hit with." Costs 1000g from vendors.
