Call To Arms is a mid-level back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Overseer Orlok (Boss) in Old Krakengard (51.6% drop rate). Intelligence +4, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "It's probably even more inspiring to the guy behind you than it is to you." Costs 455g from vendors.
