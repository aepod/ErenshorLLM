Adorned Mitts is a mid-level hand \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Prioress in Blacksalt Strand (7.6% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Void resistance +2%. "Simple leathers adorned with rare runes." Costs 2150g from vendors.
