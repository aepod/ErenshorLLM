Lifeloop is a solid upgrade ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher (25.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Hardiness is a gift" Costs 1655g from vendors.
