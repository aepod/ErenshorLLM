Flowing Blight Silk Sash is a endgame waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Parzgil (Boss) in The Blight (33.3% drop rate). Strength +5, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Effect applied once item is equipped." Costs 3955g from vendors.
