Stabilizing Belt is a solid upgrade waist piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Oarik the Failure (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (66.7% drop rate). Strength +5, +200 Health. "It'll keep you on your feet even after the most devastating blows land." Costs 1800g from vendors.
