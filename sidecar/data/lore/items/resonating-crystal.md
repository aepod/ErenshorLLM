Resonating Crystal is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Granitus (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (20.0% drop rate). Intelligence +5, Void resistance +10%, +300 Health. "Things look funny if you look through it like a telescope" Costs 3500g from vendors.
