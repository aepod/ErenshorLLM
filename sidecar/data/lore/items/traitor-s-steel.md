Traitor's Steel is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Elwio the Traitor (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (33.3% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+10), Void resistance +9%, +50 Health. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "Used by Elwio, Traitor to Vitheo" Costs 14500g from vendors.
