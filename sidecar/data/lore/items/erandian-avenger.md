Erandian Avenger is a endgame gear piece for Druids, Paladins, and Reavers. Dropped by Raval, The First Paladin (Boss) in Soluna's Landing (20.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Wisdom +15, Magic resistance +2%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "This shield seems to convey a will to it's holder - To stand against seemingly impossible odds and prevail." Costs 5855g from vendors.
