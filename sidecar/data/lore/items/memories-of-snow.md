Memories of Snow is a starter weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Dream Invader (25.0% drop rate). Also drops from Invader of Dreams. Massive Intelligence (+25), 1 damage. Special: 20% chance on ATTACK:. "It emits the scent of a cold snowy night" Costs 4565g from vendors.
