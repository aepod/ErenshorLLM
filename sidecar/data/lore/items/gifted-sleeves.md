Gifted Sleeves is a solid upgrade arm piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Julianna Priel (Boss) in Prielian Cascade (50.0% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+20), Wisdom +15, Poison resistance +5%. "Sleeves that are bestowed by Savannah Priel upon those with the gift of magic" Costs 2455g from vendors.
