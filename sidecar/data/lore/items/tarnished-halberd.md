Tarnished Halberd is a mid-level gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Spectral Castle Guard in Lost Cellar (13.4% drop rate). Also drops from Spectral King's Guard and Spectral Guard. Strong Strength (+12), Endurance +12, +25 Health. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "The aged metal never loses its edge, but it feels unnaturally light to swing." Costs 750g from vendors.
