Bandit's Mask is a mid-level head piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Spiritist (Boss) in Old Krakengard (7.8% drop rate). Also drops from Overseer Orlok. Charisma +4, Elemental resistance +3%. "You don't even recognize yourself." Costs 1245g from vendors.
