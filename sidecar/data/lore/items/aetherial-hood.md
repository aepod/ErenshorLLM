Aetherial Hood is a solid upgrade head \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aetherfiend in Loomingwood Forest (12.1% drop rate). Also drops from Shadow Man. Strong Strength (+10), Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "It's completely weightless" Costs 2300g from vendors.
