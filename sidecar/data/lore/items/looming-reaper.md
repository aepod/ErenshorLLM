Looming Reaper is a solid upgrade weapon piece. Dropped by The Gardener of Loomingwood (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (25.0% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+20), Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +6%. "Harvester of the Loomingwood forest, its blade is caked with strange sap" Costs 3300g from vendors.
