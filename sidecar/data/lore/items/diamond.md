Diamond is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (87.1% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice) and Diamond Hound. Costs 1000g from vendors.
