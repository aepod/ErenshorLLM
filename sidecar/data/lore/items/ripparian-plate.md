Ripparian Plate is a mid-level chest piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Armorer Nell (3.4% drop rate). Also drops from Captain Ovid and Celeso the Historian. Massive Strength (+20), Endurance +20, Magic resistance +8%. "Worn by the soldiers of Ripper" Costs 9500g from vendors.
