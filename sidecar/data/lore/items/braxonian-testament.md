Braxonian Testament is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Historian of Braxonia (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (50.0% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+20), Charisma +10. "The cryptic runes contain secrets you will never know." Costs 19000g from vendors.
