Inspiration is a mid-level ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lazalith, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (14.1% drop rate). Agility +2, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Effect applied once item is equipped." Costs 2100g from vendors.
