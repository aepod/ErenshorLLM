Searing Wristwrap is a solid upgrade bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Overseer Refleigh (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (14.5% drop rate). Dexterity +5. "The leather is baked by what must have been an immense heat." Costs 1915g from vendors.
