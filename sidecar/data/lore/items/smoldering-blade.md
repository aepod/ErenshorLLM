Smoldering Blade is a solid upgrade gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Blazefiend (Boss) (25.0% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Endurance +20, +25 Health. Special: 33% chance on ATTACK:. "This blade has seen flames, and still holds their heat." Costs 3120g from vendors.
