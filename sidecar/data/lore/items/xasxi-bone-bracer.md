Xasxi Bone Bracer is a solid upgrade bracer piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Xasxi (Boss) in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (66.7% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "A hollowed bone of a wild beast" Costs 2478g from vendors.
