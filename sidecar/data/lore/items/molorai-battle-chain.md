Molorai Battle Chain is a mid-level chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Outrider in The Duskenlight Coast (17.9% drop rate). Also drops from Overseer Refleigh and Molorai Specialist. Strong Dexterity (+15), Magic resistance +10%, Poison resistance +10%. "Standard Issue Molarai Chain" Costs 1955g from vendors.
