Ocean Aged Ring is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Pamia the Coward (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (66.7% drop rate). Intelligence +6, Magic resistance +3%, Elemental resistance +3%. "It spent centuries beneath the waves, waiting to be recovered" Costs 6000g from vendors.
