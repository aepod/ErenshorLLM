Rotting Metal Shield is a starter gear piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Skeleton Captain (Boss) in The Bonepits (9.7% drop rate). Strength +3, Void resistance +1%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "A standard metal shield that has seen better days." Costs 350g from vendors.
