Spinesnap Hide Hood is a solid upgrade head \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Spinesnap Gorilla in Mysterious Portal (3) (7.6% drop rate). Strong Agility (+15), Wisdom +15, Elemental resistance +5%. "Made from a creature who lives in a dream" Costs 1950g from vendors.
