Tattered Leather Tunic is a starter chest piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Plaxitheris (Boss) in Stowaway's Step (19.4% drop rate). Also drops from Grassland Ogre Gatherer and A Seafiend. "It's brittle, but better than nothing at all." Costs 5g from vendors.
