Pearlescent Kelp Totem is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Algaeth Bilgeback (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (14.3% drop rate). Intelligence +9, Magic resistance +4%, Elemental resistance +4%. "Inscribed on it is a name: "Farfathom"" Costs 1700g from vendors.
