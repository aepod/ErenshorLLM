Belt of Ages is a solid upgrade waist \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Protected Memory in Blooming Sepulcher (25.0% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+14), +195 Health. "This belt has been worn throughout time by powerful beings" Costs 14000g from vendors.
