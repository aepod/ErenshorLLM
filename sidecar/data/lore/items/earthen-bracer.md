Earthen Bracer is a mid-level bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Druo the Reborn (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (43.4% drop rate). Magic resistance +5%, Poison resistance +5%, Elemental resistance +5%. "Made of a strange combination of vines and clay." Costs 755g from vendors.
