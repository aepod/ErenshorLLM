Backplate is a mid-level back \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Bassalt Vallano (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (50.0% drop rate). Strength +5, +15 Health. "A simple sheet of metal to protect your back" Costs 955g from vendors.
