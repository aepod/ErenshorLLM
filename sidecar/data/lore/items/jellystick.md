Jellystick is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ragewhip (Boss) in Rottenfoot (50.0% drop rate). Also drops from Swamp Nettle. Dexterity +6. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "The Jelly's tentacles stiffen when they dry." Costs 1200g from vendors.
