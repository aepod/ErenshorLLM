Ripparian Axe is a starter gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Ripper DeMerth (12.1% drop rate). Also drops from Ripper Soldier and Armorer Nell. Strength +7, +65 Health, 30 damage. "A standard Ripparian Axe" Costs 3299g from vendors.
