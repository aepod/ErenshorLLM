Twilight Shield is a starter gear piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Lost Sea Giant in Vitheo's Watch (13.7% drop rate). Also drops from A Lost Sea Giant. Strength +3. Special: 45% chance on BASH:. "Staring at it gives a feeling of loneliness." Costs 350g from vendors.
