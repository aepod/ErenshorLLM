Sivakaya's Touch is a best-in-slot ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Eldoth Molorai (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (25.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Endurance +15, Void resistance +5%. "This ring may have at one point been worn by Sivakaya herself" Costs 15000g from vendors.
