Elderstone Ring is a solid upgrade ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Slumbering Queen (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (52.3% drop rate). Magic resistance +10%, Poison resistance +10%, Elemental resistance +10%. "Made from pure Elderstone, its power is immense." Costs 1655g from vendors.
