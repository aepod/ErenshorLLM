Chitin Protector is a solid upgrade chest piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Shelled Monstrosity (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (33.3% drop rate). Strong Endurance (+10), Wisdom +10, Poison resistance +2%. "It only took a little modification to be pretty comfortable." Costs 6500g from vendors.
