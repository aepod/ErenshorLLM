Sivakayan Breeches is a solid upgrade leg piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sivakayan Voidweaver in Silkengrass Meadowlands (7.4% drop rate). Also drops from Sivakayan Devoted and Sivakayan Faithbound. Strong Agility (+10), Intelligence +10, Magic resistance +10%. "They flow with your every move." Costs 3100g from vendors.
