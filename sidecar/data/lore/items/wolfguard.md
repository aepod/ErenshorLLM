Wolfguard is a starter bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Battlefist (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (33.3% drop rate). Strength +2, +30 Health. "It's supposed to guard against bites, but it's pretty flimsy." Costs 20g from vendors.
