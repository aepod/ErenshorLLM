Workworn Bracer is a mid-level bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral Torturer (Boss) in Lost Cellar (33.3% drop rate). Strength +3, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Worn by the very men who built Port Azure" Costs 2000g from vendors.
