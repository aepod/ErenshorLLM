Reaver's Steel is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Reaver of Sivakaya (6.8% drop rate). Massive Strength (+35), Endurance +15, Magic resistance +2%. Special: 33% chance on ATTACK:. "Created by Sivakaya herself, and gifted to the First Reaver" Costs 65000g from vendors.
