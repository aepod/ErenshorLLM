Cold Steel Helm is a mid-level head piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Molorai Militia Warrior in Old Krakengard (5.1% drop rate). Wisdom +2, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +2%. "Wearing it gives visions of a winter night" Costs 30g from vendors.
