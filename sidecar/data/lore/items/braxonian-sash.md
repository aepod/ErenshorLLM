Braxonian Sash is a solid upgrade waist piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Apha, Priest of Brax (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (67.9% drop rate). Also drops from Upha, Priest of Brax. Strong Endurance (+10), Elemental resistance +2%, +200 Health. "Meant to bolster his fragile followers" Costs 1800g from vendors.
