Compression Wraps is a starter leg piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Sorrowful Spirit in Island Tomb (5.1% drop rate). Agility +4, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "They make a weird noise when you walk but they feel great." Costs 55g from vendors.
