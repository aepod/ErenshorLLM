Weak Wand is a starter weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Mourning (Boss) in Island Tomb (41.0% drop rate). Also drops from Islander Bandit. Charisma +5, 4 damage. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Resist Type: Magic" Costs 200g from vendors.
