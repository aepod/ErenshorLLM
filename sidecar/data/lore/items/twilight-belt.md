Twilight Belt is a mid-level waist piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Restless Thoughts (Boss) in Mysterious Portal (1) (33.3% drop rate). Dexterity +4, Void resistance +3%, +180 Health. "Reminds you of fading summer days" Costs 1800g from vendors.
