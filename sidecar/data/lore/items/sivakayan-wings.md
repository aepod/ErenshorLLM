Sivakayan Wings is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Reaver of Sivakaya (10.8% drop rate). Also drops from Assassin of Sivakaya and Grand Arcanist of Sivakaya. Massive Strength (+25), Dexterity +25, Magic resistance +5%. "Your movements appear fluid and effortless" Costs 17550g from vendors.
