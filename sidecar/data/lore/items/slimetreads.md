Slimetreads is a starter foot \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gloopa (Quarter) (38.5% drop rate). Strength +6, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +2%. "The grip on these boots is otherwordly." Costs 2300g from vendors.
