Spectral Dagger is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by The Crypt Guardian (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (14.8% drop rate). Also drops from Vithean Blademaster and Vithean Betrayer. Strength +2, 12 damage. "You cannot feel it in your hand, but somehow its strikes land true." Costs 2120g from vendors.
