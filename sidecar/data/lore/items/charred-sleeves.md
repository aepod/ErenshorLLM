Charred Sleeves is a solid upgrade arm \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Stoneman Colossus (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (66.7% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Dexterity +10, Magic resistance +1%. "Made of stone seared smooth by extreme heat" Costs 2000g from vendors.
