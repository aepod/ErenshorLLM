Statuesque Greaves is a best-in-slot leg piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Blight Wyrm (Boss) in The Blight (33.3% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Endurance +15, Poison resistance +4%. "They give purpose to each step, seemingly leading you into battle on their own accord" Costs 22500g from vendors.
