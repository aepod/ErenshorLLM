Ring of the Pious is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Assassin of Sivakaya (6.5% drop rate). Also drops from Grand Arcanist of Sivakaya and High Paladin of Sivakaya. Massive Strength (+25), Wisdom +25, +85 Health. "A sign of true faith to Sivakaya and her dark gift" Costs 12000g from vendors.
