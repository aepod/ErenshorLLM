Arboreal Cape is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sylvan Giant in Fernalla's Revival Plains (50.0% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+10), Agility +10, +200 Health. "Woven from leaves and twigs" Costs 7550g from vendors.
