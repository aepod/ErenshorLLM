Rotten Chain Tunic is a mid-level chest piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gruhglor (Boss) in Fernalla's Revival Plains (19.5% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Dexterity +15, Magic resistance +7%. "Worn by disciples of Sivakaya" Costs 1565g from vendors.
