Intricate Sleeves is a solid upgrade arm \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Balaste the Fallen (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (66.7% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+12), Wisdom +12, +25 Health. "Worn by the Virophan upper class" Costs 2750g from vendors.
