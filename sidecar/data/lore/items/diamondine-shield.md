Diamondine Shield is a mid-level gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Stoneman Diamondine (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (66.7% drop rate). Strong Endurance (+10), Intelligence +10, Magic resistance +1%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "The shine on this shield could blind your opponent" Costs 1200g from vendors.
