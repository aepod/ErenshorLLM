Reinforced Scale Belt is a mid-level waist piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Deepling Brute in The Elderstone Mines (10.2% drop rate). Also drops from A Nagalok. Magic resistance +7%, Poison resistance +7%, Elemental resistance +7%. "Made from hardened scales." Costs 1700g from vendors.
