Parallax, Key to the Void is a best-in-slot gear piece. Dropped by Astra, Rogue of the Stars (Boss) in Soluna's Landing (16.7% drop rate). Massive Strength (+35), Dexterity +30, Magic resistance +5%. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "The blade is said to be able to cut tears in the fabric of reality, exposing the void that lurks behind it." Costs 48000g from vendors.
