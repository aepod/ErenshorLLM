Shadow Gloves is a solid upgrade hand \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sivakayan Spymaster (Boss) in Ripper's Keep (66.7% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+15), Strength +10, Void resistance +3%. "They're not solid until you touch something" Costs 17500g from vendors.
