Necromantic Bow is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Faceless Necromancer in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (7.3% drop rate). Massive Dexterity (+25), Agility +25, Void resistance +3%. Special: 9% chance on ATTACK:. "It's not clear what a necromancer would want from this bow" Costs 6500g from vendors.
