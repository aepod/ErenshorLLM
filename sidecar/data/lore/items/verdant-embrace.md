Verdant Embrace is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Fallen Fernalla (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (35.6% drop rate). Also drops from Fallen Fernalla. Massive Strength (+20), Intelligence +20, Void resistance +10%. "A lingering bit of Fernalla's goodwill can be felt in its threads" Costs 27500g from vendors.
