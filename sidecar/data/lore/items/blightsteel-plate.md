Blightsteel Plate is a endgame chest piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Harbinger of Corruption (Boss) in The Blight (33.3% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +2%. "Pitted and Rotting, its glory must once have been immeasurable" Costs 9500g from vendors.
