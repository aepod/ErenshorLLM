Abyssal Plate is a endgame chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sivakayan Voidmaster (4.5% drop rate). Massive Endurance (+30), Strength +25, Magic resistance +3%. "Physical blows are seemingly lost this metal, absorbed into nothingness" Costs 38000g from vendors.
