### Effects

### Casting Time

Instant

### Duration

10

### Cooldown

12s

## Acquisition

### Taught by

Fishing Pole

# Overview

Fishing is an activity you can unlock by obtaining a  Fishing Pole (In Stowaway's Step this is purchasable from from Treven Pines.)

**How to Fish:**

1. Equip the pole and face a body of water,
2. Right click the fishing pole to cast your line
3. Wait ~10 seconds to finish, no inputs required.
4. You may catch a fish (or possibly something else!)

You can also cast with a hotkey if you equip the rod and then drag and drop it from the main hand to the action bar.
