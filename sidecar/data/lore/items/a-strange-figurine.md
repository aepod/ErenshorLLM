## A Strange Figurine

### Type

Summoning Item, Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

Recovering the Nighthollow Candle

### Crafting recipe

1x  Mold: A Strange Figurine
1x  Sivakayan Ore
1x  Braxonian Ore
1x  Remnants of a Daydream
1x  Remnants of a Nightmare

Used to summon Candlekeeper at the chess board in the Braxonian Desert. Once it is consumed it turns back into Mold: A Strange Figurine.

A Strange Figurine

A small statuette with a worn felt bottom as though its been slid to and fro.

The top is worn where it has been gripped, despite having just been forged anew.
