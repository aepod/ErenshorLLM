## Guide of Offering

### Type

### Reward from

Wishlan's Hope Pt. 2

Guide of Offering

The faithful kneel before the flame, laying a stone paired with a token of his Lover, its surface warm with ancient memory. Beside it, a tool or token of their choosing—a silent request for favor.

One paired stone speaks a whisper. Two, a plea. Three, a chorus the Elder God cannot ignore. A request for favor is essential, but granting may not be.
