Frozen Lava Ring is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Historian of Braxonia (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (50.0% drop rate). Strong Agility (+10), Wisdom +10, Elemental resistance +6%. "Its hot and cold at the same time" Costs 10250g from vendors.
