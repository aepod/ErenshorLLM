Granite Belt is a solid upgrade waist piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Granitus (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (20.0% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Endurance +15, Magic resistance +1%. "You'd think a belt would help hold your pants up better than this does." Costs 3955g from vendors.
