Bracelet of the Vessel is a best-in-slot bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Vessel Siraethe (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (28.8% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+13), Wisdom +13, Magic resistance +3%. Special: 10% chance on CAST:. "It emits an aura of hope and of fear" Costs 17855g from vendors.
