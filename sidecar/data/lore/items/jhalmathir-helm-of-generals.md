Jhalmathir, Helm of Generals is a solid upgrade head \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Evadne the Corrupted (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (29.2% drop rate). Strength +8, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +3%. "Worn by the famed Generals of Brax" Costs 2300g from vendors.
