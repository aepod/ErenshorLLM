Ancient Guardian Gloves is a mid-level hand piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Worn Ancient Guardian in Faerie's Brake (1.6% drop rate). "Some of the fingers won't bend anymore" Costs 1200g from vendors.
