Shackle of Bidding is a consumable item. Dropped by Morian the Ascended (Boss) in Silkengrass Meadowlands (66.7% drop rate). Strength +2, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Activatable: Summon Fallen Skeleton" Costs 2200g from vendors.
