Fungal Scab Cape is a mid-level back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Druo the Reborn (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (14.8% drop rate). Strength +4, Magic resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +1%. "Pieces flake away as you move, refreshing the horrid aroma." Costs 2000g from vendors.
