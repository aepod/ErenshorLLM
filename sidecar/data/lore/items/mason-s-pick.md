Mason's Pick is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Mason (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (33.3% drop rate). 1 damage. "A specialized mining pick" Costs 2000g from vendors.
