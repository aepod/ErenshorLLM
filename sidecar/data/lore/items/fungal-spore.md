Fungal Spore is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Abyssal Lord (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (86.5% drop rate). Also drops from A Grassland Ogre Plaguebringer and Abyssal Fungus Protector. Costs 35g from vendors.
