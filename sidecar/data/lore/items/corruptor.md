Corruptor is a best-in-slot weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Azynthian Keeper (Boss) in Secluded Sanctuary (33.3% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+40), Charisma +25, +400 Health. Special: 20% chance on ATTACK:. "It brings rot and death to all it touches" Costs 25000g from vendors.
