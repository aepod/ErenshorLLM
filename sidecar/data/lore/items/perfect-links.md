Perfect Links is a endgame bracer piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (50.0% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+12), Charisma +12, Void resistance +3%. "An incredible piece of craftsmanship" Costs 8000g from vendors.
