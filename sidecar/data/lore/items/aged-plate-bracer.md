Aged Plate Bracer is a mid-level bracer piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral Guard in Lost Cellar (3.1% drop rate). Also drops from Spectral High Guard and Spectral King's Guard. Dexterity +4, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +1%. "A very old plate Bracer" Costs 250g from vendors.
