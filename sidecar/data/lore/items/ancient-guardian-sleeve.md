Ancient Guardian Sleeve is a mid-level arm piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Worn Ancient Guardian in Faerie's Brake (1.6% drop rate). Strength +5, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "Simply unbreakable" Costs 2999g from vendors.
