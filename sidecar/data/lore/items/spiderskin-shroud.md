Spiderskin Shroud is a solid upgrade head piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Soul Linked Spider in Shivering Tomb (2) (50.0% drop rate). Also drops from Soul Linked Spider and Soul Linked Spider. Strong Strength (+10), Wisdom +10, Poison resistance +8%. "The skin is silky smooth and oddly comfortable" Costs 6585g from vendors.
