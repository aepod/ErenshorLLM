Ring of the Old is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Darkwarden Awxa (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (66.7% drop rate). Strength +8, +60 Health. "A ring that represents lost rituals" Costs 9500g from vendors.
