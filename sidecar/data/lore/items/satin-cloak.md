Satin Cloak is a starter back piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Zashlyn Bloodbane (Boss) in The Bonepits (5.8% drop rate). Agility +2, +5 Health. "A flowing Satin Cloak - looks like it came out of a coffin." Costs 100g from vendors.
