Digested Gloves is a solid upgrade hand \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Vizijivi (Boss) in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (50.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+12), Wisdom +12, Void resistance +3%. "Some of the magic still lingers, so does some of the previous owner." Costs 2475g from vendors.
