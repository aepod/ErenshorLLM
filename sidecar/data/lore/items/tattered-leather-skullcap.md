Tattered Leather Skullcap is a starter head piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Plaxitheris (Boss) in Stowaway's Step (19.4% drop rate). Also drops from Grassland Ogre Gatherer and Grassland Ogre Hunter. "A well used leather cap." Costs 3g from vendors.
