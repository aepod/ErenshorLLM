Channeling Gloves is a mid-level hand \- relic item piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Ughl of the Skies (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (50.0% drop rate). Intelligence +3, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. Costs 350g from vendors.
