Fernallan Sceptre is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Maple (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (50.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+12), +25 Health. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Wielded by the Followers of Fernalla" Costs 2600g from vendors.
