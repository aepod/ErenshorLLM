**Breaded Island Fin** is a Consumable in Erenshor.

# Location

Breaded Island Fin is created as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island by trading the Baking Oven in the kitchen the correct ingredients, as listed in  The Recipe:

*  Island Fin
*  Bread
*  Brackfruit Juice
*  Coal

While the other three items can be found around the house, the Island Fin can be caught exclusively on Bellwain Island:

Location: Day Catch Rate: Night Catch Rate
Pond: 39.9%: 39.9%
Ocean: 23.8%: 19.0%

# Description

Crafting Breaded Island Fin rewards the player with 20 experience; this can be repeated infinitely.

After creating Breaded Island Fin, it can be delivered to the picnic blanket near the large tree at the back of the house. Upon doing so,  Mid Day on the Isle will appear.

Breaded Island Fin can also be consumed to activate  Nourished.
