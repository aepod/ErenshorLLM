Rotten Hands is a best-in-slot hand piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Blight Wyrm (Boss) in The Blight (33.3% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Dexterity +10, Void resistance +6%. "These aren't gloves. These are literally a rotting pair of hands" Costs 21250g from vendors.
