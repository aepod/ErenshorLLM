Braxonian Plate Boots is a endgame foot piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Braxonian Weapon Master (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (1.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Honor Guard and Braxonian High Guard. Strong Strength (+10), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +2%. "Legendary Plate Armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 11000g from vendors.
