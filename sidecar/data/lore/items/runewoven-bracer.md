Runewoven Bracer is a mid-level bracer \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Tek-Nu Dah (Boss) in Rottenfoot (51.9% drop rate). Also drops from A Rottenfoot Warrior and A Rottenfoot Witchdoctor. Intelligence +5. "It's made from ancient fabric, reclaimed from the sea long ago." Costs 365g from vendors.
