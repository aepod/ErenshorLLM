Stone Sword is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Granitus (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (20.0% drop rate). Also drops from Cinder and Stoneman. Wisdom +8, Void resistance +4%, 25 damage. "It feels ancient, but its edge is pristine" Costs 1900g from vendors.
