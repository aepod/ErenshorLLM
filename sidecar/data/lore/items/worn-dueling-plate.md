Worn Dueling Plate is a starter chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (20-30) in Treasure Hunting (25.6% drop rate). Strong Strength (+18), Dexterity +18, Magic resistance +3%. "It's worn by time, not by blades. Its wearer must have been skilled." Costs 11000g from vendors.
