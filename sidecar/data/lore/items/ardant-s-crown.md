Ardant's Crown is a endgame head \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Prince Ardant (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (33.3% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+20), Charisma +20, Magic resistance +3%. "Crown of Prince Ardant of Braxonia" Costs 1950g from vendors.
