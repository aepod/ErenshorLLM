Provoker is a solid upgrade gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, and Reavers. Dropped by Mephallo (Boss) in Shivering Step (66.7% drop rate). Strong Charisma (+12), Void resistance +8%, +120 Health. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "Something about this shield sends your opponent into a blind rage" Costs 2650g from vendors.
