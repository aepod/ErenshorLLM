### Type

### Related quest(s)

**The Flint** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

This note can be found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

The Flint talks about the  Rainbow Flint and how it can be used to light the torches near the statue on the island various colors.

The Flint

Bellsie always carried her flint, now it's been confined to my safe - for losing it would be be removing the last of her from this place.

If only I could recall the beautiful array of colors she would create at the Angel Torches, it would be like being with her one last time... I can feel a strong presence of her in that place and I long for it back.

The note instructs the player to figure out what color to light each torch surrounding the statue by collecting the other notes. Once they are are all lit the correct colors, the statue in the middle will rise up.
