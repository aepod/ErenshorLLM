Braxonian Wrap is a solid upgrade chest piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Apha, Priest of Brax (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (25.4% drop rate). Also drops from Upha, Priest of Brax. Intelligence +7, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "Worn by Braxonian wizards and priests" Costs 2200g from vendors.
