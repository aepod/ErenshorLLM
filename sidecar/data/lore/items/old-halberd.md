Old Halberd is a starter gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Sylvan Giant Runt (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (25.0% drop rate). 24 damage. "An Old Halberd, taken from a Giant" Costs 500g from vendors.
