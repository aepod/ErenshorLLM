Chewed Armwraps is a solid upgrade arm \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Dream (Boss) in Shivering Tomb (1) (50.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Poison resistance +1%, Void resistance +2%. "Torn by the teeth of an imaginary beast" Costs 7850g from vendors.
