Steadfast Girdle is a endgame waist \- relic item piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by The Abomination (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (50.0% drop rate). Strong Endurance (+12), Dexterity +12, Poison resistance +3%. "It does miracles for your posture" Costs 12500g from vendors.
