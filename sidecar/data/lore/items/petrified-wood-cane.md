Petrified Wood Cane is a endgame weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (50.0% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+12), +245 Health, 15 damage. "It's hard to tell what the original wood was." Costs 3550g from vendors.
