Brute's Chain Collar is a mid-level neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Goliath (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (20.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Elemental resistance +1%. "It's uncomfortable and that makes you very grouchy." Costs 1465g from vendors.
