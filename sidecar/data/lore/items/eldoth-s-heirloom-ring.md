Eldoth's Heirloom Ring is a best-in-slot ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Eldoth Molorai (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (50.0% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+25), Intelligence +15, Void resistance +5%. "Decreases your aggro generation by 60%" Costs 42505g from vendors.
