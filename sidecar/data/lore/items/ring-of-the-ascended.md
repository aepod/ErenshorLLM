Ring of the Ascended is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Apparition of Vitheo (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (35.1% drop rate). Massive Dexterity (+25), Strength +10, Void resistance +3%. "Vitheo's prized ring" Costs 12000g from vendors.
