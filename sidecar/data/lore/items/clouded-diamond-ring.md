Clouded Diamond Ring is a starter ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Available from vendors for 45g. Charisma +5, Magic resistance +10%, +5 Health. "A cloudy diamond still shines in stunning ways."
