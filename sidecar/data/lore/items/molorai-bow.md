Molorai Bow is a mid-level gear piece. Dropped by Molorai Militia Warrior in Old Krakengard (5.1% drop rate). Dexterity +6, 23 damage. "Standard issue to Molorai troops, though they never seem to be used" Costs 400g from vendors.
