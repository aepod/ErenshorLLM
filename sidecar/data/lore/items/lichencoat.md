Lichencoat is a endgame chest \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Abyssal Lord (Boss) in Abyssal Lake (50.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+15), Wisdom +15, Poison resistance +6%. "The lichens crawl across your skin, filling your wounds" Costs 19500g from vendors.
