End's Bane is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Tiallia Priel (Boss) in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (25.0% drop rate). Strong Strength (+10), Void resistance +4%, 12 damage. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Sword of Tiallia Priel" Costs 2455g from vendors.
