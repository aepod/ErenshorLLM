Feathery Vambraces is a solid upgrade arm piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aviak (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (50.0% drop rate). Strong Agility (+15), Endurance +10, Poison resistance +1%. "They make you feel like you could fly" Costs 11500g from vendors.
