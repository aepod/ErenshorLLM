Spell Scroll: Moonburst is a mid-level gear piece. Dropped by Molorai Captain Sigrid (Boss) in Old Krakengard (12.7% drop rate). Also drops from Gloopa (Quarter) and A Subterranean Goop. Costs 1655g from vendors.
