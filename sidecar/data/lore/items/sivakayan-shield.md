Sivakayan Shield is a solid upgrade gear piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gherist Morthario (Boss) in Rottenfoot (26.9% drop rate). Also drops from Reaver of Sivakaya and Assassin of Sivakaya. Strength +9, Magic resistance +3%, +200 Health. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "A standard shield traditionally used by Sivakayan Knights" Costs 2650g from vendors.
