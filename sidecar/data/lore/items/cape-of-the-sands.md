Cape of the Sands is a solid upgrade back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Overseer Refleigh (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (14.5% drop rate). Also drops from Molorai Archaeologist and Molorai Archaeologist. Magic resistance +6%, Poison resistance +8%, Elemental resistance +8%. "Very protective!" Costs 1500g from vendors.
