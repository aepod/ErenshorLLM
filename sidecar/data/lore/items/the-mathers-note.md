The Mathers Note

I fear for our safety. I fear that our time is nearing its end.

There have been cloaked figures lurking on the beach at night, after dark. I wonder if they know what knowledge I possess... and how they plan to extract it from me. I can only wonder what Eldoth has asked of them...

If you find this letter after my demise, see my Brother Yarren in Goodsoil. I've given something of great interest to him for safe keeping. Ask for him - he's often toiling away out of sight.
