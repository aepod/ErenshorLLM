Metal Girdle is a starter waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gort (Boss) in Hidden Hills (50.0% drop rate). Dexterity +2, Void resistance +3%, +10 Health. "It's not adjustable and it doesn't really fit" Costs 5g from vendors.
