Azynthian Armguards is a endgame arm piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Oracle Tretha (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Dexterity +20, +75 Health. "Made of bone and silk" Costs 18586g from vendors.
