# Item Quality

# Item Quality

Almost all equipment in the game have rare "blessed" versions with increased stat bonuses. These appear with a sparkling animation in their background.

Stinging Bracer with all different qualities

## Blue Blessed Items (+1)

There are three ways to obtain blue blessed items:

* Any equipment that is dropped by an enemy has a 1/100 chance of being a blue sparkling item
* Any crafted equipment that is created using  Ancient Coal is guaranteed to be a blue sparkling item
* Upgrading non-blessed equipment at the Braxonian Flame Well
* This is done by placing 1-3x  Sivakrux, a matching amount of  Offering Stones and an unblessed item
* The chance that the item is blessed is based on how many  are used - 40%/80%/100%

Stat benefits:

* Existing stats and resistances are increased
* Resonance/Damage are by 1
* Item proc chances are not affected

## Purple Blessed Items (+2)

These items can only be obtained in one way -

* Upgrading a blue blessed item with the recipe  Mold: An Otherworldly Box which requires a  Planar Stone

Stat benefits:

* Stats and resistances are further increased
* Resonance/Damage are increased by an additional 1 point (for a total of 2 over the unblessed version)
* Item proc chances are not affected
