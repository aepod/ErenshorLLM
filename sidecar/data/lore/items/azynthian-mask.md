Azynthian Mask is a best-in-slot head \- relic item piece for Arcanists and Reavers. Dropped by Azynthian Keeper (Boss) in Secluded Sanctuary (41.1% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+20), Wisdom +20, Void resistance +5%. "Your thoughts speed up and your awareness sharpens. You feel you are close to grasping an unspoken truth." Costs 21250g from vendors.
