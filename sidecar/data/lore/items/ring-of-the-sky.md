Ring of the Sky is a consumable item. Dropped by Tinkles (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (4.0% drop rate). Strength +3, Magic resistance +5%, Elemental resistance +3%. "If it fits you, how did it fit a faerie?" Costs 200g from vendors.
