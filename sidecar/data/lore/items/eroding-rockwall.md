# Eroding Rockwall

## Eroding Rockwall

### Type

Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

The Mystery of Bellwain Island

**Eroding Rockwall** is a Note in Erenshor.

# Location

This note can be found on Bellwain Island as part of the quest The Mystery of Bellwain Island.

# Description

Eroding Rockwall describes the crumbling foundation surrounding the island.

Eroding Rockwall

Bellsie always preferred to relax on the back patio, though she worried greatly about the eroding retaining wall that held it all together.

I had promised to have a swim around it to verify its integrity, but it wasn't until I was making preparations to leave that I noted the extent of its disrepair.

Bellsie always was right.

Taking a hint from the message in the note, jump off the back patio and into the ocean to discover a small cave. Inside, pick up the  Bellwain Attic Key from the treasure chest.
