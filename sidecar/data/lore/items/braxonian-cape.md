Braxonian Cape is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Braxonian Weapon Master (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (66.7% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+20), Strength +15, Elemental resistance +5%. "Blessed by the priests of Brax" Costs 12555g from vendors.
