Ancient Guardian Boots is a mid-level foot piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Worn Ancient Guardian in Faerie's Brake (1.6% drop rate). Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Some of its past footprints are probably fossilized now" Costs 1580g from vendors.
