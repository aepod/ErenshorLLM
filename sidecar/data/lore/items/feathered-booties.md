Feathered Booties is a solid upgrade foot \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Pteriaped Matron in Loomingwood Forest (66.7% drop rate). Strong Agility (+10), Charisma +10, Poison resistance +2%. "Woven from colorful feathers" Costs 1025g from vendors.
