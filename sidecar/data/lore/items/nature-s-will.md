Nature's Will is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Fallen Fernalla (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (35.6% drop rate). Also drops from Fallen Fernalla. Massive Intelligence (+25), Wisdom +25, Magic resistance +2%. Special: 50% chance on BASH:. "The sight of it sends most opponents into fits of tears" Costs 15855g from vendors.
