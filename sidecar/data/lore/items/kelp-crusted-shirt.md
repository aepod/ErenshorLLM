Kelp Crusted Shirt is a starter chest piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Blunga in Willowwatch Ridge (83.3% drop rate). Strength +2. "The dried kelp adds a layer of protection" Costs 5g from vendors.
