Molorai Warstomps is a mid-level foot piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Overseer Laseal (Boss) in The Duskenlight Coast (55.3% drop rate). Agility +8, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +4%. "Every footstep is thunderous and intimidating" Costs 1000g from vendors.
