Sableheart's Harness is a consumable item. Dropped by Sableheart, Steed of Sivakaya (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (33.3% drop rate). Strength +8, +500 Health. "Worn by a once magnificent dragon" Costs 24550g from vendors.
