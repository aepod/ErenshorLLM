Wardwarped Cape is a endgame back \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Wardwarped Alpha (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (33.3% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+20), Wisdom +20, Magic resistance +4%. "The threads are woven into strange shapes, which come together to make the cape whole" Costs 15450g from vendors.
