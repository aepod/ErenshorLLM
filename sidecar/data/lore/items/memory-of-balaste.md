Memory of Balaste

It recalls images of Vitheo's mortal form being laid to rest, surrounded by his most loyal followers. A notable gap between two men can be seen - someone is missing.

The missing person is not mourned, but hated. He has done something terrible.

Balaste lays a hand on Vitheo's helmet, and the memory fades.
