Spell Scroll: Winter's Bite is a mid-level gear piece. Dropped by Rottenfoot Swampwalker in Fernalla's Revival Plains (4.6% drop rate). Also drops from Apha, Priest of Brax and Upha, Priest of Brax. Costs 4550g from vendors.
