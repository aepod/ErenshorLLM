Runed Gloves is a mid-level hand \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, and Reavers. Dropped by A Deepling Crimsonite in The Elderstone Mines (2.6% drop rate). Also drops from A Deepling Lunarite and A Deepling Verdite. Strength +8, Elemental resistance +1%, Void resistance +1%. "The runes are embroidered in with a strange, metallic thread." Costs 1985g from vendors.
