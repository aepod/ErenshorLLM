Instructions for Warmth

A cave sits dark, hollow and cold,
Its flame long dead, its soul grew old

With Eternal Ice and Death's Dust true,
An Aged Tooth and a Thief's touch too,
The soul of a beast makes the flames renew

The flame will rise with a deathly heat,
As the living decay, their quest complete.

The recipe calls for:

*  Eternal Ice
*  Death's Decay
*  Ancient Fang
*  Thief of Breath
*  Soul of a Ghostly Beast
