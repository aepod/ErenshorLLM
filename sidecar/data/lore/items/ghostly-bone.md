Ghostly Bone is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Azynthian Firebone in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (91.9% drop rate). Also drops from Azynthian Icebone and Greater Azynthian Firebone. Costs 12g from vendors.
