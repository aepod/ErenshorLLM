Pendant of Petrified Wood is a mid-level neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by A Grassland Ogre Plaguebringer in Underspine Hollow (1.3% drop rate). Charisma +6, +75 Health. "The dried, stone-like wood constantly sprouts flowers" Costs 700g from vendors.
