Ghostly Sash is a solid upgrade waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ghost of Plaxitheris (Boss) in Shivering Step (50.0% drop rate). Void resistance +4%, +100 Health. "Sometimes it just falls off for no reason" Costs 2105g from vendors.
