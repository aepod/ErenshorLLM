Nightfall is a starter gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by A Skeleton Captain (Boss) in The Bonepits (9.7% drop rate). Also drops from Wiggleribs. 13 damage. Special: 3% chance on ATTACK:. "It casts a shadow that's darker than it should be" Costs 55g from vendors.
