Centering Cord is a endgame waist \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Corrupted Fungal Bear in Blooming Sepulcher (66.7% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Intelligence +15, +350 Health. "It keeps you calm, it keeps you centered" Costs 25000g from vendors.
