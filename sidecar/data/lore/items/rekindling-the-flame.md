Rekindling The Flame is a endgame gear piece. Dropped by Elwio the Traitor (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (100.0% drop rate). Also drops from Rekindling The Flame and The giant rests until the flames are relit. When once again both burn bright we might rekindle its fury..
