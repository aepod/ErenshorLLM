Volcanic Sceptre is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Volcanic Giant (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (50.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+12), Wisdom +10, Elemental resistance +15%. Special: 12% chance on ATTACK:. "Forged in magma and aged in battle" Costs 2515g from vendors.
