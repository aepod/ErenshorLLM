Aether's Blessing is a solid upgrade neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aetherfiend in Loomingwood Forest (20.5% drop rate). Also drops from Shadow Man. Strong Agility (+12). "As you put it on, you feel yourself lift slightly off the ground" Costs 2600g from vendors.
