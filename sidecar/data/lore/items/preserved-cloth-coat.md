Preserved Cloth Coat is a solid upgrade chest piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Diamond Colossus (Boss) in Rockshade Hold (50.0% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+12), Wisdom +12, Magic resistance +2%. "A wonderfully crafted shirt, preserved in pristine condition." Costs 3300g from vendors.
