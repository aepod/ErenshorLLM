Polished Steel Armguards is a mid-level arm piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Overseer Orlok (Boss) in Old Krakengard (3.2% drop rate). Strength +4, Magic resistance +5%, Poison resistance +1%. "You can check out your muscles and your reflection at the same time." Costs 1250g from vendors.
