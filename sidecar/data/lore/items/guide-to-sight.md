## Guide to Sight

### Type

Quest Item

### Related quest(s)

Seeing the Truth

You can get this item from fishing anywhere in Shivering Step. Reading it begins and completes Seeing the Truth causing a bridge to appear.

Guide to Sight

With determination and focus, the world shifts, and what was once shrouded emerges in spectral light. Apparitions of truth drift through the air, casting faint glows on long-forgotten paths. All one must do is see.

The bridge is yours to cross.
