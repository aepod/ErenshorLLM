Strategist's Ring is a mid-level ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Chazza Priel (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (50.0% drop rate). Charisma +8, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%. "Donning this ring allows you to process things... differently." Costs 2355g from vendors.
