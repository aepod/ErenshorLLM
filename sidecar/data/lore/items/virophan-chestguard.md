Virophan Chestguard is a solid upgrade chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Oarik the Failure (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (33.3% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+10), Agility +10, Magic resistance +5%. "Sturdy but freeing" Costs 4100g from vendors.
