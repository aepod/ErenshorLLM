Blade of the Sea is a mid-level weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Greater Vithean Elemental in The Duskenlight Coast (33.3% drop rate). Strength +2, 15 damage. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Across the blade, water seemingly clings to the metal" Costs 1000g from vendors.
