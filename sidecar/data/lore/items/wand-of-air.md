Wand of Air is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Greater Vithean Elemental in The Duskenlight Coast (33.3% drop rate). Intelligence +7, Magic resistance +3%, +30 Health. Special: 25% chance on ATTACK:. "A soft breeze blows off of the wand" Costs 1200g from vendors.
