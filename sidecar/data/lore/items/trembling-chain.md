Trembling Chain is a solid upgrade neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Warded Shadow (Boss) in Shivering Tomb (2) (38.1% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+15), Charisma +10, Void resistance +5%. "It trembles against your skin, as though it's fearing unseen something nearby" Costs 4500g from vendors.
