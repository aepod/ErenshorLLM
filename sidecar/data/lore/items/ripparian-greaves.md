Ripparian Greaves is a starter leg piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Armorer Nell (3.4% drop rate). Also drops from Captain Ovid and Celeso the Historian. Strength +8, Magic resistance +4%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Standard issue to Ripper's troops." Costs 7500g from vendors.
