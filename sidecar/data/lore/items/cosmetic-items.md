## Cosmetic Items

Cosmetic slots in the inventory

Cosmetic items are items that affect the visuals of your character, but do not offer any statistic or equipment bonus. There are three cosmetic slots in the inventory screen, denoted by the green border. Cosmetic items placed in these slots will appear on the character's model. If an item placed in that slot is not a cosmetic item, it functions as a regular inventory slot.

Most cosmetic items are Masks, but there are lanterns/light sources as well that provide a small amount of illumination around your character. The  Belt Lamp can be purchased from vendors in starting areas. The  Shivering Belt Lantern is obtained from Kio the Light Keeper in the Shivering Step and is used as part of that zone's events (though other lamps will work as well). The  Keeper's Lantern functions as a light source, but also allows a player to set their bind point at nearly any position in the world rather than only at wishing wells.
