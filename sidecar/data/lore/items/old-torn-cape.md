Old Torn Cape is a mid-level back piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by An Apparition of Sivakaya (Boss) in The Bonepits (26.2% drop rate). Also drops from Sylvan Giant Runt and A Lesser Avatar of Sivakaya. Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +2%, Elemental resistance +2%. "Who knows who used to wear this..." Costs 255g from vendors.
