Aged Plate Sleeves is a mid-level arm piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectral Castle Guard in Lost Cellar (3.9% drop rate). Also drops from Spectral Guard and Spectral High Guard. Strength +5, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "The satin lining is still soft and smooth" Costs 350g from vendors.
