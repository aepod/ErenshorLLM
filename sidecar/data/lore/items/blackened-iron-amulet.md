Blackened Iron Amulet is a mid-level neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gruhglor (Boss) in Fernalla's Revival Plains (19.5% drop rate). Dexterity +7, +50 Health. "Rough, but not rusted." Costs 550g from vendors.
