Sivakayan Straightsword is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gherist Morthario (Boss) in Rottenfoot (28.4% drop rate). Also drops from Sivakayan Raider and Darkwarden Awxa. Void resistance +4%, 15 damage. Special: 20% chance on ATTACK:. "A standard Sivakayan sword." Costs 2000g from vendors.
