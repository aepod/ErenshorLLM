Ring of Visions is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Spectre in Fernalla's Revival Plains (2.6% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Wisdom +10, Magic resistance +4%. "This ring was present for world changing events" Costs 4500g from vendors.
