Faerie Sleeves is a mid-level arm \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Tinkles (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (4.0% drop rate). Intelligence +5, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. "This used to be normal plate armor, but now it's covered in a strange fungus." Costs 850g from vendors.
