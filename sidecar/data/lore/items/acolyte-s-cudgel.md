Acolyte's Cudgel is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Ecclesiarch (Boss) in Blacksalt Strand (7.6% drop rate). Endurance +5, Void resistance +3%, 12 damage. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Something primal overcomes you as you hold this weapon." Costs 1900g from vendors.
