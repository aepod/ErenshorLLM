Lost Wizarding Hat is a solid upgrade head piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Arcane Pupil in The Duskenlight Coast (5.1% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+20), Charisma +10, Magic resistance +5%. "Worn by Sivakayan and Braxonian pupils long ago" Costs 18545g from vendors.
