Eldoth's Finery is a best-in-slot chest piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Eldoth Molorai (Boss) in Azynthi's Garden (25.0% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+30), Wisdom +30, Magic resistance +3%. "Effect applied once item is equipped." Costs 28000g from vendors.
