Braxonian Leather Tunic is a endgame chest piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (12.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Massive Dexterity (+20), Agility +20, Magic resistance +2%. "Legendary Leather Armor of Brax's Soldiers" Costs 15000g from vendors.
