Mithril Short Sword is a starter weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (20-30) in Treasure Hunting (25.6% drop rate). Strong Intelligence (+10), Charisma +10, Magic resistance +5%. Special: 5% chance on ATTACK:. "Mithril is a strange, magical metal" Costs 11000g from vendors.
