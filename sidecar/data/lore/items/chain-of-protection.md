Chain of Protection is a solid upgrade neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Soul Linked Spider in Shivering Tomb (2) (50.0% drop rate). Also drops from Soul Linked Spider and Soul Linked Spider. Magic resistance +8%, Poison resistance +8%, Elemental resistance +8%. "The arcane bows before you, and you feel suddenly connected with it all" Costs 4500g from vendors.
