Spritely Leggings is a mid-level leg \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Massia the Fallen (Boss) in The Duskenlight Coast (50.0% drop rate). Agility +5, Void resistance +5%, +50 Health. "Immaculately stitched with golden threads." Costs 900g from vendors.
