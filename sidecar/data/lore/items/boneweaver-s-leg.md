Boneweaver's Leg is a mid-level weapon piece for Arcanists and Druids. Dropped by Boneweaver (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (33.3% drop rate). Intelligence +6, Poison resistance +2%, +200 Health. Special: 12% chance on ATTACK:. "Torn from a ghostly spider" Costs 3500g from vendors.
