Living Chainmail Jacket is a mid-level chest \- relic item piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Tinkles (Boss) in Faerie's Brake (23.9% drop rate). Also drops from Botanist. Endurance +5, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +2%. "It crawls across your skin as you wear it." Costs 1200g from vendors.
