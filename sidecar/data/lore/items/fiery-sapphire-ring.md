Fiery Sapphire Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sand Mummy in Braxonian Desert (9.1% drop rate). Also drops from Scorched Skeleton. Charisma +5, Elemental resistance +10%, +5 Health. "Adorned with a wonderfully orange sapphire." Costs 45g from vendors.
