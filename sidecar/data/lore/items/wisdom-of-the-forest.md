Wisdom of the Forest is a solid upgrade head piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Diva (Boss) in Loomingwood Forest (50.0% drop rate). Massive Wisdom (+20), +85 Health. "It whispers untold stories known only by the trees" Costs 5000g from vendors.
