Singularity, Vessel of Creation is a best-in-slot weapon piece. Dropped by Astra, Rogue of the Stars (Boss) in Soluna's Landing (16.7% drop rate). Massive Intelligence (+25), Wisdom +10, Magic resistance +6%. Special: 33% chance on ATTACK:. "The flame that resides within is the seed of all existence" Costs 33000g from vendors.
