Fungal Edge is a solid upgrade gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Ravager, The First Infected (Boss) in The Elderstone Mines (45.8% drop rate). Endurance +8, Poison resistance +5%, +10 Health. "It makes you want to wash your hands so badly." Costs 2500g from vendors.
