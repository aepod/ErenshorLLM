Chapters of Descent, Passage 3

The Wisp dares to defy the divine metamorphosis bestowed upon Sivakaya by Azynthi. Their misguided pursuits to strip her of the bestowed power and revert to a perceived former glory are treacherous.

Sivakaya's transformation is no descent from grace but an ascension to a realm of dark magnificence, and all who fail to stand against those who seek to unravel this sublime tapestry woven by the shadows themselves shall fall to it.
