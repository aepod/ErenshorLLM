Blightsteel Greaves is a endgame leg piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Harbinger of Corruption (Boss) in The Blight (66.7% drop rate). Strong Strength (+12), Endurance +12, Magic resistance +2%. "A strange growth of hair-like vines crawls across the metal" Costs 6900g from vendors.
