Brackwood Mace is a starter weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Vevillo Polter (Boss) in Stowaway's Step (13.7% drop rate). Also drops from Aranuil Olaseo and Odyss Repman. +10 Health, 6 damage. "The mace is suprisingly light and easy to handle. Even a child could be dangerous with this thing." Costs 20g from vendors.
