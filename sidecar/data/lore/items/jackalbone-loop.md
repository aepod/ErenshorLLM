Jackalbone Loop is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lupine Horror in The Blight (33.3% drop rate). Endurance +5, Void resistance +6%, +165 Health. "It's hard to tell which bone this was exactly" Costs 5500g from vendors.
