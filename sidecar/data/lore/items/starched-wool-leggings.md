Starched Wool Leggings is a solid upgrade leg piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Addison Priel (Boss) in Prielian Cascade (50.0% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+10), Agility +10, Poison resistance +2%. "They're stiff and itchy" Costs 6000g from vendors.
