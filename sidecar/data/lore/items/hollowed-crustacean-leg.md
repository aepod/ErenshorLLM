Hollowed Crustacean Leg is a mid-level bracer \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Old Brackey (Boss) in Vitheo's Watch (70.1% drop rate). Strength +4, Magic resistance +1%, Poison resistance +1%. Costs 850g from vendors.
