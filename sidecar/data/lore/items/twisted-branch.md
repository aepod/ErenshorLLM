Twisted Branch is a endgame weapon piece. Dropped by Botanist in Azynthi's Garden (Dimensional Rift) (7.7% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+14), Intelligence +10, +125 Health. Special: 20% chance on ATTACK:. "It writhes in your hand as though some unseen breeze is flowing by." Costs 17584g from vendors.
