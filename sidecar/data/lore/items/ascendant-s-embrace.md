Ascendant's Embrace is a endgame chest piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Balthom, Soldier of Sivakaya (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (33.3% drop rate). Massive Endurance (+25), Strength +20, Magic resistance +4%. Costs 38000g from vendors.
