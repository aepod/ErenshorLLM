Thryne, Sledge of Creation is a best-in-slot gear piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Vessel Siraethe (Boss) in Jaws of Sivakaya (28.8% drop rate). Massive Strength (+30), Endurance +20, Magic resistance +10%. Special: 3% chance on ATTACK:. "A hammer that helped to build reality itself" Costs 48000g from vendors.
