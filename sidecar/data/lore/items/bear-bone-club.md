Bear Bone Club is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Yarren Mathers (100.0% drop rate). Also drops from Elysia Thistlewick and Sprig Laisey. Intelligence +2, Magic resistance +5%, 12 damage. "Boiled clean and unnaturally white." Costs 200g from vendors.
