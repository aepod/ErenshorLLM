Wild Colossus Ring is a solid upgrade ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Xasxi (Boss) in Malaroth's Nesting Grounds (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +4%. "The essence of a true colossus lives within this band" Costs 4500g from vendors.
