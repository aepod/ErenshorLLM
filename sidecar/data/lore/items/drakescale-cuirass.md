Drakescale Cuirass is a starter chest piece for Druids, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (20-30) in Treasure Hunting (25.6% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +1%. "Almost as neat as a dragonscale one" Costs 16550g from vendors.
