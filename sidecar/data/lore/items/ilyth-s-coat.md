Ilyth's Coat is a endgame chest piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Darkwarden Ilyth (Boss) in Vitheo's Rest (50.0% drop rate). Massive Strength (+25), Dexterity +25, Magic resistance +5%. "Worn by one of Sivakaya's top Darkwardens on Erenshor" Costs 32500g from vendors.
