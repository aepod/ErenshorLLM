Ring of Repeated Thoughts is a starter ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (30+) in Treasure Hunting (12.8% drop rate). Strong Charisma (+15), +300 Health. "It does that thing where you wake up at 2am panicking about what you said at dinner" Costs 16500g from vendors.
