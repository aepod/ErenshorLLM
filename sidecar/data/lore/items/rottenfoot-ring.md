Rottenfoot Ring is a mid-level ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Rottenfoot Mauler in Rottenfoot (5.8% drop rate). Magic resistance +3%, Elemental resistance +3%, +25 Health. "Plain and unremarkable" Costs 150g from vendors.
