Albino Bear Hood is a solid upgrade head piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Albino Kodiak in Silkengrass Meadowlands (13.5% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+15), Magic resistance +3%, Elemental resistance +3%. "From the rare Albino Kodiak" Costs 1500g from vendors.
