Polished Steel Belt is a solid upgrade waist piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Braxonian Honor Guard in Fallen Braxonia (2.3% drop rate). Strength +3, Magic resistance +9%, Poison resistance +9%. "It's ugly. No wonder they buried it." Costs 3200g from vendors.
