Braxonian Royal Armband is a endgame arm piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Statue of Brax (Boss) in Fallen Braxonia (12.6% drop rate). Also drops from Braxonian Planar Guard (Fire) and Braxonian Planar Guard (Ice). Strong Intelligence (+15), Wisdom +10, Poison resistance +2%. "Legendary Garb of Brax's Arcanists" Costs 13250g from vendors.
