Cold Steel Greaves is a mid-level leg piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Militia Warrior in Old Krakengard (5.1% drop rate). Endurance +3, +5 Health. "The metal groans woefully with each step" Costs 65g from vendors.
