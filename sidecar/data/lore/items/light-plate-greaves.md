Light Plate Greaves is a starter leg piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Sylvan Giant Runt (Boss) in Willowwatch Ridge (23.9% drop rate). Also drops from Plaxitheris and A Skeleton Captain. "Very light weight and thin armor. It's better than nothing." Costs 55g from vendors.
