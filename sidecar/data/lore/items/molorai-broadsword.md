Molorai Broadsword is a mid-level weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Molorai Outrider in The Duskenlight Coast (20.5% drop rate). 11 damage. "A generic sword used by the Molorai" Costs 25g from vendors.
