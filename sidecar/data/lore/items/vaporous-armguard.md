Vaporous Armguard is a starter arm piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Dream Invader (25.0% drop rate). Also drops from Invader of Dreams. Strong Dexterity (+15), Strength +10, Void resistance +15%. "They seem to be made of air, yet they allow nothing to pass through" Costs 8555g from vendors.
