Seafarer's Pendant is a starter neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Aragath Swellsteer (100.0% drop rate). Strength +2, Elemental resistance +5%, +10 Health. "The gem projects images of beauty lost aeons ago." Costs 455g from vendors.
