## Dock Pass

### Type

Quest Item

### Other sources

### Related quest(s)

Stowaway Registration

The pass is required to finish Stowaway Registration By handing it to Controller Wendyl.

Dock Pass

The dock pass for Stowaway Step will grant you passage to the rest of the island, and the right to settle and build a life there.

Many are under the impression that it grants through-passage to the Erenshor mainland, but alas, it does not.
