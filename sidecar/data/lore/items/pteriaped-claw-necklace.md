Pteriaped Claw Necklace is a solid upgrade neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Pteriaped Matron in Loomingwood Forest (33.3% drop rate). Massive Charisma (+20), Magic resistance +5%, Poison resistance +5%. "The claws are as sharp as talons. After all, they are talons." Costs 1000g from vendors.
