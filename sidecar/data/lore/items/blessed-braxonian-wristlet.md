Blessed Braxonian Wristlet is a endgame bracer \- relic item piece for Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Kingsman (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Also drops from Hand of the King. Strong Dexterity (+12), Elemental resistance +3%, +15 Health. "Legendary Leather armor of Brax's Soldiers, once held by Brax himself." Costs 10000g from vendors.
