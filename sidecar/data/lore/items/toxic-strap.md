Toxic Strap is a starter waist \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gloopa (Quarter) (38.5% drop rate). Strong Dexterity (+10), Poison resistance +3%, Void resistance +2%. "Something about it gives you a sense of freedom." Costs 3000g from vendors.
