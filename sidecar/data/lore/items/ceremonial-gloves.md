Ceremonial Gloves is a starter hand \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Failed Cremation (Boss) in Island Tomb (48.6% drop rate). Intelligence +2, Magic resistance +1%, Void resistance +1%. "For special occassions" Costs 350g from vendors.
