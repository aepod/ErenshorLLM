Nature's Promise is a solid upgrade neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Maple (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (1.7% drop rate). Also drops from Greater Fawn and Risen Hierophant. Massive Wisdom (+20), Intelligence +14, Void resistance +12%. "Only the most devout had the honor of donning these" Costs 13110g from vendors.
