Ripparian Soldier Helm is a starter head piece for Paladins and Reavers. Dropped by Hector Yvey (12.1% drop rate). Also drops from Mavik Estonia and Ripper Soldier. Strong Strength (+15), Endurance +10, Magic resistance +2%. "Ancient etchings cover the interior" Costs 6500g from vendors.
