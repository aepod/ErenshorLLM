Birdbone Band is a solid upgrade ring piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Pteriaped Matron in Loomingwood Forest (66.7% drop rate). +50 Health. "Effect applied once item is equipped." Costs 1655g from vendors.
