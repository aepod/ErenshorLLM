Thundermakers is a starter foot piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Lost Treasure (30+) in Treasure Hunting (12.8% drop rate). Strong Strength (+15), Endurance +15, Magic resistance +2%. "Even the lightest step shakes the ground beneath your feet" Costs 15850g from vendors.
