Fungal Longsword is a solid upgrade weapon piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Vinx (Boss) in Blooming Sepulcher (50.0% drop rate). Intelligence +8, Poison resistance +6%, 14 damage. Special: 10% chance on ATTACK:. "Spores fly about as you swing" Costs 10500g from vendors.
