Rottenfoot Tribal Strap is a mid-level chest \- relic item piece for Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Rottenfoot Mauler in Rottenfoot (5.8% drop rate). Wisdom +7, Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +1%. "The sign of a high ranking shaman among the Rottenfoot tribes." Costs 1100g from vendors.
