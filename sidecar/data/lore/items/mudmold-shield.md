Mudmold Shield is a mid-level gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Rosle of the Spirits (Boss) in Underspine Hollow (33.3% drop rate). Strong Wisdom (+15), Magic resistance +3%, Poison resistance +5%. Special: 75% chance on BASH:. "Created from some sort of mud, it's stunningly strong." Costs 1200g from vendors.
