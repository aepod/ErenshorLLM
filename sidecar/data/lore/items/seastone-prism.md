Seastone Prism is a mid-level neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Algaeth Bilgeback (Boss) in Windwashed Pass (85.7% drop rate). Strong Charisma (+15), Magic resistance +4%, Poison resistance +4%. "From somewhere beneath the waves" Costs 700g from vendors.
