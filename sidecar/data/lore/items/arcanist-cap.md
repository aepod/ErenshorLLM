Arcanist Cap is a mid-level head piece for Arcanists and Stormcallers. Dropped by Alexander Farland (20.4% drop rate). Also drops from Arbant Sparrow and Cerbantias Flameward. Intelligence +5, Magic resistance +5%. "Old worn stiching and torn fabric keeps this thing from being classy." Costs 255g from vendors.
