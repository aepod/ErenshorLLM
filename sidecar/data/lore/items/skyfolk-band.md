Skyfolk Band is a endgame ring \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Winkles in Ripper's Keep (51.0% drop rate). Also drops from A Faerie Enchantress and A Faerie Foliagian. Strong Wisdom (+15), Intelligence +12, +55 Health. "Soft clouds can be seen flowing through the metal" Costs 11550g from vendors.
