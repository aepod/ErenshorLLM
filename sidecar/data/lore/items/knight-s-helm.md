Knight's Helm is a mid-level head piece for Paladins, Reavers, and Windblades. Dropped by Gruhglor (Boss) in Fernalla's Revival Plains (19.5% drop rate). Strength +5, Magic resistance +2%, Poison resistance +1%. "It's apparent that the previous wearer met an unfortunate end." Costs 1695g from vendors.
