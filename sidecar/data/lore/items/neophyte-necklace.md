Neophyte Necklace is a starter neck piece for Arcanists, Druids, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Zashlyn Bloodbane (Boss) in The Bonepits (39.0% drop rate). Also drops from Neophyte Necromancer and Boatman. Intelligence +2. "A simple charmed necklace on a leather string." Costs 12g from vendors.
