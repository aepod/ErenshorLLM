Soul Echo is a endgame neck \- relic item piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Fenton the Blighted (Boss) in The Blight (25.0% drop rate). "Putting it on makes you feel as though you're being torn in two" Costs 700g from vendors.
