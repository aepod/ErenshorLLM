Flaming Vambraces is a mid-level arm piece for Paladins, Reavers, Stormcallers, and Windblades. Dropped by Peon (Boss) in Braxonian Desert (33.3% drop rate). Massive Strength (+20), Poison resistance +1%, Elemental resistance +4%. "They're etched in faded Braxonian writings" Costs 1800g from vendors.
