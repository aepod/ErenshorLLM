Griefwarden is a starter gear piece for Arcanists, Druids, Paladins, Reavers, and Stormcallers. Dropped by Lost Treasure (30+) in Treasure Hunting (12.8% drop rate). Massive Strength (+25), Intelligence +15, Magic resistance +2%. Special: 66% chance on BASH:. "The sight of it sends most opponents into fits of tears" Costs 15855g from vendors.
