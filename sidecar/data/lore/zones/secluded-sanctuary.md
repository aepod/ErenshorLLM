# Secluded Sanctuary

# Secluded Sanctuary

## Seclduded Sanctuary

### Type

Dungeon

### Level

35

Secluded Sanctuary is a mini-dungeon accessed from a portal in Stowaway's Step that appears upon completion of Completing the Ritual. It is required for raid unlocks, as  Eldrich Crystal from Azynthian Keeper is needed to activate the  Inert Rune of Brax,  Inert Rune of Fernalla,  Inert Rune of Soluna, and  Inert Rune of Vitheo.

When you first enter, the zone will be empty except for Shattered Sivakaya. After a brief conversation, she will permanently disappear from the zone and four boss NPCs, Shadow of Vitheo, Shadow of Brax, Shadow of Soluna, and Shadow of Fernalla will spawn (all four must be fought simultaneously). Upon defeating all four of them, Azynthian Keeper will spawn.

All the "Shadow of ___" bosses will instantly respawn upon leaving and re-entering the zone.
