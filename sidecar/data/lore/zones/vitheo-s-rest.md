# Vitheo's Rest

## Vitheo's Rest

The resting place of a legendary combatant

### Type

Dungeon

### Level Range

22-35

### In Zone

Port Azure

Vitheo's Rest is a multi level dungeon and the first you encounter. It contains three separate levels locked off by keys. Vitheo's Rest is located in Port Azure behind a hidden wall near the docks.

To not get attacked by the Aquilamar you must say the passcode 'Shadowtide' to Styria Olafur

To gain access to Vitheo's Rest you must either give Vethalo Gala a  Sivakrux or alternatively you can kill Aareth Graveblade for a spare key.

Access to the Shrines and Traitor's Halls requires completion of A Dying Wish, Pt. 1 and A Dying Wish, Pt. 2, respectively, and is progressed alongside unlocking new sections of the Blooming Sepulcher dungeon. Access to Vitheo's Tomb requires acquiring the  Key to Vitheo's Tomb from The Abomination in the Traitor's Halls.

Spawning The Abomination in the Traitor's Halls requires completing The Life Flame and The Dark Flame, which in turn require acquiring the quest A Dying Wish, Pt. 4 in the Blooming Sepulcher dungeon.

The Under the Lighthouse section is accessed from a separate entrance, the Port Azure Lighthouse, after completion of Sivakayans in Port Azure.
