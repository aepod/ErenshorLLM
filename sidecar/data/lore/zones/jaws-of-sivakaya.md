### Unsealing the Jaws

Unlike most dungeons, some prerequisites must be met in order unseal the dungeon to venture further inside.

Prerequisites:

1. Complete the quest A Hermit's Request in Fernalla's Revival Plains to obtain the Notes of a Hermit
2. Read the Notes of a Hermit to obtain the quest A Note for C. Pillot . Hand them to Chazz Pillot to obtain Mold: A Strange Figurine.

Unlocking steps:

1. Complete Recovering the Nighthollow Candle and Lighting the Nighthollow Candle . This questline is started in Loomingwood.
2. Having the Nighthollow Candle in your inventory will cause A Memory of Sivakaya to appear in the entrance area. Speaking to the memory completes the quest The Guardian Awakens .
3. In the next chamber, the statue of Sivakaya will begin to rotate. Whilst facing the statue, strafe around the room meeting it's gaze (it's eyes will glow). If successful, the quest She Sees You will complete and hidden door will open granting access to the rest of the dungeon.
4. To proceed past Aeiros, Soldier of Sivakaya, players will need to complete A Pious Guest by saying Azynthi to the Ward of Piety
