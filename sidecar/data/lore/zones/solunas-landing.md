# Soluna's Landing

Soluna's Landing is located in the northern part of Erenshor. The level range is 25-33. This zone is where the goddess Soluna arrived in Erenshor, spreading celestial teachings through the Solunarians before departing, apparently pursued by a mysterious figure called Astra. The zone holds deep religious significance for Paladins.

---

NPCs include Illian Asboth, Valao the Holy (leader of Solunarian elemental servants), Granderson Jovich, Beth Constantine, Heileigh Serenie, Halford Calloe, Yale Harvey, Jerrod Tunsillo, Marcus Linera, and Natalia Linera.

---

Enemies include Celestial Matter, Living Meteorites, Solunarian Protectors, Wild Ogres, Stardust, Ancient Corpses, Ancient Celestarians, Ancient Guards, Ancient Knights, and Verdant Energy. Bosses are Raval The First Paladin, Astra Rogue of the Stars, and Freed Spirit of Raval.

---

Key quests include "Note to Soluna" (155 EXP, rewards Rune of Soluna's Landing) and "Raval's Last Wish" (400 EXP, rewards Inert Rune of Soluna). The Inert Rune of Soluna is required for accessing the Secluded Sanctuary endgame dungeon. Raval, the First Paladin, is a significant lore figure who fell defending Soluna's legacy.

---

Soluna's Landing connects to Malaroth's Nesting Grounds and Loomingwood. The celestial and ancient enemies here reflect the zone's divine history, with remnants of Soluna's arrival scattered across the landscape.
