## Wickliff

"How did he get up there?"

### Level

1-5

Wickliff is located on Stowaway's Step on the top of the hill above the Docks, you can find two merchants at either side of this town and there are plenty of NPCs living here.

NPC's who reside in Wickliff:

* Amethi Plazzo
* Davidson Malarky
* Hawley McMallis
* Isabel Schegg
* Jan Yarrow
* Kezeal the Goat
* Sean Figarello
* Timothy Allorn
* Town Crier
* Tracy Aldern
