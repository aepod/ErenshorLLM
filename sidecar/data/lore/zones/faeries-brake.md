# Faerie's Brake

Faerie's Brake is located in the southern region of Erenshor. The level range is 3-10. It is one of the early zones where SimPlayers commonly spawn and begin their adventures. The zone features magical creatures and a woodland landscape with fae-touched atmosphere.

---

NPCs in Faerie's Brake include Arabetha Songshade, Cecil Threbb, Damien Torno, Elizah Vernor, Peony Shalmo, Pierson Obero, and Valrae Cleary. Valrae is a key quest NPC offering both story and repeatable quests.

---

Enemies include Brittle Skeletons, Brown Bear Cubs, Faerie Drones, Grass Spiders, Sturdy Skeletons, Young Wolves, Worn Ancient Guardians, and Sivakayan Raiders. Bosses are Alpha Wolf, Bassalt Vallano, Grizzlepaw, and Tinkles.

---

Key quests include "Keys for Valrae" (45 EXP, rewards Molorai Utility Belt) and "Liam's Note" (65 EXP, rewards The Duskenlight Ritual quest). Repeatable quests are "Ogre Spice for Valrae" (45 EXP, rewards Forest Pendant) and "Repairing Treasure Maps" (45 EXP, rewards Treasure Map). Faerie's Brake connects to Fernalla's Revival Plains to the north.
