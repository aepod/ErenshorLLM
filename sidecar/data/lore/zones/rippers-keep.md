# Ripper's Keep

Ripper's Keep is an ancient Sivakayan fortification in northern Erenshor that has been taken over by Ripper DeMerth and her soldiers. The level range is 28-33. It connects to Loomingwood and the Abyssal Lake dungeon on its eastern side.

---

NPCs include Leena Halsted, Teodor Smart, Jeremie Galyard, Dusty McPhenn, Captain Ovid, Armorer Nell, and Comstock Retalio. Goldie Retalio runs a second Auction House location here.

---

Standard enemies include Mature Grass Spiders, Sivakayan Clerics, Sivakayan Spymasters, Sivakayan Reavers, Sivakayan Raiders, and Ripper Soldiers. Additional enemies include Celeso the Historian, Winkles, Oscar VanEllis, Goldie Retalio, and various Faerie types (Foliagan, Windchild, Enchantress). The zone boss is Ripper DeMerth.

---

Key quests include "A Trade in Ripper's Keep" (50 EXP, rewards Rune of Ripper's Keep), "A Bounty on Guard Captain Kilkay" (255 EXP, 500 Gold, rewards Ripparian Pendant), "Sivakayan Intel for Ripper" (100 EXP), "Sivakayan Intel for Kilkay" (100 EXP), and "The Ripper Ritual" (25 EXP, unlocks Mysterious Portal tier 3). The Abyssal Lake quest "Securing Port Azure" (1000 EXP, 1000 Gold) is accessed from here. A repeatable quest "Ripper's Insignia Badges" (50 EXP, 500 Gold) turns in at Port Azure.
