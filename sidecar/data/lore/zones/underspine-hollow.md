## Underspine Hollow

### Type

Dungeon

### In Zone

Fernalla's Revival Plains

The Underspine Hollow is a dungeon connected to Fernalla's Revival Plains in a cave. The only static boss spawn is Druo the Reborn - the others may be replaced with a normal ogre enemy
