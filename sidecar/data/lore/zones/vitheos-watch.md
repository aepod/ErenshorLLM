# Vitheo's Watch

Vitheo's Watch is located on the southern coast of Erenshor. The level range is 4-10. The zone is named after Vitheo, the demigod of Water and Air created by the goddess Siva to protect the land. The Vithean people were among the founders of Port Azure alongside the Fernallans and Braxonians.

---

Old Krakengard dungeon is located within Vitheo's Watch, providing higher-level dungeon content for the area. NPCs include Urland Garry, Saya Seawatcher, Yarren Mathers, and Sagan Ilth.

---

Enemies in Vitheo's Watch include Mature Grass Spiders, Young Wolves, Molorai Militia Arcanists, Molorai Militia Druids, Molorai Militia Warriors, Skeleton Soldiers, Lost Sea Giants, and Brackish Crocs. Bosses are The Lost Sea Giant King and Old Brackey.

---

The repeatable quest "Aetheria for Saya Seawatcher" (45 EXP, rewards Bottomless Elixir) and the story quest "A Hidden Cause" are available here. The zone connects to neighboring areas and provides early-level content for adventurers exploring the southern coast.
