# Stowaway's Step

Stowaway's Step is the starting zone in Erenshor, available in the game's demo. The island includes three sub-areas: the Docks (the tutorial area), the town of Wickliff, and the Island Tomb dungeon. The level range is 1-6. New players begin at the Docks and progress through the island before traveling to the mainland.

---

The Docks area serves as the tutorial zone where players learn basic controls and combat. NPCs here include Alice Hewer, Amanda Rostley, Captain Genevieve Voldance, Controller Wendyl, Corporal Slate, Customs Guard, Garrey Ambrose, Kio the Light Keeper, Margie Reever, Percy Ildia, and Zachary Yelmee. Starting quests teach movement, combat, and interaction.

---

Wickliff is the town on Stowaway's Step with vendors on either side. NPCs include Amethi Plazzo (ability vendor), Davidson Malarky, Hawley McMallis, Isabel Schegg, Jan Yarrow, Sean Figarello, Timothy Allorn, a Town Crier, Tracy Aldern, Kezeal the Goat, and Validus Greencent. Players can buy starting gear and ability books here.

---

The rest of the island features NPCs like Aldoa Manday, Annabelle Swisher, Aragath Swellsteer, Chance Barlow, Desmond Shaffer, Leo McLaney, Lucian Revald, Revan Gavault, Sergeant D'Vinn, Staggy Garth, Treven Pines, Wally Waldorf, Watchlight, and Neziroth Daggerhart.

---

Enemies on Stowaway's Step include Brackish Croc Whelps, Brittle Skeletons, Brown Bear Cubs, Grass Spiders, Young Wolves, Islander Bandits, and Rattlewrists. Bosses include Plaxitheris, Vevillo Polter, and Occuphage Eater of Eyes.

---

Key quests on Stowaway's Step include "Alice Hewer's Necklace" (15 EXP, rewards Braided Belt), "Percy's Seaspice" (20 EXP), "Stowaway Registration" (5 EXP), "Kio's Papers" (10 EXP, rewards gate key), "Wally's Moongill" (45 EXP, rewards Waldorf Family Ring), "Daggerhart Ancestral Coin" (35 EXP, rewards Copper Sword), "Whispers of Wyland" (55 EXP), "A Way To Erenshor" (25 EXP), and "Aragath's Imprisonment" (45 EXP, rewards Seafarer's Pendant). After leaving Stowaway's Step, players need the quest "The Angler's Ring" to return.
