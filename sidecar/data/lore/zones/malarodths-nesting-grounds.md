# Malaroth's Nesting Grounds

Malaroth's Nesting Grounds is located in the west of Erenshor. The level range is 20-30. The zone is home to the Malaroth species, dangerous territorial creatures that nest and breed here. It connects to Windwashed Pass, Loomingwood, and Soluna's Landing.

---

NPCs include Captured Malaroth, Colton Calavia, Tiallia Priel, Olden Lennova, Vizijivi, and Xasxi. The Captured Malaroth suggests that some of these creatures have been subdued by local inhabitants or researchers.

---

Enemies include Mature Grass Spiders, Drowned Corpses, Lost Souls, Loomingwood Minotaurs (standard and Legionnaire and Soldier variants), Solunarian Protectors, Malaroth Cubs, Mature Malaroths, Priel Deceivers, Priel Enforcers, Priel Tamers, and Vithean Elementals. Bosses are Demented Malaroth and Shivunax.

---

The Priel faction has a significant presence in the Nesting Grounds, with tamers attempting to control the Malaroth creatures. The zone serves as a transition between the western and northern regions of Erenshor, connecting to Soluna's Landing where the goddess Soluna's influence is strongest.
