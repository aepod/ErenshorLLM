# Azynthi's Garden

Azynthi's Garden is a zone located on an island in the east of Erenshor. The level range is 30-35. It is described as "The home of the Corruptor of Gods." Players reach it by ferry from the Boatman at The Blight docks. Azynthi was a magical prodigy who became corrupted after a journey beyond the Twin Soldiers, manipulating the goddess Siva into a forbidden ritual that stripped her essence and created the corrupted entity Sivakaya.

---

NPCs include a Boatman, A Strange Hermit, Molorai Dockmaster, Molorai Truthseeker, Molorai Eldermistress, Molorai Curator, Eldoth Molorai (a major faction leader), Apostalo Ostagla, Oracle Tretha, Vos Ashlyn, Oracle Miress, and Grounds Keeper.

---

Enemies include Giant Watchdogs, Molorai forces (Acquirer, Anointed, Bloodwarden, Mason, Outrider, Nightmistress, Ancille), Acolytes of Azynthi, Sivakayan Bodyguards, Sivakayan Emissaries, Mature Grass Spiders, Wild Ancestral Dogs, Deadweb Spiders, Azynthian Icebones, Azynthian Firebones, Exalted Firebones, Ghouls, Greater Azynthian variants, Faceless Necromancers, and Botanists. Bosses are Flicker of Souls and Gargoth.

---

A Dimensional Rift area within the Garden contains distinct enemies and NPCs. Key quests include "The Riftstone" (rewards Azynthian Riftstone) and "Awakening Azynthi" (250 EXP). This is one of the highest-level outdoor zones in the game.
