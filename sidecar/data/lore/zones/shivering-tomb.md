## Shivering Tomb

### Type

Dungeon

### Level Range

19-24

### In Zone

Shivering Step

Shivering Tomb is the Shivering Step equivalent of the Island Tomb. Note that unlike the Island Tomb, the two separate entrances lead to unconnected parts of the tomb. Thus, Dream and Warded Shadow are accessed through completely separate unconnected entrances. See Shivering Step Walkthrough for more information about accessing Shivering Tomb (Beware of spoilers!).
