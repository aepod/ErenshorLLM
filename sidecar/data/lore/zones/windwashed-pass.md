## Windwashed Pass

### Type

Zone

### Level

15-30

The Windwashed pass is located in the west of Erenshor. It is connected to the Duskenlight Coast, Malaroth's Nesting Grounds, Silkengrass Meadowlands and Prielian Cascade.

There is a dungeon here - Rockshade Hold
