# Fernalla's Revival Plains

Fernalla's Revival Plains is an area just north of Faerie's Brake. The level range is 6-18. The Fernallans were one of the founding peoples who joined the Solunarian Paladins to establish Port Azure. The zone is named after the demigoddess Fernalla, created by Brax in Siva's image, who represented abundance, life, and fertility.

---

NPCs include A Hermit, Addelia Zurin, Alexander Farland, Angus Hawer, Axilo O'Casta, Azure Guard, Brandt Harbar, Charlotte Darmask, Erica Spencer, Hadden Passican, Jess Webber, Lieutenant Ulrik, Riley Dallo, Thomson Rhett, Vaneyer Dallo, Varran Juctor, Victor Goldview, and Wintham McShanth.

---

Standard enemies include Brown Bears, Brown Bear Cubs, Grassland Ogres (Chieftains and Elders), Highwaymen (Marauders and Raiders), Mature Grass Spiders, Rock Minions, and Rottenfoot enemies (Tribalists, Witchdoctors, Warriors, Swampwalkers). Rare enemies include Ancient Spectres, Dryad Spirits, Risen Druids, Spectres, Spectral Bears, Verdant Energy, and Spirit of the Willow. Bosses are Gruhglor and the Sylvan Giant.

---

Key quests include "A Hermit's Request" (250 EXP, rewards Notes of a Hermit), "The Tragedy at Goodsoil," "A Note for C. Pillot" (125 EXP, rewards Mold: A Strange Figurine), "The Revival Plains Ritual" (25 EXP, unlocks Mysterious Portal tier 2), and "Hadden's Supplies" (155 EXP, rewards Journeying Boots). The repeatable quest "Druid Beads for Varran" provides 15 EXP and 20 Gold.

---

The Blooming Sepulcher dungeon is accessed from beneath the willow tree in this zone. The "A Dying Wish" quest series (Parts 1-4, each 300 EXP) begins in the Blooming Sepulcher. Underspine Hollow dungeon connects via a cave. The Spirit of the Willow here allows proficiency respeccing.

---

Fernalla's Revival Plains connects to Willowwatch Ridge, Faerie's Brake, Rottenfoot, and the dungeons Blooming Sepulcher and Underspine Hollow. The Fernalla Portal Event features a special boss that spawns as a zone-wide event.
