# Willowwatch Ridge

## Willowwatch Ridge

### Type

Zone

### Level

1-8

Willowwatch Ridge is an alternative starting zone without a tutorial and recommended for more experienced players.

There are exits to Fernalla's Revival Plains, The Bone Pits and Underspine Hollow.

This Zone is not available in the demo
