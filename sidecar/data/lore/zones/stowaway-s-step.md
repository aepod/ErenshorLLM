## Stowaway's Step

### Type

Zone

### Level

1-5

The island of Stowaway's Step includes the Docks, the town of Wickliff and the Island Tomb.

The Docks serves as the tutorial area for new players. Stowaway's Step has two vendors on either side of Wickliff. Below is a list of all NPC's and enemies in this Zone.

Click here and see Quests for Stowaway's Step.

After leaving Stowaway's Step for the first time, you will need to complete the quest The Angler's Ring to return to Stowaway's Step.
