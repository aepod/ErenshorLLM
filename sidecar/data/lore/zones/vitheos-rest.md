# Vitheo's Rest

Vitheo's Rest is a multi-level dungeon located in Port Azure behind a hidden wall near the docks. The level range spans 22-35 across multiple wings. It is "the resting place of a legendary combatant" -- the demigod Vitheo, who was created by Siva with powers of Water and Air, and fell in war against corrupted Sivakaya, achieving godhood.

---

Access requires trading a Sivakrux to Vethalo Gala or defeating Aareth Graveblade for a spare key. The NPC Styria Olafur guards the Aquilamar section, requiring the passcode "Shadowtide." Further wings require completing "A Dying Wish" quest parts and obtaining keys from bosses.

---

The dungeon has four wings. The Shrines (levels 21-24) contain Vithean Remains, Vithean Elite, and Vithean Blademasters. The Traitor's Halls (levels 30-38) feature Cursed Vithean Conspirers, Vithean Betrayers, and Cursed Vithean Traitors. Vitheo's Tomb (levels 34-38) has Verdant Energy and Vithean Immortals. Under the Lighthouse (level 35+) contains Sivakayan Unhallowed.

---

Bosses include Balaste the Fallen, Oarik the Failure, Pamia the Coward, Nazura the Traitor, Elwio the Traitor, The Abomination, The Crypt Guardian, Apparition of Vitheo, and Darkwarden Ilyth. The Abomination drops the Key to Vitheo's Tomb. The quest "A Key to Vitheo's Rest" (150 EXP) in Port Azure grants initial access.

---

Associated quests include "A Dying Wish" (Parts 1-4), "The Life Flame" (15 EXP), "The Dark Flame" (15 EXP), and "Sivakayans in Port Azure" (300 EXP, required for Under the Lighthouse access). This dungeon connects narratively with the Blooming Sepulcher, requiring progression in both.
