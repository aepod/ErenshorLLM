**A Grass Spider** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

A Grass Spider is a common pest and can be found throughout southern Erenshor, including Hidden Hills, Vitheo's Watch, Faerie's Brake1, and on Stowaway's Step.

# Appearance

A Grass Spider has black appendages and multiple eyes. Its green body and black legs help it blend in with the grass, hence its name.

# Behavior

A Grass Spider will roam through grasslands and meadows until it senses a player to attack. They are quite weak, being akin to slimes in many other games.

### Spells & Abilities

A Grass Spider does not use any spells or abilities.
