# Location

A Brown Bear can be found on Fernalla's Revival Plains and Duskenlight Coast.

# Appearance

A Brown Bear is a large bear with brown fur.

# Behavior

A Brown Bear is the adult version of A Brown Bear Cub. It will attack low-leveled players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

A Brown Bear does not use any spells or abilities.

# Notes

* There is a small chance for A Brown Bear to be replaced by the Sylvan Giant on Fernalla's Revival Plains, and the Dusken Giant in Duskenlight Coast.
