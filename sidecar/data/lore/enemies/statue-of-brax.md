# Location

Statue of Brax can be found on the lowest level of Fallen Braxonia. After making your way through the maze of ruined buildings, follow the path until you reach an opening to the left. Make your way down this path, past all the Braxonian High Guards, to find Statue of Brax at the dead end.

# Appearance

As his name suggests, Statue of Brax is a large living statue carved to look like Brax. It is many times larger than the player but otherwise appears human, though it lacks any defining facial features.

# Behavior

Statue of Brax waits on a pedestal in the deepest reaches of Fallen Braxonia, flanked by two Braxonian High Guards. Statue of Brax's detection range is quite low, allowing his guards to be pulled away and fought should players wish before engaging the boss.

### Spells & Abilities

Offensive:

*  Funeral Pyre

### Strategy

Making your way to Statue of Brax is half the battle, with several large groups of Braxonian High Guards blocking the path. Most of these can be cleared in groups of two through careful positioning, though there is also one group of three.

Upon reaching the boss, it is also possible to pull each individual guard away from Statue of Brax, where they can be dealt with individually.

Statue of Brax hits hard, and can easily one-shot even a decently geared Arcanist. If the party is running low on health and needs to reset, he is susceptible to  Dazzle.

# Notes

* Statue of Brax is the only source of Braxonian Ore in the game.
