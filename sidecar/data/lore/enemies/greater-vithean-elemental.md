# Location

Greater Vithean Elementals is a **rare** enemy with a **3% chance** to replace a Vithean Elemental on the beaches of Duskenlight Coast. Of these normal enemies, only ten have the ability to spawn the Greater Vithean Elemental in their place, so players should check the entire beach.

# Appearance

Greater Vithean Elementals are large blue spheres of water. They are the elemental embodiment of both air and sea, given form. Being the "Greater" variety of the regular Vithean Elemental they are slightly larger in size, although the difference might be difficult to spot from afar.

# Behavior

Greater Vithean Elementals bounce across the sand until sensing a target to attack. Alone they do not pose much of a threat, but their close proximity to other Vithean Elementals means that players may end up fighting multiple enemies at once.

### Spells & Abilities

Greater Vithean Elemental does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Finding Greater Vithean Elemental is half the battle, and may require players clearing out the entire beach of Vithean Elementals multiple times, or else repeatedly changing Zones in an attempt to force it to spawn. When fighting Greater Vithean Elemental, be careful not to attract the attention of the other surrounding enemies, or else keep them stunned using  Dazzle or  Sunburst.

# Notes

* It is possible for multiple Greater Vithean Elementals to be present at the same time.
