**A Brackish Croc Whelp** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

A Brackish Croc Whelp can be found exclusively outside of the lighthouse in Stowaway's Step.

# Appearance

A Brackish Croc Whelp is a small black-scaled crocodile. It is the smallest of all the croc variants.

# Behavior

A Brackish Croc Whelp roams the path on the way from the registration office to the lighthouse. It will attack players when they approach it. A Brackish Croc Whelp is the first enemy players will encounter, and they will be prompted with a short tutorial upon approaching it.

Combat

A Small Croc has wandered into the path ahead. Make sure you've equipped your weapon in your inventory. LEFT CLICK him to target him, and press Q to begin attacking. You can RIGHT CLICK or press 'C' with an NPC targeted to get a hint about how well you might fare in combat against it and whether it might attack you on sight.

### Spells and Abilities

A Brackish Croc Whelp does not use any spells or abilities.

# Notes

The necklace A Brackish Croc Whelp drops can be given to Alice Hewer to complete Alice Hewer's Necklace.
