# Location

Molorai Militia Druid can be found throughout Old Krakengard and outside the dungeon in Vitheo's Watch.

# Appearance

Molorai Militia Druids are spellcasters wearing silver and yellow clothes, gloves, and boots. They can occasionally be seen wielding a Pathfinder's Staff.

# Behavior

Molorai Militia Druid stand guard and patrol various points in and outside of Old Krakengard. They will attack players if they get too close, or if they or other nearby militia members are provoked. Molorai Militia Druids keep themselves buffed when idle, but will only cast offensive spells when in combat.

### Spells & Abilities
