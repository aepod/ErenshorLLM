# Location

Molorai Militia Arcanist can be found throughout Old Krakengard and outside the dungeon in Vitheo's Watch.

# Appearance

Molorai Militia Arcanists are spellcasters wearing a green and yellow tunic, gloves, and boots. They can occasionally be seen wearing an Arcanist Cap.

# Behavior

Molorai Militia Arcanist stand guard and patrol various points in and outside of Old Krakengard. They will attack players if they get too close, or if they or other nearby militia members are provoked. Molorai Militia Arcanists keep themselves buffed when idle, but will only cast offensive spells when in combat.

### Spells & Abilities

Defensive:

*  Minor Protection
*  Blood Feast

Offensive:

*  Brax's Touch
*  Ethereal Rending
