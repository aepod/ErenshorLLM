# Appearance

Woe is a white-haired necromancer and follower and apparent member of The Children of Sivakaya. He wears a brown robe and can occasionally be seen holding an Idol of Sivakaya.

# Behavior

Woe waits near a throne at the back of the tomb. He will attack players if they get too close, or if he or a nearby Sorrowful Spirits are attacked or provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

Buffs:

*  Minor Protection

Offensive:

*  Brax's Touch
*  Weak Poison

### Strategy

With careful positioning, it is possible to attract the attention of the nearby Sorrowful Spirits so that they can be killed before attempting to fight Woe. Although he is not particularly strong, his spells can still do a modest amount of damage. A Paladin or Duelist can interrupt Woe while he is casting with a well-timed  Kick.
