**Rattlewrists** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

Rattlewrists can be found exclusively on the path up from the Docks to the rest of Stowaway's Step.

# Appearance

Rattlewrists is a skeleton wearing tattered rags. He has manacles attached to his wrists, which possibly explains how he earned his nickname.

# Behavior

Rattlewrists stands on the path leading to Treven Pines' shop.1 It will attack players when they approach it. Rattlewrists is likely the second enemy players will encounter, and the second part of the combat tutorial. Upon moving up the hill towards Rattlewrists, a tutorial window will pop up encouraging players to make sure they've learned their starting abilities:

Spells and Skills

If there are spell books or skill books in your inventory, RIGHT CLICK on them to consume them and learn their abilities. Then open up your SPELL BOOK and SKILL BOOK with 'B' to make hotkeys for abilities that you want to use.

After defeating Rattlewrists and moving up the hill, another tutorial pop-up will also teach players about looting:

Get Paid!

When you win a battle, make sure you RIGHT CLICK on the fallen enemy to take his loot!

### Spells and Abilities

Rattlewrists does not use any spells or abilities.
