# Location

Vevillo Polter is found at the end of the Bandit's Lair on Stowaway's Step. The cave entrance can be found near Treven Pines' shop.

# Appearance

Vevillo Polter is a bandit wearing grey and red clothes, shoes, and gloves. He has brown hair, a brown goatee, and can occasionally be seen holding a Brackwood Mace.

# Behavior

Vevillo Polter waits behind a table at the back of the lair. He will attack players if they get too close, or if he is attacked or provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

Vevillo Polter does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Vevillo Polter will likely be one of the first bosses players encounter. As such, he does not have any particularly noteworthy skills. If players are struggling to defeat him, they may want to form a party or consider gaining a few levels through weaker enemies or by completing Quests in Stowaway's Step.
