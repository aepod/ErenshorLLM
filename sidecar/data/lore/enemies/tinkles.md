# Location

Tinkles has a **33% chance** to replace one of the Faerie Drones near the ruins in the northeast of Faerie's Brake. Because the Faerie Drones move about and it can be difficult to tell which one shares a spawn table with Tinkles, it is recommended for players to either clear out all of the Faerie Drones or continually re-enter the Zone from Port Azure when searching for her.

# Appearance

Tinkles is a faerie with blue wings and hair. She wears a pink top and pink and white skirt. Tinkles is several times larger than the other nearby Faerie Drones, making her easy to spot from a distance.

# Behavior

Tinkles roams amongst the rest of the faeries. She will attack players if they get too close, or if she or a nearby Faerie Drone are provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

Buffs:

*  Blood Feast
*  Flight of Foot

Offensive:

*  Brax's Touch
*  Biting Vines
*  Burning Chains

### Strategy

Tinkles is not particularly strong, though she does boast several spells that, when combined, can add up to a significant amount of damage on low-HP targets. A Paladin or Duelist can interrupt Tinkles while she is casting these abilities with a well-timed  Kick.
