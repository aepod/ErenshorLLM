**A Brown Bear Cub** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

A Brown Bear Cub are common throughout southern Erenshor and can be found in Fernalla's Revival Plains,1 Duskenlight Coast,2 Faerie's Brake, and on Stowaway's Step. A Brown Bear Cub can also be found in a small cave connected to the Island Tomb.

# Appearance

A Brown Bear Cub is a small adolescent bear with brown fur.

# Behavior

A Brown Bear Cub will roam through grasslands and meadows until it senses a player to attack. Being a cub, it is quite weak.

### Spells & Abilities

A Brown Bear Cub does not use any spells or abilities.
