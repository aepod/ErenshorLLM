**Islander Bandit** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

Islander Bandits are found in the Bandit's Lair on Stowaway's Step. The cave entrance can be found near Treven Pines' shop.

# Appearance

Islander Bandits are bandits wearing grey and red clothes, shoes, and gloves. They can occasionally be seen holding a Brackwood Mace.

# Behavior

Islander Bandits roam throughout their lair and will attack players if they get too close. There are several varieties of bandits, though the only one that has the ability to cast spells is the female bandit with hair.
