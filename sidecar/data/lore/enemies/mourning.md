# Appearance

Mourning is a faceless floating specter in brown robes and a brown belt. Although he has no feet, legs, or face, skeletal hands still stick out from the robe. He is similar in appearance to the Sorrowful Spirits that also occupy the tomb, though slightly larger.

# Behavior

Mourning waits in a back storage room of the tomb. He will attack players if they get too close, or if he or the nearby Enraged Spirit are attacked or provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

Buffs:

*  Hardened Skin

### Strategy

Mourning will likely be one of the first bosses players encounter. As such, he does not have any particularly noteworthy skills, though he will buff himself to absorb a modest amount of damage. If players are struggling to defeat him, they may want to form a party or consider gaining a few levels through weaker enemies or by completing Quests in Stowaway's Step.
