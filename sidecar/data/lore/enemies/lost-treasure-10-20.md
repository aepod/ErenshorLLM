# Location

Giving the four pieces of a  Torn Treasure Map to Cecil Threbb rewards players with a  Treasure Map, allowing them to go on a Treasure Hunt. Using this map between levels **10 - 20** will reveal the location of a Lost Treasure in one of the following locations:

* Hidden Hills
* Faerie's Brake
* Fernalla's Revival Plains

# Appearance

The location where the Lost Treasure is buried appears as a pile of leaves and dirt with a golden aura.

# Behavior

Interacting with the dirt will reveal a chest. Attacking the chest will spawn a wave of enemies which scale with the player's level. There are **2 to 5 waves** in total. They will consist of any combination of **3 to 5** of the following enemies:

* Ancient Skeleton
* Ancient Demon
* Ancient Horror

Once the final wave has been defeated, attacking the chest once more will open it, allowing you to loot the contents inside.
