**A Brittle Skeleton** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

A Brittle Skeleton are common throughout southern Erenshor and can be found in Hidden Hills, Faerie's Brake, on Stowaway's Step, and in The Bone Pits. A Brittle Skeleton can also be rarely found in the Island Tomb as well as the Braxonian Desert.

# Appearance

A Brittle Skeleton is a small skeleton wearing tattered grey pants, a torn blue shirt, and manacles on both wrists.

# Behavior

A Brittle Skeleton is a reanimated corpse with little intelligence or strength. They will roam the area until they find an adventurer to attack, but are far from dangerous unless encountered in large numbers.

### Spells & Abilities

A Brittle Skeleton does not use any spells or abilities.
