# Location

Spectral Torturer is located in the prison area of the Lost Cellar. From the dungeon entrance, take a left and make your way through the caves until you reach a large room with several Spectral High Guards.

# Appearance

Spectral Torturer is an apparition wearing black and brown armor and gloves. He wears a hood, though neither his head nor face are visible.

# Behavior

Spectral Torturer waits his prison within the Lost Cellar, facing a torture rack. He will attack players if they get too close, or if he or his guards are provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

Spectral Torturer does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

The main threat of the fight against Spectral Torturer are the Spectral High Guards. It is strongly advised to clear out the guards who roam the cellar, as well as those nearby who might be hiding behind pillars, before fighting Spectral Torturer.  Dazzle or  Sunburst can be used to stun both him and his guards, allowing players to take out each enemy one at a time.
