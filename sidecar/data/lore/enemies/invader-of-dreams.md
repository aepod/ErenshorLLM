# Location

Invader of Dreams can be found at the end of the second Mysterious Portal in Fernalla's Revival Plains. Accessing the portal requires completing The Fernallan Ritual.

# Appearance

Invader of Dreams is a large ethereal demon with glowing red eyes. It is semi-transparent, and has two large wings growing out of its back.

# Behavior

Invader of Dreams waits at the end of the path in the second Mysterious Portal, waiting for anyone brave enough to invade its realm. It will attack if players get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Invader of Dreams does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Invader of Dreams will summon an orb of Malignant Thoughts upon reaching 66% health, which continuously spawns in Nightmarian Knights, Nightmarian Arcanists, and Nightmarian Hounds until it is defeated. This portal should be targeted and destroyed as quickly as possible before the party gets swarmed by additional enemies.

At 33% health, two more Malignant Thoughts are formed. Just like before, these should immediately be taken out to avoid too many additional enemies entering the fight.

# Notes

* Invader of Dreams is an instanced boss, meaning you can leave the zone and he will immediately respawn.
