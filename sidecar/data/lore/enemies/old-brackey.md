# Location

There is a **79% chance** for Old Brackey to replace A Brackish Croc in the center of the beach on the coast of Vitheo's Watch.

# Appearance

Old Brackey is easily spotted from the top of the cliff leading down to the beach due to his size. Although similar in appearance, he is much larger than the other Brackish Crocs in the area.

# Behavior

Old Brackey roams the beach and will attack low-level players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Old Brackey does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Although he only attacks physically, Old Brackey can easily overpower an underleveled or undergeared character. Players may wish to steer clear until they've explored some of Old Krakengard and gained some additional experience and gear.

When fighting Old Brackey, be wary of the surrounding Brackish Crocs to avoid adding additional enemies to the fight.

# Notes

* It is possible to receive two pieces of the same equipment upon defeating Old Brackey.
