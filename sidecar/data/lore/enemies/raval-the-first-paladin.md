# Location

Raval, The First Paladin is found in a hidden building in the northeast of Soluna's Landing. There is a small gap in the cliffs near the hill with three Wild Ogres. In the subsequent valley, head northwest and into the large back room of the building to find Raval.

# Appearance

Raval, The First Paladin appears as a skeletal figure wearing orange and silver armor. He also wears bracers and boots, and wields a sword.

# Behavior

Raval, The First Paladin waits behind the table underneath a large picture for players to approach. He will attack if players get to close, or if he or his nearby guards are provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

Raval, The First Paladin does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Being a Paladin, Raval is fairly tanky, although he does not use any of the spells or abilities you'd expect from his class. It's a good idea to take out the Ancient Knights that flank him as quickly as possible, or otherwise keep them stunned using  Dazzle,  Sleep, or  Sunburst, as they have the ability to heal Reval for large amounts of health using their  Supreme Healing.

# Notes

* Beating Raval causes the Freed Spirit of Raval to show up in the cemetery behind the building, where the quest Raval's Last Wish can be acquired.
