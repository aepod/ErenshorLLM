**Risen Pilgrim** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

Risen Pilgrim is a special variant of A Brittle Skeleton found throughout Silkengrass Meadowlands.

# Appearance

Risen Pilgrim is a small skeleton wearing tattered grey pants, a torn blue shirt, and manacles on both wrists.

# Behavior

Risen Pilgrim is a reanimated corpse with little intelligence or strength. They will roam the area until finding an adventurer to attack, but are hardly dangerous unless encountered in large numbers.

### Spells & Abilities

Risen Pilgrim does not use any spells or abilities.
