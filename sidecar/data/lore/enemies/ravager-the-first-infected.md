# Location

Ravager, The First Infected can be found in a dead-end of the Elderstone Mines surrounded by several glowing mushrooms. When entering the mines from Blacksalt Strand, following the path down into the caves and turn right, then left. Ravager will be at the end of the path.

# Appearance

Ravager, The First Infected is a larger version of the Fungus Ravaged Brutes, with red and pink pustules covering its body and several gashes sewn together by roots.

# Behavior

Ravager, The First Infected roams his alcove and will attack players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Defensive:

*  Hardened Skin

Offensive:

*  Minor Lightning
*  Jolt

# Strategy

Ravager, The First Infected is a relatively straightforward fight, as he has no guards or minions players need to worry about. Ravager uses several offensive spells, though they can be mitigated through resists or interrupted with a well-timed  Kick.
