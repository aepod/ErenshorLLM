# Location

Dusken Giant is a **rare** enemy with a **1% chance** to replace Brown Bear Cubs, Brown Bears, Spectral Bears, and Dryad Spirits in the southern section of Duskenlight Coast, with the exception of those in the eastern corner where Massia the Fallen spawns. There are 20 possible spawn points for the Dusken Giant, for a total **18.2% chance** to spawn him when entering the Zone.

# Appearance

Dusken Giant is a large demi-human creature with light blue skin and a white beard. He wears a sash across his chest, a horned helmet on his head, and a green and brown kilt. As the name implies, Dusken Giant is massive and can easily be spotted from afar when entering the zone.

# Behavior

Dusken Giants roam the landscape until sensing a target to attack. Not only are they aggressive to players, but they will also attack other enemies in their immediate vicinity.

### Spells & Abilities

Offensive:

*  Tangle

### Strategy

Finding Dusken Giant is half the battle, and may require players clearing out low-level spawns multiple times, or else repeatedly reloading the zone in an attempt to force him to spawn. When fighting Dusken Giant, make sure to stay close to him; he can use Tangle to lock a target in place for several seconds, which may end up keeping a member of your party out of attack range.

# Notes

* It is possible for multiple Dusken Giants to be present at the same time.
