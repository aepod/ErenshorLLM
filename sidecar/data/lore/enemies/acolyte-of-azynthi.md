Once Eldoth Molorai has been defeated, type in chat:
code
/shout Azynthi

/code

whilst in Azynthi's Garden. This will permanently unlock the ability to summon Acolyte of Azynthi.

He can then be fought by simply typing Azynthi while next to A Strange Hermit.

The Acolyte of Azynthi does constant AoE damage and summons Greater Azynthian Icebone and Greater Azynthian Firebone in pairs during combat.
