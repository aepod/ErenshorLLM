# Location

Volcanic Giant can be found in the central crater of the volcano in Rockshade Hold.

# Appearance

Volcanic Giant is an orange ethereal giant wearing a helmet and armor.

# Behavior

Volcanic Giant roams the volcano's crater and will attack players if they get too close. His detection radius is incredibly small, however, which means players will likely need to engage Volcanic Giant in order to draw him out of the crater.

### Spells & Abilities

Offensive:

*  Brax's Rage

### Strategy

Fighting Volcanic Giant in the volcano's crater causes constant ticks of **175** damage from the heat. Players can pull Volcanic Giant out of the crater to avoid this damage if they wish, although it should not be enough damage to matter too much at the level Volcanic Giant should be fought.
