# Location

Blight Wyrm roams The Blight and can be encountered throughout the area.

# Appearance

The Blight Wyrm is a deep purple dragon with a black carapace and large horns.

# Behavior

Exploring The Blight can be extremely dangerous, as Blight Wyrm can be found roaming throughout most of the zone. It has incredibly acute senses, and will zero in on adventurers at quite a large distance. Because it can be quite difficult to spot due to the area's thick haze, players should instead listen for the idle sound cues that signal Blight Wyrm is approaching.

### Spells & Abilities

On-Hit:

* **Blight Shock** (15% chance to stun all targets in the immediate vicinity)

### Strategy

The Blight Wyrm is one of the most difficult enemies in the game, and should not be fought during the player's first venture into The Blight.

Because Arcanists have a low health pool and might easily be one-shot if they draw aggro, it's a good idea to bring a second Paladin to the fight. Double Debuffs such as  Glare of Soluna and  Theft of Vigor can also greatly decrease the Blight Wyrm's damage. Positioning is also greatly important to avoid Blight Wyrm's on-hit stun; the party's healer and damage dealers should avoid standing near the tank.

# Notes

The Blight Wyrm can be killed with  Wyrm's Bane if you are low level and dying to it, though this will prevent you from getting loot and experience.
