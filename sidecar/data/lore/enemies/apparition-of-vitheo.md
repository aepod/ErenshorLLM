Apparition of Vitheo is found in the final wing of Vitheo's Rest and is the final boss of the zone. He will heal to full health throughout the fight multiple times, signified by the following phrases:

1. So it's a battle then!
2. Yes! Let's try something else
3. You're worthy!

Once he yells 'You're worthy!' he enters the final phase and his damage increases until killed.
