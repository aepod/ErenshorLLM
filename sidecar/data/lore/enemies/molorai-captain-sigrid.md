# Appearance

Molorai Captain Sigrid is a bearded man in black armor with a red scar under his right eye. He is flanked by two Molorai Attack Dogs.

# Behavior

Molorai Captain Sigrid waits in his room at the back of the bunk area of the fort. Although he will not attack as soon as players open the door, he will become aggressive if players get too close or Provoke him or his dogs.

### Spells & Abilities

Molorai Captain Sigrid does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Molorai Captain Sigrid should not pose much of a threat for players who have managed to make it through the fort to him. To make the fight even easier, his dogs can be quickly dispatched once the fight begins or stunned using  Dazzle.
