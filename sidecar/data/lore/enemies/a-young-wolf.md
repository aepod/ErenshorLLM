**A Young Wolf** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

A Young Wolf are common throughout southern Erenshor and can be found in Hidden Hills, Vitheo's Watch, Faerie's Brake, Willowwatch Ridge and on Stowaway's Step.

# Appearance

A Young Wolf is a small pup with grey and white fur.

# Behavior

A Young Wolf will roam through grasslands and meadows until it senses a player to attack. Being adolescents, they are quite weak.

### Spells & Abilities

A Young Wolf does not use any spells or abilities.

# Notes

* There is a small chance for A Young Wolf to be replaced by an Alpha Wolf in both Hidden Hills and Faerie's Brake.
