# Location

A Lost Sea Giant can be found on the eastern cape of Vitheo's Watch, near the ruins.

# Appearance

A Lost Sea Giant is a ghostly blue apparition. A Lost Sea Giant can be male or female, with the female variants being three levels higher than that of the males. There is a **50%** chance for each variant to spawn.

# Behavior

A Lost Sea Giant roams the ruins on the coast. It will attack players if they get too close, or if it or a nearby Lost Sea Giant are provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

A Lost Sea Giant does not use any spells or abilities.
