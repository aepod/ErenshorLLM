# Location

There is an **80% chance** for The Lost Sea Giant King to replace the Lost Sea Giant on the far eastern cape of Vitheo's Watch.

# Appearance

The Lost Sea Giant King uses the same sprite as the male variants of Lost Sea Giants, but is slightly larger. He is a blue, semi-transparent apparition wearing a helmet, armor, and gloves. He can also occasionally be spotted wielding the Sea Giant's Cutlass.

# Behavior

The Lost Sea Giant King roams the ruins on the coast. It will attack players if they get too close, or if it or a nearby Lost Sea Giant are provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

The Lost Sea Giant King does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

The Lost Sea Giant King should not be much of a threat alone, but be careful not to get his attention too early while clearing out the regular mobs around him.
