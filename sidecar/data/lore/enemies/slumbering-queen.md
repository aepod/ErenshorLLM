# Location

Slumbering Queen is located in the Elderstone Mines, on the hill in the center of the glowing mushroom room near the Rottenfoot entrance. From the Blacksalt Strand entrance, head down the slope and turn right after the gap in the cave wall, then turn right again at the end of the path. Follow the cave to eventually reach the room with the Slumbering Queen.

# Appearance

Slumbering Queen is a large red spider with black streaks running across its legs and back.

# Behavior

Slumbering Queen sleeps on its mound in the center of the room. It will not attack players until it is woken up by being attacked or hit with a spell. Upon being woken up, nearby Fungal Drones will also attack the party.

### Spells & Abilities

Slumbering Queen does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

The main danger of fighting the Slumbering Queen are her nearby Fungal Drones, who will swarm to her aid when she is attacked. They should either be dealt with individually before fighting her, or players should be prepared with crowd control abilities like  Dazzle and  Sunburst to keep them from overwhelming the party.

# Notes

* Farming Slumbering Queen is an easy way to acquire Ancient Coal, which can be used in Crafting to make upgraded equipment.
