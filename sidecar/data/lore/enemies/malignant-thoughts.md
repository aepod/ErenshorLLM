# Location

Malignant Thoughts are unique to the second Mysterious Portal. One is found on the path through the dungeon, while three more are summoned during the fight with the Invader of Dreams.

# Appearance

Malignant Thoughts is a floating purple orb.

# Behavior

Malignant Thoughts are stationary and do not attack the player, though they can and should be attacked and destroyed themselves. They will summon either a Nightmarian Knight, Nightmarian Arcanist, or Nightmarian Hound five times in quick succession, as well as two more times at much longer interval. After seven enemies have been summoned, Malignant Thoughts becomes inactive.

### Spells & Abilities

Malignant Thoughts does not use any spells or abilities.

# Notes

* Although they will eventually stop summoning new enemies, it's still a good idea to take out Malignant Thoughts as quickly as possible to avoid the party being overwhelmed.
