# Location

Rotting Mummy is a rare variant of A Sorrowful Spirit and can occasionally replace them in the eastern part of the Island Tomb on Stowaway's Step, near where Failed Cremation spawns.

# Appearance

Rotting Mummy is a corpse covered in bandages.

# Behavior

Rotting Mummy stand in the dark corridors of the tomb, waiting to ambush unsuspecting adventurers. While they are unable to use any spells, their physical attacks are relatively strong compared to other enemies in the dungeon.

### Spells & Abilities

Rotting Mummy does not use any spells or abilities.
