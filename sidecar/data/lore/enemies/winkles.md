Winkles is a rare spawn in the western part of Rippers Keep. Look for the faerie camps around the pond. Winkles may replace a faerie in one of these camps; therefore, the best practice to get Winkles to spawn is to kill all the faeries, freeing up the spawn points for a chance for Winkles to spawn.
