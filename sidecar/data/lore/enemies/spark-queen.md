# Location

Spark Queen is located in the Lost Cellar. After entering the dungeon, simply go straight. Spark Queen can be found atop a small hill before the path slopes down towards the Cellars.

# Appearance

Spark Queen is a large insect with brown legs and red wings. It has two large horns and mandibles, as well as a brown exoskeleton running along its back.

# Behavior

Spark Queen waits atop its mound at the end of the cave. It will attack players if they get too close, or if the nearby Spark Soldiers are provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

Spark Queen does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Spark Queen should only be slightly more difficult than the normal insects players have faced leading up to it. It is also possible to pull the attention of the nearby insects if they wander far enough away, avoiding alerting the Spark Queen.  Dazzle or  Sunburst can also be used to help remove them from the fight.

# Notes

* Because of its close proximity to the Spark Royal, both bosses can quickly and easily be farmed simultaneously.
