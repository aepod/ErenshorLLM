**A Sorrowful Spirit** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

A Sorrowful Spirit can be found in the deeper parts of the Island Tomb2 on Stowaway's Step.

# Appearance

A Sorrowful Spirit is a small floating spirit in brown robes. While it has no head or legs, it does have two skeletal arms and hands extending from its robes.

# Behavior

A Sorrowful Spirit roams the tomb and will attack players if they get too close. They are physically weak, preferring to cast spells that both buff themselves and harm the player's party.
