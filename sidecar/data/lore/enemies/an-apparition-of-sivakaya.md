# Location

An Apparition of Sivakaya is found in the deepest part of The Bone Pits in front of the statue of Sivakaya.

# Appearance

An Apparition of Sivakaya is a large red demon, with limbs that appear to float in the air independent of each other.

# Behavior

An Apparition of Sivakaya will not move until it spots a player to attack or is provoked. Upon An Apparition of Sivakaya targeting a player, the surrounding Neophyte Necromancers will also become aggressive and swarm the party.

### Spells & Abilities

An Apparition of Sivakaya does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

An Arcanist's  Dazzle can help with crowd control. Additionally, it is possible to preemptively defeat Neophyte Necromancers without drawing the attention of An Apparition of Sivakaya if they wander far enough away from the altar.
