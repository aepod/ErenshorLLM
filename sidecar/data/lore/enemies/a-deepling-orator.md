# Location

A Deepling Orator has a **95% chance** to spawn in the Elderstone Mines on the path between the cliff overlooking the lake and the dead-end where Ravager, The First Infected can be found.

# Appearance

A Deepling Orator is a serpentine enemy wearing bangles on each wrist and a belt. It is larger than the other Deeplings, with dark blue hands and scales. Its belly is white.

# Behavior

A Deepling Orator roams the mines and will attack players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Defensive:

*  Minor Regeneration

Offensive:

*  Moonburst
*  Ethereal Rending
*  Jolt

# Strategy

A Deepling Orator uses a wide variety of offensive spells, so raising your Magic and Elemental Resists can greatly negate its damage. Be wary of adds joining the fight; players can either clear out nearby enemies before engaging A Deepling Orator, or else use crowd control like  Dazzle or  Sunburst to stun them.
