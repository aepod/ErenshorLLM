Overseer Orlok is a Boss in Erenshor.

# Appearance

Overseer Orlok is a tall man in green and red armor with a banner on his back. He is flanked by two Molorai Bodyguards.

# Behavior

Overseer Orlok waits in the tower at the top of the fort. Although he will not attack as soon as players open the door, players should still be cautious when entering the hallway as Overseer Orlok and his guards may attack from around the corner.

### Spells & Abilities

### Strategy

This fight is considerably more difficult than that with Sigrid, so players may wish to gain a few more levels before challenging Overseer Orlok.

Overseer Orlok will continually buff himself while idle, but will no longer cast these spells once combat begins and they will quickly wear off. While Overseer Orlok alone can already overwhelm an underprepared party, his bodyguards can be lethal at lower levels if they are not quickly stunned with  Dazzle or  Sunburst.
