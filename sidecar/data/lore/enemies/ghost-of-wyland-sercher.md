# Appearance

Ghost of Wyland Sercher is a glowing blue spirit wearing tattered brown clothes. There is a red glow from under his hood and mask, where only two white eyes are visible.

# Behavior

Ghost of Wyland Sercher waits in a back storage room of the tomb. Although he will not attack players the moment they open the door, his detection range is quite large and he may be notice the player's party even before they've entered the room.

### Spells & Abilities

Offensive:

*  Magic Bolt
*  Minor Lightning
*  Tangle

### Strategy

Ghost of Wyland Sercher will likely be the final boss players encounter before leaving Stowaway's Step. Although his health is not very high, his AC is a step up from anything players have fought before, making him slightly more difficult to hit. He has several spells in his arsenal, including Minor Lighting. This is an AoE spell that can damage the entire party, though it is fairly weak. A Paladin or Duelist can interrupt Ghost of Wyland Sercher while he is casting with a well-timed  Kick.
