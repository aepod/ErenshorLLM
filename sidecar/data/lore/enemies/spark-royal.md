# Location

Spark Royal is located in the Lost Cellar. After entering the dungeon, go straight and take a right at the fork. Spark Royal can be found in the dead end only slightly further down the path.

# Appearance

Spark Royal is a large insect with a blue body and purple legs. It has two large horns and mandibles, as well as a brown exoskeleton running along its back. It is guarded by two Spark Soldiers.

# Behavior

Spark Royal waits in a dead end towards the beginning of the cave. It will attack players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Spark Royal does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Although Spark Royal should not pose much of a threat to an adequately leveled and geared party, combined with its guards a Paladin might find themselves overwhelmed.  Dazzle or  Sunburst can momentarily take them out of the fight while they are killed or the party focuses on taking out the Spark Royal.

# Notes

* Although its stats are primarily geared towards magic users, Royal Carapace is an excellent and reliable early upgrade for shield-using Paladins.
* Because of its close proximity to the Spark Queen, both bosses can quickly and easily be farmed simultaneously.
