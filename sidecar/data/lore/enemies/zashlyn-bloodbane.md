# Location

Zashlyn Bloodbane can be found in The Bone Pits. After entering, go straight ahead into the ruins and turn left. Zashlyn Bloodbane will be in the center of the garden-like room.

# Appearance

Zashlyn Bloodbane is a necromancer with a long grey beard. He wears green robes.

# Behavior

Zashlyn Bloodbanes waits in the middle of the ruins, and will attack players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Offensive:

*  Weak Poison
*  Magic Bolt

### Strategy

Zashlyn Bloodbane is very similar to Woe in the Island Tomb on Stowaway's Step. A Paladin or Duelist can interrupt Zashlyn Bloodbane while he is casting with a well-timed  Kick.
