# Location

Spike has a **50% chance** to replace the center Nagalok against the giant rune at the back of the Nagalok Den in northeast Duskenlight Coast.

# Appearance

Spike is a large purple Nagalok with massive blue spikes protruding from its back, head, and arms.

# Behavior

Spike waits in the back of the cave. It will attack players if they get too close, or if it or the nearby Nagaloks are attacked or provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

Spike does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Spike is much more durable and hits much harder than the other Nagaloks in the cave. Players can either take out the two Nagaloks near him quickly, or stun them using  Dazzle or  Sunburst to help the party's tank more effectively block Spike's attacks.

# Notes

* Helm of the Deep is an excellent mid-game Helmet for a tank Paladin, making Spike quite worth seeking out.
