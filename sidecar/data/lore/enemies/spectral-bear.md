# Location

Spectral Bears can be found on Fernalla's Revival Plains and Duskenlight Coast.

# Appearance

Spectral Bear is a large green ethereal bear. It is semi-transparent.

# Behavior

Spectral Bear is the ghostly form of A Brown Bear. It will attack low-leveled players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Spectral Bear does not use any spells or abilities.

# Notes

* There is a small chance for Spectral Bear to be replaced by the Sylvan Giant on Fernalla's Revival Plains, and the Dusken Giant in Duskenlight Coast.
