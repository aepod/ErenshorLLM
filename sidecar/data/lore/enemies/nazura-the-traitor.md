Nazura the Traitor can be found in the second wing of Vitheo's Rest, he guards the Blackflame Brazier.

This boss is not always guaranteed to spawn. Sometimes a Cursed Vithean Conspirer, Cursed Vithean Traitor or Vithean Betrayer will appear in front of the Brazier instead.
