# Location

Alpha Wolf is a **rare** enemy, replacing A Young Wolf in Hidden Hills at a **25%** chance and in Faerie's Brake at a **12%** chance.

# Appearance

Alpha Wolf is a grey wolf, but is slightly larger than the younger wolf cubs.

# Behavior

Alpha Wolf roams amongst its pack, protecting the wolf cubs. It will attack players if they get too close, or if it or a nearby enemy are provoked.

### Spells & Abilities

Alpha Wolf does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Alpha Wolf is, oddly enough, weaker than the younger wolf cubs. It has less HP and AC than the younger varieties, and shouldn't be an issue for a character of any level to take down.
