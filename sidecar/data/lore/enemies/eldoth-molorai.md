# Location

Eldoth Molorai is found on the top floor of the building in the middle of Azynthi's Garden. His room is hidden behind a bookcase, which requires the  Intricate Key to open. This key is dropped by the Molorai Eldermistress, who can be found on the balcony on the opposite side of the building.

# Appearance

Eldoth Molorai is a tall man in finery and a large top hat. He wears brown boots and a red and black cape, which is clasped around his neck with a gold chain. Although it is unconfirmed, it is quite likely that Eldoth Molorai (and many of his followers) are vampiric in nature, further evidenced by his sharp teeth and innate lifesteal.

# Behavior

Eldoth Molorai hides in his secret library and will attack players when they enter. As players explore the Garden, Eldoth Molorai will also shout various messages, encouraging his supporters and taunting the player.

### Strategy

Fighting Eldoth Molorai at full strength is incredibly difficult and not recommended. Instead, players should seek out and destroy the four Flicker of Souls scattered throughout Azynthi's Garden before challenging him:

* Near the front steps of the castle.
* Inside a mausoleum in the graveyard.
* In a seemingly inaccessible section of the hedge maze.
* Sealed inside a room on the first floor of the castle.

Each of the Flickers are immune to damage until given a  Riftsnare Gem. These can be found by defeating Bosses in the Dimensional Rift version of Azynthi's Garden, reached by completing The Riftstone and acquiring the  Azynthian Riftstone:

* Grounds Keeper in the hedge maze, in front of the statue of Sivakaya.
* Vos Ashlyn in the graveyard.
* Exalted Firebone, on the second floor of the castle in the middle room.
* Oracle Miress, in the room to the left when facing Exalted Firebone.
* Oracle Tretha, on the balcony on the top floor of the castle.

Each Flicker of Souls destroyed weakens Eldoth Molorai, up to a maximum of four times. Players will need to work quickly, however, as they will respawn about 28 minutes (in a full group of 5) after they've been destroyed and subsequently grant Eldoth Molorai his power back.

When fighting Eldoth Molorai, be aware of his innate lifesteal: if your DPS is not high enough to counteract his healing, you will need to upgrade your gear or gain additional levels before challenging him again. Even after being weakened, Eldoth Molorai still hits hard, but does not use any special skills or abilities. With enough damage and a tank able to withstand his hits, Eldoth Molorai will eventually fall.

# Notes

* Players will need to neutralize the Flickers of Souls each time they challenge Eldoth Molorai if they wish to weaken him again.
* There is currently a known bug causing Eldoth Molorai to not grant any Experience or his related Steam Achievement when he is defeated after being fully weakened. This can be bypassed by only killing three of the four Flicker of Souls. Note that this will, however, cause Eldoth Molorai to be more difficult.
* Eldoth Molorai may occasionally sense players through the walls, floor, or ceiling of his room and attack without provocation.
* If Eldoth Molorai is respawned via a  Time Stone, all Flicker of Souls will respawn as well and must be destroyed to weaken him again.
