# Location

Monarch of the Flame is summoned by bringing a  Monarch of Flame to the Braxonian Game Board, located in the northwest corner of the Braxonian Desert.

# Appearance

Monarch of the Flame is a red skeletal figure cloaked in flames. He wears red armor, including bracers, greaves, and a spiked helmet.

# Behavior

Monarch of the Flame appears when his game piece is brought to the game board, but will not attack until players have engaged him in combat. For every **8%** of health lost, he will summon a Hand of the King to join the fight.

### Spells & Abilities

Monarch of the Flame does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Players should be very careful not to underestimate the guards Monarch of the Flame summons, as they can quickly overwhelm the party if too many are brought into the fight and not disposed of. Players should make sure they are set as the Main Assist, and immediately change targets once a Hand of the King is summoned to ensure all Simulated Players attack the guard, rather than potentially summon another by continuing to damage Monarch of the Flame. Continue whittling down Monarch of the Flame in this way little by little, and he will eventually fall.

# Notes

* Hand of the King is a basic copy of Kingsman, although it has about half the health. They can drop all of the equipment Kingsman can at a much lower rate, and may also potentially drop another Monarch of Flame game piece, allowing players to potentially fight Monarch of the Flame several times in a row.
