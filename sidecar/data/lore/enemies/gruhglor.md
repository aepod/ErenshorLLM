**Gruhglor** is a Boss in Erenshor.

# Location

Gruhglor is found on a plateau in northern Fernalla's Revival Plains near the entrance to Rottenfoot.

# Appearance

Gruhglor is a large golem made of brown and grey stones. He is also covered in several wreaths of vines and moss.

# Behavior

Gruhglor roams the plateau above the encampment of Rottenfoot Warriors and Rottenfoot Swampwalkers and will attack players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Gruhglor does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Gruhglor is likely one of the first bosses players will encounter with a summoning mechanic: when he reaches 60% and 20% health, he will spawn two Rock Minions nearby. These additional enemies should be taken out or stunned quickly, as they will reduce the ability of the party's Paladin to effectively tank.

Gruhglor is one of two bosses that drop the level 3 class-specific Aura items, making him well worth farming.1
