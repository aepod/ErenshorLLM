# Location

Vithean Elementals can be found exclusively on the beaches of Duskenlight Coast.

# Appearance

Vithean Elementals are blue spheres of water. They are the elemental embodiment of both air and sea, given form.

# Behavior

Vithean Elementals bounce across the sand until sensing a target to attack. Alone they do not pose much of a threat, but their close proximity to each other means that players may end up fighting multiple at once if they aren't careful.

### Spells & Abilities

Vithean Elemental does not use any spells or abilities.

# Notes

* Vithean Elemental can rarely be replaced with the Greater Vithean Elemental.
