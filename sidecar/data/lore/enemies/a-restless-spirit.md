**A Restless Spirit** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

A Restless Spirit can be found throughout the Island Tomb on Stowaway's Step.

# Appearance

A Restless Spirit is a floating green ghost. It is semi-transparent and wears an ethereal helmet on what appears to be a skeletal head.

# Behavior

A Restless Spirit roams the halls of the tomb and will attack players if they get too close. They can often be found around corners, waiting to ambush unsuspecting adventurers.

### Spells & Abilities

A Restless Spirit does not use any spells or abilities.
