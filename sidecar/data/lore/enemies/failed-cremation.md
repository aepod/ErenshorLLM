# Appearance

Failed Cremation is a skeleton, likely reanimated by the necromancers in the tomb who failed to control him.

# Behavior

Failed Cremation waits in a back room of the tomb. He has no detection range, meaning players will not attract his attention unless they initiate the fight or get immediately next to him.

### Spells & Abilities

Offensive:

*  Theft of Life

### Strategy

Failed Cremation is the second most difficult boss in the tomb, though he still does not pose much of a challenge. His AC is relatively high compared to most other enemies in the dungeon, making him slightly more difficult to hit. Additionally, Theft of Life can heal back some of Failed Cremation's health, though a Paladin or Duelist can interrupt Failed Cremation while he is casting with a well-timed  Kick.
