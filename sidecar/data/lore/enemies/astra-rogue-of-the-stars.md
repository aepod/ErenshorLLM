# Location

Astra, Rogue of the Stars appears in the central crater of Soluna's Landing after all nine Stardusts have been defeated.

# Appearance

Astra is a giant blue dragon with red eyes and large black horns.

# Behavior

Astra appears in a brilliant flash of white light after the last Stardust has been destroyed, and will attack players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

On-Hit:

*  Brax's Fury ( **20%** chance)

Offensive:

* **Cosmic Breath** (Base **2,000** damage AoE)

### Strategy

Summoning Astra, Rogue of the Stars requires players to give each of the nine Stardusts scattered throughout the crater a  Lost Faith to make them vulnerable, and then defeat them. Once the last Stardust has fallen, Astra will immediately spawn in the center of the crater.

Astra is one of the most difficult enemies in the game; players should be at or around level 35 and have several pieces of Blessed end-game gear before attempting to face her. Resistances are especially important, as Cosmic Breath's damage can be greatly lowered by stacking Magic and Elemental resistances.

Because Arcanists have a low health pool and might easily be one-shot by Astra, one strategy is to bring a second Paladin to the fight. Double Debuffs such as  Glare of Soluna and  Theft of Vigor can greatly decrease Astra's damage.

# Notes

* Defeating Astra, Rogue of the Stars is required to complete Raval's Last Wish.

* If Astra, Rogue of the Stars is respawned via a  Time Stone, all Stardust will respawn as well and must be destroyed again.
