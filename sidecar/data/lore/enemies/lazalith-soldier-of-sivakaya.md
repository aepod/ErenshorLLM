**Lazalith, Soldier of Sivakaya** is a Boss in Erenshor.

# Location

Lazalith, Soldier of Sivakaya can be found in a secret courtyard behind the hidden church in Blacksalt Strand. Accessing the church requires the  Old Church Key from completing The Mathers' Demise. Once inside, head straight down the hallway and click the wall the statue of Sivakaya faces to reveal the passage to Lazalith.

# Appearance

Lazalith is a demon with large horns, blue and yellow armor, and an emaciated, skeletal body.

# Behavior

Lazalith, Soldier of Sivakaya waits in the courtyard behind the church and will attack players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Lazalith, Soldier of Sivakaya does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Lazalith isn't particularly noteworthy as it doesn't use any special abilities and has relatively low strength, although its durability can make the fight take longer than usual. Defeating Lazalith is required in order to complete The Mathers' Demise, Part 2.
