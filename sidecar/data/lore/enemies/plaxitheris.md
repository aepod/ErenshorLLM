# Location

Plaxitheris can be found atop a hill on the northern cliffs of Stowaway's Step, past Treven Pines' shop and through the stone archway.

# Appearance

Plaxitheris is a Mature Malaroth that has somehow ended up on Stowaway's Step. It has sharp teeth, two large horns, and spikes protruding from nearly everywhere on its body.

# Behavior

Plaxitheris waits atop a rock on top of the hill. His detection range is rather large, and he will attack players should they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

Plaxitheris does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Plaxitheris has strong physical attacks and can easily take down an underleveled or undergeared tank faster than they can be healed. Make sure your Paladin is well-equipped before attempting to take on Plaxitheris, or consider backing them up with multiple party members assisting with healing.

# Notes

* Plaxitheris drops A Moongill, which can be used to complete Wally's Moongill if the player does not wish to spend time trying to Fish for it.
