# Location

A Deepling Verdite can be found throughout the central caves of the Elderstone Mines.

# Appearance

A Deepling Verdite is a serpentine enemy wearing bangles on each wrist and a belt. As the name implies, its body is a deep green. It has sharp claws and a forked tongue.

# Behavior

A Deepling Verdite roams the mines and will attack players if they get too close. They are physically weaker than the Deepling Crimsonites, but make up for this by casting a small variety of spells.
