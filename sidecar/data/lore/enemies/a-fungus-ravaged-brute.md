# Location

A Fungus Ravaged Brute has a **25% chance** to replace A Deepling Brute along the path to Ravager, The First Infected in the Elderstone Mines.

# Appearance

A Fungus Ravaged Brute is dark green with red and pink pustules dotting its body. It also has several large gashes along its body, seemingly stitched together with roots.

# Behavior

A Fungus Ravaged Brute stands guard in the caves and will attack players if they get too close. It is physically slightly stronger than the Deepling Brutes it replaces, but is otherwise rather unremarkable.

### Spells & Abilities

A Fungus Ravaged Brute does not use any spells or abilities.
