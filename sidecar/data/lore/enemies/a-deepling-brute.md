# Location

A Deepling Brute can be found in the Elderstone Mines, along the path to Ravager, The First Infected.3 Additionally, a single Deepling Brute spawns past the lake near Gloopa.

# Appearance

A Deepling Brute is humanoid in appearance, wearing fur-lined sleeves, brown boots, and a green and brown kilt.

# Behavior

A Deepling Brute stands guard in the mines and will attack players if they get too close.

### Spells & Abilities

A Deepling Brute does not use any spells or abilities.
