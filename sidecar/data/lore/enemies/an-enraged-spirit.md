**An Enraged Spirit** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

An Enraged Spirit can be found throughout the Island Tomb on Stowaway's Step.

# Appearance

An Enraged Spirit is a floating red ghost resembling a woman. It is semi-transparent and wears an ethereal flowing dress.

# Behavior

An Enraged Spirit stand at various points in the tomb and will attack players if they get too close. They can often be found around corners and behind doors, waiting to ambush unsuspecting adventurers.
