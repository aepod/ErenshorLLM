**A Mature Grass Spider** is an enemy in Erenshor.

# Location

A Mature Grass Spider is a common pest and can be found throughout most of northern Erenshor, including Fernalla's Revival Plains, Blacksalt Strand,1 Ripper's Keep, Malaroth's Nesting Grounds, and Azynthi's Garden. It can also be found on the fields of Vitheo's Watch.

# Appearance

A Mature Grass Spider has black appendages, a green body, and multiple eyes. It is the larger adult version of A Grass Spider.

# Behavior

A Mature Grass Spider will roam through grasslands and meadows until it senses a target to attack. Although they are larger than their younger brood, they are still quite weak and shouldn't pose much of a challenge to players by the time they reach the zones A Mature Grass Spider can be found in.

### Spells & Abilities

A Mature Grass Spider does not use any spells or abilities.
