Located in the rocky northern mountains which is reached by a gigantic slope in the central area of the Braxonian Desert.

To Kill Evadne you need the Tempestalia, Ring of Storms from the quest chain Orion Mycon's Lost Ring. The effect from the ring allows you to put out the brazier making her vulnerable.
