**Goliath** is a Boss in Erenshor.

# Location

Goliath is found in Blacksalt Strand, patrolling the mountains between the entrances to the Braxonian Desert and Elderstone Mines.

# Appearance

Goliath is a large ape. It is a much bigger version of Seaside Gorilla with brown fur and orange eyes.

# Behavior

Goliath roams the mountains and will attack players or NPCs in its vicinity.

### Spells & Abilities

Goliath does not use any spells or abilities.

### Strategy

Players wandering into Blacksalt Strand for the first time may be underleveled for a fight with Goliath, making him a dangerous foe to encounter due to his durability and high attack strength. Because he will aggro onto nearby NPCs, players can get the nearby Azure Guard to assist in the battle by dragging the boss near him. He is one of two bosses that drop the level 3 class-specific Aura items, making him well worth farming.1
