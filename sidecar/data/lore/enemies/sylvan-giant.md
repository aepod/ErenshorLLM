# Location

Sylvan Giant is a **rare** enemy with a **1% chance** to replace Brown Bear Cubs, Brown Bears, Spectral Bears, and Dryad Spirits in the western part of Fernalla's Revival Plains. There are 13 possible spawn locations for the Sylvan Giant in Fernalla's Revival Plains, for a **12.2%** total chance to spawn each time you enter the Zone.

Sylvan Giant can also spawn in Silkengrass Meadowlands at a **25% chance** at a single point in front of the shrine where Edgar Taether can be found.

# Appearance

Sylvan Giant is a large demi-human with light skin and a grey beard. He wears a sash across his chest, a horned helmet on his head, and a green and brown kilt. As the name implies, Sylvan Giant is massive and can easily be spotted from afar when entering the zone.

# Behavior

Sylvan Giants roam the landscape until sensing a target to attack. Not only are they aggressive to players, but they will also attack other enemies in their immediate vicinity.

### Spells & Abilities

Offensive:

*  Tangle
*  Biting Vines

### Strategy

Finding Sylvan Giant is half the battle, and may require players clearing out low-level spawns multiple times, or else repeatedly reloading the zone in an attempt to force him to spawn. Despite his high level, Sylvan Giant only uses low-level spells and can easily be tanked by a well-geared Paladin at a much lower level, allowing players to take him on relatively early.

# Notes

* It is possible for multiple Sylvan Giants to be present at the same time.
* Because Sylvan Giant is aggressive and will fight other enemies in the same zone, it is possible for him to be killed without any effort by the player. This is most likely to occur if a Sylvan Giant spawns near the Spectres in Fernalla's Revival Plains. While players will not receive any experience, they can still loot his corpse to receive high-end gear very early in the game.
