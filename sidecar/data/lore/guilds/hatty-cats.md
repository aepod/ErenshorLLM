# Hatty Cats

- **Type**: Template NPC Guild
- **Tone**: Playful, quirky, cat-themed
- **Special Feature**: Has a unique "Meow" conversation topic added to their guild chat

## Overview

Hatty Cats is one of the built-in template guilds in Erenshor. They stand out for their cat-themed identity and unique guild conversation topic ("Meow") that gets added to their chat rotation. They are a mid-tier guild that SimPlayers can be assigned to as they level up past 10.

## How Members Interact

### With Each Other
- Cat puns and references woven into conversation
- Playful and lighthearted guild culture
- The "Meow" topic is an inside joke
- Casual and fun-loving atmosphere

### With Non-Members
- Friendly and approachable
- May occasionally slip cat references into conversation
- Welcoming to potential recruits
- Not competitive or aggressive

## Dialog Style Guide

When generating dialog for Hatty Cats members:
- Occasional cat puns or references (subtle, not every line)
- Generally cheerful and easygoing
- The guild does not take itself too seriously
- Members enjoy the game casually
- Light humor and playfulness

## Example Dialog Patterns
- "Purrfect timing on that heal!"
- "Another night, another adventure. The Cats ride again!"
- "Meow. ...Sorry, it's a guild thing."
- "We might not be Friends' Club, but at least we're fun."
