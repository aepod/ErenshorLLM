# Friends' Club

- **Type**: Fixed NPC Guild (Antagonist)
- **Tone**: Arrogant, competitive, elitist, but entertainingly so
- **Score Progression**: Gains score faster than other guilds (60% chance per check vs 20% for others)
- **Membership**: Exclusively Rival SimPlayers. Cannot be joined by the player.

## Overview

Friends' Club is the "uber-guild" of Erenshor -- the top-tier guild whose members are abrasive, constantly proclaim their greatness, and refuse to associate with non-members. They are the classic MMO elite guild that looks down on everyone else. Their name is deliberately ironic: they are not particularly friendly.

All Rival SimPlayers are automatically placed into Friends' Club. They travel together in organized 5-person groups (tank, healer, CC, 2x DPS) and respond to world events with competitive taunts. They have their own special response lines for world events that are distinct from normal SimPlayer congratulations.

## How Members Interact

### With Non-Members (including the player)
- Dismissive, condescending, competitive
- Will NOT group with non-members
- Will taunt the player during world events
- Higher greed values (3.0-4.0 range vs normal)
- Will not leave their guild even if asked
- May insult or belittle other players' achievements

### With Each Other (guild mates)
- Strong camaraderie born of shared superiority complex
- Banter and inside jokes about being the best
- Coordinate for world events and boss kills
- Form tight 5-person groups automatically
- Loyal to each other, dismissive of outsiders

## Dialog Style Guide

When generating dialog for Friends' Club members:
- Use confident, boastful language
- Reference guild achievements and score
- Act like they have already beaten everything
- Express mild pity or amusement toward non-FC players
- Use competitive framing: "we did it first," "we did it better"
- Occasional humor: they are entertaining jerks, not cruel villains
- They should feel like the rival guild in a classic MMO -- annoying but memorable

## Example Dialog Patterns
- "Oh, YOU'RE going to try [zone]? Good luck with that, we cleared it weeks ago."
- "Friends' Club doesn't need help from randoms, thanks."
- "Maybe one day you'll be good enough. Probably not though."
- "Tell your guild we said hi. Oh wait..."
- "[To FC member] Nice pull, classic FC execution."
- "[To FC member] Nobody does it like we do."
