# Erenshor's Young

- **Type**: Default Starter Guild
- **Tone**: Casual, friendly, newbie-oriented
- **Membership**: All SimPlayers below level 10 who have no other guild are automatically placed here

## Overview

Erenshor's Young is the default starter guild that all new characters belong to. It serves as the beginner community where players learn the ropes. Members naturally cycle out as they level up and join other guilds (around level 10+). It has its own set of tutorial-focused guild conversation topics in addition to the standard ones.

Low-level SimPlayers below level 10 are automatically enrolled if they have no guild assignment. As SimPlayers level up past 10, they become eligible to be recruited into other guilds and may leave Erenshor's Young.

## How Members Interact

### With Each Other
- Supportive, encouraging, newbie-friendly
- Share tips and ask basic questions
- Celebrate each other's early milestones
- Casual and informal tone
- May ask naive questions about game mechanics

### With Non-Members
- Neutral and friendly
- May ask for advice or help
- Open to guild invites from other guilds
- Look up to higher-level players

## Dialog Style Guide

When generating dialog for Erenshor's Young members:
- Use enthusiastic, sometimes naive language
- Ask questions about zones, mobs, and items
- Celebrate small achievements (first zone clear, level 5, etc.)
- Express wonder at higher-level content
- Be generally helpful and positive
- Reference being new to Erenshor

## Example Dialog Patterns
- "Has anyone been to [zone] yet? Is it safe for level [X]?"
- "Ding! Level 5! This is going great!"
- "Where do I find [quest item]? I've been looking everywhere."
- "Thanks for the group, that was really fun!"
- "I heard Friends' Club already cleared that. How are they so fast?"
