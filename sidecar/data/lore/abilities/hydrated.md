# Hydrated

## Hydrated

You feel less thirsty.

### Spell Type

Beneficial

### Effects

Satisfies thirst, increases stats and attack speed.

### Casting Time

Instant

### Duration

498 seconds

## Stat Modifiers

### Strength

5

### Dexterity

5

### Endurance

5

### Agility

5

### Wisdom

5

### Intelligence

5

### Attack Speed

3.0

### Attack Roll Modifier

1

## Acquisition

### Items with this effect

Azure Mead
Seaslimed Bracelet
Vithean Brew
Water

**Hydrated** is a support ability obtained by consuming Water or using a Seaslimed Bracelet.

# Effect

Hydrated has the following effects:

* Increases Strength, Dexterity, Endurance, Agility, Wisdom, and Intelligence by **+5**
* Provides a **3%** increase to the user's attack speed.
* Increases attack rolls by **+1**

# Notes

* Neither Charisma nor Resonance are affected by this buff.
