# Imbued Arrow

## Imbued Arrow

### Spell Type

Ranged

### Classes

Stormcaller (1)

### Effects

Fires an arrow, if fired while casting a spell, the spell instantly completes but gains a 3x penalty to cooldown and the arrow will have a 100% chance to also proc that spell

### Casting Time

Instant

### Cooldown

2 seconds

### Target Damage

3
