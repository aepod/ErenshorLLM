# Affinity for Suffering

# Affinity for Suffering

## Affinity for Suffering

You feel a desire for pain

### Spell Type

Beneficial

### Classes

Reaver (1)

### Effects

For every 10% of missing life, you gain 2% haste. This effect is applied after all other haste sources are counted. The haste cap does not apply.

### Mana Cost

20

### Casting Time

2.0 seconds

### Duration

900 seconds

### Cooldown

9 seconds

## Acquisition

### Taught by

Spell Scroll: Affinity for Suffering
