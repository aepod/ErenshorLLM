# Quest for Suffering

## Quest for Suffering

You feel a desire for pain

### Spell Type

Beneficial

### Classes

Reaver (16)

### Effects

For every 10% of missing life, you gain 4% haste. This effect is applied after all other haste sources are counted. The haste cap does not apply.

### Mana Cost

450

### Casting Time

2.0 seconds

### Duration

900 seconds

### Cooldown

9 seconds

## Acquisition

### Taught by
