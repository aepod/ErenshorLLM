# Portal to Reliquary

## Portal to Reliquary

You step into a conjured portal

### Spell Type

Misc

### Effects

Teleport you and your party to Port Azure's Reliquary, and leave behind a portal in the world that you can return to from the Reliquary.

### Mana Cost

500

### Casting Time

4.5 seconds

## Acquisition

### Items with this effect

Box of Portals
