**Nourished** is a beneficial Status Effect obtained through a wide variety of Consumable items.

# Effect

Nourished has the following effects:

* Increases Hit Points by **+25**
* Increases Mana by **+25**
* Increases Movement Speed by **+1**

# Notes

* Bread is a universal common drop from all enemies, making it an easy way to keep this buff active.
