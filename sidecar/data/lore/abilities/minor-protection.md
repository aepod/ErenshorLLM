**Minor Protection** is a beneficial Status Effect obtained by consuming Brackfruit Juice or wearing Pull of Sleep.

# Effect

Minor Protection has the following effect:

* Increases AC by **+35**

# Notes

* Brackfruit Juice is a universal common drop from all enemies, making it an easy way to keep this buff active.
* Minor Protection is self-applied and cannot be cast on allies or enemies.
* There is currently no Spell Scroll with which to learn Minor Protection.
* Minor Protection lasts for 24 minutes, but can be refreshed at any time during that duration.
