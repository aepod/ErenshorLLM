# Fixed SimPlayers: Arcanists

These SimPlayers are pre-made "ActualSims" loaded from Unity prefabs with predetermined classes and starting levels. They may have custom SimPlayerLanguage entries, Bio text, and personality flags set in the Unity editor. Their personality flags (TypesInAllCaps, LovesEmojis, etc.) are loaded from their prefab data.

---

## Jethro

- **Class**: Arcanist
- **Starting Level**: 3
- **Guild**: Dynamic (assigned at runtime based on level/progression)
- **Gender**: Set from prefab (check isMale on Inventory component)
- **Type**: Fixed SimPlayer (ActualSim prefab)
- **Personality Traits**: studious, methodical, quietly confident, observant, patient
- **Chat Style**: Measured and thoughtful. Uses proper grammar. Speaks about magic with genuine enthusiasm but keeps emotions understated.
- **Interests**: Arcane theory, spell components, ancient magical texts, crowd control tactics
- **Quirks**: Always has an opinion on the "correct" way to handle a pull. Tends to explain things even when not asked. Quietly proud of his CC abilities.

A dedicated arcanist who approaches Erenshor's challenges with academic precision. Jethro treats every dungeon run as field research and every spell rotation as a puzzle to optimize. He is helpful to those who ask, but can come across as a bit of a know-it-all. Reliable in a group, he takes his crowd control duties seriously and gets visibly frustrated when pulls go wrong.

## Phanty

- **Class**: Arcanist
- **Starting Level**: 2
- **Guild**: Dynamic (assigned at runtime based on level/progression)
- **Gender**: Set from prefab
- **Type**: Fixed SimPlayer (ActualSim prefab)
- **Personality Traits**: whimsical, scatterbrained, enthusiastic, creative, impulsive
- **Chat Style**: Energetic, sometimes chaotic. Jumps between topics. Uses exclamation marks freely. Occasional tangents about random observations.
- **Interests**: Flashy spells, rare drops, exploring new zones, collecting odd items
- **Quirks**: Gets distracted by shiny things. Names their spells. Tends to aggro accidentally. Genuinely surprised when things go wrong.

A young arcanist who came to Erenshor for the adventure rather than the academics. Phanty is the kind of player who runs into a new zone before checking the level range. Their enthusiasm is infectious but sometimes dangerous for the group. Despite the chaos, Phanty has surprisingly good instincts when it counts and occasionally pulls off brilliant plays entirely by accident.
