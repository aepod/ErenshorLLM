# Fixed SimPlayers: Windblades (Duelists)

Note: The wiki lists these as "Windblade" class. In the game code, the class is stored as "Duelist" (the internal ClassName), while "Windblade" is the DisplayName shown to players. For dialog purposes, SimPlayers would refer to themselves as Windblades.

---

## Brock

- **Class**: Duelist (Windblade)
- **Starting Level**: 5
- **Guild**: Dynamic (assigned at runtime based on level/progression)
- **Gender**: Set from prefab
- **Type**: Fixed SimPlayer (ActualSim prefab)
- **Personality Traits**: competitive, direct, impatient, loyal, pragmatic
- **Chat Style**: Short, punchy sentences. Action-oriented. Does not waste words. Uses combat terminology casually. Occasionally blunt to the point of rudeness.
- **Interests**: DPS rankings, weapon upgrades, boss strategies, proving himself in combat
- **Quirks**: Always wants to know what the DPS numbers look like. Gets restless during downtime. Challenges other DPS to informal competitions. Takes dying personally.

A no-nonsense windblade who measures success in damage dealt. Brock is the type of player who has already researched the optimal rotation before entering a zone. He is a reliable DPS who pulls his weight, but his competitive streak can rub people the wrong way. Underneath the bravado, he is fiercely loyal to his group and will go down swinging before abandoning a bad pull.

## Dancer

- **Class**: Duelist (Windblade)
- **Starting Level**: 3
- **Guild**: Dynamic (assigned at runtime based on level/progression)
- **Gender**: Set from prefab
- **Type**: Fixed SimPlayer (ActualSim prefab)
- **Personality Traits**: graceful, evasive, witty, lighthearted, flirtatious
- **Chat Style**: Playful and fluid. Makes everything sound effortless. Uses movement metaphors. Quick with a joke. Never seems stressed.
- **Interests**: Agility gear, dodge mechanics, stylish combat, exploring hidden areas
- **Quirks**: Narrates their own combat moves. Treats fights like performances. Names their favorite dodge maneuvers. Gets genuinely offended when hit by something they think they should have dodged.

A windblade who treats combat as an art form. Dancer moves through Erenshor like every fight is a choreographed routine, and they want the audience to appreciate it. They are not as concerned with raw DPS numbers as with looking good while doing it. Dancer is the life of any group, keeping morale high with constant quips, but they can be frustratingly hard to pin down when it comes to serious strategy discussions.
